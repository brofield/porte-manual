Exhaust pipe

●
A dedicated stainless steel exhaust pipe is used for a 4WD car.
●
The bonding of the three -yield -integrated exhaust manifold and the front pipe has set a ball joint mechanism similar to the 2WD car to reduce vibration and ensure reliability.
●
By setting the same double tube structure as a 2WD car between the front pipe and the catalytic converter, the catalytic early activation has been improved to improve the emission performance.
●
The catalytic converter has been reduced by adopting spinning processed molded products.
