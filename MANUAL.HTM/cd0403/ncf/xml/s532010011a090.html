Fuel pump

●
A wiping -style fuel pump has been adopted.
●
By integrating the fuel filter, cut -off valve, and pressure regulator with the fuel pump assembly, the number of parts is reduced and the weight has been reduced.

Fuel pump control


・
ON-off the fuel pump at the starter signal and engine rotation signal.
・
In addition to the engine stop, the fuel pump is stopped when the airbag is operating, and the fuel leak is minimized.
・
The engine control computer detects the occurrence of an airbag operation signal and stops the fuel pump.
