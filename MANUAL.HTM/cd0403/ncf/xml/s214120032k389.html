Related parts


　Data communication module

●
The data communication module is a dedicated high -speed communication module that wirelessly sends and receive G -BOOK networks and various information.(It does not support voice communication.) In addition, the mounting position is the floor panel at the bottom of the passenger seat.
　Data communication antenna

●
It is a glass paste antenna compatible with 800MHz band.The pasting position is the upper center of the wind shield glass.
