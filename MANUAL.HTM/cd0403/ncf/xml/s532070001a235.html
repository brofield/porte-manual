Energy absorption mechanism


・
The steering column is fixed to the steering support part of the instrument panel rein horsement with a rewer bracket and a break -wake bracket.When the driver is shocked by the steering wheel, the steering wheels and wheel pads absorb energy, the steering support color of the rower bracket is damaged, leaving a steering column bracket spacer and a break -wafe bracket, and the steering column tube is in front of the vehicle.Move.At this time, the energy of the impact is absorbed by deforming the EA plate placed in the lover bracket.At that time, the rower bracket is separated to move the steering column.
・
The impact from the steering gear direction is absorbed by the shrinking mechanism adopted in the interlaider shaft.
