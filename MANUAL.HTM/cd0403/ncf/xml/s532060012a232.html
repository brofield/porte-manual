Skid control computer

●
A skid control computer processes signals from each sensor to control ABS, TRC, VSC and brake assist.In addition, communicate with the engine control computer and output the control signal.

VSC control


・
When the vehicle status is determined from the information of the wheel speed sensor, yolly sensor, linear G sensor, and the steering sensor, and the vehicle is determined to be a rear -wheel sliding trend or the front wheel slide tendency, the brake hydraulic in each wheel according to the degree of the trend.Control, engine fuel cut control, etc.


TRC control


・
When the driving wheel starts to slip on a slippery road, the wheel speed of the drive wheels does not slip, greatly higher than the estimated vehicle speed calculated from the wheel speed.The TRC control performs brake control and engine control of both wheels due to the size of the slip.


Initial check function


・
After turning on the ignition switch, when the vehicle speed is about 6 km/h or higher, the solenoid valve and motor of the brake actuator are activated sequentially to perform electrical checks.In addition, during the initial check, the sound of the solenoid valve and the motor is sounded in the engine room, but it is not abnormal.
