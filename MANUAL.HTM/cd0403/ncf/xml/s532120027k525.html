Manual leveling function

●
The same manual leveling function is adopted as a car equipped with a halogen head lamp and a vehicle with a conventional discharge head lamp.The head lamp leveling actuator is driven by the state signal of the headlamp leveling switch in the indoor headlamp (top and the lowest direction at level 5), and the direction of the optical axis is changed by moving the direction of the head lamp reciprocal in the up and down direction.I will do it.
●
The mounting position and function of the headlamp leveling switch / head lamp leveling actuator are the same as the conventional discharge head lamp equipped.
　■ Attention ■
　
Usually, use the head lamp leveling switch at a level 0 position.
If the irradiation range of the headlamp is higher than usual due to loading of luggage or boarding the crew, etc., operate the switch according to the condition of the luggage and crew, and turn the optical axis of the headlamp down.Also, after removing the luggage or crew, be sure to return the switch to level 0.
For optical axis adjustment, perform the head lamp leveling switch at a level 0.
