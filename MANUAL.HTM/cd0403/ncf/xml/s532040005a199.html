Full -time 4WD summary

●
Flex full -time 4WD has a differential -driped coupling with a differential response type on the propeller shaft, and if there is a rotation difference between the front and rear wheels, the driving force is immediately transmitted to the rear wheel.Realizes stable maneuverability and stability on various roads.In addition, the rotary revised coupling also absorbs the rotation speed difference of the front and rear wheels such as when turning.
●
Flex full -time 4WD does not require center dialogue and differential restriction mechanisms in the Transxle part, simplify the system, and the driving force distribution of the rear wheels is small, so the transfers and rear falentials can be minimized.The 4WD system can be reduced.

●
If the rotation number difference between the front and rear wheels is small, such as when traveling straight, the rotation of the rotary librade coupling is small and is running close to the FF.
●
If there is a rotation number difference in the front and rear wheels, such as cornering, snowy roads, climbing, starting, acceleration, etc., the rotary triprade coupling acts, the driving force is transmitted to the rear wheel, and it is in a 4WD state.。
