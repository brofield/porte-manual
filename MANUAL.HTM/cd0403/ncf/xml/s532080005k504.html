Outlet control

●
In addition to manual control, an auto control that automatically controls the mode door switching servo motor has been adopted.
Manual control


・
Switch to the outlet according to the operation position of the mode switching switch.

Mode door switching servo motor control


・
By turning on the Auto switch of the air conditioner control panel, the mode door switching servo motor is rotated to any position according to the target door opening calculated by the required spray temperature (TAO).Further, by detecting the actual door opening by detecting the actual door opening with a potenthawometer in the sarbo motor in the mode door switching for the calculated target door opening, the target door opening and the actual door opening as shown in FIG.Control to match the degree.


At low temperature FOOT/DEF control


・
When the following conditions (A or B) are established during warm -up control, the outlet mode is switched to Foot/DEF, preventing the cloudiness of the window at low temperatures.
