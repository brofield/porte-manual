Vane pump & reservoir

●
A small and lightweight vane pump with a built -in flow control valve has been adopted.
●
The reservoir is a pump -up type.As a result, the number of parts such as hydraulic piping and reservoir brackets has been reduced, and the structure is simplified and lighter.
●
A waterproof cover has been set on the reservoir cap to support the low position of the reservoir.
●
The vane pump is made of aluminum die -cast housing and steel plot -made pulley to reduce weight.The sliding resistance has been reduced by shortening the rotation radius and width of the vane.As a result, low -torque is possible, and fuel consumption has been reduced.The optimization of the cam curve reduced the amount of leakage inside the oil to suppress hydraulic pulsation and reduce vibration.
