Hot wire type airflow meter

●
A small and lightweight hot wire type plug -in type airflow meter has been adopted.Along with this, the air cleaner was mounted on the upper side of the transmission, miniaturized, and reduced weight and compact.
●
The hot wire type plug -in type airflow meter has been pierced to the detection portion and directly measured the rate of mass flow to improve detection accuracy and reduce intake resistance.
●
The bypass -style measurement is not easily affected by the air cleaner from the air cleaner.In addition, a low loss is realized by the flow path structure with low fluid resistance, and a large amount of air can be measured from a small amount from a small amount.
Hot wire type airflow meter operation


・
Bypass a part of the inhaled air and measure the amount of inhaled air with the heat ray measuring part.The heat ray measurement unit uses a platinum filament (platinum heat wire), and the bridge circuit is configured with the resistance for the intake temperature measurement and the heating of heating resistance (heater) to measure the inhaled air of the engine.Due to the characteristics of the hot wire type airflow meter, the quality flow rate can be measured directly directly, so there is no need for density correction for changes in the intake temperature, but engine control such as EFI requires information on intake temperature.For this reason, a compact thermister -type intake temperature sensor is built in the airflow meter, and the intake temperature is detected.
