If an abnormality occurs in the system, the driver will be warned by lighting the lamp.In addition, when the ignition switch is turned on, it turns on for about 3 seconds for the lamp valve check, and then turns off.

Warnin Grand Pu


ABS Warnin Grand Pu
The driver warns the lamp lighting that the ABS or brake assist system is abnormal.
Brake Warnin Grand Pu
In addition to the warning of normal parking brakes on and when the brake fluid decreases, the driver warns that abnormalities that cannot be operated not only ABS but also EBD due to simultaneous lighting with the ABS Warnin Gramp.
VSC Warning Lump
If an abnormality occurs in the VSC system, the driver will be notified by the lamp lighting.Also, in the diagnodes mode, the diagnostic code is displayed by blinking the lamp.
Slip indicator lamp
Displays the driver by flashing the lamp that TRC and VSC are being operated.
