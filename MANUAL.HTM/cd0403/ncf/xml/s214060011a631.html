Related parts


　Front wheel speed sensor & rotor

●
The front wheel speed sensor was attached to the left and right front wheel steering knuckles.The sensor rotor was attached to the outer periphery of the drive shaft and the number of teeth was 48.The wheel speed sensor is composed of magnets and coils, and when the sensor rotor cells rotate, the magnetic flux that penetrates the coil of the wheel speed sensor changes, causing an exchange voltage to the coil.The wheel speed is detected by changing the frequency of this AC voltage in proportion to the wheel rotation speed.

　Rear wheel speed sensor & rotor

●
Axle hub integrated rear wheel speed sensor and rotor have been set.The structure and operating principle of the wheel speed sensor are basically the same as the front desk.

　Skid control computer

●
Outputs the operation signal to the brake actuator so that the wheel speed sensor of each wheel speed sensor is controlled according to the road surface condition.

Fail Safe function


・
If an abnormality occurs in skid control computers and brake actuators, the ABS Warnin Gramp is turned on and prohibits the operation of ABS and brake assist systems.If an abnormality that cannot be functioned by the EBD occurs, the brake warnin group is further turned on to prohibit EBD control.

Diagnostic function


・
Using a diagnostic function, we considered service.Please refer to the repair letter for details.

　Brake actuator

●
The brake actuator and the skid control computer are integrated to achieve small and lighter weight.

Hydraulic circuit




Holding / reduced pressure solenoid valve


・
The control signal from the skid control computer operates the two solenoid valves to switch the brake hydraulic circuit to three modes: pressure increase, maintain and decompression.
