Clutch with centrifugal hydraulic cancellation mechanism


・
At the time of shifting 4-2 speed and 3-4 speeds, which require high response properties, a high-response and smooth shift characteristics are realized by adopting a centrifugal hydraulic cancellation mechanism in the C1 clutch mechanism that performs and releases.。As the number of clutch portions rises, centrifugal force acts on the oil in the clutch, increasing the hydraulic pressure, causing a cooperative to be performed early.In addition, a shift shock may occur due to the rotation difference between the input side and the output side.Therefore, by setting a cancellation hydraulic chamber facing the piston hydraulic chamber of the clutch mechanism, the piston acts the same size and opposite centrifugal hydraulic pressure, eliminating its effect.


Engding torque control


・
At the time of shifting, the output torque is temporarily reduced by delaying the ignition timing of the engine, the clutch in the transmission is smoothly engaged, and the fluctuation of the output shaft torque during shifting is reduced.


Line hydraulic control


・
By controlling the line hydraulic pressure using linear solenoids (SLT), the line hydraulic pressure is optimally controlled based on torque information from the engine side.This makes it possible to control the line hydraulic pressure according to the engine output and driving state.


Clutch coordination hydraulic control


・
Control the clutch tolerance hydraulic pressure using linear solenoids to ensure smooth shifting characteristics.It is possible to monitor the input axial rotation number and signals from various sensors, and control the clutch cooperative hydraulic pressure in detail according to the engine output and situation.


Flex lockup control


・
The transmission efficiency has been greatly enhanced by controlling the power transmission distribution of the lock -up clutch (mechanical power transmission) and the torque converter (power transmission by fluid) according to the driving conditions.In the flicks lock -up system, the intermediate mode (giving a slight slip to the lockup clutch) is set to the low -vehicle speed range, and the lock -up area is further expanded.In addition, by lock -up operation even during deceleration, the fuel cut area can be expanded, which can significantly improve fuel efficiency.The operation of the flicks lock -up is operated under the following conditions.

・
The flex lock -up is operated by 2nd (only at acceleration), 3rd, 4th gear steps (when accelerating and deceleration), and O/D switch OFF.
・
The operation of the flicks lock -up system can also cause oil deterioration.Therefore, be sure to use a dedicated auto fluid type T-ⅳ.In addition, if a different type of oil is mixed or replaced, the flex lock -up system may not be operated normally.

Former slope shift control


・
Fourth slope shift control is a driving on a winding road with up and down, and if it is judged as climbing when climbing, it will limit the upshift to 4th to achieve smooth running.Also, at the time of falling hills, if the descent is determined by the throttle opening and vehicle acceleration in 4th, when the brake operation is detected, it will downshift to 3rd to generate moderate engine brake power.The system is composed of throttle position sensors and vehicle speed sensors as well as normal ECT control.

・
The judgment of the climbing and downhill road is performed by comparing the standard acceleration recalled in the computer and the actual acceleration calculated from the speed sensor signal.The fact that the actual additional speed is smaller than the standard acceleration is determined as a downhill road, and the case where the actual additional speed is greater than the standard acceleration is determined as a descent road.


Diagnostic function


・
If you detect abnormalities such as a speed sensor or each solenoid, turn on the check engine warnin gland in the combination meter assy and warn the driver.We adopted a new diagnostic for service.By connecting S2000 to DLC3, the diagnosis result of the abnormal part can be displayed.Please refer to the repair letter for the diagnostic item list.

Fail Safe function


・
It is a function that does not impair the drivingability even if an abnormality occurs in the sensor and each solenoid.See the repair letter for details.
