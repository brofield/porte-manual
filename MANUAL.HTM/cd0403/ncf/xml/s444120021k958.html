2005 specification DVD navigation system

●
The DVD navigation is composed of a down -type display panel and the main unit.
●
The display panel part has a color LCD panel compatible with the display screen size 6.5 -inch, a color LCD panel compatible with operation, and displays the map screen and audio display and operation screen.In addition, a backlight for LCD panel lighting is built in.In addition, a panel switch is placed around the display.
●
The main body has a built-in main computer, AM/FM tuner circuit, CD player, DVD-ROM player, etc., to detect switches and draw audio screens.
●
The multi -display uses a LCD panel of the TFT (Thin Film Transistor: thin film transistor) method, and the vertical of a general TV screen is wide 9:16 vertical 79.3mm x width 143.4mm.It is used as a display screen.The liquid crystal cell is subdivided into 234 vertical and 400 pixels, and is mixed with colored filters by rgb video signal by completely closed to fully opened by RGB video signal., You can express all colors.

DVD navigation system operation




Panel switch operation
・
The display screen of the DVD navigation is composed of information mode screen, navigation mode screen, etc., and each mode screen can be called from the panel switch around the display.


Opening and closing the display panel, insertion and discharge operation of disk
・
With the change of the design design, the operation of opening and closing and tilt will be performed on the TILT / EJECT screen displayed by pressing the "TILT" switch on the panel switch.
・
The tilt operation is performed by touching the "TILT" switch on the TILT / EJECT screen, and each time it is touched, it can be tilt in cyclick in three steps from about 7 ° to 14 ° → 21 °.
・
The opening and closing operation of the display panel is linked to the insertion and emission operation of the disk (DVD-ROM disk for maps and music for music), and touch the "MAP" or "CD" switch on the TILT / EJECT screen.By doing so, the display panel is opened, and each disk can be inserted and discharged.The display panel is closed by pressing the "TILT" switch on the panel switch while the display panel is opened.
DVD navigation design






Touch switch operation
・
The operation after calling each mode screen (audio, information, etc.) is performed by a touch switch that can be input by directly touching the switch frame displayed on the display screen with a finger.
