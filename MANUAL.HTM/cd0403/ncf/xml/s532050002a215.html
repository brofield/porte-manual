Axle hub conclusion nut


・
The fixing method of the axle hub conclusion nut has been made as a type.As a result, we have secured the conclusion performance, preventing noise from the axle, reducing the number of parts, and improving assembly workability.In addition, by nuts to 12 points nuts, the surface pressure of the nut seat surface has been equalized, and excellent conclusion performance has been secured.

Attending
Axle hub conclusion nuts are not reused parts because they are shaky and surface treatment for stable friction coefficient.Also, when removing the nut, please remove the nuts completely and loosen the nuts to prevent the screw of the drive shaft.
