Air cleaner

●
A lightweight resin air cleaner case and an air cleaner cap have been adopted.
●
A small and lightweight hot wire type plug -in type airflow meter has been installed on the air cleaner cap.
●
By making the inside of the air cleaner cap as a funnel shape, inhalation ventilation resistance has been reduced.
●
The adoption of a full fabric air element that does not use the outer frame or rubber seal is used, and the case has been reduced and the case is smaller and installed directly above the transmission.
●
The mounting of the air cleaner cap was simplified as a hinge structure, the components were simplified, and the weight was reduced, and the service was considered.
