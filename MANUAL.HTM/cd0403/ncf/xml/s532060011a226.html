ABS with EBD

Commentary


●
In order for cars to run, bend, and stop, the tires are basically gripped the road surface and fulfilling them as tires.However, in the case of sudden braking or in a place where road surfaces such as snowy roads and puddles are slippery, the wheels may lock when the car is still moving and slip between the tires and the road.Therefore, ABS (anti -lock brake system) detects wheel speed at such braking and controls all four brake hydraulic pressure by computer control.This is a device that maintains the operability of the steering wheel and the stability of the vehicle by preventing tire locks and ensuring sufficient brake performance by this control.
●
ABS with EBD has appropriately controlled the braking force of front and rear wheels and left and right wheels, regardless of the load change due to vacancy and loading, in addition to the conventional ABS function, ensuring excellent brake effects.


Front and rear bracket power distribution control


・
The front and rear braking power distribution control is a realization of an appropriate braking force of the front and rear wheels according to the vehicle driving state, with the aim of fully drawing out the "stop" performance, which is the basic function of the brake.
・
As a result, the braking force of the rear wheels can be effectively used in accordance with the load change due to the loading state and deceleration, so that the braking force is particularly reduced at the time of loading, and the performance of excellent brakes has been ensured.


Left and right braking power distribution control


・
Left and right braking power distribution control controls the braking force of the left and right rings to ensure the stability of the vehicle at the time of turning, ensures the effect performance of the excellent brake while ensuring vehicle stability at the time of braking.did.


Notes on ABS with EBD


・
ABS is not a device that draws out the performance that exceeds the ability of the tire, so be careful about speed, distance between vehicles, etc., and drive safely.
・
When installing other than specified parts, such as tires other than specified size, ABS control has an adverse effect.
・
A motor sound may be heard by the initial check immediately after running the engine and running for the first time, but it is not a failure.
・
If you step on the brake pedal lightly during the initial check, it may feel heavy, but it is not a failure.
・
Under the following conditions, the braking distance at the time of ABS operation may be longer than the ABS non -equipped vehicle.

Provisions

When driving on gravel roads and new snow roads
When tire chain is installed
When overcoming steps such as seams on the road
When traveling on bad roads such as uneven roads and cobblestones
