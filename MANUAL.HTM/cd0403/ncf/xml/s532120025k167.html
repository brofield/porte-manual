Speedometer

●
A PM -type stepper motor has been adopted for the speedometer guideline driving movement.
●
The meter using a stepper motor may have a slight movement of the meter when the power is turned on the meter (such as opening and closing the door or when the ignition switch is turned on).

・
A stepper motor, which controls the guidelines by the startup of electromagnetic coil, may move the guideline because the power to return the guidelines to zero will not work if the power supply is not supplied to the combination meter.For this reason, the initialization operation is performed in which the guidelines are returned to zero when the combination meter power is turned on (when the door is opened or when the ignition switch is turned on).

Speedometer operation


・
The meter computer inputs a vehicle speed rectangle wave signal equivalent to 4 pulse per rotation axis output from a skid control computer, and detects the vehicle speed by counting the waveform.
・
The meter computer displays the vehicle speed by controlling the rotation angle, speed, and direction of the guideline by the stepped motor driver circuit based on the detected vehicle speed.
