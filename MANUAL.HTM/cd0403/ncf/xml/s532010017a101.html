Oil pump

●
A compact and highly efficient cycloid gear type is adopted, and the drive system is directly driven by crankshaft.In addition, the structure was simplified by integrating with the timing chain cover, and the engine was reduced.
●
The inner relief method that returns the relieved engine oil to the oil pump inhalation side has been adopted to reduce the fluctuation of the oil surface.In addition, two relief holes were set to two holes.These have stabilized the oil supply.
●
The engine oil remains in the pump when the engine is stopped, and the hydraulic rise when the engine is started has been accelerated.
