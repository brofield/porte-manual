Cam position sensor (G2 signal)

●
An electromagnetic pickup sensor with high detection accuracy has been adopted.Detect a cylindrical size and a real camshaft angle.
●
A cam-position sensor detects the protrusion of the camshaft timing rotor (360-180-180 ° Ca), which is attached to the rear end of the cylinder head and fixed to the intake cam shaft.As the camshaft rotates, the air gap between the timing rotor protrusion and the cam position sensor changes the magnetic flux that passes through the coil portion of the cam position sensor, causing electricity in the coil portion.This generated voltage appears as an AC voltage because the timing rotor protrusion is opposite to when approaching the cam position sensor.
