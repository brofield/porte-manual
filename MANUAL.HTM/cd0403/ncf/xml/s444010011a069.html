General engine control system

●
Uses TCCS (TOYOTA Computer Controlled System) that comprehensively controls fuel injection control (EFI), ignition time control (ESA), idol rotation speed control (ISC), VVT-I control, etc. with high accuracy.We have improved high performance, high output, low fuel efficiency and emission performance.
●
VVT-I is used to switch the phase of the intake cam shaft depending on the driving state.This has improved comprehensive performance, such as fuel economy, output, torque characteristics and emission performance.
●
Diagnostic and fail -safe functions are set to take into account services and safety.

Control list


Control name
function
Fuel injection control (EFI)
The basic injection time calculated according to the condition of the engine is applied by the signal of each sensor and performs an appropriate fuel injection.
Control for ignition (ESA)
At the basic ignition time calculated according to the condition of the engine, the correct ignition is performed by adding the signal of each sensor.
Knock judgment control
The knocking is determined by the knock sensor signal.
Torque control during ECT shifting
The shift shock is reduced by delaying the ignition timing at the time of shifting.
Idol rotation control control (ISC)
R-ISCV controls the first idle rotation speed according to the cooling water temperature of the engine and the idol rotation speed after the engine warm-up.
VVT-I control
The phase of the intake cam shaft is variable according to the engine condition, improving the engine output, reducing emission gas, and improving fuel efficiency.
Electric fan control
Controls the ON-OFF and fan speed of cooling fan depending on the cooling water temperature and the state of the air conditioner.
Fuel pump control
The fuel pump is ON-OFF by the starter signal and engine rotation signal.
Air Concut control
At the time of acceleration, the aircon compressor is cut to ensure drivingability.
Canister purge control
We control the purging flow rate of the canister according to the cooling water temperature and operation status.
O2Sensor heater control
O depending on the cooling water temperature and driving status2ON-OFF is the sensor heater.
Diagnostic
Using the Diagnosis Tool S2000, accurate and detailed failure diagnosis, such as calling the SAE standard diag code data and active tests.
Fail safe
When an abnormality occurs in each sensor's signal, continue to control or stop the engine using the standard value in the engine control computer.
