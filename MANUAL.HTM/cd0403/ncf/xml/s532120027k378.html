Forget to erase the lamp warning system

●
When one door is opened in the headlamp or tail lamp (ignition switch off), a lamp that warns for forgot about the lamp is used, and the lamp is used to be forgotten, and all vehicles have been set as standard.
●
The circuit that controls the system is integrated into a meter computer in the combination meter, and a combination meter that blows a warning buzzer is built into a combination meter.
Lamp to forget to warning system function


・
Open the door of the driver's seat when the headlamps or tail lamps are lit and the ignition key is pulled out (key an lock warning switch OFF) (driver's door cartechi lamp switch ON) and the buzzer blows with continuous sound.*Warn that you forget to erase the lamp.
・
Forgetting to erase the lamp, the blowing of the walling buzzer is stopped by turning off the lamp or closing the driver's door.
・
If the headlamps or tail lamps are lit and the ignition key is inserted (key an rock warning switch ON), if the door of the driver's seat is opened, the key removal warning function is prioritized, and the buzzer is 0.5 seconds periods.It blows with the intermittent sound.
Attending
*
In the case of a car with a lamp auto -cut system, the buzzer will blew when the headlamp or tail lamp is turned on again after the lights are turned off by lamp auto cut.

・

・
By opening the driver's seat door from this state (the driver's door carteci lamp switch ON), the driver's computer door -to -carte lamp switch on signal is input to the meter computer, and the meter computer blows the buzzer with a continuous sound.
