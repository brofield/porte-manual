Vsc

●
VSC is a system that ensures stability in the turn direction of vehicles, while ABS and TRC mainly aims to ensure stability during braking and acceleration due to brake operation and accelerator operation.Normally, vehicles are stably turned in accordance with the steering operation, but depending on the road surface, the speed of the vehicle, the time of the emergency turning, or the external factors, the front -wheel slide or the anterior front wheel sliding tendency.In such cases, the VSC automatically performs the engine output and the brake control of each wheels to ensure stability, and alleviates strong front wheels or strong backlaps.In addition, even in situations that do not exceed the limit of the vehicle, it suppresses unstable behavior while ensuring braking force during turning and at high speed straight.
