A diffusion type was adopted in the injection port of the front washer nozzle of all vehicles, and it was excellent in cleaning properties when using washer.In addition, the mounting position was considered under the hood panel, so the appearance was considered.
●
The spread -type washer nozzle vibrates the flow by the water flow when the washer solution passes, and the washer solution is sprayed in a widespread wiping range of the front wiper wiping range.In addition, by reducing the flow rate of the central part of the spray range, we carefully consider not to interfere with the driver's visibility when using washer while driving.
●
A 2.5L washer tank was adopted for all cars and attached to the lower part of the front fender apron.
●
Two outlet pumps are used for the washer pump of all cars and the front rear.
●
All cars and washer tanks have a built -in washer inlet with a built -in level gauge, and the service is excellent when injecting a washer solution.

The outlet pump switches the flow path of the washer/rear to the front/rear by correcting/reverse the motor rotation depending on the direction of the current.When the front waser switch is turned on, the current from the + B power supply flows from contact D → washer motor → contact A → ground, and the motor operates in a regular state and sends a washer to the front side.When the rear washer switch is turned on, the current from the + B power supply flows from the coil to the ground, so the contact B / contact C is turned on, then the + B power → contact point B → washer motor → contact point C → earth.The motor works in a reverse state and sends a washer solution to the rear side.
