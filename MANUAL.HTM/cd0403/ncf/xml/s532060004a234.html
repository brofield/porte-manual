Parking brake

●
Foot -type parking brake has been adopted.In addition, the release method adopted a step -off type and considered operability.
●
The parking brake pedal was considered as an indoor adjustment type.

Foot -type parking brake structure


・
It is composed of parking brake pedals, sector, pole, pole spring and tension spring, locking and unlocking the parking brake by pedal operation.


Lock operation


・
When you step on the parking brake pedal, Paul moves while overcoming the sector's tip.When you release your feet from the pedal in this state, the pole will engage the sector's teeth and the parking brake pedal will be locked.


Unlocking


・
With the parking brake pedal locked, and then stepped on the parking brake pedal, the pole springs will act on the pole, the sector and the pole will be separated, and the parking brake pedal will be free.The parking brake pedal is pushed back to the cancellation position by tension spring force, and the pole moves along the sector guide and resets it.
