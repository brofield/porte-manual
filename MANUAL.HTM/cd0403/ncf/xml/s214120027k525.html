Manual leveling function

●
The head lamp leveling switch in the car room (the top direction at the top 0, the lowerest direction at level 5) drives the head lamp leveling actuator, and the reflector is moved up and down to adjust the optical axis.Please adjust the optical axis with the head lamp leveling switch “0”.
●
The head lamp leveling switch is attached to the right side of the steering column in the instrument loower panel, and the head lamp leveling actuator is attached to the back of the headlamp.
