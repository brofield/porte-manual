List of position detection switch function

Slide de Iiglorer Contact operation




Operating

Full rock motor normal operation (sliding door half door → full closing)
When the slide door control relay detects a half latch switch OFF, the regular relay is turned on after 0.3 seconds.As a result, the current flows from PB → DC+→ full lock motor → DC- → reverse relay → ground, and the full rock motor operates the full rock motor and turns on the original switch.After that, when the sliding door is fully closed, the full latch switch is OFF and the pole switch is ON → OFF, the full -rock motor is turned off to stop the full lock motor.
Full rock motor reversal operation (latch pole initial positioning operation)
After the full operation ends, the slide door control relay turns off the latch and pole to the initial position, and turns on the reversal relay about 0.1 seconds after that.As a result, the current flows from PB → Reversal Relay → DC- → Full Lock motor → DC+→ straight relay → ground, and the full rock motor operates.When the initial position of the latch pole is terminated and the original position switch is turned off, the reversal relay is turned off to stop the full lock motor.


Activation when the operation does not end within the predetermined time

If the full -rock motor is not completed within about 3 seconds, it will be transferred to reversal, and if the reversal operation is not completed within about 3 seconds, the full rock motor will be stopped at that point.


Slide de Iiglorer Mecha Cancellation Organization


・
If the full lock motor does not work for some reason, you can unlock the sliding door by operating the sliding door handle (inside/outside).
・
When the door handle is operated, the cable is drawn and the open lever No.2 rotates in the A direction, which rotates the open lever No.1 in the pins in the B direction.
・
With the rotation of the open lever No. 1, the cancellation lever connected to this rotates in the C direction, and the passive lever is removed and rotates in the D direction to release the latch.
・
At the same time, the rotation of the open lever No. 1 rotates the lift lever in the E direction to reduce the pole and the latch is free.
・
When the full lock motor is operating, if the sliding door is opened by a mecha -channel operation, and the door is closed again, the full lock motor operates once, and then the reversal is performed, and the slide dedicated arock ASSY is performed.Return (Easy Clarer) to the initial state.
