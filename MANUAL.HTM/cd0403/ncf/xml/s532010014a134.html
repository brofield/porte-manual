Crankshaft

●
The crankshaft center line is 12mm shifted on the cylinder bore center line.The crankshaft was offset, reducing the friction loss by reducing the piston side force, improving the thermal efficiency by changing the piston speed, reducing fuel consumption.
Reduction of piston side force


The maximum combustion pressure is efficiently transmitted to the crankshaft, and the force in the piston direction is reduced and the friction loss of the piston is reduced during the combustion process.

Changes in piston speed


In the first half of the combustion process, an area where the piston speed slows down (Fig. 1).As a result, the combustion is performed in a close position due to the piston, which increases the combustion pressure and improves thermal efficiency (Fig. 2).
