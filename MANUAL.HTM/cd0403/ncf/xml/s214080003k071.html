Air conditioner unit air duct

●
By adopting a two -layer unit in the air conditioner in the air conditioner unit of the cold region specification car, it has been compatible with anti -fabrications while reducing the ventilation loss when the internal and external two -layer control conditions are established.
●
The rear heater duct has been adopted as a cold district specification car to ensure the heating performance in the rear seats.
●
By adopting a small wide -angle high -flo -defloster with the inner wall shape of the front defloster, the defrostar performance has been achieved.
●
The evaporator adopts a small, lightweight MS evaporator (MS: Multi Tank Super Slim Structure) to enhance the heat exchanger efficiency of the evaporator during cooling and achieve a daise.
●
SFA heater core (SFA: Strait Flow Aluminum) was adopted for the heater core.
●
A clean air filter that removes pollen and dust and cleans the indoor space is set as standard.
