2003 specification "Wide Multi AV Station" operation




Panel switch operation
・
As before, the display screen of the "Wide Multi AV Station" is composed of a TV mode screen, information mode screen, navigation mode screen, etc., and each mode screen can be called from the panel switch placed at the bottom of the display.Masu.


Display panel opening and closing operation
・
As with before, the display panel part is a downturn type, and the operation of Open / Close / Tilt is performed by switching.


Touch switch operation
・
As before, the operation after calling each mode screen (TV / information, etc.) is performed by a touch switch that can be input by directly touching the switch frame displayed on the display screen with a finger.


Audio operation
・
After pressing the voice recognition utterance switch, displaying the audio recognition mark on the screen, the voice command can be performed by uttering the voice command to the audio recognition microphone, so that the operation is excellent.
・
There are "standard mode" and "expansion mode" for voice operation, and each can be switched on arbitrarily depending on the application.

Hands -free system configuration




Voice recognition microphone / audio recognition utterance switch (Optional setting for dealers)


The voice recognition microphone is a single -direction box -type microphone.The mounting position was the upper part of the steering column for the voice recognition microphone, and the voice recognition utterance switch was the left side of the steering column.


Mobile phone cradle (optional options with retailers)


We will broadcast the audio and data communication between your mobile phone and the "Wide Multi AV Station".The mounting position is a passenger seat seat.


Telephone switch (Optional option setting for retailer)


Two switches for hands -free calls are provided with off -hook and on -hooks.The mounting position is the right side of the steering column.


　Data communication module

●
The data communication module is a dedicated high -speed communication module that wirelessly sends and receive G -BOOK networks and various information.(It does not support voice communication.) In addition, the mounting position is the floor panel at the bottom of the passenger seat.
　Data communication antenna

●
It is a glass paste antenna compatible with 800MHz band.The pasting position is the upper center of the wind shield glass.
