Brake actuator

●
The brake actuator and the skid control computer are integrated to achieve small and lighter weight.

Holding / reduced pressure solenoid valve


・
The control signal from the skid control computer operates the two solenoid valves to switch the hydraulic circuit of the brake to three modes: pressure increase, maintain and decompression.
