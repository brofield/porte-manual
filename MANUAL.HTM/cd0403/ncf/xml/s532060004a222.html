Rear brake

●
Inner diameter 200mm size leading trailing type drum brake has been adopted.
●
Aluminum wheel cylinder has been adopted to reduce weight.
●
Inklimental hydraulic adjuster has been adopted for clearance adjustment between shoes and drums.

Incremental hydraulic auto adjuster


・
Normally, the operation of the rear brake is pushed out until it comes into contact with the inside of the drum.The auto adjust lever rotates in the C direction by spring, centered on the Adjust Lever Supporial B, while hitting the point A of the strut.At this time, if the shoe clearance exceeds the specified amount, the tip of the auto adjust lever rotates the adjustment bolt by one dentition in the D direction, and the strut is moved in the direction in which the shoe spreads.As a result, the initial position of the shoe is expanded and the clearance of the drum and shoe is properly maintained.
