Exhaust manifold

●
The exhaust manifold is made of stainless steel with excellent heat resistance and corrosion resistance.
●
By reducing the brunch pipe length and reducing the brunch pipe diameter, the catalytic warm -up and medium -low speed torque have been improved.In addition, cars with 1NZ-FE engines have reduced the heat capacity by reducing the brunch pipes and collective pipes, improving the warmth of catalytic, and reducing the weight.
●
The heat insulator is only on the top, and by providing a cooling hole, the surface temperature of the manifold has been reduced to ensure durability.
