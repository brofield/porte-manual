Slide de Iiglor System

●
The sliding door was set as standard to the sliding door, which all closed the door due to the operation of the full rock motor in a half -door state, and the sliding door could be closed with a light force.
●
The Easy Clarer Mechanism (Slided Arrock ASSY) has simplified the structure and optimizes the control method of the drive portion, reducing the weight and reducing the sounding sound.
●
The adoption of the "sliding door normal -time power supply unit" has been reduced by simplifying the system and reducing the number of parts by unnecessary junctions and power relays.
●
A mecha -channel mechanism has been set that can cancel the operation of the slide de scaled looser by operating the sliding door handle (inside/outside).In addition, by optimizing the structure of the mechacancel mechanism, operating power was reduced during mechanical cancellation operation.

Slide de Ijiglor System Function


・
Sliding the sliding door to a semi -door state, activating the full lock motor and automatically closed the sliding door.After the door is fully closed, the full rock motor is operating to return the latch mechanism to the initial position, ensuring smooth operability of the sliding door.
・
By adopting a mecha -cancellation mechanism, the mechanical mechanism unit that connects the full lock motor and the latch mechanism can be separated by drawing the sliding door handle (inside/outside).This enables the opening and closing of the sliding door even if troubles such as not operating the full rock motor occur.
