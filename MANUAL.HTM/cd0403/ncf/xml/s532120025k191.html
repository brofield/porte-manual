Tachometer

●
A PM -type stepper motor has been adopted for the guideline driving movement of the tachometer.
●
The meter using a stepper motor may have a slight movement of the meter when the power is turned on the meter (such as opening and closing the door or when the ignition switch is turned on).
Tachometer operation


・
The meter computer inputs a rectangular wave signal equivalent to 2 pulse per rotation output from the engine control computer, and the number of engine speed is detected by counting the waveform.
・
The meter computer displays the engine speed by controlling the rotation angle, speed, and direction of the guideline by the stepper motor driver circuit, based on the detected engine speed.
