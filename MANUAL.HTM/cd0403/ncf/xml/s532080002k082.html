Air conditioner system settings
The air conditioner system has two types of manuals and auto, manual air conditioners are used for 130i, and auto air conditioners are used for 150R and 130i C package vehicles.
Air conditioner unit air duct
Adopts an air conditioner unit that integrates blower units and cooler units.
A two -layer unit of internal and exterior is used for the cold district specification car.
Adopted a wide -angle small high flowdefloster.
The rear heater duct with excellent heating performance to the rear seats is adopted, and standard is set as a cold region specification vehicle.
Evaporator
Adopted a small, lightweight MS Evaporator (MS: Multi Tank Super Slim Structure).
Heater core
Adopted a small, lightweight SFA heater core (SFA: Strait Flow Aluminum).
condenser
Adopts a high -efficiency and lightweight sub -cool condenser.
compressor
Uses a scroll compressor (SCSA06 type).
Clean air filter
Adopt a pollen removal type and set as standard for all vehicles.
Refrigerant
HFC-134A (R-134A), which does not contain chlorine in the air conditioner in consideration of the destruction of the ozone layer, is adopted.
