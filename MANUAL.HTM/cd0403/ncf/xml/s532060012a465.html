Brake actuator

●
Master cylinder cut Solenoid valve, holding solenoid valve, reduced pressure solenoid valve, pump and reservoir, etc., adjust the brake hydraulic to each wheel cylinder.In addition, it has been a skid control computer integrated type, and has been miniaturized.

Hydraulic circuit map




Holding / reduced pressure solenoid valve


・
The control signal from the skid control computer operates the two solenoid valves to switch the brake hydraulic circuit to three modes: pressure increase, maintain and decompression.

Master Cylinder Cut Solenoid Valve


・
Master cylinder cut Solenoid valve and linear solenoid valve have been adopted to smooth the hydraulic change during brake assist control.
・
By controlling the opening and closing status of the valve, the hydraulic pressure generated by the pump has been tuned, and the control hydraulic pressure according to the operating state has been possible.


Master cylinder pressure sensor


・
The master cylinder pressure sensor is built in the brake actuator.Outs the Master Cylinder pressure signal to the skid control computer.


ABS operation with EBD


・
Four -wheeled hydraulic control is performed by the operation of holding, reduced pressure solenoid valves, pumps and reservoirs.

Rear wheels horizontal slide control control


・
The hydraulic pressure generated by the pump in the brake actuator is guided to the wheel cylinder of each wheel, and the brake hydraulic is controlled to suppress the rear wheel or the front wheels.
・
In the rear -wheeled side slide control control, the front wheel brake outside the turn is activated, and the rear wheel brake is activated as needed.The illustration is when the rear wheels are controlled by the rear wheels while turning right.
