Function Overview
・
By linking navigation and G -BOOK online services, we provide road conditions such as traffic congestion in real time.The main functions include G route search and congestion prediction.


G route search
・
VICS's latest road traffic information and past statistical data will comprehensively predict future road conditions, explore the optimal route that avoids traffic congestion.


Congestion prediction
・
The congestion prediction time and congestion increase / decrease predictions are displayed based on traffic congestion prediction information from the G -BOOK Center.
