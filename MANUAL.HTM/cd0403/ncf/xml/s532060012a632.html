TRC & VSC & Brake Assist Overview

●
A skid control computer processes signals from each sensor to control ABS, TRC, VSC and brake assist.In addition, communicate with the engine control computer and output the control signal.

The main components and functions of the entire system


Components
function
Wheel speed sensor
Detect the wheel speed and enter into a skid control computer.
York -rate sensor & linear G sensor
Detect the yaw angle speed (rotation movement) and the front, left, left and right acceleration and enter into the skid control computer.
Parking brake switch
Detect the operation of the parking brake and enter into the skid control computer.
Stop lamp switch
Detect the brake operation status and enter into the skid control computer.
Steering sensor
Detect the steering angle and steering direction of the steering and enter into the skid control computer.
Master cylinder pressure sensor
Detect the pressure of the master cylinder and enter into a skid control computer.
TRC OFF switch
Switch the ON/OFF of TRC.
Combination meter
Slip indicator lamps, brake warnin grids, ABS Warning Lamps, and VSC Warning Lamps have been set in the combination meter.
Brake actuator
The output signal of the skid control computer controls the hydraulic pressure of each of the four wheel cylinders.
Motor drive relay
Supply and block the power to the pump motor of the brake actuator.
Motor blocking relay
The brake actuator blocks the power supply to the pump motor.
Solenoid relay
Supply the power to the solenoid valve of the brake actuator.
VSC buzzer
Informs the driver in which the VSC is being operated by the intermittent sound of the buzzer.
Skid control computer
The signal from each sensor is processed, and the brake control of the ABS, TRC, VSC, and brake assist is performed.In addition, communicate with the engine control computer and output the control signal.
Engine control computer
By communicating with a skid control computer, the engine output is controlled when TRC and VSC operate.
DLC3
By connecting and operating the diagnostic tool, you can read the data of the diagnostic cord and computer.
