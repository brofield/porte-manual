●
The Tilt Organization is composed of a pair of tilt steering stopper, rock bolt, break -wake bracket, steering column bracket spacer, steering support color and tilt lever, making it possible to adjust the angle mainly with the steering column bracket spacer.Masu.

Operation of steering shaft


・
The steering column tube is held by the load generated by the cam mechanism placed between the tilt attachments and the tilt lever.The tilt steering stopper rotates in synchronization with the tilt lever operation.As a result, Mountain Mountain gets on the mountain on the tilt steering stopper side, expanding the interval between the cam mechanism.As a result, the tilt attachment is sandwiched between the break -wafracket and the column is locked.
