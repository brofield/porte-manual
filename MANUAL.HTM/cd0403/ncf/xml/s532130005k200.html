Regulation of sliding door manual opening and closing by intermediate stopper


・
When the rotation arm of the window regulator descends or more due to the rotation of the sliding door power window motor, the directly connected plate rotates in the A direction.When this plate contacts the lever, the lever rotates in the B direction and the cable is drawn.(Part A)
・
The cable is connected to the ratchet built into the looper roller base, and the cable is pulled out by the bite of the ratchet and the hook, and the hook is set up by the spring power.If you try to fully open the sliding door in this state, the stopper set on the hook and the rowar roller guide will interfere and regulate that the door does not open to the fully open position.(Part B)

Regulation of power slide doors by intermediate stopper


・
The slide door control relay detects the position of the sliding door by entering the pulse signal from the hole IC, and when the power slide operation is stopped by the intermediate stopper, it keeps the sliding door for about 30 minutes at that position.Masu.(Reference power slide door system)
