Parking brake prevention warning buzzer operation


・
If the car speed is 5km/h or more when the parking brake is ON, the buzzer will be blown out with a 0.7 second period of intermittent sound as a warning warning of pulling brake.
・
If the parking brake is turned on during the deceleration of 10 km/h or less, the buzzer will be blown off if the vehicle speed is 4 km/h or more after 1 second after the state.
・
When the parking brake is turned off during the buzzer, the buzzer is stopped, but when the parking brake is turned on again when the vehicle speed is 5 km/h or more, the buzzer blew again.If the vehicle speed is less than 4 km/h, the buzzer will be stopped.
・
Even if the car speed is 5 km/h or more at the time of the parking brake, if the vehicle speed changes to less than 4 km/h after 1 second, the bus will be regarded and the buzzer will not be blown.

Operating circuit

・
The combination meter enters a vehicle speed signal from a skid control computer.In addition, the ON/OFF signal of the parking brake switch is entered directly.When the ON signal of the parking brake switch is entered at the time of IG ON, the brake warnin glander is turned on through the internal active circuit, and when the vehicle speed corresponds to the buzzer blowing conditions is entered, the warning buzzer is blown.
