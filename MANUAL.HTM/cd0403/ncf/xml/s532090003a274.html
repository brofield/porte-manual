SRS Airbag & Preten Shoner Seat Belt Overview

●
The SRS airbag system is composed of driver's seat, passenger seat SRS airbag Ass, center airbag sensor ASSY, front airbag sensor, pliten shoner + seated with a forefin resource.

Main configuration parts and functions


Components
function
Spiral cable
The center airbag sensor is transmitted to the driver SRS airbag ASSY the ignition signal from ASSY.
Driving seat, passenger seat SRS airbag ASSY
It is composed of inflators and bags.The gas generated by the inflator from the ignition signal from the center airbag sensor ASSY will instantly inflate the bag and alleviate the impact on the passenger.
Priten Shonor + Seat Belt with Force Remitter
The gas generator is ignited by a ignition signal from the center airbag sensor ASSY, instantly winding the seat belt to enhance the restraint effect of the passenger.In addition, the Force Rimiter Mechanism relieves the impact on the crew.
SRS Airbag Warnin Grand Pu (built -in combination meter)
In the event of a system abnormality, light or flash and warn the driver.
Front airbag sensor
Detects the deceleration rate in the case of collision and sends a signal to determine the ignition judgment to the center airbag sensor ASSY.
Center airbag sensor ASSY (computer)
It consists of a safing sensor, ignition judgment circuit, etc.The safing sensor detects a deceleration at the time of collision, and makes a ignition judgment based on the signal from the front airbag sensor, and sends a ignition signal to the SRS Airbag ASSY and a pliten shoner + force -remedier seat belt.In the event of a system abnormality, a failure diagnosis of the system is performed by switching to diagnodes mode.
