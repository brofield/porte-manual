Refrigerant

●
In consideration of the destruction of the ozone layer, HFC-134A (R-134A), which does not contain chlorine, is adopted in the air conditioner refrigerant.
●
In order to prevent compressor oil incorrect filling, the type of refrigerant and applied compressor oil are specified in the radiator upper support section and the compressor section.
　■ Attention ■
　
The air conditioning system that uses HFC-134A (R-134A) uses a joint of dedicated parts and each component.If you use other parts other than the special part, you may cause a refrigerant leak.
Please note that compressor, compressor oil, O-ring, etc. may be used other than HFC-134A.
