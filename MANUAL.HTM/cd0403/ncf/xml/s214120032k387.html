Hands -free call function

●
By connecting your mobile phone to the "Wide Multi -AV Station" using a hands -free system, you can use a telephone function without directly operating your mobile phone.
Attending
The mobile phone can only be used by the specified model.
●
After transmitting or after receiving a call, calls are made by voice recognition microphone and vehicle -mounted speakers.
Dissemination


・
When you press the off -hook switch on the phone switch while stopping or press the "information / G" switch on the panel switch, touch the "TEL" in the information mode screen displayed to move to the telephone number input screen.You can make a call in a way.
Transmission screen




How to send dial


Item
Method
Dial transmission
Enter the phone number and make a call.
Phonebook
Call the phone number from the phonebook and make a call.
One -touch dial transmission
Select the registered telephone number (one touch dial) and make a call.
History transmission
Call the number you made in the past or the number you received in the past to make a call.


・
In addition to the above basic transmission functions, it enables transmission from the navigation screen (when you have a phone number data) and the transmission by voice recognition, and responds to various situations.

Incoming call


・
When you arrive, the ringtone sounds and displays the screen during the incoming call.When outputting a ringtone, mute (eras) the audio and navigation guidance.
・
You can receive the phone by touching the "call (phone mark)" on the screening screen or pressing the off -hook switch on the phone switch.

Hands -free system configuration




Voice recognition microphone / audio recognition utterance switch (Optional setting for dealers)


The voice recognition microphone is a single -direction box -type microphone.The mounting position was the upper part of the steering column for the voice recognition microphone, and the voice recognition utterance switch was the left side of the steering column.


Mobile phone cradle (optional options with retailers)


We will broadcast the audio and data communication between your mobile phone and the "Wide Multi AV Station".The mounting position is a passenger seat seat.


Telephone switch (Optional option setting for retailer)


Two switches for hands -free calls are provided with off -hook and on -hooks.The mounting position is the right side of the steering column.
