Knock sensor

●
A flat (non -resonance) knock sensor has been adopted.
Knock sensor operation


・
If the knock sensor receives vibration from the cylinder block, the inertia will delay the movement of weights.Therefore, pressure is applied to the piezoelectric ceramic provided between the cylinder block and the weight, causing voltage.The voltage is almost constant in all frequency bands, and the engine control computer detects the targeted frequency and determines that it is knocking.
