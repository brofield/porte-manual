Fuel tank

●
A 4WD car -type fuel tank is adopted.Like a fuel tank for 2WD vehicles, the use of lead is abolished as an aluminum plated steel sheet, so that the effect on the environment was considered.
●
In order to clarify, simplify and remove fuel at the time of dismantling, the fuel tank is set on the lower surface of the fuel tank.
●
The capacity is 45L similar to the 2WD car.
