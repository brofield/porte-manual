Power sliding door reversal function


・
By performing one of the following operations while the power slide door is operating, the direction of the sliding door can be flipped in the open operation and open operation when it operates.

Operation of inverted function

・
Operate the sliding door handle (inside/outside).
・
Operate the power slide door switch (Impanes switch, center pillar switch).(OFF → ON)
・
Operate the transmitter button.(Do not need to be pressed)


Power slide door sandwiching prevention function


・
While the slide door closet is operating, the touch sensor attached to the front edge of the sliding door detects (compressed), and the operation direction is reversed to operate open.
・
If you detect the same direction in a row more than two times or more, the power slide operation will be stopped at that point.In this case, operate the door manually to open and close the door.If the sliding door is fully closed or fully opened by manually, it will return to power slide operation.

Power slide operation stop condition due to sandwiching prevention function

・
While opening operation, "pinched detection" → inverted (closed) activation "Put in detection" → Inverted (open) activation "Put in pinch detection" → Power slide operation stopped.
・
While closing the closing, "pinch detection" → inverted (open) activation "pinched detection" → Inverted (closed) activation "sandwiched in" → Power slide operation stopped.


Slide door glass interlocked intermediate stop function


・
If the sliding door glass is open or more, the door is open to the front of the normal open position to prevent sandwiching between the sliding door sash and the quarter pillar.(Slide door reference)
・
If the sliding door is stopped in the middle position, hold the sliding door at that position up to about 30 minutes.When the system is retained by the system, the alarm buzzer will be blown for about 2 seconds and the door can be opened and closed.*
Attending
*
To prevent the sliding door from closing rapidly during the slope, the system is braked for about 8 seconds after the sliding door is free.

Fuel lid interlock function


・
If the fuel lid is opened while the power slide door is operating (only during the opening operation), the power slide operation is prohibited at that point.
・
After the power slide operation is prohibited, open and close the door manually.In addition, if the sliding door is fully closed by manual, it will return to power slide operation.

Power slide door sandwiching prevention operation



Putting in between due to overload detection

When a foreign substance is inserted while the power sliding door opening and closing is operated, the slide door control relay detects the change in the pulse width output from the hole IC, and the sliding door drive motor is inverted.Overload detection is corrected at certain intervals in accordance with changes in motor rotation speed (sliding door movement speed).



Opening operation

When an overload is detected during the open operation, the open relay is turned off, and the open relay is broken for about 0.1 seconds at the same time, the closed relay is turned on, and the sliding door drive motor is reversed.If the overload is detected again while the inverted closed operation is operated, the closed relay will be broken for about 0.1 seconds at the same time as OFF, and the open relay will be turned on at the end of the bun, and the sliding door drive motor will be reversed again.Furthermore, if the second overload is detected during the reversal opening operation, the open relay is turned off, the warning buzzer is blown for about 2 seconds, then the magnet clutch is turned off and the system is stopped (switched to manual operation).


Close operation

When the overload is detected while the closed operation is operated, the closed relay is turned off for about 0.1 seconds at the same time as the closed relay, and the open relay is turned on at the end of the sliding door, and the sliding door drive motor is reversed.If the overload is detected again during the reversal opening operation, the open relay will be broken for about 0.1 seconds at the same time as OFF, and the closed relay will be turned on at the end of the sliding and the sliding door drive motor will be reversed again.Furthermore, if the second overload is detected while the inverted closing operation is operated, the closed relay is turned off, the warning buzzer is blown for about 2 seconds, then the magnet clutch is turned off and the system is stopped (switched to manual operation).


Proserable operation by touch sensor

If the touch sensor set on the front edge of the door is compressed while the power slide door is closed, the resistance value of the sensor changes.When the slide door control relay is input to the touch sensor or control relay, the closed relay is bleeded for about 0.1 seconds at the same time as OFF, and the open relay is turned on at the end of the bun, so that the sliding door drive motor is reversed to the open side.。

Slide door glass interlocked intermediate stop operation



Intermediate stop operation outline

・
The slide door control relay calculates the range (intermediate range) in which the hook interferes with the stopper (intermediate range), and the intermediate stop operation starts by detecting insertion (overloaded by stopper) within that range.


Intermediate stop operation

・
Open operation is started by operating switch/button or handle operation, and when the slide door control relay detects the intermediate range, the open relay is opened and the sliding door driving motor stops rotation.At this time, the magnet clutch is kept on the ON state, and the sliding door is kept at the intermediate stop.
・
After about 30 minutes after the above operation starts, the slide door control relay blows the alarm buzzer for about 2 seconds and offers the magnet clutch to free (manual opening and closing).*I'll do it.

Attending
*
Brake control is performed for about 8 seconds from magnet clutch OFF to prevent sudden opening and closing of sliding doors by slope.
