Disadvantage head lamp feature


・
The discharge lamp is a headlamp that uses an HID valve (high brightness discharge light) as a light source.
・
The light emitted from the HID valve becomes a natural daylight similar to sunlight, irradiating the front to a wider and farther, to help ensure the effective visibility of the driver, and the power consumption is low, so the burden on the battery.I have the advantages that are light and the lifespan of the valve is long.
・
The discharge headlamp has a small and lightweight light control computer while realizing instantaneous lighting and power supply supply of the HID valve, and a manual leveling mechanism that can adjust the upper and lower optical axis as an anti -lighting device for oncoming vehicles.It is done.

HID valve configuration


・
In the light -emitting tube of the HID valve, rare gas (xenon gas) and metal oligon (zinc, sodium scandium) are enclosed, and arc discharges are performed in the light -emitting tube due to the voltage on the base and the lead wire.。
・
The outer tube of the light emission tube is reduced to UV -cut glass that absorbs ultraviolet rays, which is equivalent to ultraviolet radiation as halogen valve.
・
The light color of the discharge headlamp changes over time of use time.
・
