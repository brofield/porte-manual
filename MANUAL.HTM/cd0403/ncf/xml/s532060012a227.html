Brake assist system

●
Urgent brakes are determined by the speed and amount of the brake pedal, which generates a strong braking force based on it.
Brake -assisted way of thinking


・
During the panic, the braking speed of the brake is fast, but it cannot be stepped on a small braking force.(A) in the figure
・
In addition, these drivers may not be able to step on brake pedals for a long time in the latter half of braking, and may decrease.(B in the figure)
・
"Brake assist" estimates the driver's urgent braking will when the brakes are stepped fast, and ensures braking power even if you can't step too hard.(C) in the figure
・
"Brake assist" reduces braking power assistance when the driver is consciously loosened after operation, and reduces discomfort.(D in the figure)


Precautions of brake assist


・
Brake assist is not a device that brings out the performance that exceeds the original ability of the brake.Please be careful about the car speed, the distance between the vehicles, etc. and perform safe driving.
・
If the brake pedal is stepped at a steep speed, the brake may be strongly applied and a small hitting can be generated, but this is not an unusual phenomenon when the brake assist is operating normally.
・
If you step on the brake pedal deeply at the time of stop, there may be a "click" sound, but it is not anomalous when the assist mechanism is working properly.
