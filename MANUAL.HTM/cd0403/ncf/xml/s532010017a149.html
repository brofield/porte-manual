Lubrication in general

●
The lubrication system adopted a full pressure transmission and a full filter system.By extending the oil restorer of the cylinder block to the oil pan split surface, the oil returning from the cylinder head does not directly hit the crankshaft, reducing the friction.
●
The engine oil adopted a low -viscosity oil to reduce friction.
●
The oil level gauge is made of resin and placed on the front of the engine room to consider the service.
