Rear wiper

●
The rear wiper has been adopted and the standard is set to all vehicles.
●
The resin was adopted as the rear wiper arm & blade material, and the wiper arm and blade were integrated, so that the wiping arms and blade were united, the appearance was excellent.In addition, the method of attaching the wiper arm and blade is to be replaced with the winter blade by using the structure that can be removed.
●
The rear wiper & washer switch were integrated with the wiper control switch to have excellent use.
●
The length of the wiper blade is 300mm.
●
The winter blade options have been set for all cars.
