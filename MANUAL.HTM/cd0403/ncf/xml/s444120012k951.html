DVD navigation operation




Panel switch operation
・
By pressing the panel switch around the display, basic operations such as the audio source switching are performed.


Touch switch operation
・
As in the past, the operation after calling the audio mode screen by panel switch operation is performed by directly contacting the switch frame (touch switch) displayed on the screen with the finger.


Disk insertion / emission operation
・
The insertion and discharge of the music CD is performed by touching the "CD" switch on the TILT / EJECT screen displayed after touching the "TILT" of the panel switch.
