"Wide Multi AV Station" TV mode function



TV mode screen
●
By pressing the "TV" switch on the panel switch, the TV mode screen is moved and the TV broadcast screen is displayed.
●
By pressing the "audio" switch of the panel switch during the TV broadcast screen, it will be transferred to the TV mode operation screen, and the on -screen display such as the reception and receiving channel of TV broadcasting will be displayed, and the switch of the TV channel / auto.You can preset etc.
●
The display of the TV broadcast screen has been displayed only when the ignition switch is ON or ACC, and the parking brake can be activated only when it is stopped.In other cases, you can only hear TV audio.
TV mode screen display






TV mode setting screen
●
Migrate to the TV mode setting screen by touching the "Settings" of the panel switch during the TV mode screen.
●
On the TV mode setting screen, you can display the reception channel No. and the broadcasting station name, display the preset channel channel No. and the broadcasting station name, switch the wide mode, change the sound quality, and adjust the sound quality.。
