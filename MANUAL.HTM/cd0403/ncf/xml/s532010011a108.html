R-ISCV

●
Attached to the throttle body, it changes the angle of the rotary valve by the duty signal from the engine control computer, and controls the amount of air to bypass the throttle valve.The amount of air is determined by the time of ON / OFF of the signal from the engine control computer (duty ratio).In addition, the drive circuit was converted into an IC and was built into R-ISCV.

R-ISCV operation


Subsidy at startup
The duty ratio is increased to increase the duty ratio at the start of the engine and several seconds after starting, ensuring the startability of the engine.After starting, change the duty ratio according to the cooling water temperature to control the engine speed.
Predictive control
When the electric load changes, when the air conditioner switch is switched, the load on the engine changes and the engine speed changes.When these signals are detected, the signal is sent to R-ISCV according to each condition, which changes the R-ISCV passing air to reduce the fluctuation of the engine speed.
Feedback control and electric load idle up
When the engine speed is measured for a certain period of time and there is a difference between the idle rotation speed and the target rotation number, the signal is sent to the R-ISCV to change the R-ISCV passing air and approach the target rotation number.In addition, the idle rotation speed is increased according to the electric load, and the engine idle is stabilized.
Idol -up rotation speed frequency during air conditioner operation variable control
The air conditioner is controlled in two stages by the load of the air conditioner when the air conditioner is operating, and the minimum necessary idle -up rotation is ensured to ensure fuel efficiency and drivingability.
Power steering when activating idle up speed control control
The number of idle -up revolutions during the power steering is controlled by the engine control computer under the signal of the power steering hydraulic sensor, ensuring drivingability.
