Cylinder head

●
A diagonal skish structure has been adopted in the combustion chamber.In the compression process, the ignition rate was improved by optimizing the flow of mixture, and the combustion process promoted the propagation of flame to improve the combustion efficiency.In addition, the mask part around the valve has been deleted to improve inhalation efficiency.
●
In addition to the adoption of a vertical intake port, the valve pin angle is 33.5 ° to improve the intake efficiency.
●
The water jacket has been adopted a concentrated vertical flow method to improve cooling properties around the combustion chamber and spark plug.In addition, by eliminating the stagnation of the cooling water -sewage side, the combustion chamber wall temperature has been uniform.
●
The water outlet and the bypass passage have been integrated, and the simplification of components has been reduced and compact.
●
The structure is to attach the fuel injection directly, and the HC emissions have been reduced by suppressing the fuel wall adhesion.
