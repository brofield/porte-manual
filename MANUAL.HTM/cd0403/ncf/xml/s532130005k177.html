Slide door motor ASSY

●
The sliding door -driven motor ASSY is composed of the motor body, drums, magnet clutch, and hole IC, and the hole IC detects the position of the 64 -pole magnet installed on the outer periphery of the magnet as a pulse signal, and input it.The sliding door control relay detects the opening / closing speed, position, and travel direction of the sliding door (opening and closing direction).
●
The slide door control relay has a signal from the hole IC, and about 200 mm before the full closing at the time of closing the closure of the loading load, and the open operation to reduce the shock at the time of fully opened.We carry out low -speed control from 80mm.
●
When operating the door in a free state of the sliding door (the magnet clutch is OFF), the mechanism to adjust the torque of the clutch and brake when the door speed exceeds a certain level is used.

Sliding door speed detection method


・
The slide door control relay detects the amount of sliding door movement by counting a pulse of one cycle output by the Hall IC1 (DS1).At the same time, the current sliding door is detected by detecting a different pulse HI/LO cycle depending on the rotation direction of the motor output by the hole IC2 (DS2), and the current sliding door is memorized.I am aware of the location.


Sliding door position detection method


・
The slide door control relay detects changes in sliding speed and movement speed by detecting changes in the motor rotation speed from the pulse interval output by the hole IC1 (DS1).As a result, the load on the sliding door is recognized and the overload detection of the sandwich prevention function is controlled.
