Gear train

●
CR-CR-type planetary gear is placed as a 4-speed mechanism department in the input shaft section.As a result, the geartrain mechanism placed on the inter -medium -weight shaft is greatly reduced and the weight of the counter shaft is greatly reduced, greatly reducing the weight of the unit and the inertial weight of the geartrain mechanism.Masu.In addition, as a hydraulic control mechanism, the number of shifting elements such as one -way clutches and brakes has been reduced by adopting clutch toe clutch control.
