Center airbag sensor (computer)

●
The center airbag sensor assembly is composed of G sensors, ignition judgment circuits, backup power supply, etc.
●
The G sensor has adopted a semiconductor G sensor that has a linear output characteristics for the deceleration.
●
The driver's seat, the passenger seat airbag, and the pliten shoner ignition judgment will be performed by the signal from the front airbag sensor and the G sensor.In addition, we have a diagnostic diagnosis in the event of an abnormal system with a diagnostic function.

It consists of a backup condenser that supplies power to the system when a collision or a power supply system is broken, and an increase in pressure that performs pressure.
Computer (ignition judgment circuit)
A predetermined operation is performed by a signal from the front airbag sensor, and a ignition signal is sent when the calculation value exceeds the set value in advance.In addition, a failed diagnostic circuit diagnoses whether there is a failure of the entire system, notify the driver the system abnormalities, and diagnose the system abnormalities using the diagnostic function.


Activation judgment


・
SRS airbags and pliten shonner seat belts are ignited by the shock of the previous end.Judgment is based on the front satellite sensor signal and the center airbag sensor ASSY G sensor and sapping sensor signal.
・
The center airbag sensor ASSY has a proven track record, so even after checking the primary check, the airbag warnin group is turned on.This warning cannot be erased.


Operation of diagnostic circuit


・
The operation of the diagnostic circuit is displayed by the warnin gland in the meter.The diagnostic circuit is operated in two periods, a primary check and a constant check.

Primary check


・
When the ignition switch is turned on, light the airbag warnin glamp for about 6 seconds and perform a primary check.During this period, the operation diagnosis of the center airbag sensor will be performed as an ignition prohibition state.If an abnormality is detected in this primary check, the airbag warnin glamp will be lit or blinking without off the light after 6 seconds.
・
The operated center airbag sensor remains on the warnin glander even after the primary check, regardless of the failure.

Always check


・
After the primary check, if the airbag warnin gland is turned off, it will be ignited, and the diagnostic circuit will always check for any abnormalities in the system.If a failure is detected during this check, it will light or flash the airbag warnin glamp.(The warning display of the power supply voltage decrease is turned off after the power supply voltage has returned properly.)

Diagnostic function


・
By switching to diagnodes mode, you can display the diagnosis result of the abnormal part from the number of flashing indicator lamps.In addition, a new diagnostic function is adopted and the diagnostic code can be read by connecting the diagnostic tool to DLC3.Please refer to the repair letter for details of the diagnodes mode.
