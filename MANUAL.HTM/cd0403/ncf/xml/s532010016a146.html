Water pump

●
A small and lightweight water pump is adopted, and the back drive by V -ribd belt is used.By setting the belt center near the center of the bearing, a small bearing can be adopted, and it has been compact and lighter while ensuring reliability.
●
The timing chain cover and cylinder blocks have been compacted by configuring a vortex.In addition, the efficiency has been highly efficient by optimizing the feather shape of the rotor.
