Cooling fan system

●
The engine control computer controls the rotation speed of the cooling fan in two stages.At low -speed rotation, the rotation speed of the fan motor is reduced by via the register.

Attending
The register is hot.It is installed in the wheel cover of the left side member and is not usually touched, but be careful when removing the wheel cover.
