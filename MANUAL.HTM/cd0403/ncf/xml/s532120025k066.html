・

SRS Airbag Warning Indicator Lamp


・
The meter computer constantly enters a pulse signal from the center airbag sensor ASSY, and the lighting/extinguished SRS airbag warning indicator lamp is controlled by the signal condition.If an abnormality from the center airbag sensor ASSY is abnormal and the pulse signal cannot be determined or the signal cannot be received for about 10 seconds or more, the meter computer lights the SRS airbag warning indicator lamp.
