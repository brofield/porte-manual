HDD navigation operation




Panel switch operation
・
The display screen of the HDD navigation is composed of a TV mode screen, information mode screen, navigation mode screen, etc., and each mode screen can be called from the panel switch located around the display.


Opening and closing the display panel, insertion / emission operation of disk
・
The display opening and closing operation and the insertion / emission operation of the disk are performed by touching "Disc", "MD", and "Disc & MD" on the TILT / EJECT screen displayed by pressing the "TILT" switch of the panel switch.



Touch switch operation
・
The operation after calling each mode screen (TV / information, etc.) is performed by a touch switch that can be input by directly touching the switch frame displayed on the display screen with a finger.


Voice operation (when the audio recognition switch / microphone is installed in the option setting option)
・
After pressing the voice recognition switch, displaying the audio recognition mark on the screen, the audio command can be performed by uttering the audio recognition microphone to perform navigation and the like, and has excellent operability.
・
There are "standard mode" and "expansion mode" for voice operation, and each can be switched on arbitrarily depending on the application.

Color LCD panel


・
The display uses a LCD panel of the TFT (Thin Film Transistor: thin film transistor), and uses 79.7mm vertical x 143.8mm in vertical: 9:16 (wide screen) x 143.8 mm as the display screen.The liquid crystal cell is subdivided into a pixel of 234 vertical and 1200 in the width, and the liquid crystal cells (pixels) corresponding to each color filter are mixed with the RGB video signal from fully closed to fully opened., You can express all colors.
