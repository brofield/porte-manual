"Wide Multi AV Station" system major components list


Parts name
Main function
“Wide Multi AV Station” (made by Fujitsu Ten)
Various information and operation status are displayed by inputting and processing the main built -in parts (main computer, display panel, TV tuner) and data from components placed in various parts of the vehicle.
Main computer
Back guide monitor computer with RGB video signal from navigation computer*Input and control via, output to the display panel.
The display and function of the "Wide Multi -AV Station" system are controlled according to each operation signal input.
Adjust the color balance of the display screen.
Display panel
The RGB video signal from the main computer displays a map screen and operation screen with a color LCD panel, and displays a TV broadcast signal by the video signal from the TV tuner.
Output each operation screen to the main computer.
TV tuner
Receive and select TV broadcasting radio waves entered from the selector TV antenna amplifier, output the video signal to the display panel, and the audio signal to the speaker.
Navigation computer (made by Aisin Aye Dublyu)
The position of the vehicle is detected by the signal from the built -in GPS receiver.
The direction of travel is detected by a signal from the built -in gyro sensor.
The map information data read by the built-in DVD-ROM player and the calculated position are output to the "Wide Multi AV Station" as the RGB video signal.
The mileage is calculated by the vehicle speed signal from the combination meter.
Output the navigation guidance audio signal to the right front speaker.
DVD-ROM disk for maps
One card is loaded on a built-in DVD-ROM player on the navigation computer, and the data of the map information, guidance audio, and destination search is memorized.
GPS antenna (made by Aisin Seiki)
Receive the orbital signal and signal transmission time that is constantly transmitted from the GPS satellite placed on the orbit surrounding the earth at an altitude of about 20,000 km/h, and output it to the GPS receiver.
TV antenna
Receive the TV broadcast radio wave with four antennas placed on the left and right quarter glass, and output it to the selector TV antenna amplifier.
Selector TV antenna amplifier (made by Fujitsu Ten)
Among the four TV antenna signals, a signal with a good reception sensitivity is selected and amplified, and it is output to the built -in TV tuner to the "Wide Multi AV Station".
speaker
Outputs navigation information signals and audio / TV audio.
Radio antenna
Receive radio broadcast radio waves in the antenna placed on the right quarter glass and output it to the "Wide Multi AV Station".
Combination meter
Car speed signal "Wide Multi AV Station" Navigation Computer / Back Guide Monitor Computer*Output to.
Tail lamp tray
Output the tail lamp or to the signal to the "Wide Multi AV Station".
Parking brake switch
Output the parking brake on signal to the "Wide Multi AV Station".
Neutral start switch
Output the shift position signal to the "Wide Multi AV Station".
Back guide monitor computer*(Made by Aisin Seiki)
The video signal from the navigation computer is relayed and outputs to the "Wide Multi AV Station".
