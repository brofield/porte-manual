HDD navigation (CD / MD type)

●
HDD navigation (CD / MD type) with an audio function of 2005 specification is adopted and combined with 4 speaker systems.

●
Compatible with the wireless communication system "Bluetooth", when using Bluetooth compatible mobile phones, hands -free calls can be performed without connecting cables or cradles, which is excellent in use.For details on the Bluetooth compatible function, refer to the visual system.
●
A touch switch that can perform the audio general operation by directly touching the switch frame displayed on the screen with a finger, and has excellent use.
●
By adopting the CD text display function, the information (artist name, song name, etc.) input in the CD being played in advance can be displayed.In addition, by built -in MD player compatible with MDLP (Long Play), MDs recorded on MDLP -compatible decks and the like are enabled, and the functionality is excellent.
●
By connecting voice recognition switches and microphones (optional options with retailers), the audio operation function that enables audio system and navigation systems can be operated.
●
By adopting an IEEE1394 standard HDD update connector, it has excellent service.

HDD navigation (CD / MD type) operation




Panel switch operation
・
Press the panel switch around the display to perform basic operations such as audio mode switching.


Touch switch operation
・
The operation after calling the audio mode screen by panel switch operation is performed by directly touching the switch frame (touch switch) displayed on the screen with the finger.


Voice operation (when the audio recognition switch / microphone is installed in the option setting option)
・
After pressing the voice recognition switch, displaying the audio recognition mark on the screen, and speaking toward the audio recognition microphone, it is possible to perform each function operation such as switching the audio source, and is excellent in use.did.See the visual system for more information about voice operation.


Disk insertion / emission operation
・
The insertion and emission of the CD are performed by touching the "Disc" switch on the TILT / EJECT screen displayed after touching the "TILT" of the panel switch.
