Power window motor ASSY

●
Power windmorter ASSY is composed of the power window motor body, connector, and gear section.
●
Pulse sensors are integrated in the connector portion for the sandwiching mechanism, and the rotation of the power window motor is detected.
Hall IC


・
The two holes IC outputs pulse signals synchronized with motor rotation to the IC in the power window switch by magnets provided in the power windmotor warmer gear.

・
The power window switch detects the amount of door glass by counting a pulse of one cycle per rotation axis output by the hole IC1.At the same time, the HI/LO period of the pulse that different pulse is detected depending on the rotation direction of the motor output by the hole IC2, and the position of the current door glass is recognized by memorizing these.I am doing it.*The power window switch controls various functions based on the location information obtained from these two holes.
Attending
*
Since the location information of the door glass is backed up by + B power supply, if the + B power supply to the power window switch is cut off, it is necessary to initialize.

・
At the same time, the power window switch detects changes in the movement speed of the door glass by detecting changes in the rotation speed of the motor from the pulse interval output by the hole IC1.As a result, the load on the door glass is recognized, and the inadvertible prevention mechanism and the overload stop function are controlled.
