TRC

Commentary


●
Opening the throttle too much when starting or accelerating on slippery roads, etc. can cause the driving wheels to slip due to excessive torque and impair the starting acceleration and maneuverability.TRC is a system that suppresses drip of the drive wheels by controlling the driving wheel brake hydraulic control, ensures driving force according to the road surface, and ensures the start of the vehicle starting acceleration, straightness and turning stability.。TRC has the following features.・ The subtle accelerator work required for starting and acceleration on the slide road is not required.・ Realizes excellent maneuverability and stability during acceleration.・ You can turn more stably even during acceleration during turning.・ Stable acceleration can be obtained even on the ground surface with different road surface conditions on the left and right driving wheels.
