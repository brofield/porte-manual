Fuel lid interlock

●
When the fuel lid is open, the half -open stopper on the Roir Roller Guide is operated, which interferes with the sliding door side of the sliding door side, and regulates that the sliding door on the left does not open up to the fuel lid.Masu.At this time, the slide door control relay prohibits the power slide operation of the sliding door.

Regulation of sliding door manual operation and closing operation by fuel lid interlock


・
When the fuel lid is opened, the half -open stopper control pin is pushed out by the spring, the half -open stopper control lever attached to the base rotates in the C direction, and the connected cable is drawn.(Part A)
・
When the cable is pulled, a half -open stopper provided in the center of the Rewer rail stands up.If you try to fully open the sliding door in this state, the half -open stopper will interfere with the looper bracket set on the sliding door side, and the sliding door and the fuel lid will not interfere.(Part B)

Regulation of power slide door operation by fuel lid interlock


・
The sliding door has a fuel lid opening detection switch to detect the rotation of the half -open stopper lever, and the ON signal is output to the slide door control relay at the time of fuel lid, when fuel lid is closed.
・
The slide door control relay prohibits the power slide door operation control by entering a fuel lid open detection switch OFF signal.(Reference power slide door system)
