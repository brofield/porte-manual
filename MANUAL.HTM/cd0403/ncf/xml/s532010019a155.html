Alternator

●
IC regulator with an integrated alternator with a slow encouraging function has been adopted.
●
Alternator power generation cut control has been adopted.The engine control computer outputs an alternator's power down signal when accelerating vehicles or low electricity loads.As a result, the fuel efficiency has been improved by reducing the generating load (engine load) of the alternator.
