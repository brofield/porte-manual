Current location display function


・
The position of the vehicle detected by the GPS navigation is displayed on the map screen by combining the vehicle speed signal from the combination meter and road map data from the navigation computer.
・
If you are using the location information from the GPS satellite, display the "GPS" mark under the direction mark displayed on the left side of the screen.
・
If you receive the VICS information displayed on the map, you can display the information provision time under the VICS mark as a VICS time stamp.
Current location display screen





Map display function




Map scale
・
In areas with maps of 1/20.48 million to 1/5000 scale and urban maps, you can display 1/2500 -scale city maps.
・
Switching the scale is performed by touching the "details" or "wide area" switch displayed on the screen.In addition, by touching the "Details" / "Wide Area" switch, you can directly select the scale by directly touching the 13 -stage scale gauge displayed.


Move map (scroll)
・
The cursor key is displayed by touching the part that is not displayed on the touch switch on the current location display screen, and the map is scrolled by touching the cursor key in the direction you want to see the map.In addition, the place name near the center of the screen can be displayed during map scrolling.


Map
・
The direction of the map can be switched to one of the North -up display (displayed so that the north is always up regardless of the vehicle travel direction) or the heading up display (displayed so that the vehicle travel direction is always up).Masu.The display switching is performed by touching the direction mark displayed on the map screen.


Front wide display
・
You can display the map in the vehicle travel direction widely.In addition, the display switching can be performed with "various settings" on the menu screen.


Route direction up
・
In the route guide at the time of 3D display, the map is displayed so that the direction of the route travels is upwards, so that the route in front is easy to see.


Map color change
・
You can select and switch from 5 types of map colors (normal fresh cool chicworm).


3D display
・
Maps can be displayed three -dimensionally.In addition, you can adjust the viewing angle when displaying 3D.In addition, switching to 3D display can be performed by "display change" on the map display screen.


2 Screen display
・
The display of the screen can be divided into two left and right, and the screens with different scales or 3D displays and the screen such as North -Up can be displayed on the left and right screen.In addition, you can switch to each setting, such as switching for maps and displaying facilities independently on the left and right screen.The switching of the display can be performed by "display change" on the map display screen.


City map display
・
If the current location is in the city map display area and the 1/5000 scale screen is displayed, touching the "city map" on the screen, detailed urban maps (1/5000 scales or 1/2500 scale.) Can be displayed.By switching to the 1/2500 -scale city map display when displaying 3D, you can display a real city map that expresses the building in a three -dimensional expression in addition to the city map display.


Visual map
・
In the map display, the thickness of the road on the map is changed according to the actual width of the road, and the landmark on the map is displayed three -dimensionally.In addition, the display ON/OFF can be performed from "various settings" on the menu screen.


Three -dimensional landmark display
・
You can display a three -dimensional facility mark while displaying a map screen below 1/80,000.


Facility display
・
The facility selected while displaying a map screen under 1/80,000 can be displayed on the map screen with a mark character.By touching the "all genres" on the screen, the genre up to 5 facilities can be displayed at the same time.


Urban Highway Map
・
When displaying a map of 10/160,000 or less with a scale of 10,000 or more, the displayed road is limited to urban highway (Metropolitan Expressway, Hanshin Expressway, Nagoya Expressway), and the interchange, junction and route number are displayed.can.
・
The display ON/OFF is performed by "View Change" on the map display screen.


Season Leisure Land Mark
・
While displaying a map screen of 10/80,000 or less, the seasonal leisure land mark (eg, cherry blossom spots in February to April, famous places for autumn leaves in September to November) are displayed on the map screen.can.

・
Switching the display is performed from "Various settings" on the menu screen.

Registration


・
You can register, modify, and delete 10 memory points (100 normally memory, 1 home, 5 special memory) and 10 for detour memory points.In addition, you can enter the name and phone number and select the mark display.

Route information


・
When the destination is set, the information route from the current location to the destination will be displayed and the route guide will be provided by voice.
・
The start of the route guide will be touched on the screen after searching for the route, or if you press "Current location / guide voice" on the panel switch, or run for about 10 seconds after the entire route display screen is completed.Start the route guide to your destination.


Destination settings
・
By pressing the "destination setting" switch on the panel switch, the settings can be performed by migrating to the destination setting screen and searching for the destination by the above "Map Call List".In addition, it is possible to set multiple destinations (up to 5) and change the order of multiple destinations.
Destination setting screen






5 Route Simultaneous Search
・
At the same time, you can search for five routes, and select the guide route from recommended routes, paid road priority, general road priority, distance priority, and different routes.In addition, the five searches are color -coded, displayed in a batch display on the screen, and touch the "All Trial List" on the screen, and the price (minimum fee) (minimum fee) is selected.Estimated), all processes (km), and time required (guideline) can be displayed.


Passing point setting
・
You can set, add, modify, and erase the passing points at the time of route guidance, set priority on paid roads/general roads, specify departure locations, and specify the entrance interchange.


Inspection of detour area consideration
・
By setting the area you want to bypass in advance, you can search the route that avoids the detour when searching for the route.You can register up to 10 detour memory points.


Intersection guide
・
The intersection in the route guide is determined, and the enlarged map near the intersection is displayed and guided.In addition, by expressing narrow angle branches such as the three -dimensional intersection and the entrance to the interchange, it is displayed in an easy -to -understand manner.


Seasonal regulation section information
・
If a route including a section where traffic is restricted for a long period of time is searched, the corresponding part of the guide route will be displayed in a different color, and the message will be displayed on the screen.You can do it.In addition, the ON/OFF of the message is performed in the "various settings" on the menu screen.


Highway mode
・
You can switch to the highway mode screen when you are guiding you on the highway and see the information on the service area, parking area and interchange.In addition, when it reaches the exit interchange or junction of the expressway (about 1 km in front of the city high -speed, capital, and urban high speed), the branch diagram will be displayed and guided.
・
If you do not provide route guidance, you can display the highway mode screen only at high speeds between urban areas.


Illustration guide
・
The three -dimensional guide screen (illustration) will be displayed at the entrance of the urban highway (Metropolitan Expressway, Nagoya Expressway, Hanshin Expressway) and provided guidance in an easy -to -understand manner.


Re -voice
・
In the route guidance, the guidance according to the distance to the next turning point is performed by voice.In addition, by pressing the "current location / guide voice" switch on the panel switch, you can reconfirm the voice guidance that was spoken earlier.


Destination arrival information
・
As you approach the destination, you will be able to guide you to the destination by voice.


Continuous lane (lane) guide
・
During the route guidance, you can continue to display a lane (lane) that can run at all intersections of two or more lanes.


Triotal direction arrow display
・
If your vehicle position is out of the guidance route during the route guide, display the direction of the destination with an arrow.


Traffic accident frequent point warning
・
When a traffic accident frequently occurs during the route guide approaches, the traffic accident frequency mark is displayed on the map screen.


Road shape warning
・
During the route guidance, when the joining, sudden curves and railroad crossings approach, the road shape warning is performed by voice, and a road shape mark is displayed on the map screen.


Rotary information
・
If there is a rotary on the guide route, we will guide you with the rotary entrance and exit as a branch.


Price guide
・
By setting the vehicle number classification (1/3/5, 7) in advance, the toll fee on the toll road will be displayed and the payment guidance will be provided in front of the tollgate or bill inspection.


On -route scrolling
・
By touching the cursor key displayed on the screen, the next destination or the following facilities (selected from each genre such as GS, convenience store, parking lot, bank, post office, etc.)You can scroll automatically.The display of all routes is performed by "Display change" on the map display screen.


All routes
・
You can display all routes of the set guide route and display each information on the information route, such as all processes and tolls.The display of all routes is performed by "Display change" on the map display screen.


Route re -search
・
Re -search for information routes from the current location, re -search for all guidance routes, and set up exploration conditions (recommended, paid roads, general roads, distance priority, different routes), and re -search (only in the case of information routes)You can.


Route automatic exploration
・
In the route guide, if your vehicle position is out of the guidance route, you can automatically search the guide route and display a new guide route from your current location.
