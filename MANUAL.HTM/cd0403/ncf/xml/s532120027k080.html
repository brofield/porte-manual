Auto -leveling system

●
The leveling function of the car equipped with a discharge head lamp has been changed from conventional manual leveling to auto -leveling system.The auto -leveling system is a system that controls the headlamp leveling actuator according to changes in the vehicle posture, and keeps the direction of the optical axis constant, preventing dazzling light on on -the -other vehicles and the previous car.
●
A head lamp leveling computer that controls an auto -leveling system is located in the instrument panel, and a high control sensor that detects changes in vehicle posture is placed in the rear suspension pension.
Auto -leveling operation


・
The head lamp leveling computer that controls the auto -leveling system drives the headlamp leveling actuator according to the driving status and posture change of the vehicle by inputting the vehicle speed signal and the high control sensor from the height control sensor, and the headlamp leveling actuator is driven.Controls the optical axis of.
