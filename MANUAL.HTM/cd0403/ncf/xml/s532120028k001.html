Front wiper

●
Washer with a mist function has been used as a standard for all vehicles.
●
The mounting structure of the wiper motor and the link is used as a modular type of the frame -integrated type, reducing the sound of the wiper operation sound, and the wiping angle of the wiper is small.
●
The U -Hook type was adopted as a wiper arm and wiper blade method, and the workability was excellent at the time of appearance and blade replacement.
●
The length of the wiper blade was 550mm on the driver's seat and 425mm for the passenger side.
●
The wiper blade is a cross -sectional shape with no metal contact, and by applying a graphite coating to the blade rubber surface, the impact sound during blade reversal and the reduction of chatter when wiping is reduced.
●
The wiper control relay was built into the wiper control switch to simplify the wire harness.
●
The winter blade options have been set for all cars.The driver's side has been able to select a blade with fins, and has excellent wiping on the wiper during high -speed driving.

Front wiper operation




Intermittent operation / time adjustment interim
・
When the wiper control switch is operated in the int position, the current from the condenser C1, which is the charging completed state, flows from the wiper control switch int1 terminal to the wiper control switch INT2 terminal → TR1, and turns TR1.
・
When TR1 is turned on, the current flows from the wiper control switch + S terminal → wiper control switch + 1 terminal → wiper motor → earth, and the motor rotates.
・
At the same time as the motor rotates, the capacitor C1 is discharged through the wiper control switch int1 terminal → the wiper control switch INT2 terminal, and when the discharge is completed, the TR1 is turned off, so the relay contact is turned off and the wiper motor stops.
・
When the relay contact is turned off, the capacitor C1 starts charging again, continues to turn off TR1 until the charge is completed, which is the intermittent stop time.
・
When the condenser C1 is completed, TR1 turns on, and the relay contact is turned on again.
・
The repetition of the discharge and charging is the intermittent operation.
・
Time adjustments are adjusted by adjusting the volume (variable resistance) to change the amount of current flowing from + IG to condenser C1, and the charging time of the condenser C1 changes from the minimum to about 1.6 seconds to a maximum of about 10.7 seconds.This changes the relay contact OFF time (intermittent operation time) when intermittent operation.


Washer linked operation
・
When the washer switch is turned on, the current flows out from + B power → washer motor → washer switch W terminal → washer switch EW terminal → earth.
・
At the same time as the washer solution erupts, the current flows to TR2, so TR2 turns on.When TR2 is turned on, TR1 also turns on, current flows on the relay coil, and the relay contact is turned on.
・
When the relay contact is turned on, the current flows from wiper control switch + S terminal → wiper control switch + 1 terminal → wiper motor → ground, and rotates the wiper motor.
・
When the motor rotates, the condenser C2 is discharged for about 2.2 seconds after the washer switch is off because the capacitor C2 has been charged.
・
By discharging the capacitor for about 2.2 seconds, the TR1 continues to turn on for about 2.2 seconds, so the wiper motor continues to rotate, and when the condenser C2 is completed, the wiper motor stops.
