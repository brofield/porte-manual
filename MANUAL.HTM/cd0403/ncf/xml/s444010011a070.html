Fuel injection control (EFI)

●
An EFI-L method that detects inhaled air by the airflow meter and controls fuel injection.
●
The injection method was adopted an independent injection method (injection of each cylinder with a crankshaft 2 rotation).When starting, a group injection ( # 1 + # 4, # 2 + # 3) is performed and sprayed once in a crankshaft one rotation.When the engine speed reaches higher than the specified value, switch to an independent injection.In addition, if the water temperature is high, such as when restarting after the engine is stopped, an independent injection will be performed from the start.
●
In the fuel injection, a synchronous injection that is always informed by a signal from each sensor at the basic injection time and is always injected at the same position, and a non -synchronous injection that is injected at the time of detecting a signal by a signal from each sensor regardless of the crank angle.there is.In addition, a fuel cut is performed to temporarily stop fuel injection according to the operation status to protect the engine and improve fuel efficiency.
Asynchronous injection


・
In order to ensure the startability and acceleration at the time of acceleration, a certain amount of injection is performed simultaneously immediately after the signal from each sensor enters separately from the normal fuel injection (synchronous injection).In addition, the injection time is extended by asynchronous injection during synchronous injection.

Asynchronous injection

Asynchronous injection when starting
Immediately after the starter signal is entered, an asynchronous injection is performed once to improve the startability.
Asynchronous injection at acceleration
Asynchronous injection is performed when the increase in the throttle opening is higher than the setting value, and the response at the time of acceleration is improved.
Asynchronous injection when the engine rotates
If the engine rotation drops sharply during the fuel cut and the fuel cut, a asynchronous injection is performed to secure operation.


Synchronous injection


・
Synchronous injection time can be expressed in the following formula.
・
When starting the engine, the injection time is determined by the engine speed, battery voltage and cooling water temperature.In addition, the amount of injection is increased or decreased depending on the cranking time to prevent over -injection at the start.

Synchronous injection = A. Basic injection time + B. Correction injection time + C. Invalid injection time

A. Basic injection time
This is the basic injection time determined by the amount of inhaled air and the engine speed.
B. Correction injection time
The signal of each sensor is a coefficient to make the air -fuel ratio mixed according to the engine status at that time.
B-1 intake absurdening correction
When the inhalation air temperature is low, the air density increases, so increase.
B-2 warm-up increased weight correction
When the cooling water temperature is low, increase the amount and ensure drivingability during the cold.
B-3 increase after starting
After starting the engine, increase the amount of cooling water temperature and engine speed, stabilizing the engine rotation after starting.The increase ratio is the maximum immediately after starting, and then gradually decreases.
B-4 Translement Vacuum-fuel ratio correction
The acceleration deceleration is determined from the change in the amount of inhaled air, and the weight or weight loss is performed according to the condition.
B-5 wall adhesion correction
Calculate the fuel wall attached from the cooling water temperature, engine speed, and the throttle opening.
B-6 High load increase correction
High -load operation is determined by the signal from each sensor, and increasing the amount is prevented from rising exhaust temperature.
B-7 air-fuel ratio feedback control
O2Correction is performed by the signal from the sensor, and the air -fuel ratio is controlled in a narrow range near the a theory of the highly purified performance of the triple catalyst.
C. Disabled injection time
Correct the lag delay of the injector.


Fuel cut


・
The fuel injection is temporarily stopped for engine protection and fuel efficiency.
Fuel cut


Delivery fuel cut
When the engine control computer is decelerated (when the engine control computer is judged to be throttle OFF), the engine speed is higher than the specified value, and the fuel injection is stopped to prevent the catalytic overheating and the fuel efficiency improvement.If the cooling water temperature is low, the fuel cut rotation speed and the number of return rotations will be higher.
Fuel cut by engine speed
If the engine speed is higher than the specified value, the fuel injection is stopped to prevent excessive rotation.
"N" → "D" Fuel cut when shifting in range
If the engine speed is or higher, the fuel injection is stopped during the shift lever operation to reduce the shift shock and the A/T protection.
