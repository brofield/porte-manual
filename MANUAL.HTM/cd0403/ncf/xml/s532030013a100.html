Rabinio -type planetary gear structure


・
Rabinio -type planetary gears are composed of inter -medium -weight shafts, front planetary sangiers, rear planetary sangiers, planary short pinions, planetary long pinions, and planetary ring gears.
・
Ravinio -type planetary gears are driven from three systems: interlaider shaft, front planetary solan gear and rear planetary sangiers.
・
In addition, the output part of the driving force is performed in one planetary ring gear, and is transmitted to a default via the planetary output shaft and counter drive gear.
・
By switching the frictional element, the driving force is changed, and the short and long planetary gear of a short planetary gear can cause a gear ratio of 4 steps and one step of retreat.


Rabinio -type planetary gear operation



1st gear

・
The lock of the planetary carrier regulates both the planetary short pinion and the planetary long pinion.As a result, the driving force from the front planetary sangiaga is transmitted to the ring gears using the planetary short pinion and planetary long pinion as idolaging.
・
Gear ratio: Planetary ring gear (69) / Front planetary sangya (24) = 2.875


2nd gear

・
The lock of the rear planetary solan gears allows the planetary short pinion and planetary long pinion to transmit the driving force to the planetary ring gear.
・
Gear ratio: Planetary ring gear (69) / (Front Planetary Sangiya (24) + rear Planetary Sangi (20)) = 1.568


3rd gear

・
The input is transmitted to the planetary carrier.At the same time, the input of the same rotation number is transmitted to the front planetary solar gear, and the entire gear unit is the same as the input shaft.
・
Gear ratio: 1.000 (directly connected)


4th gear

・
The input is transmitted to the planetary carrier, and the number of rotation is transmitted as the input axis.At this time, since the rear planetary sangiers are restricted in rotation, the planetary long pinion is revolved while rotating and transmits the rotation speed that has increased to planetary ring gears.
・
Gear ratio: Planetary ring gear (69) / (Rear Planetary Sangiers (30) + Planetary Ring Gear (69)) = 0.697


REV gear

・
The input is transmitted to the rear planetary sangiaga, transmitting the planetary long pinion fixed by the planetary carrier to the planetary ring gear as an idolaga.At this time, the planetary long pinion works as an idolaga.
・
Gear ratio: Planetary ring gear (69) / rear planetary solar gear (30) = 2.300
