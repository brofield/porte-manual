OCV for VVT-I

●
By controlling the position of the spool valve by the duty signal from the engine control computer, the oil supply of the VVT-I controller to the ocean room and the late angle room is adjusted.

At the time of proceeding


・
The OCV spool valve moves to the left side, the VVT-I controller is applied to the ocean room, and the intake cam shaft moves toward advancement.


Time late angle


・
The OCV spool valve moves to the right side, the VVT-I controller is applied to the late angle room, and the intake cam shaft moves in the late angle direction.


Time of maintenance


・
The hydraulic pressure of the VVT-I controller and the late-angle room are maintained as they are, and the phase of the intake cam shaft is also maintained.
