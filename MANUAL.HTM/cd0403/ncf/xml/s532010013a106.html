Intake mani -hold

●
A resin intake mani -hold that is integrated with the configuration parts and vibration welding is adopted.
●
By using resin, the intake temperature has been reduced, and the weight has been significantly reduced and compact.In addition, high output has been achieved by improving the surface roughness inside the port.
●
By gradually changing the long brunch and the port cross section, we have increased torque up and high -speed at low and medium speeds.
●
Intake mani -hold gaskets have an O -ring type to consider the service.
●
The negative pressure port is integrated with the intake manly hold, and the structure is simplified and the weight is reduced.
●
2NZ-FE and 1NZ-FE have the same basic shape, but the port diameter, the length of the port, and the surge tank / resonator capacity are different.
