High rigidity

●
In order to ensure body rigidity, each part structure has been optimized, such as effectively arranging re -in horsements and other members on a highly impactful area.This ensures excellent body rigidity while adopting a large open sliding door, and achieving stability and quietness as well as collision safety performance at a high level.
●
In order to end the front of the front part at the time of the collision, the reinforcement and bulk head in front of the sliding door are optimally placed.In addition, reinforcements next to the floor tunnel contribute to the floor deformation suppression in the case of front collision.
