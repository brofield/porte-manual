Back Guide Monitor System Function Overview

●
The back guide monitor synthesizes on the image of the back monitor camera attached to the back of the vehicle, and displays the distance approximately a guide, extension of vehicle width, predictive route, and parking guide line on the screen of the "Wide Multi AV Station"., A system that assists retreat operation.At the time of parallel parking, the output of the guidance sound can be performed.
●
By setting the shift position "R" (retreat) when the ignition switch is ON, the image behind the vehicle from the back monitor camera is displayed on the "Wide Multi AV Station" screen.。
　■ Warning ■
　
The back guide monitor is an auxiliary device for easily performing backward checks, so be sure to drive while directly checking the safety of the rear and surroundings.
