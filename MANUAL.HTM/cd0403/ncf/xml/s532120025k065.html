Water Tempala Chagage

●
A low -temperature indicator lamp and a high water temperature indicator lamp that displays the engine cooling liquid temperature by lighting/off.
●
When the engine cooling liquid temperature is about 117 ° C or higher, the high water temperature warning indicator lamp (red) flashes.
●
When the engine cooling liquid temperature is less than about 50 ° C, the light temperature indicator lamp (blue) is displayed.
●
If the engine cooling liquid temperature is within the range of about 55 to 116.5 ° C, the temperature is appropriate, and the high water temperature warning and low -temperature indicator lamp are turned off.

Water Tempala Chagage operation


・
The engine control computer outputs the engine cooling liquid temperature detected by the water temperature sensor as a liquid temperature signal.The meter computer lights the low -temperature indicator lamp until the engine cooling liquid temperature reaches about 55 ° C according to the liquid temperature signal.When the engine cooling liquid temperature reaches 117 ° C, the high water temperature warning indicator lamp flashes at a 0.6 second period (about 0.3 seconds/about 0.3 seconds OFF).
