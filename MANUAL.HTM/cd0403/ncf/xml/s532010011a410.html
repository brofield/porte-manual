A/F sensor (AIR-FUEL RATIO SENSOR)

●
A/F sensor (Kunen Hena) has been adopted to ensure that the air -fuel ratio is in the exhaust gas.In order to promote the early activation of the sensor, it is a laminated sensor structure, and the engine starts early air -fuel feedback control has been achieved.
●
A/F sensor (Kuunen Hesensa) outputs lean or rich signal on the theoretical aircraft ratio O2The engine control computers can control the air -fuel ratio in a more specific output characteristics of the sensor (oxidizes ensensor).
●
In order to activate the A/F sensor (Kunen Hena), the sensor (zirconia element) must be heated.The cup -type sensor is indirectly heated through the atmosphere, so it takes time to activate, while the laminated sensor directly heats the zirconia element to reduce the activity time of the sensor.In addition, the miniaturization of zirconia elements has reduced heat trout.
●
The installation position is the exhaust manifold part in the upper triple catalyst.
