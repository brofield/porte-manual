Brake booster

●
9 "Single -type brake booster has been adopted.
●
A mechanical brake assist system has been adopted.

Structure of brake booster with mechanical brake assist


・
A hook, slide valve, and spring that operates due to a certain amount of relative displacement between the input rod and the power piston is set on the brake booster flow control valve.


Activation of brake booster with mechanical brake assist (brake non -activated state)


・
Like a general brake booster, the control valve is open, and the transformation room and the constant pressure room are in contact.


Activation of brake booster with mechanical brake assist (when brake assist)


・
When the brake pedal is stepped on, the control valve closes and the air valve opens, as in the general brake booster.At this time, an air is introduced in the constant pressure room and the booster works.
・
In addition, both the control valve and the air valve are closed in the brake operation and holding state, and the transformation room and the constant pressure room are kept at a certain level.


Activation of brake booster with mechanical brake assist (when brake assist operation)


・
If the operation speed of the brake is large, a major relative displacement will occur on the input rod and power piston, and the hook will come off the slide valve.The slide valve outside the hook presses the control valve and opens a larger air valve than when it works.As a result, more air is introduced by the constant pressure room, and you can get a large output.

Attending
Even if you step on the brake at the normal speed, if you step beyond the assistance limit, or if the negative pressure in the booster is small, the travel distance and speed of the power piston will be reduced.The brake assist is activated due to the relative position change between the power piston and the input rod, but the braking force is the same as the normal operation because it is a part that exceeds the assistance limit.

Activation of brake booster with mechanical brake assist (after brake assist operation)


・
With the brake OFF, the hook returns to the initial position against the slide valve.


Brake -assisted way of thinking


・
During the panic, the braking speed of the brake is fast, but it cannot be stepped on a small braking force.(A) in the figure
・

・

・
"Brake assist" reduces braking power assistance when the driver is consciously loosened after operation, and reduces discomfort.(D in the figure)


Precautions of brake assist


・
Brake assist is not a device that brings out the performance that exceeds the original ability of the brake.Please be careful about the car speed, the distance between the vehicles, etc. and perform safe driving.
・
If the brake pedal is stepped at a steep speed, the brake may be strongly applied and a small hitting can be generated, but this is not an unusual phenomenon when the brake assist is operating normally.
・
If you step on the brake pedal deeply at the time of stop, there may be a "click" sound, but it is not anomalous when the assist mechanism is working properly.
