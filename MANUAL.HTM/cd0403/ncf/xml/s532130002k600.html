Brake pedal retreat reduction structure

●
By moving the brake pedal downward during the front collision, the brake pedal retreat reduction structure, which minimizes the retreat of the brake pedal, has been adopted to relieve the impact on the lower limbs of the driver.
●
When the brake booster part is pushed in due to a retreat of an engine during a collision, the clip (bolt) that fixes the brake pedal support and the instrument panel rein Housing has left the brake pedal support, and the brake pedal support is the guide as it is.Move along the plate to the back and down.As a result, the brake pedal step surface is moved to the forward and lower.
