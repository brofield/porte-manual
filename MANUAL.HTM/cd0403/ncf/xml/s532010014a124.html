Cylinder head -related items

●
A resin engine cover has been provided at the top of the cylinder head cover to reduce the engine noise and improve its appearance.
●
The cylinder head cover is made of lightweight and high rigid aluminum alloy.The conclusion with the cylinder head has been tightened, which has improved the seal.
●
The cylinder head cover gasket has a low acrylic rubber ring type.The sticker of the spark plug tube part has been integrated to simplify the structure and improve the workability when attaching and detaching.
●
The cylinder head is made of lightweight and high -rigid aluminum alloy.Integrated#1 intake and#1 exhaust cam caps, built-in VVT-I operating oil passage, optimization of thickness, etc. has been reduced.
●
The cylinder head gasket has adopted a two -layer type with a shim around the cylinder bore.The adoption of the shim has increased the seal surface of the cylinder bore, ensuring gas sheet and durability.
