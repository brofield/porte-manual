A 2 -way exhaust system has been adopted inside the main muffler to improve exhaust efficiency and engine output while ensuring quietness.A variable valve to adjust the exhaust gas passage area has been set.The variable valve is closed during idling and low rotation by return spring.

2way exhaust system operation

When the valve is closed
Due to the low pressure of exhaust gas in the main muffler, the variable valve is closed, and exhaust gas only enhances the expansion ratio through only small holes, and functions as a muffler that emphasizes the silence function.
When the valve is opened
As the engine speed increases, the pressure of the exhaust gas in the main muffler increases, the variable valve is opened and the aisle is expanded, which functions as a low -back pressure type muffler.
