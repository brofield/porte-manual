Cylinder block

●
In addition to integrating the water pump vortex and the inlet aisle of the pump, the cooling water return passage, water inlet, etc. from the cylinder head have been integrated to reduce weight and compact.
●
The water jacket was opened as an open deck tip, deepening the counter bolt of the cylinder head bolt to reduce the bore deformation.In addition, by casting the water jacket between the bore to the inside as much as possible, the cooling performance between the bore has been improved to improve the performance and the oil consumption has been reduced.
●
The oil return passage from the cylinder head was extended to the oil pan split surface, so that the oil did not hit the crankshaft directly, the friction was reduced.
●
The crankshaft bearing cap has been concluded in the plastic zone, and the conclusion power has been stabilized.
●
By pressing the oil seal directly into the cylinder block without using the rear oil seal retainer, it has been compact by shortening the overall length of the engine.
●
The rear end is a morning glory shape, and the binding rigidity with the transmission is enhanced, and the rigidity of the entire power plant has been ensured.
