Back monitor camera

●
The back monitor camera is composed of an image signal output unit consisting of an image input unit using an ultra -wide -angle lens and a small (1/4 inch) color CCD camera.
●
The image signal output part is a CCD (Charge Couped Device) images of the images captured (input) by the lens to convert the image to an electrical signal according to the intensity of light and back guide monitor computer (Output to the "Wide Multi AV Station" equipped car).
●
The image screen of the back monitor camera is inverted in the room mirror to match the back, so the output video from the camera itself is reversed.Therefore, it is important to note that when you actually look at the back and the image on the screen, the image on the screen is reflected on the left and right (the one on the left side of the vehicle is displayed on the left side of the screen, the one on the right side of the vehicle is displayed on the right side of the screen).
