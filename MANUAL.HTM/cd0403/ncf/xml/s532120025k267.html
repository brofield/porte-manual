Fuel gauge

●
The fuel gauge displays the fuel remaining amount by lighting/off the 8 -segment burger.
●
The liquid volume signal in the fuel tank from the fuel sender gauge drives the LCD driver to light the applicable LCD segment (fuel remaining display function).
●
The fuel gauge No.2 segment in the meter turns off (the remaining fuel amount is about 6.8L), and at the same time, the No.1 segment "E" mark begins to blink and warns that the fuel remaining is low (fuel residue.Quantity warning display function).

Fuel gauge operation


・
The display is rewritten when the average value of the input signal, which has been 256 times every 0.1 seconds, is increased or reduced by 12 consecutive times by the input signal from the fuel sensor gauge.When the ignition switch is turned on, the average value of 4 times is displayed.
