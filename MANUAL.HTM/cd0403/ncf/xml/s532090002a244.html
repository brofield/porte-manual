Features of SRS airbag system


SRS Airbag System


SRS airbag
If you receive a strong shock that causes serious harm to the passenger from the front of the vehicle, the body of the seat belt will be blown up instantly, inflating the bag that will be a cushion instantly, and the passenger's head and chest will be injured.Reduce it.
Preten Shonor+Force Remitter Seat Belt
The Priten Shonor Mechanism will enhance the vehicle's restraint effect by instantly drawing the seat belt when a strong impact from the front of the vehicle.
The Force Limiter Mechanisms maintains the prescribed value when the seat belt is applied above the specified value and reduces the impact on the chest.


SRS Airbag & Preten Shoner Seat Belt System Activity


・
The SRS airbag & pliten shonner seat belt is operated when a certain shock is perceived in front of the front collision.
・
The operation of the SRS airbag is assumed that the collision speed is about 25 km/h or more on the wall that does not deform or moved during a collision, about 30 °.
・
In addition, the front airbag sensor and the sapping sensor may operate in a situation where a strong impact from the lower part of the vehicle is detected because the deceleration rate is detected during collisions.(When hitting a curb, when you fall into a deep hole or groove, you jump and hit the ground, or fall from the road.)


SRS Airbag & Preten Shoner Seat Belt System Non -operating case


・
The front airbag sensor and the sapping sensor detect the deceleration at the time of the collision working from the front direction of the vehicle, so depending on the situation, the airbag system may not operate.Also, if the deceleration rate does not reach the prescribed value of the sensor even in front of the collision, the system will not work.
