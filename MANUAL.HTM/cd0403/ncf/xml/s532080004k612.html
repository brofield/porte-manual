Scroll compressor

●
The SCSA06 type (scroll compressor with a CS type oil separator) has been adopted to save power.
Structure of the scrolling part


・
It consists of a whirlpool -type fixed scrolling and a fixing scrolling and a movable scroll.The fixed scrolling is integrated with the housing, and the volume of the space that can be partitioned by both scrolling changes, inhalation, compression and discharging of refrigerant gas because the movable scroll is revolved while maintaining the same posture by the rotation of the shaft.In addition, the pins attached to the rear of the movable scroll to prevent the rotation of the movable scrolling and only revolve.


Operation of the scrolling club


・
When the volume of the compression chamber formed between the fixed scrolling increases due to the revolution of the movable scroll, the refrigerant gas is inhaled from the inhalation port.(Inhaled operation)
・
As the inhalation of the inhalation progresses further, the volume of the compression chamber gradually decreases.In accordance with this, the inhaled refrigerant gas is gradually compressed and is sent to the center of the fixed scroll, and the compression of the refrigerant gas is completed by rotating the movable scrolling about two rotations.(Compression operation)
・
When the compression of the refrigerant gas is completed and the refrigerant pressure increases, the discharge ports are pressed from the discharge port provided in the center of the fixed scroll and the refrigerant gas is discharged.(Discharge operation)


CS type oil separator


・
A CS -type oil separator has been adopted as a separator that reduces the circulation rate of compressor oil, which is stuck in the refrigerant and circulates inside the frozen cycle.The CS -type oil separator is separated from the discharge gas entrance by attaching a cylindrical pipe into the separator case into a refrigerant and oil by centrifugal force, and suppressing oil leaks to the discharge side serviceport.I am doing it.As a result, the oil circulation rate has been reduced and power savings have been made.


CS type oil separator function


・
The discharge from the discharge gas entrance and oil flows while rotating around the cylindrical pipe in the oil separator.At this time, the refrigerant gas is separated into a refrigerant and oil with different specific gravity due to the centrifugal force generated at the time of rotation, and the light -specific refrigerant is from the discharge side serviceport to the outside of the compressor through the pipe, and the heavy oil in the shutter is the oil in the shutter.It is discharged from the emission hole and stored in the oil storage room.The stored height difference is supplied to the compressor and circulates inside the compressor.
