Helpnet system handling precautions


・
Depending on the radio wave condition, emergency calls and calls cannot be made.
・
Outside the mobile phone service area, it is not possible to call emergency calls even if the emergency call is started.If you can't do an emergency call, please contact us from the nearest public phone.
・
In the event of a vehicle collision, if the equipment required for communication occurs or damage, emergency calls and calls may not be possible.
・
If the voltage of the battery or the connection with the battery is cut off, it may not be possible to call.
・
While waiting for rescue after emergency call, evacuate to a safe and safe place to talk with the center.
・
Stop in a safe place for emergency calls by button operation.
・
All communication charges will be borne by the customer.
・
Please do not use some phone functions (dial locks, etc.) as they hinder emergency calls.In addition, the functions that hinder emergency calls differ depending on the telephone or telecommunications carrier used.

Hands -free system configuration




Voice recognition microphone / audio recognition utterance switch (Optional setting for dealers)


The voice recognition microphone is a single -direction box -type microphone.The mounting position was the upper part of the steering column for the voice recognition microphone, and the voice recognition utterance switch was the left side of the steering column.


Mobile phone cradle (optional options with retailers)


We will broadcast the audio and data communication between your mobile phone and the "Wide Multi AV Station".The mounting position is a passenger seat seat.


Telephone switch (Optional option setting for retailer)


Two switches for hands -free calls are provided with off -hook and on -hooks.The mounting position is the right side of the steering column.
