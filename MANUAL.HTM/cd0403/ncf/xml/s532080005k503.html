Air volume control

●
In addition to manual control, auto control such as air volume multiple stages, warm -up, and late -force control is adopted.
Blower startup control


・
When a blower starts, the blower voltage at the time of Auto LO is output to the blower motor controller to protect the blower motor controller from the rapid startup current when the blower motor is started.

Manual control


・
Make the air volume according to the operation position of the blower switch.

Multi -step control of air volume


・
By turning on the AUTO switch of the air conditioner control panel, the blower motor controller is automatically controlled by the voltage of the auto -aircon -ammempling fire, and the air volume is controlled in multiple stages according to the required balloons (TAO).


Warm -up control


・
When the outlet mode is FOOT or Bi-Level, turn off the blower motor until the engine cooling liquid temperature is higher than a certain level.When the cooling liquid temperature is T2 ° C or higher, the blower motor rotates with LO, and is turned off below T1 ° C.The cooling liquid temperature is compared with the cooling liquid temperature and the air volume calculated from the required blown temperature (TAO), and is automatically controlled by the smaller air volume.When the cooling liquid temperature is T3 ° C or higher, the blower motor is rotated HI, and then controlled by the calculation of the required blown temperature (TAO).


Slow winding control


・
Late -winding control is calculated when the amount of air is calculated by the solar radiation sensor, comparing the calculation air volume by the solar radiation sensor than the calculated air volume (TAO), and the amount of air calculated by the necessary blowout temperature (TAO).If the calculation of the solar radiation sensor is smaller, compare the calculation air volume with the necessary blowout temperature (TAO) and the slow calculation wind volume.This prevents hot air outlet from the center register during Face and Bi-Level.

As shown in the figure, rotate the blower motor in manual Lo mode for about T1 seconds.Then compare the delay calculation wind volume to about T2 seconds with the required blowout temperature (TAO) or the calculated air volume by the solar radiation sensor, and automatically control the air volume.After about T2 seconds, it is controlled by the calculation of the required blown temperature (TAO).


Solar radiation control control


・
Due to Face or Bi-Level, the signal (solar radiation amount) from the solar radiation sensor changes the air volume at the time of LO level (minimum air volume) to five levels by the signal (solar radiation) from the solar radiation sensor.If the sunlight is strong, increase the blower speed and control the blower speed if the sun is weak.
