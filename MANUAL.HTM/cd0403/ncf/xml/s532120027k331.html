Light control computer

●
Light control computer is a device that supplies the exchange (AC) power supply required to light the HID valve.
●
The frequency of the AC power supply is several hundred Hz or more, so it is considered so that it does not flicker when lighting, and electromagnetic shield measures have been applied so that it does not affect in -vehicle radio due to electromagnetic waves.

Light control computer operation


・
Light control computers achieve instantaneous lighting and stable lighting of HID valve by controlling high voltage of up to about 23000V.In addition, since this device generates high voltage, various failed functions are included, and the reliability of heat, humidity, vibration, etc. is also considered.


HID valve lighting control flow

1. High voltage (about 20000V) is applied between the two electrodes of the HID valve.
2. After a high -pressure Xenon gas in the light emission tube, starts discharge.
3. Discharge the light -emitting tube temperature.This evaporates metal iodide.
4. The evaporated metal iodine (sodium, scandium) is dissociated with metal atoms and iodine in the discharge arc.
5. The metal atoms are excited and emit light with the spectrum of metal atoms (sodium, scandium).


Light Control Computer Fail Safe


・
Light control computers have the following fail -safe functions for high voltage.

If the input voltage is out of the operating voltage (9 to 16V), the headlamp is stopped and the headlamp is re -lamp as soon as it returns to the operation current range.However, if the input voltage decreases after lighting, the lighting is continued until the lighting limit voltage (6V or less) is reached.

If a lamp voltage is detected, the headlamp is discontinued and the power is re -turned and the power is re -installed (head lampsch OFF → ON or ignition switch OFF → ON).


Handling precautions for light control computers


　■ Warning ■
　
The output terminal of the light control computer has a high voltage, so it is very dangerous, so be careful when handling it.In addition, a caution label was placed on the headlamps and light control computers to prevent danger.


　■ Attention ■
　
Please be sure to replace the valve of the discharge headlamp at the dealer.
Do not touch the glass part of the valve and the electrode part because it is dangerous due to high voltage.
The bulb lights should be done after the assembly is completely completed, and do not use the power supply other than the car.
In the case of a light control computer and headlamps (list of replacement requirements) in the light control computer and headlamps, replace the light control computer or headlamps.

Replacement requirements list

When there is a clear deformation (sled, twisted, dent, dent, cracking, etc.) on the light control computer.
If there is a crack / chip of the resin portion of each connector and valve socket, or a deformation of the terminal part.
When the contact with the ground part on the reflector side is unstable due to the transformation of the socket cover.
When the high -pressure harness / power wire harness is scratched.

Please note that light control computers are not necessarily normal even if they are temporarily operated normally.
