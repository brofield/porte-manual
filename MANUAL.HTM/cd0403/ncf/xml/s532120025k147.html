Seat belt non -wearing warning system

●
A flashing sheet belt non -wearing system that promotes wearing seat belts in the driver's seat and passenger seat has been adopted.The arrangement of the warning indicator lamp was set in the combination meter for the driver's seat, and the passenger side side was in the air conditioner control panel.
●
A crew detection sensor that detects the presence or absence of a cushion on the portion of the passenger seat cushion.
Seat belt non -wearing warning function




Driving seat seat belt Non -wearing Warning
・
When the ignition switch is turned on, the driver's seat belt non -wear Warning indicator lamp starts blinking and promotes the driver to wear the seat belt.
・
When the driver's seat belt is detected (the tung plate is locked to the driver's seat belt inner buckle), the warning indicator lamp turns off.


Passenger seat seat belt Non -wearing Warning
・
When the crew detection sensor detects that there is a passenger seat crew when the ignition switch is turned on, the passenger seat belt non -wearing Warning indicator lamp starts blinking, promoting the passenger seat crew to wear the seat belt.
・
When the passenger seat belt is detected (the tung plate is locked to the passenger seat belt inner buckle), the warning indicator lamp turns off.


Seat belt non -wearing warning operation




Driving seat seat belt Non -wearing Warning
・
For the meter computer, if the driver's seat belt is not worn when the driver's seat belt is not locked when the driver's seat belt is not locked at the time of the ignition switch, enter the driver's seat belt buckle switch ON.As a result, the meter computer turns TR1 and flashes the LED for the driver's seat seat belt Warning indicator.
・
When the driver's seat belt is worn (the tung plate is locked to the buckle), entering the driver's seat seat belt buckle switch OFF, the meter computer turns off the LED for the driver's seat belt non -worn Warning indicator.


Passenger seat seat belt Non -wearing Warning
・
The meter computer is a passenger seat crew boarding and the passenger seat belt is not locked (the tang plate is not locked) when the meter computer is ON ON, and the crew detection sensor ON and the passenger seat seat belt Backle Switch ON.Enter.As a result, the meter computer turns TR2 and blinks the passenger seat belt belt warning indicator.
・
The crew detection sensor is turned off, or the passenger seat belt is worn (the tung plate locks to the buckle), and when the passenger seat buckle switch is entered, the meter computer turns off the over -the -pass seat belt non -wearing warning indicator.。
