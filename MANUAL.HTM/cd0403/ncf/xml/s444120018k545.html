Back guide monitor system control ("Wide Multi AV Station" equipped car)

●
The back guide monitor computer has a back monitor camera attached to the back of the vehicle, and the signal of each guide line created based on vehicle status information based on the steering angle sensor, etc.In addition to output, a voice guide will be provided by the right front speaker.
●
The "Wide Multi AV Station" is displayed on the display on the display by entering signals from the back guide monitor computer and controlling the internal main computer.
