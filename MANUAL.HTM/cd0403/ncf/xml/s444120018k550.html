Back guide monitor system handling precautions

●
Please note the following when using the back guide monitor system.
Back guide monitor in general precautions


・
Do not overestimate the back guide monitor.Please be careful to do the retreat operation as well as the back guide monitor -in -case car.
・
Never retreat while looking at the screen.The actual situation is different from the video projected on the screen, and if you look at the screen alone, you may hit a car or cause unexpected accidents.When retreating, be sure to use the room mirror and door mirror together to directly check the backward and surrounding safety.
・
Do not give a strong impact or power to the back monitor camera.The camera may cause a crazy malfunction.Adjustment for the camera requires special tools, so be sure to go to the dealer.
・
Use with the backdoor closed.
・
Do not use when the road surface is slippery (when the road surface is frozen, snowy roads, etc.) or using a tire chain.If you use it, you may hit a car or lead to an unexpected accident.

Display precautions regarding the use of a parallel guide


・
Even if you park in a parallel with a column guide, you may hit the car depending on the operation method, road conditions, road conditions, and your own vehicle, so be sure to drive while directly checking the back and surrounding safety.Please do.
・
Use a parallel guide when parking on a flat and straight road.Do not use when the parking position is a curve or slope.You may hit a car or lead to unexpected accidents.

Back guide monitor and other precautions


・
If you attach a light -type license plate, the light of the license plate will be reflected in the back monitor camera, making it difficult to see the display of the back guide monitor screen, so simultaneous simultaneous light guide monitor systems.Do not install.
・
"System initialization" is displayed on the screen after the battery replacement, etc., but if you cut the steering to the left and right, or if you run for about 5 minutes, the initialization will be terminated and return to the normal display.
・
If you replace the tire size with a different one, the expected proceeding route may be inaccurate.
・
Be sure to check the backwards and surrounding safety directly with the naked eye when retiring from the vehicle.Never retreat while looking at the screen.
・
There is a limit in the range that the back monitor camera is projected, and it is not reflected near both corners of the bumper or under the bumper.
・
Since the back monitor camera uses a special lens, the distance interval on the screen is different from the actual.
・
The screen may be difficult to see when the light of the sun or headlamps hits the lens directly, when the temperature near the lens is high or low (such as night) or when the temperature near the lens is high or low.。
・
If the glass on the front of the lens is dirty, a clear image will not be obtained.Remove if water drops, snow, mud, etc. adhere.
・
