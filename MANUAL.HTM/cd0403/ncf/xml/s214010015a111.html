Exhaust pipe

●
A stainless steel exhaust pipe with excellent heat resistance and corrosion resistance has been adopted.
●
A ball joint mechanism was provided on the combination of exhaust manifold and front pipe to reduce vibration and ensure reliability.
●
By using the pipe part between the catalyst converter as a double tube structure, the catalyst was developed early to improve the emission performance.

Ball joint mechanism


・
The ball joint mechanism is composed of flange with a spherical surface and bolts and springs that support the gasket seal.It can be more compact than flexible pipes and can be mounted at the optimal position for vibration.
・
Vibration and noise are reduced by absorbing the vibration from the engine and the resonance vibration of the exhaust pipe in the ball joint (flange and gasket spherical surface).
