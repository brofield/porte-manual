Power window initialization operation


・
In this system, pulse sensors are electrically read by the motor rotation of the door glass, and based on this information, various functions are controlled.
・
If the battery is re -connected by inspection or maintenance ( + B power supply for the first time), the connector of each power window switch is removed, so the system remembers the current door glass location information is lost, so initialization (initialization ().It is necessary to perform the initialization) operation and "learn" the door glass position.
・
If the system has not been able to "learn" the current door glass position, please note that some functions, such as sandwiching, will not work.

Precautions when the system has not been able to "learn" the door glass position

・
The sandwich prevention mechanism does not work.
・
One -touch auto operation does not work.
・
Remote control operation of each door glass cannot be performed from the driver's master switch.

Manual up & down function


・
With the ignition switch ON, the corresponding door glass is up or down while operating the door switch on the upside (pull) or down side (pressing).
・
By operating the switches for each door of the MPX master switch, you can remotely control the manual operation of each door glass from the driver's seat.In addition, if you release your hand from the switch, the door glass will stop at that position.

Auto -up & down


・
By operating the door switch on the upside (pull) or down (press) on the ignition switch on, the corresponding door glass automatically performs auto -up or auto -down operation to the full -closed position.
・
By operating each door switch on the MPX master switch, you can remote the auto operation of each door glass from the driver's seat.
・
In each of the auto operation, the switch is interrupted by operating the switch on the opposite side of the operation direction and the opposite side.

Power window prevention mechanism


・
If the door glass inserts a foreign substance while the manual up or auto -up is operating, the door glass automatically operates about 50 mm (or about 1 second).In this case, if the door glass is not reached about 200 mm, it will be downed until the opening amount is about 200 mm (or for about 5 seconds).

Putting prevention mechanism cancellation operation

In the unlikely event that the door glass cannot be closed due to malfunction of the sandwiching mechanism, it can be canceled by holding the relevant door switch automatic up for about 10 seconds or more to cancel the operation of the sandwiched prevention mechanism.


Power window key OFF operation

Fail safe regarding the detection of door glass location information


・
If an abnormality is detected in a hole IC that detects the position, speed, and direction of the door glass, or the power window switch is "learned" as a full closing of the door glass, and an error exceeding the specified amount of the actual door glass position.If it occurs, it will be transferred to the Fail mode by self -diagnosis.
・
When the transition to the fail mode due to self -diagnosis, the position information of the door glass is the same as in the state where it has not been "learned", and for about 45 seconds after the switch operation or the ignition. Until OFF, blink the knob lighting of each door switch.
