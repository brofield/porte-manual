Collision safety body (GOA)

Collision safety body (GOA)

●
The crushable body and the rear part and the high -rigidity of the crushable and high -rigid cabins have effectively absorbed and distributed the energy at the time of collision, and a collision safety body (GOA) that minimizes the deformation of the cabin.
●
Based on a collision simulation by computer and many actual vehicle tests, a rational body that effectively absorbs and disperses the energy at the time of collision, not only in front and side collisions, but also on the offset on front of the vehicle.It was a structure.

Interior energy absorption structure


・
A large front bumper reinforcement is adopted at the front end of the front side member, and the energy at the time of collision is made efficiently distributed to the left and right front side members.
・
The front side member has made the whole straight shape and sets crash bed at the front end to efficiently absorb the energy at the time of collision.In addition, the rear part is set to a front side member rein horsement, and the rigidity is ensured by configuring a double closing surface.
・
The setting of a large B -shaped cross section with high energy absorption efficiency, and the buckling control of the front side member (adopting crash bead) reduced the amount of stroke and reduced the barrier intrusion.As a result, the structure is not easily broken in large parts during low -speed collisions.


Side portion energy absorption structure


・
Around the door opening, optimally placed a high -strength skeleton member, such as rein horsements, increases strength, and sets various reinhoses in each pillar section to enlarge the cross -sectional area.By doing so, it is a reasonable body structure that effectively absorbs and disperses the energy in the event of a side collision while ensuring the strength of the cabin skeleton.

・
The door has a structure in which the door side impact protection beam is placed to efficiently absorb and disperses the energy at the time of collision.In addition, the door strength is ensured by optimally placing rein horsements inside the belt line and the door panel.


Brake pedal retreat reduction structure

Brake pedal retreat reduction structure

●
By moving the brake pedal downward during the front collision, the brake pedal retreat reduction structure, which minimizes the retreat of the brake pedal, has been adopted to relieve the impact on the lower limbs of the driver.
●
When the brake booster part is pushed in due to a recession of the engine during a collision, the clip (bolt) that fixes the brake pedal support and the instrument panel rein Housing has left the brake pedal support, and the brake pedal support is the guide as it is.Move along the plate to the back and down.As a result, the brake pedal step surface is moved to the forward and lower.

Front seat

Front seat

●
A manual 6 -way sheet with a pump -type lifter mechanism was adopted for the driver's seat, and a manual 4 -way sheet with a seat back table mechanism was adopted in the passenger seat.
●
Springs are used for seat cushions in the driver's seat to ensure excellent ride comfort.In addition, both the driver and the passenger seat are made of the seat back, and the structure is a structure that supports the upper body on a wide range.
●
A long -slide seat mechanism (750mm slide) was adopted for the passenger seat.In addition, a long -slide seat power supply mechanism was adopted under the passenger seat so that the wire harness under the seat was not exposed during slide.

Manual lifter mechanism


・
When raising the sheet, the pinoon gear rotates in the same direction by operating the pump type lifter knob in the arrow C direction.As a result, the lifter gear rotates in the arrow E direction, centered on the fulcrum Q.
・
The connection link is attached to the lifter gear, and when the lifter gear rotates in the arrow E direction, the consolidated link moves in the F direction, and the Velcank is rotated in the arrow G direction around the fulcrum R.In conjunction with the Velcank rot rotating in the arrow G direction and lifting the rear end of the seat, the front lifter link rotates toward the fulcrum S around the fulcrum S, and the entire sheet lift up.The brake mechanism is incorporated into the pump -type lifter knob and the pinoon gear linked, and the pinion gear does not reversal against the load from the seat cushion.
・
To lower the sheet, operate the pump type lifter knob in the opposite direction to the arrow C direction.The lifter adjustment amount is 45mm.


Long -slide seat for passenger seats


・
If the passenger seat slide amount is small, the harness to supply to the sheet is always in the space under the seat, and the harness will not be exposed outside.
・
Even if the passenger seat slide amount is large, a long -slide seat power supply structure has been adopted so that the harness does not expose from below the seat during the slide.
・
In the power supply case fixed under the carpet, the wire harness is posted in a U -shaped U -shaped.One wire harness terminal is fixed to the power supply case, and one is connected in the order of the case -by -side protector and the fixed protector.
・
Sliding the seat and the wire harness works along the rails with a movable protector and a fixed protector.At this time, the two protectors are connected by strings, and the wire harness is not directly loaded during slide.
・
In this way, the structure is not exposed outside the seat in a long -slide sheet.
