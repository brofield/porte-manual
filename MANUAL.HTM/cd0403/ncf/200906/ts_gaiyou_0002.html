Anti -Lift Geometry (Eta Beam Suspension)


・
The floating (lift) of the rear of the vehicle generated at the time of braking is caused by the decrease in the rear wheel load from the movement of the center of gravity of the vehicle body caused by the inertia.
・
The braking force on the road surface is supported by the geometric intersection (or) of the suspension.At this time, the braking power (BF) generates the force of the intersection direction (BF1) and the force that acts in the ground surface (BF2).
・
The force that works in the ground surface direction can change the size and direction of the force by changing the height of the intersection (or), and set it higher than the road surface to the opposite direction to the loading (W) direction.It acts on and suppresses the amount of lift at the rear of the vehicle.
