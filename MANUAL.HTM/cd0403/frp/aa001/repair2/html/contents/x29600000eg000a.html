Oil pump ASSY overhaul (detachment / decomposition)

printing

List of preparation



1. Remove the oil pump relief valve

  1. Remove the plug and remove the spring and relief valve.



2. Oil pump relief valve inspection



  1. Apply the engine oil to the relief valve and make sure that when inserted into the valve mounting hole of the oil pump body, it falls smoothly with its own weight.



3. Oil pump ASSY inspection

  1. Remove the oil pump cover




    1. Remove two bolts and three screws and remove the oil pump cover.

  2. Oil pump gear set removed

    ■ Attention ■
    Do not change the combination and assembly direction.

  3. Oil pump gear set inspection

    1. Apply the engine oil to the driven rotor and the dried rotor, assemble it in an oil pump bondy, and then rotate smoothly.

  4. Chip clearance inspection




    1. The gap between the driven rotor and the dried shift rotor is measured using a chicknes gauge.

      Reference value:
      0.06-0.18mm
      limit:
      0.28mm

  5. Body clearance inspection




    1. The gap between the driven rotor and the oil pump body is measured using a chickness gauge.

      Reference value:
      0.250-0.325mm
      limit:
      0.425mm

  6. Side clearance inspection




    1. The gap between each rotor and the oil pump cover is measured using a direct regulation and a chickness gauge.

      Reference value:
      0.03-0.09mm
      limit:
      0.15mm

  7. Installation of oil pump gear set




    1. Apply the engine oil to the driven rotor and the driver to assemble the mark to the cover side.

  8. Installation of oil pump cover




    1. Attach the oil pump cover with two bolts and three screws.

      Reference value:
      T = 8.8n · m {90kgf · cm} (bolt)
      T = 10n · m {105kgf · cm} (Screw)



4. Installation of oil pump relief valve

  1. Apply engine oil to the relief valve and assemble it into an oil pump bondy with the spring.

  2. Attach the oil pump relief balburplug.

    Reference value:
    T = 24n · m {245kgf} cm}
