20. Valve seat inspection

  1. Apply Komyo -dan lightly to the valve face.

  2. Press the valve to the valve seat lightly.

    ■ Attention ■
    Do not rotate the valve.

  3. Inspect the hit position of the valve face and the valve seat.

    1. If there is no hit over all the valve face, replace the valve.

    2. If there is no hit over the valve sheet, fix the valve sheet.




    3. Confirm that the hit of the valve seat is 1.0-1.4 mm in the center in the center.



21. Intake valve seat modification

■ Attention ■
The end of the sharpening surface is gradually relaxed so as not to make steps on the cutting surface.




  1. If the hit surface of the seat is high, modify it using a 20 ° and 45 ° cutter head.




  2. If the hitting surface of the sheet is low, fix it using a 45 ° and 75 ° cutter head.

  3. Use the compound to perform the valve arrangement.

  4. After the matching, check the hit surface of the valve.



22. Exome Storbulve Seat Correction

■ Attention ■
The end of the sharpening surface is gradually relaxed so as not to make steps on the cutting surface.

  1. If the hit surface of the seat is high, modify it using a 20 ° and 45 ° cutter head.

  2. If the hitting surface of the sheet is low, fix it using a 45 ° and 75 ° cutter head.

  3. Use the compound to perform the valve arrangement.

  4. After the matching, check the hit surface of the valve.

28. Camshaft oil clearance inspection

  1. Clean the camshaft and bearing cap.

  2. Set the camshaft on the cylinder head.




  3. Set the press gauge in the axial direction of the camshaft journal.

  4. Attach the camshaft bearing cap.

    Reference value
    T = 23n · m {235kgf · cm} (No.1)
    T = 13n · m {130kgf · cm} (No.2)

    ■ Attention ■
    Do not rotate the camshaft during measurement.

  5. Take off the bearing cap.




  6. Measure the widest part of the press gauge.

    Reference value
    0.035-0. 072

    limit
    0.008 m

    1. If the limit is above the limit, replace the cylinder head.

    ■ Attention ■
    After measurement, remove the press gauge completely.
