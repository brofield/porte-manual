Regular inspection work guidelines 1

Replacement (3/7)

(4) <Inspection method Symbol mark>

  1. Please refer to the repair document, as the terms of the inspection work and the reference value of the item to be determined by measuring and determining.
  2. The inspection time is listed on each page.The items with "◆" are indicated that inspection can be omitted according to the mileage from the previous inspection (distance items).
  3. Explanation of terms
play The operation parts of each mechanism, such as the brake pedal, have a clearance to make the functions successful, and this clearance is called "play".
Gag The sliding part and the like have a large clearance due to wear, and the one that exceeds the reference value is called "gata".
Dedicated It refers to the degree of weakness, such as a spring or rubber.
