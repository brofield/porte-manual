Regular inspection work guidelines

Introduction

The current inspection and maintenance mechanism was stipulated at the time of the enforcement of the Road Transport Vehicle Law in 1958, but the durability due to the progress of automotive technology in recent years, improvement of reliability, and decreased annual mileage due to changes in use.Against this background, this time, the revision of the inspection and maintenance system, such as the abolition of one -month inspection of business passenger cars and reducing regular inspection items.

However, although the advances of technology have improved durability and reliability, the car consists of many parts, and as it is a machine, it is avoided to wear or deteriorate each part in the process of use.No.The wear and deterioration of each part can lead to a deterioration of performance, and keeping it as it is affecting safety and pollution prevention, and also causes the life of the car to shorten the life of the car, so the car is safe and comfortable.There is still a need for regular inspection and maintenance to go.

This book explains the work guidelines, judgment methods, and work procedures for regular inspection and maintenance of Toyota vehicles based on the new automobile inspection standards that have been enforced from May 1, 2000, based on this revision of the vehicle law.Please use it as a material for conducting accurate and quick work of inspection and maintenance.

Please note that this book may change the work guidelines, judgment method, etc. due to subsequent changes in vehicles.
