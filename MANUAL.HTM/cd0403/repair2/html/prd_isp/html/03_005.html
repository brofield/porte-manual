Regular inspection work guidelines 2

Replacement (1/2)
1. Private riding, etc.

(1) "● ◆" indicates the time specified by law, and "◆" shows the distance item.

The 12 -month distance item is 5,000 km or less from the previous regular inspection date of the car or the previous periodic inspection, and the distance item for 24 months is the travel distance of the car registration date or the previous regular inspection.In the case of the following (the first time is 15,000 km or less), the inspection can be omitted only once.However, it cannot be omitted twice in a row.

Daily inspections are performed at an appropriate time judged from the mileage, operation state, etc.

(2) ○ indicates the inspection time specified by Toyota.

(3) △ indicates the timing of inspection during severe conditions specified by Toyota (how to use it harsh).(Excluding cabarier)

(4) "Replacement of the entire kilogram" and "year -by -year exchange" indicate the time specified by Toyota, and replace the ones listed as soon as possible.

[] Indicates the first exchange time.

"" Indicates the time of replacement during severe conditions.

(5) In the 24th month after registration, inspection items will be performed every 12 months, and for the 36th month and the 24th month, the inspection items will be performed every 24 months.

(6) One month for a new car will be performed one month after registration or at 1,000 km driving, either early.

The 6 -month inspection of the new car will be conducted 6 months after registration.
