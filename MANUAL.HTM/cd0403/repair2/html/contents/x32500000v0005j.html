Control system CAN communication system CAN bus main line disconnection inspection (DLC3 branch line/main line/terminal resistance)

printing

Function explanation


  1. If the resistance value between 6 (Canh) ← → 14 (CANL) of DLC3 is 67Ω or more, a disconnection of the CAN bus main line and the DLC3 branch line may be considered.


Symptoms
Suspicated part
The resistance between 6 (Canh) 14 (Canl) of DLC3 is 67Ω or more
  1. (1), (1 '), (2), (2') Disconnected or connector defective (main line disconnection)
  2. Brake actuator ASSY (VSC ECU) Bad
  3. Steering sensor (steering angle sensor) bad
  4. (8), (8 ') Disconnected or connector defective (DLC3 branch line disconnection)
  5. Wiring harness connector (J/C) Defective
