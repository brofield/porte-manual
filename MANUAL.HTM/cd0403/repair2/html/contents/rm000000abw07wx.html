Subject analysis from the ECU control system method of troubleshooting

printing


  1. At the time of troubleshooting, there is no prejudice in order to accurately confirm the defective phenomenon and make accurate judgments.In order to confirm the defect phenomenon, it is very important to ask customers about the situation when a problem occurs.
  2. The following five items are important in performing defect analysis.Sometimes it is useful, such as problems that seem to have occurred in the past, and repair history, so it is necessary to collect as many as possible and accurately grasp the relationship with the defective phenomenon.The interview table is described in the diagnosis section of each system.

  1. What is-vehicle model, system name
  2. When-Date, frequency
  3. Where-course status
  4. Status-Driving status, weather

  5. How did it happen-defective phenomenon
