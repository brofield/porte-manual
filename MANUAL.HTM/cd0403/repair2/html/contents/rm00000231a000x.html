    1. Strict ban on manual emergency calls with help nets

    1. If there is no urgency, do not press the emergency call button.At the time of inspection, it is performed by "manual maintenance inspection".

      ■ Attention ■
      1. If there is no urgency such as an accident, if you connect to the center due to an emergency call, an unnecessary burden will be applied to the center, and other emergency calls may be delayed.
      2. If an emergency vehicle is dispatched due to a misinformation, the burden may be charged or punished by related laws and regulations.
      3. If you make a mistake, you will be charged unnecessary telephone usage fees.

    2. If you make a mistake


      1. If you report, you will be told that it is a misinformation by calling with the center.

      2. The report can be terminated only by the center side, and the report from the vehicle cannot be interrupted or terminated.

      3. If the "misinformation" is not transmitted, the fire department may be reported to the fire and the police may be dispatched by the agreement with the center.

          4. About the helpnet service automatic maintenance inspection

          1. The emergency call system always performs communication tests with the center and automatic inspection of the system so that it can be functioned correctly.This operation for automatic maintenance and inspection is not required, but is performed in the following manner.

            □ Participation □
            1. Automatic maintenance and inspection are performed for each period specified by Nippon Emergency Call Japan Corporation.
            2. Automatic maintenance and inspection will end in 1-2 minutes after the start.
            3. During the automatic maintenance inspection, calls and communications cannot be performed by hands-free G-BOOK.In this case, wait a few minutes and then re -operate.
            4. If communication is interrupted for some reason after the start of automatic maintenance inspection, automatic maintenance inspection will start again at the next IG switch ACC.
            5. Since Bluetooth mobile phones are used for communication, all data communication telephone usage fees performed by equipment inspection by automatic maintenance and inspection are borne by the customer.
