Function explanation


  1. Each solenoid adjusts the pressure acting on each wheel cylinder and controls the vehicle.
  2. Solenoids are not turned on during the output of abnormal diagnostic cords, so the solenoid is forced off by the fail -safe function.
  3. Solenoid relay and solenoids are built into the brake actuator.
