DTC F/D Water temperature sensor circuit

printing

List of preparation


Function explanation


  1. The thermista assembly (water temperature sensor) is attached to Huta core to detect the temperature of the engine cooling water and control the "auto" of the heater and air conditioner.The resistance value of the thermista ASSY (water temperature sensor) changes due to changes in the cooling water temperature, the lower the cooling water temperature, the greater the resistance, and the higher the cooling water temperature, the lower the resistance value.。The Center Cluster Module Switch outputs a voltage (5V) to the thermistor ASSY (water temperature sensor) to read changes in voltage due to the change in the resistance value of the thermistor ASSY (water temperature sensor).
