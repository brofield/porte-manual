    1. CAN bus diagnosis (CAN bus connection ECU confirmation)

      □ Participation □
      SST (Tascan) can display ECUs and sensors that are successfully connected to CAN communication.

      1. Select the 'CAN Bus Connection ECU Confirmation' on the CAN bus diagnosis screen.

        SST
        09991-70200




      2. The screen is displayed, and an ECU and sensor currently connected to the CAN communication is displayed.
