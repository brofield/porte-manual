Side Turn Signal Lamp ASSY LH removal

printing

List of preparation



1. Outari View Mirror LH removed



  1. Push down the upper part of the mirror and tilt the mirror surface.

  2. Put the protective tape on the position shown in the figure.

  3. Use the roof molding rimover to remove the four claws.



2. Outmiller Cover LH removed



  1. Push the claw A, B · C · D from the back side of the Outmiller Cover LH.

  2. The floating place triggers and removes eight claws.




  3. The Outmiller Cover LH is removed as shown in the figure so that the Outmiller Cover LH does not hit the side turn signal lamp ASSY LH.



3. Remove the side -turn signal lamp ASSY LH



  1. With two screws, separate the side turn signal lamp ASSY LH.

  2. Cut the connector and remove the side turn signal lamp ASSY LH.
