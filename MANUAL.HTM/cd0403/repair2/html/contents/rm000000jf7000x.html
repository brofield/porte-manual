Shift lock equipment function inspection with key -in -torch

printing


1. Shift lock operation inspection

  1. Make the shift lever P range.

  2. Make the ignition switch Lock.

  3. After checking that the shift lever is operated, it is not possible to shift other than the P range, then check that the ionition switch can be shifted other than the P range when the brake pedal is stepped on.



2. Key Into Rock operation inspection

  1. Make the ignition switch ACC.

  2. Step on the brake pedal and shift the shift lever other than the P range.

  3. Inspect that the ignition key cannot be turned to the Lock position.



  4. The shift lever is shifted to the P range, the ignition key is turned to the Lock position, and the ignition keys are removed.
