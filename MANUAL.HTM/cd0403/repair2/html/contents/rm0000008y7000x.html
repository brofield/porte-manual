Circuit description


The crank Posysillon Sensho System is composed of the No. 1 crank angle sensation and pickup coil.The No. 1 crank sensation has 34 convex portions with two teeth for the detection of two deaths, and is attached to the crankshaft.The NE signal sensor outputs 34 signals per turn for one engine.The engine controlled compilation detects a crankshaft angle and engine speed by NE signal, based on the G2 signal.
