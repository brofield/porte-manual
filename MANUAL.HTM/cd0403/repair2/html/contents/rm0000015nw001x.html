DTC No.
DTC detection conditions
DTC output -related items
C1243/43
Deselation sensor (G sensor) does not change between 30 km/h and 0 km/h, 16 times or more
  1. Deselation sensor (G sensor) internal sticking
  2. Wire harness disconnection, short
  3. Failure of connectors, deformation of terminals, corrosion
  4. Brake actuator ASSY
C1244/44
Deselation sensor (G sensor) disconnected or short state continues for 1 second or more
  1. Wire harness disconnection or short
  2. Failure of connectors, deformation of terminals, corrosion
  3. Deceleration sensor (G sensor)
  4. Brake actuator ASSY
C1245/45
With a car speed of 30 km/h or more, the difference between the front and rear G calculated from the G sensor value and the front and rear G, which is calculated from the vehicle speed, continues for 60 seconds or more.
  1. Deselation sensor (G sensor) Installation status
  2. Wire harness short
  3. Deceleration sensor (G sensor)
  4. Brake actuator ASSY
