Circuit explanation


The G-Book system communicates between devices by AVC-Lan Plus.In the case of abnormalities such as shorts of communication lines,+B shorts and GND shorts in the AVC-Lan Plus circuit, the G-Book system will not work properly due to the interruption of communication.
