Remove the engine mounting insulator RR (1NZ-FE (4WD))

printing

List of preparation



1. Exhaust pipe ASSY CTR removes



  1. Remove the bolt and remove the exhaust pipe clamp.

  2. Remove the two exhaust pipe support and remove the exhaust center pipe ASSY.

  3. Remove the exhaust pipe gasket.



2. Proposhaft ASSY



  1. Mark the propeller shaft asser and propelline thimidiite shaft ASSY.

  2. Remove four bolts, washer and nuts each, and separate the propeller shaft ASSY from the interimide shaft ASSY.




  3. After removing the propeller shaft ASSY, insert the SST into the extension housing.

    SST
    09325-20010

    ■ Attention ■
    Do not damage the oil seal.



3. Remove the engine mounting insulator RR



  1. Support the engine with a mission jack via a piece of wood.




  2. Remove the two bolts and shift the power steering link asser.




  3. Remove two nuts and two bolts and remove the engine mounting insulator RR.
