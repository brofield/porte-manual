  1. Remove the clip and ventilation hose from the cylinder head cover.Cut 2.

7. Camshaft No.2



■ Attention ■
When the chain is turned off and the camshaft is rotated, the crankshaft is turned left so that the valve is fully opened and the piston does not interfere with the piston, and the crankshaft Dampa cut is in the figure in the figure.Do from.




  1. No.1 Cylinder compressed death point set.

    1. Rotate the crankshaft damper correctly and match the timing mark (notch) to the chain cover 0 °.

    2. Confirm that there is a timing mark of the camshaft timing gear at the position of the figure.

      □ Participation □
      If the mark does not match, rotate the crankshaft and match it again.




  2. Apply paint mark to each plate of the chain corresponding to the timing mark of the camshaft timing gear.




  3. Use the socket hexagon wrench 8 to remove the chien cover service hole scriey plug.




  4. Use a flathead screwdriver to hold the tensioner stopplate from the chain tensioner service hall.

    □ Participation □
    By raising the stopper plate of the chain tensioner, the plunger lock is unlocked.




  5. Use a monkey wrench from the state where the stopper plate of the chain tensioner is raised, the hexagonal part for the Camshaft No.2 is slightly turned to the right to make the chain tensioner plunger is pressed.

    □ Participation □
    By rotating the camshaft No.2 slightly, the plunger is in the back.




  6. Hold the monkey wrench and remove the flather from the plunger in the back.

    ■ Attention ■
    Do not move monkey wrench.

    □ Participation □
    By removing the flathead screwdriver, the stopper plate is exposed and locked.




  7. The stopper plate of the chain tensioner is poured down and insert a φ3mm rod or a hexagonal wrench into the hole of the stopper plate from the locked state.

    □ Participation □
    If the φ3mm stick or a hexagonal wrench is difficult to insert into the hole in the stopper plate, turn the camshaft No.2 slightly to the left and turn the right again to make it easier to insert.

  8. Fix it with tape so that the φ3mm stick or a hexagonal wrench does not fall off.




  9. The hexagon for service No.2 of the camshaft is held with a monkey wrench and loosened the flange bolt.




  10. In the order of the figure, remove the camshaft bearing No.1 and No.2.

    ■ Attention ■
    Loosen the bolt evenly while holding the camshaft No.2 horizontally.




  11. Remove the flange bolt with a little camshaft No.2 and remove the camshaft No.2 and camshaft timing gear.



8. Remove the camshaft



  1. In the order of the figure, remove the camshaft bearing kyatsup No.2.

    ■ Attention ■
    Loosen the bolt evenly while holding the camshaft horizontally.




  2. Hold the timing chain by hand and remove the camshaft.




  3. Hang the timing chain with a rope or wire.

    ■ Attention ■
    Be careful not to enter anything into the chain cover.



9. Camshaft timing gear ASSY

  1. Fix the camshaft to the vise and make sure that the camshaft timing gear ASSY does not turn.

    ■ Attention ■
    It is too tight with vise and does not hurt the camshaft.




  2. As shown in the figure, the four -journal oil hole is closed with a vinyl tape.

    □ Participation □
    Since the oil hole is located in the groove, two places are closed through rubber pieces.

  3. Open a hole in the vinyl tape, which is blocking two areas (advanced oil holes, late -angle oil holes) on the other side.




  4. Ol a hole in the vinyl tape (advanced angle oil hole, late -angle oil hole) 150kpa {1.5kgf/cm in two places)2Add the air pressure of about}.

    ■ Attention ■
    If the air pressure is applied, the oil may scatter, so it will be covered with a row.




  5. When the air pressure on the late -angle oil hole side is weakened from the above state, it is confirmed that the camshaft timing gear ASSY rotates in the progressive direction.

    □ Participation □
    The lockpin is released and rotates in the direction of progress.

  6. When the camshaft timing gear ASSY moves to the maximum angle position, the air pressure on the late -angle oil hole is removed, and then the air pressure on the advanced angle oil hole is eliminated.

    ■ Attention ■
    If the air pressure on the advanced angle oil hole side is removed first, the camshaft timing gear ASSY will suddenly move in the late angle direction, and there is a risk of damage such as lock pin, so the air pressure on the late angle oil hole is always.Pull out first.




  7. Remove the camshaft timing gear ASSY flange bolt.

    ■ Attention ■
    1. Never remove the other four bolts.
    2. When reusing the camshaft timing gear ASSY, unlock the lock pin and use it.
