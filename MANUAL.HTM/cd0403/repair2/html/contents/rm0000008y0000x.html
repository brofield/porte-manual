Circuit explanation


While the engine is cranking, current flows from the ST terminal of the ignition switch to the starter relay coil, and the current flows from the ST terminal to the STA terminal (STA signal) of the engine controlled compilation.When the STA signal and the NE signal are entered into the engine control composer, the current switch is turned on by the current transistor and the coil of the E.F.I. Circuit opening relay flows, and the fuel pump is turned on and operates.do.While the NE signal is entered (the engine is rotating), the transistation switch inside the engine control componizuta continues to turn on (E.F.I. Circuit opening relay ON), so the fuel pump will continue to work during that time.
