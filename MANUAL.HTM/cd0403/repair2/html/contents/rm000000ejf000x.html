List of preparation



1. Waterfircap SUB-ASSY



■ Attention ■
  1. If the waterfira cap is dirty, be sure to wash off dirt with water.
  2. Before using a radiator cap tester, wet the relief valve and pressure valve with cooling or water.
  3. When using a radiator cap tester, tilt 30 ° or more and check.

  1. Slowly pump the tester using a radiator cap tester, and check if air comes out of the vacuum valve.

    standard:
    Pumping speed once more than 3 seconds

    ■ Attention ■
    Pumping at a certain speed.

    □ Participation □
    If the air does not come out of the vacuum valve, replace the waterfira cap.

  2. Pump the tester to measure the open pressure of the relief valve.

    standard:
    Pumping speed 1 second or more

    ■ Attention ■
    This pumping speed is only for the first pumping.(To close the vacuum valve) The pumping speed may be slow.

    Reference value:
    93-122kpa {0.95-1.25kgf/cm2}

    limit:
    78kpa {0.8kgf/cm2}

    □ Participation □
    1. Use the maximum measured value of the tester as an open pressure.
    2. If the open pressure is lower than the lowest value, replace the waterfira cap.
