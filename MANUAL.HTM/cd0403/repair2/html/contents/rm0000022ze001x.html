1. Head lamp leveling computer ASSY initialization procedure

  1. Vehicle status confirmation

    1. Make the vehicle in the following state.

      1. Lower luggage that can be loaded and unloaded outside the car (equipped with spare tires and in -vehicle tools).
      2. The crew is zero.
      3. The headlamp turns off.
      4. There is no change in the vehicle height at the stop (vehicle posture is stable).
