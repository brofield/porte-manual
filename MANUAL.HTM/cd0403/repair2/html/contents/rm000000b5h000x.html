List of preparation



1. Door control transmitter module set removed




  1. Use a precision flash driver to remove the cover.

    ■ Attention ■
    Put tape etc. on the tip to prevent damage.




  2. Remove the module set from the transmitter housing case



2. Transmitter battery removes



  1. Use coins to remove the cover.




  2. Use a precision flash driver to remove the exit [lithium battery (CR2016)].

    ■ Attention ■
    1. Do not press the terminal part with your finger.
    2. The terminal part is deformed when the combination of the (lithium battery (CR2016)] is forcibly removed.
