List of preparation



1. Backdoor glass cleaning

  1. Remove the clip and spacer.(When the glass is reused)

  2. Use a scraper to cut off the remaining adhesive and onside molding.(When the glass is reused)

    ■ Attention ■
    Be sure to leave a little adhesive.

  3. Clean the glass outer periphery with white gasoline.

    ■ Attention ■
    Clean the new glass.



2.

  1. Apply a dedicated primer to the outside molding mounting part.

    ■ Attention ■
    1. Do not paint thickly.
    2. Attach it to about 3 minutes after drying.



  2. Attach a cross -side molding to the position in the figure.





3. Back door glass spacer installation

  1. Apply a dedicated primer to the spacer mounting part.

    ■ Attention ■
    1. Do not paint thickly.
    2. Install after drying (about 3 minutes).




  2. Attach a backdoor glass spacer to the position of the figure.

    □ Participation □
    1. Attach to the position where the spacer is.
    2. Attach inside from the primer application range.



4. Back door glass installation

  1. Body mounting face cleaning




    1. If the adhesive on the vehicle side is remarkably uneven, smooth with a knife.

      ■ Attention ■
      Be sure to leave a little adhesive.

  2. Work before glass installation

    1. Put the suction rubber on the glass.

    2. The glass is placed on the body, and the width of the glass is uniform (the gap between the body and the glass end surface is uniform).

    3. Paste the gum tape on the glass and the body panel, and mark with a pen.(New article)

      □ Participation □
      When reusing glass, check and modify the matching position.

    4. Take off the glass.

    5. Apply a dedicated primer to the part where the body surface on the vehicle is exposed (no adhesive remains).

      ■ Attention ■
      1. Do not paint thickly.
      2. Do not apply to the adhesive.
      3. Panel seams and spot welding parts are applied with special attention.
      4. Attach the glass after drying (about 3 minutes).

    6. Paste the butyl tape to the damaged clip position.(In case of clip damage due to glass reusing)

    7. Set the adhesive on the sealant gun.



    8. Apply the adhesive into a bead as shown in the figure.



      □ Participation □
      Cut the nozzle tip as shown in the figure.

  3. Glass installation

    1. Use suction rubber to match the matching mark and attach the glass.

      ■ Attention ■
      1. Attach glass after the primer on the body side dries (about 3 minutes).
      2. Make sure that the spacer is in line with the room.
      3. Check the gap between the body and the glass.

    2. Press the front of the glass lightly to completely adhere.

      □ Participation □
      Pressing power: 98n {10kgf} or more

    3. Fix the adhesive protruding and deficiency with a spatula.

    4. Hold the glass with a protective tape until the adhesive is cured.

    5. Extra adhesive is removed before hardening.

      □ Participation □
      For the minimum drying time and driving time after bonding, follow the instructions or instruction manuals of each adhesive manufacturer.



5. Back window glass channel RH installation

  1. Apply the primer to the body mounting surface.

  2. Combine the two clips and attach the back window glass channel RH.



6. Back window glass channel LH ​​installation

  1. The RH side is performed in the same procedure as LH.



7. Water leak inspection and repair

  1. After adhesion, apply water from the outside of the vehicle to confirm that there is no leak in the room.

  2. In the event of a leak, the three cement black is applied after drying.
