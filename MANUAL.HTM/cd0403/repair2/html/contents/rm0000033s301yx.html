Circuit description


Kunen Hena generates voltage (*) according to the actual air -fuel ratio.This sensor voltage is sent to the engine control computer as a feedback so that the engine control computer can control the air -fuel ratio.Based on this signal, the engine control computer determines the deviation from the aorial aircraft fuel ratio and controls the fuel injection time.

Kunen Hena is a flat type, and is integrated with a heater that warms a solid electrolytes (zirconia material).This heater is controlled by the engine control computer.When the amount of inhaled air is low (the exhaust gas temperature is low), the current flows to the heater to warm the sensor to make the correct oxygen -containing detection easier.In addition, the sensor and heater part are narrower than the conventional one.The heat generated by the heater is transmitted to solid electrolytes via alumina, which promotes sensor operation.

A trio -catalytic converter is used to enhance the high purification rate of carbon monoxide (CO), hydrocarbon (HC), and nitrogen oxides (HC) in exhaust gas.Effective use of the converter is accurately controlled so that the air -fuel ratio always approaches the theoretical level as much as possible.

*: The numbers change inside the engine control computer.Since Kunen Hena is a current output component, the current is converted to a voltage inside the engine control computer.A certain voltage is displayed when measured with the Kuunen Hena or engine control computer connector.




Diag detection conditions
  1. In the air -fuel ratio feedback, when the oxygen sensor is rich in output, the engine control computer sets the Diag Cord of P2195/21 and turns on the check engine warnin glandamp.
    Also, in the air -fuel ratio feedback, when the oxygen sensor has lens output, the engine control computer sets the P2196/21 diagcode and turns on the check engine warnin group.。
  2. When the fuel cut current is about 3.6 mA or more (P2195/21) or about 1.0 mA (P2196/21), the engine control computer sets a diagcode and lights the check engine warnin group.Let me do it.

Confirmation driving
By performing a confirmation, you can confirm the reproduction of the diag code.




ST
09991-70201

  1. Connect SST (Tascan) to DLC3.
  2. Ig ON.
  3. Follow the screen display of SST (Tascan) to delete the diag code.(The point isreference)
  4. Wait for 30 seconds with Ig off.
  5. Start the engine, turn off all accessories switches, and warm up until the engine cooled water temperature is stabilized.(Engine cooling water temperature is 75 ° C or higher) (a)
  6. Drive for more than 10 minutes for 60 to 100 km/h.(B)
  7. Make the shift lever two positions.(C)
  8. In order to drive the vehicle at a speed suitable for fuel cut, the speed is 60 km/h or more with the accelerator pedal stepped on for 10 seconds or more.(D)
  9. Release the accelerator pedal and carry out fuel cuts for 4 seconds or more.(E)

    □ Participation □
    When the vehicle speed is 35 km/h or more, the fuel cut is executed when the accelerator pedal is released.(The fuel cut stops with an engine speed of about 1000 rpm or less.)

  10. Decelerate the vehicle until 10 km/h.(F)
  11. Repeat the procedure from (c) to (f) three or more times.
  12. According to the screen display of SST (Tascan), check the diagcode judgment result.(The point isreference)

    ■ Warning ■
    When performing this driving test, be sure to follow the Road Traffic Law, such as the speed limit, and implement it.

    □ Participation □
    If the diagcode judgment result is being judged or not possible, after running the vehicle again for more than 10 minutes at 75 to 100 km/h, then repeated (C) to (f) 3 or more times, then the diagcode judgment.Check the result.

□ Participation □
  1. If the output voltage of the Kunenhe -sensa remains about 3.3 V in any state, including the above state, there is a possibility that the Kuunen Hena has a disconnected circuit.(This occurs even when Kuunen Hena Harter is the opening route.)
