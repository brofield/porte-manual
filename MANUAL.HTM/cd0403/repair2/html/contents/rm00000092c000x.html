DTC P0710/38 oil temperature sensor system

printing

List of preparation


Function explanation


The oil temperature sensor is attached to the valve body, the oil temperature in the transmission hydraulic control circuit is detected, and a signal is input to the engine control compilation compilation.If an abnormality occurs in the oil temperature sensor, the 4 -speed and lock -up control will be discontinued as the fail -safe function.


DTC No.
DTC detection conditions
  1. Diagnostic condition
  2. Abnormal state
  3. Abnormal period
Inspection site
P0710/38
  1. none
  2. Short circuit of oil temperature sensor circuit or disconnection after 15 minutes after starting the engine
  3. 0.5 seconds or more
  1. Wire harness and connector (oil temperature sensor system)
  2. Transmitsu Shi -young Wire (oil temperature sensor)
  3. Engine control compilation
