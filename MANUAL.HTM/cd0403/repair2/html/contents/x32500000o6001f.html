Differency system (4WD) Notes

printing



    1. Before disassembly, wash the sand, mud, etc. attached to the outer rear dialable assembly, so that it does not enter the interior of the rear dial ASSY during disassembly and mounting.


      2. When disassembling the joints of light alloy components such as the rear dialy carrier cover, it is broken lightly with a plastic hammer with a driver or the like.


        3. The disassembled parts are always organized, tidy, and protect them from dust.


          4. Each part is thoroughly washed and dried before mounting, and then applied to Toyota genuine high poid gear oil SX.Aluminum, rubber and ring gear set bolts are not washed with alkaline drugs.In addition, rubber material parts such as O -rings and oil seals are not washed with washing oil (white gasoline, etc.).


            5. Apply the Toyota genuine high pooid gear oil SX to the sliding surface and the rotating portion.


              6. When fixing the parts to the vice, do not fix it directly to the vise, but always fix it through an aluminum plate.


                7. Be careful not to damage the case of oil leakage, as it may cause oil leakage.


                  8. Before applying the sealing agent, remove the old sealing agent attached to the sticker and wash the seal with white gasoline.


                    9. After attaching the seal part, leave the oil immediately and leave it for more than an hour or more.


                      10. Be careful not to damage the oil seal, O -ring, and gasket, as it may cause oil leakage.


                        11. Be careful not to damage the lip and outer periphery of the oil seal when pressing the oil seal.


                          12. When replacing the bearing, replace the outer lace and the inner race as a set.
