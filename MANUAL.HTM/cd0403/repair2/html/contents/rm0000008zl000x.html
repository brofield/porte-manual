List of preparation



1. Installation of oil pump seals

  1. Apply a small amount of MP grease No.2 to the lip of the new oil seal.

    ■ Attention ■
    Do not adhere to the lip part such as sandwiching and sand.




  2. Use the SST to even hit the oil seal to the end of the oil pump evenly.

    SST
    09223-22010

    ■ Attention ■
    1. Do not driven the oil seal diagonally.
    2. Wipe off the grease attached to the crankshaw Dampa.



2. Crankshaft Dampa SUB-ASSY installation

  1. Combine the straight pin hole of the crankshaft damp and the crankshaft straight pin and attach the crankshaft damper.




  2. Use SST to fix the crankshaft damper and tighten the bolt.

    SST
    09213-58013 (91111-50845)
    09330-00021
    Reference value
    T = 128 n ・ m {1305 kgf / cm}

    ■ Attention ■
    After installing the SST, the SST fixing bolt may contact the chin cover, so attach the SST while checking the mounting position.



3. Installation of engine mounting insulator SUB-ASSY RH

  1. Operate the jack and attach the engine mountain mounting in theleurer RH with five bolts and nuts.



4. Bane pump V belt (power steering belt) installation

  1. The V belt is temporarily attached to each pulley.

    ■ Attention ■
    1. There is no adhesion of liquids such as oil and foreign matter on the belt and each pulley.
    2. There is no slab of the belt on the belt.



5. Vane pump V belt (power steering belt) adjustment



  1. Adjust the tension of the V belt and tighten the bolt B for adjustment.

    Reference value

  2. Tighten the fixing bolt A.

    Reference value

6. Fan & Alternator V belt installation

  1. The V belt is temporarily attached to each pulley.

    ■ Attention ■
    1. There is no adhesion of liquids such as oil and foreign matter on the belt and each pulley.
    2. There is no slab of the belt on the belt.



7. Fan & Alternator V belt adjustment



  1. Use a hub nut wrench or bar to draw the alternator to the vehicle front side to adjust the tension of the Juan & Alternator V belt.

  2. Tighten the adjustment bolt A and then tighten the fixing bolt B.

    Reference value
