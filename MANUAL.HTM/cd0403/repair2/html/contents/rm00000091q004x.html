DTC P0710/38 oil temperature sensor system

printing

List of preparation


Function explanation


The oil temperature sensor is attached to the valve body, the oil temperature in the transmission hydraulic control circuit is detected, and a signal is entered in the engine control computer according to the oil temperature.If an abnormality occurs in the oil temperature sensor, the 4 -speed and lock -up control will be discontinued as the fail -safe function.


DTC No.
DTC detection conditions
1. Diagnosis conditions 2. Abnormal state 3. Abnormal state
Inspection site
P0710/38
  1. none
  2. Short circuit of oil temperature sensor circuit or disconnection after 15 minutes after starting the engine
  3. 0.5 seconds or more
  1. Wire harness and connector (oil temperature sensor system)
  2. Transmission wire (oil temperature sensor)
  3. Engine control computer
