Roof drip side finish molding LH installation

printing

List of preparation



1. Roof drip side finish molding clip No.1 installation

■ Attention ■
  1. When the clip is used, when it is out of use, attach it in the same procedure.
  2. When the clip is off, the body side and the double -sided tape remaining in the clip are not peeled off.
  3. The primer should use the one bundled in the diabond.(Although it is written as a primer, the primer is not dried because it is a two -liquid type adhesive.

□ Participation □
  1. Because it is a 2 -type adhesive, it can be applied to the adhesive surface after mixing the 2 solution in a separate container.
  2. Mixed ratio adhesive: Primer = 10: 1




  1. After applying the primer to the body side of the clip mounting surface and the clip mounting position with the attached dash, immediately apply the diabond into a bead with a diameter of 2-3 mm.

    ■ Attention ■
    Although it is written as a primer, the primer is not dried because it is a two -liquid type adhesive.



  2. After applying the diabond, apply the clip to the body seat immediately, rub it lightly to apply the adhesive and primer, then attach the clip to the position of the figure and press it firmly.



  3. The molding is done after more than 30 minutes after pressing.

    □ Participation □
    1. Initial hardening time 30 minutes
    2. Completely curing time 24 hours



2. Roof drip side finish molding LH installation

  1. Attach a roof drip side finish molding LH to the vehicle.
