Function explanation


  1. The ABS CUT relay is linked to the ON/OFF of the IG switch and supplies power.
  2. While ABS, TRC, VSC, and brake assists are operating, the skid control computer turns on the ABS MTR relay and activates the ABS pump motor.
  3. If the voltage to the motor relay (+BM) decreases from the DTC detection condition due to lack of output of the battery or alternator, this code may be remembered.
