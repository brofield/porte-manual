    1. Repair documents can be divided into three rows: [Diagnosis], [Diagnosis, replacement, disassembly, assembly, inspection / adjustment work], and [Completed Inspection].

    2. This book describes [Diagnosis] and second steps in the first step in [Detectable, replacement, decomposition, assembly, inspection, and adjustment work], and omitted the description of [Completion Inspection] in the third process.

    3. In this book, the description of the following element work is omitted, but it will be implemented in the actual work.


      1. Jack and lift work.

      2. Clean and clean the removal parts to be performed as needed.

      3. Visual inspection.

        1. The configuration diagram is posted for each section or title as necessary.



        2. The configuration diagram clearly specifies unusable parts, grease coating points, pre -coat bolts and tightening torque in the figure.



        3. Tightening torque, oil and oil coating place, and non -reused parts are described as the point of the procedure.
