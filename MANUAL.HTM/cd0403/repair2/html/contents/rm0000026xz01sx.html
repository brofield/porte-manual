Confirmation driving
By performing a confirmation, you can confirm the reproduction of the diag code.




SST
09991-70201

  1. Connect SST (Tascan) to DLC3.
  2. Ig ON.
  3. Follow the screen display of SST (Tascan) to delete the diag code.(The point isreference)
  4. Wait for 30 seconds with Ig off.
  5. Start the engine, turn off all accessories switches, and warm up until the engine cooled water temperature is stabilized.(Engine cooling water temperature is 75 ° C or higher) (a)
  6. Idling for more than 15 minutes.(B)

    □ Participation □
    Check [ECU Data Monitor] - [Purge VSV Driving Duty ratio] of SST (Tascan).When this value is 5 to 95%, the result of the diagcode judgment result is determined.

  7. According to the screen display of SST (Tascan), check the diagcode judgment result.

    ■ Warning ■
    When performing this driving test, be sure to follow the Road Traffic Law, such as the speed limit, and implement it.

    □ Participation □
    If the diagcode determination result is not determined or cannot be determined, run the vehicle for more than 10 minutes at 40 to 60 km/h (C) and check the diagcode judgment result.
