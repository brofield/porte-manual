          4. Notes on the back guide monitor system (car equipped with a back guide monitor system)

          1. If the television camera controller is removed by replacing the navigation computer, the "system is initialized" will be displayed, and a correction of the rudder neutral point will be required.

          2. When the battery terminal is removed, the "system is initializing" is displayed, and the correction of the rudder angle neutral is required.
