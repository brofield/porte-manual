Brake actuator ASSY (with VSC)

printing



  1. Brake actuator operation inspection

    1. Preparation before inspection


      1. Connect the SST (Tascan) and start the engine.

        SST
        09991-70200

      2. Operate according to the SST (Tascan) screen display and select an active test mode.

    2. Actuator motor operation inspection

      ■ Attention ■
      Do not drive the motor for more than 5 seconds to protect the motor.If you want to operate continuously, leave more than 20 seconds.


      1. Turn on the motor relay.

        standard
        There is a driving noise of the motor

      2. Turn off the motor relay.

      3. Hold the brake pedal for 15 seconds while stepping on with a certain force.

        standard
        The pedal does not enter

      4. Turn on the motor relay while stepping on the brake pedal.

        standard
        There is no pulsation on the pedal

      5. Turn off the motor relay and release the brake pedal.

    3. Solenoid operation inspection

      ■ Attention ■
      1. Do not drive motors and solenoids for more than 5 seconds to protect motors and solenoids.If you want to operate continuously, leave more than 20 seconds.
      2. Do not step on the brake pedal while operating alone only by the decompression valve.
      3. Do not turn on solenoids without instructions.


      1. Step the brake pedal and hold it as it is.

      2. Turn on SFRH and SFRR solenoids at the same time.

        standard
        The pedal does not enter

      3. Check the condition of the brake pedal when SFRH and SFRR solenoids are turned off.

        standard
        The pedal enters

      4. Turn on the motor relay.

        standard
        The pedal is returned

      5. Inspect the solenoids of other wheels in the same way.

        □ Participation □
        Front right (SFRH, SFRR), front left (SFLH, SFLR), rear right (SRRH, SRRR), rear left (SRLH, SRLR)
