        3. Data communication module inspection territory

        1. The data communication module uses radio waves (au CDMA2000 1X method).Radio and communication lines have some uncertain elements due to time and location factors.Therefore, the abnormalities related to the connection may be a temporary phenomenon due to radio waves, etc., so it is necessary to determine the frequency of occurrence and comparison with the CDMAONE mobile phone.

The G-Book terminal and the data communication module have a one-on-one registered G-Book service.Therefore, if any of them are replaced, the connection cannot be made unless re-registration to the G-Book support center.It is not possible to check the connection by temporarily connected the substitute.
