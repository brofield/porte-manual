Remove the oil pump seal

printing

List of preparation



1. Remove the front tire RH


2. The engine Anda cover RH is removed

  1. Remove two bolts and two screws.

  2. Remove the nut and remove the engine undercover RH.

    □ Participation □
    The nut is tightened with the LH side.



3. Fan & alternator V belt removed



  1. Loosen the adjustment bolt A and the fixing bolt B.

  2. Loosen the tension of the V belt and remove the V belt.



4. Remove the vane pump V belt (power steering belt)



  1. Loosen the fixing bolt A and bolt B for adjustment.

  2. Remove the V belt by loosening the tension of the V belt.



5. Enter the engine mounting insulator SUB-ASSY RH



  1. Supporting the engine with a jack via a piece of wood.




  2. Remove the 5 bolts and nuts and remove the engine mountain mountaine -in -wolator RH.



6. Remove crankshaft Dampa SUB-ASSY



  1. Operate the jack, lower the engine front side, and set the SST to set.

  2. Use SST to fix the crankshaft damper and remove the bolt.

    SST
    09213-58013 (91111-50845)
    09330-00021

    ■ Attention ■
    After installing the SST, the SST fixing bolt may contact the chin cover, so attach the SST while checking the mounting position.

  3. Take off the crankshaft damper.

    □ Participation □
    If the crankshaft damper does not come off by hand, use a plastic hammer to remove it.



7. Remove the oil pump seal



  1. Use a cutter knife to cut the lip of the oil seal.

  2. Use a flathead screwdriver with a protective tape to remove the oil seal.

    □ Participation □
    After removing the oil seal, make sure that the crankshaft part is not scratched.If there is a scratch, modify it with sandpaper ( # 400).
