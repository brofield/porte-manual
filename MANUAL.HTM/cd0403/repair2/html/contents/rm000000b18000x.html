Circuit explanation


Airbag Warnin Gramp is built into the combination sijon meter ASSY.

If the airbag system is normal, the Airbag Warnin Gramp turns on for about 6 seconds after turning on the IG switch and turns off automatically.If the airbag system has a problem, the airbag warnin gland will light or flash to inform the defect.

If the connector of the airbag sensor ASSY CTR is in a half -matched or half -kango detection pin, the warnin gland will light up.
