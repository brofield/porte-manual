  6. Sensor rotor abnormality (foreign substance attached, tooth loss)
  7. Speed ​​sensor circuit wiring resistance
  8. Brake actuator ASSY

The speed sensor detects wheel speed and sends a signal to a skid control computer.These signals are used for ABS control.

A coil and magnet are built in the tip of the speed sensor, and when the sensor rotor is rotated, a magnetic force is generated in proportion to the score.

The skid control computer detects the speed of each wheels by the frequency of this AC voltage.
