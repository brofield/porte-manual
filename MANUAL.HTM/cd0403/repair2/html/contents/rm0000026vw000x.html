Installation of rear axle ASSY (4WD)

printing

List of preparation



1. Rear axle shiaper bearing installation

  1. Use white gasoline to clean the bearing retainer OUT.

  2. Attach the bearing retainer gasket to the bearing retainer OUT.




  3. Bearing the claws in the bearing retenger girt.

  4. Attach the bearing retainer OUT to the axle shaft.

  5. Apply MP grease No.2 to the outer surface of the new bearing outer race.




  6. Use SST and press to press new bearings.

    SST
    09515-21010
    09608-32010



2. Attach the rear skid control rotor



  1. Use SST and press to press the new skid control rotor.

    SST
    09515-21010
    09608-32010

    ■ Attention ■
    Do not damage the skid control rotor.



3. Installation of the rear axles yaft inner tenalite

  1. Heat the new inaltainer to about 150 ° C.

    ■ Warning ■
    Because the inalitea is high, always wear gloves and work.

    ■ Attention ■
    In the retainer, the surface is slightly yellow when the surface is 150 ° C or more, so it does not heat any more.




  2. Use SST and press to attach the inaliteer in the direction of the figure.

    SST
    09515-21010
    09608-32010

    ■ Attention ■
    Attach the surface of the inalite to the bottom.



4. Lear Axle housing end gasket installation

  1. Use white gasoline to clean the backing plate and axle housing.




  2. Round the backing plate and attach the end gasket.

    ■ Attention ■
    1. Do not apply unreasonable power to the brake tube.
    2. Make the gasket cut off down.

  3. Set 4 bolts.



5. Lear Axle shaft oil seal installation

  1. Apply MP grease No.2 to the lip part of the new oil seal.




  2. Use SST and hammer to driven an oil seal.

    SST
    09517-12010



6. Installation of rear axle shaft



  1. Put the protrusion of the bearing retainer OUT downward, use SST, and insert the axle shaft into the axle housing.

    SST
    09520-00031

    ■ Attention ■
    1. Do not damage the oil seal.
    2. Do not damage the speed sensor rotor.




  2. With 4 nuts, attach the bearing retainer OUT to the backing plate.

    Reference value
    T = 66 N ・ M {670 kgf ・ CM}



7. Installation of Brake Uri Turn Spring


8. Parking brake shoe rat set LH installation



  1. Use SST to attach a strat set.

    SST
    09703-30010



9. Rear axle swing and axial rattling inspection

  1. Remove the rear tire

  2. Remove the rear brake drum

  3. Rear Axle Hub Hub Bearing Axial Ruts Inspection




    1. The dial gauge is set in the position of the rear axle hub, and the rattling direction of the rear axle hub bearing is inspected.

      limit
      0.05mm

      ■ Attention ■
      If it exceeds the limit, replace it with a new rear axle shaft bearing.

  4. Rear axle hub swing inspection




    1. Set the dial gauge in the position of the rear axle hub to check the swing of the rear axle hub.

      limit
      0.07mm

      ■ Attention ■
      If it exceeds the limit, replace it with a new rear axle shaft.

  5. Rear brake drum installation

  6. Rear tire installation

    Reference value
    T = 103 n ・ m {1050 kgf / cm}



10. Lear brake drum installation

  1. Attach the rear brake drum.



11. Adjustment of brake drums and lining gaps



  1. Turn the adjuster with a flathead screwdriver from the backing plate service hall, and let the brake shoe ASSY contact the rear brake drum.




  2. Turn the adjuster by turning the adjuster with another flathead screwdriver while pressing the rear brake automatic adjust lever LH with a flathead screwdriver.

  3. The brake shoe Assy is no longer in contact with the rear brake drum, and the adjuster is further reduced to contract to contract.



12. Speed ​​sensor RR LH installation

  1. Attach the speed sensor RR LH with bolts.

    Reference value
    T = 8.0 n ・ m {82 kgf / cm}

    ■ Attention ■
    1. When the sensor body is installed, the sensor is not damaged.
    2. Do not bite foreign matter.
    3. When installing, make sure that the sensor wire does not twist.
