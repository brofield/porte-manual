    1. When hands -free calls are not available

    1. In the following cases, you cannot make a call using hands -free.


      1. When connecting Bluetooth

        1. When outside the call area
        2. When the line is crowded, such as when the line is crowded
        3. In emergency call with helpnet, during automatic maintenance checks or manual maintenance inspections
        4. When the phonebook data is transferred from the phone
        5. When the phone is locked
        6. When the phone is being used, such as during data communication
        7. When the phone is broken
        8. When the phone is not connected
        9. When the battery level of the phone is insufficient
        10. When the power of the phone is off
        11. When the Bluetooth settings of the mobile phone are not hands -free mode
        12. Other when the phone itself cannot be used

    2. When contracting for interrupt calls (catchphones) or three -way calls, use the interrupt call (catchphone) or three -way calls on the telephone itself.

    3. While using i-mode, vodafoneLive!, Ezweb, the hands-free function cannot be used.

    4. If you are using a helpnet, hands -free can not be used during the helpnet operation.In addition, during the helpnet operation, the line cut cannot be performed by hanging the phone.Hands -free can be transmitted while waiting for automatic arrival.
