Brake Master Cylinder Assembly

printing

List of preparation



1. Brake Master Cylinder SUB-ASSY inspection

  1. Inspect that there is no damage inside the brake mast cylindaboday.



2. Brake master cylinder kit installation



  1. Apply rubber grease to the new No.2 piston, No.1 piston and piston guide.

  2. Install the No.2 piston to the master cylindabose.

    ■ Attention ■
    Insert the piston straight so that the inside of the cylindabode is not damaged.

  3. Attach the plate, cylinder cup and piston guide to the No.1 piston.

  4. Attach the No.1 piston straight to the master cylindabose.

    ■ Attention ■
    1. Insert the piston straight so that the inside of the cylindabode is not damaged.
    2. Does not hurt the lip part of the cylinder cup.

  5. With the piston pushed in, insert a straight pin from the brake master cylinder union mounting part.

    ■ Attention ■

    Make sure that the straight pin and piston are attached.




  6. With the piston pushed in, use the SST to attach a new hole napping ring.

    SST
    09905-00013 (09904-00090)

  7. Apply rubber grease to the new O -ring.

  8. Attach the O -ring to the master cylindabose.



3. Installation of check valve gromet

  1. Brake Master Cylinder Attach two check valve glots to ASSY.

    ■ Attention ■

    Confirm that foreign substances are not attached to the grommet and mounting part.



4. Brake Master Cylinder Union installation



  1. Use a ping -punch (5mm) and a hammer to hit the brake master cylinder ring and attach the brake master cylinder union.
