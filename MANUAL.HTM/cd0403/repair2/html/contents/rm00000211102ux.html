Circuit explanation


  1. The crank position sensation system is composed of crank angle sensor and pickup coils.The crank angle sensor has 34 convex portions with 2 teeth for the detection of two teeth, and is attached to the crankshaft.The NE signal sensor outputs 34 signals per engine.The engine control computer detects a cylinder and a crankshaft angle and engine speed by the NE signal based on the G2 signal.

Inspection procedure


□ Participation □
If the DTC P0335 is re -emitted after the crank position sensor diagnosis procedure is performed, check the part related to the following cam position sensor.
  1. Attachment of the cam position sensor.
  1. Attachment of camshaft.
  1. Attached state of the cam position sensor cat.


□ Participation □
  1. Read freeze frame data using SST (Tascan).The freeze -frame data records a part of the engine operation state in the event of a problem, which helps you take a trouble shot.
  2. If you do not find any problems with the trouble shooting of the diag code P0335 and P0339, you may have a problem with the mechanical system.
