List of preparation



1. Long -seat ASSY RH installation

  1. Operate the seat track adjusting handle to slide the front seat ASSY to make it a front mort.

    ■ Attention ■
    Make sure you are locking.

  2. Put the sheet Assies on the vehicle and match the pins on the front seat leg ASSY RH part and the hole on the vehicle side.

    ■ Attention ■
    If you have a seat track adjusting handle, the slide synchronization position will be shifted.

  3. Connect the vehicle side wire harness and the seat side wire harness.

  4. Temporarily tighten the rear side of the seat Assy with two bolts.

  5. Operate the seat truck adjusting handle to slide the front seat ASSY to make it a rear mole.

    ■ Attention ■
    Make sure you are locking.

  6. Temporarily tighten the front side of the sheet Assy with two bolts.

  7. Operate the seat track adjusting handle to slide the front seat ASSY to make it a front mort.

    ■ Attention ■
    Make sure you are locking.

  8. Tighten the two bolts on the rear side of the sheet Assing in order of inner → outa.

  9. Operate the seat truck adjusting handle to slide the front seat ASSY to make it a rear mole.

  10. Tighten the two bolts on the front side of the sheet asser in the order of inner → outa.

    Reference value

  11. The claws of the front seat leg cover are adjusted.



2. Slide adjuster lock synchronous inspection

  1. When the slide is operated, check that the left and right adjuster locks at the same time.

    □ Participation □
    If there is a synchronization of the slide, adjust the front sheet fixing bolt and adjust it.



3. Seat belt function inspection

  1. Turn on the ignition switch.

  2. Sit on the front seat ASSY RH, and when the tanging plate of the seat belt is not inserted in the buckle, check the driver's seat belt warning indicator.

  3. When the tanging plate of the seat belt is inserted in the buckle, check that the driver's seat belt warning indicator of the combination meter Assy turns off.
