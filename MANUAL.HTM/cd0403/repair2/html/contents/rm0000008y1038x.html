Circuit description


Driving method FF:
Transfer pulse signal output from ABS speed sensor attached to each wheel is converted to car speed pulse signal (equivalent to a trance axle rotation axis 1 rotation/4 pulse) by brake actuator ASSY (skid control computer).It is output to the combination meter assembly and input to the engine control computer.




Driving method 4WD:
The vehicle speed sensor outputs 4 pulse signals every time the rotor shaft (rotating by the transmission power shaft via a driven gear).The signal is turned into an accurate pulse waveform by the waveform formed in the circuit in the combination meter assassy, ​​and is transmitted to the engine control computer.It determines the vehicle speed based on the frequency of such a pulse signal.
