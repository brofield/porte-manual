4. Front axle swing and axial rattling inspection

  1. Remove the front tire




  2. Front disc brake caliper cut off

    1. Remove the two bolts and disconnect the disc brake caliper ASSY from the steering knuckle.

      ■ Attention ■
      The caliper is suspended with a string.

  3. Remove the front disc

  4. Front axle hub bearing shaft direction backlash inspection




    1. Set the dial gauge in the position of the front axle hub to check the gata in the shaft direction of the bearing.

      limit
      0.05mm

      ■ Attention ■
      If it exceeds the limit, replace it with a new bearing.

  5. Front axle hub swing inspection




    1. Set the dial gauge at the position of the front axle hub to check the shake of the bearing.

      limit
      0.07mm

      ■ Attention ■
      If it exceeds the limit, replace it with a new bearing.

  6. Installation of front disk




  7. Front disc brake caliper mounting

    1. Attach the disc brake caliper ASSY to the steering knuckle with two bolts.
