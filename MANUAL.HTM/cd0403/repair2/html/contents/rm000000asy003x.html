■ Attention ■
  1. Since the Iridium plug is used, no gap adjustment is made.
  2. Iridium, do not scratch the platinum chip.
  3. If it is significantly dirty due to sludge, replace it with a new spark plug.
  4. When replacing with a new spark plug, the caps covered for advanced protection are not removed until the engine is attached to the engine.
  5. If the spark plug is dropped, replace it with a new spark plug.
  6. The gap is performed by visual inspection, as the iridium chip may be damaged.
  7. If there is damage, replace the spark plug.
