Circuit description


The inlet air tempara chasensa is provided in the intake airflow meter Sub-assy and senses the inhaled air temperature.The resistance value of the thermista incorporated into the inlet air tempara chaz sensor changes due to changes in inhaled air temperature.The lower the inhalation air temperature, the greater the resistance of the thermista, and the higher the higher the resistance, the lower the resistance value.The inlet Air Tenpara Chasensa is connected to the engine control computer, and a 5V power supply voltage is supplied via the resistor R from the THA terminal of the engine control compilation.In other words, since the resistor R and the inlet air tempara censor are connected in series, the resistance value changes due to changes in the inhaled air temperature, and the potential of the THA terminal also changes.Based on this signal, the engine control computer increases the amount of fuel injection during cold and improves driverability.
