51. Cooling solution (Toyota genuine super LLC) replenishment

  1. Close the radiator drain kotsuku plug and injection of cooling water until overflowing from the radiator injection.[*1]

    □ Participation □
    The radiator inlet hole and radiator outlet hose are compressed several times by hand and the water level of the radiator injection is lowered.

  2. Tighten the radiator cap.

  3. Inject the cooling solution into the radiator reserve tank to the upper limit.

  4. Warm up the engine until the thermostat opens.

    □ Participation □
    Compress the radiator inlet hole and outlet hose several times during warm -up.

  5. Stop the engine, wait for the cooling to cool, remove the radiator cap to check the water level.

  6. If the water level is lowered, repeat from [*1].

  7. When the water level does not fall, adjust the cooling solution of the radiator reservoir battank.
