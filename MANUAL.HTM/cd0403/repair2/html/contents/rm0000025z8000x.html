5. Fuel tank main tube cut off



  1. Spread the tube joint clip and pull out in the direction of the arrow.




  2. Pull out the main tube and separate it.

    ■ Attention ■
    1. Clean the mud and other foreign substances attached to the clip before work.
    2. Since the fuel tube joint is a fuel pump tube and fuel suction plate with the O -ring, the work is carried out with care for the connection of the connection and the attachment of foreign substances.
    3. The removal work is always done by hand and do not use tools.
    4. Don't forcibly bend, fold or rotate a nylon tube.
    5. After cutting, protect the connection with a plastic bag.
    6. If the nylon tube and the fuel suction plate are sticking, press or pull to release the stick.
