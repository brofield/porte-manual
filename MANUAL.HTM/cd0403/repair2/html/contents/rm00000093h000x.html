Front drive shaft inspection

printing


1. Front drive shaft inspection

  1. When the joint is moved up and down, left and right, or axial direction, it operates smoothly and checks that there is no significant play.

  2. Inspect that there is no crack, damage and grease leakage of the joint boots.

    ■ Attention ■
    Drive shaft ASSY is carried horizontally.




  3. Confirm that the front drive shaft has the following dimensions.
