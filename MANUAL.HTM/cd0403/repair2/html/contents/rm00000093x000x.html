Front suspension Lower arm No.1 LH removal

printing

List of preparation




1. Front suspension arm SUB-ASSY LWR No.1 LH inspection

  1. Jack up the front of the vehicle.




  2. Sprinkle up and down the rower arm with the power of the hand (294N {30kgf} or more) to confirm that the ball joint has no rattling.



2. Front tire direct condition inspection


3. Remove the steering column hole cover plate

  1. Remove the two clips and remove the hole cover plate.



4. Steering sliding York SUB-ASSY disconnection



  1. Attach the seat belt so that the steering wheel does not rotate.

    □ Participation □
    Perform to prevent spiral cable disconnection.




  2. Loosen the bolts on the column side (A) of the sliding yoke.

  3. Remove the bolt on the gear side (B) of the sliding yoke.

  4. Mark the sliding yoke and the interimide shaft, and separate the sliding yoke.




  5. Remove the clip A and separate the hole cover from the body.

    ■ Attention ■
    Do not damage clip B.



5. Remove the front tire


6. The engine Anda cover LH is removed

  1. Remove two bolts and two screws and remove the engine undercover LH.



7. The engine Anda cover RH is removed

  1. Remove two bolts and two screws.

  2. Remove the nut and remove the engine undercover RH.

    □ Participation □
    The nut is tightened with the LH side.



8. Engine ASSY W/Transxle support

  1. Use an engine jack to hold the engine ASSY W/Transxle via a piece of wood.



9. Remove the front stabilizer bolt

  1. Fix the front stabilizer barbolt with a spanner (10mm) and remove the two nuts.

  2. Remove 4 front stabilizer cushions retener No.1, 4 front stabilizer cushions, front stabilizer barcedo -cushion retainer No.2 and front stabilizer bolt.




  3. The other side is removed in the same procedure.



10. Pressive tube Assemble



  1. Remove the bolt and separate the cramps of the Tekshiya Feed Chew ASSY from the cross member.




  2. Use the Union Nut Wrenten 17 to separate the pressure feed tube ASSY.



11. Tie rod end sub-assy LH separation

  1. Remove the cotter pin and castle nut.




  2. Use SST to separate the tie rod end from the steering knuckle.

    SST
    09628-62011

    ■ Attention ■
    Does not hurt the dust cover of the ball joint.



12. Tie rod end sub-assy RH cut off
SST
09628-62011

□ Participation □
The RH side is performed in the same procedure as the LH side.



13. Front suspension arm sub-assy lwr No.1 LH separation

  1. Remove the cotter pin and castle nut.




  2. Use SST to separate the ball joint part of the Rewer Arm No.1 from the steering knuckle.

    SST
    09628-00011

    ■ Attention ■
    1. Does not hurt the dust cover of the ball joint.
    2. Be sure to hang it with a string to prevent SST from falling off.



14. Front suspension arm sub-assy lwr No.1 RH separation
SST
09628-00011

□ Participation □
The RH side is performed in the same procedure as the LH side.



15. Front suspension cross member SUB-ASSY



  1. Remove the bolts and two nuts and separate the mounting -in -yujerter RR from the cross member.

  2. Support cross members with mission jacks.




  3. Remove the four bolts and remove the cross member with the steering link asser.



16. Front suspension arm sub-assy lwr No.1 LH is removed

  1. Loosen the bolt (A).




  2. Fix the nut and loosen the bolt (b).

    ■ Attention ■
    Since the nut uses a nut with a protrusion for a rotation, make sure to turn the bolt and loosen it.
