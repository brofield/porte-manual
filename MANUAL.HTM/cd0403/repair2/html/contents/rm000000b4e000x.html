Headlamp assembly

printing


1. Installation of headlamp No.1 valve (halogen headlamp)

  1. Attach the head lamp valve with a set spring.

    ■ Attention ■
    Do not touch the glass part of the valve.



2. Head lamp socket cover installation (halogen headlamp)




  1. Attach the head lamp socket cover to the lamp bodie.

    ■ Attention ■
    1. Push the outer periphery of the socket cover until it hits the lamp bondy.
    2. Push the inner circumference of the socket cover (around the valve) until the valve bracket part can be confirmed all over the circumference.



3. Head lamp light control computers ASSY installation (discharge head lamp)



  1. Connect a connector and attach a light control computer with three screws.

    □ Participation □
    If there is no gap between the gaps to be attached, the left and right adjustment of the screw is left and right to make the gap between the left and right.If it is difficult to install only for left and right adjustment, rotate the up and down adjustment of the Eming screw.

    ■ Attention ■
    If you turn the Eming screw more than necessary, the reflector may interfere with the headlamp bondy or damage the Eming screw.




  2. Attach the head lamp light control computer ASSY with four screws.



4. Discharge headlamp valve installation

  1. At the set spring, attach the discharge headlamp valve.

  2. Connect the socket of the light control computer.



5. Installation of head lamp socket cover (discharge headlamp)

  1. Combine the claws and attach the socket cover.



6. Installation of clearance lamp valve

  1. Attach a clearance lump valve to the valve socket.

  2. Turn the valve socket and attach a valve socket and a clearance lamp valve integrally.



7. Installation of the front turn signal lamp valve

  1. Attach the front turtle signal lamp valve to the valve socket.

  2. Turn the valve socket and attach the valve socket and the front -turning signal lamp valve together.
