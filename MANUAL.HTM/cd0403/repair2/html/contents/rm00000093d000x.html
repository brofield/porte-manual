4. Installation of front axle ASSY LH

  1. Press the front axle ASSY to the outside of the vehicle, and insert the drive shaft ASSY spline into the axle ASSY and insert it.

    ■ Attention ■
    1. Do not push the front axle ASSY out of the vehicle more than necessary.
    2. Do not damage the oil seal.
    3. Do not damage the drive shaft outboard boots.
    4. The speed sensor harness clamp does not transform.
    5. Do not damage the speed sensor rotor.
    6. Confirm that there is no foreign substance in the speed sensor rotor and the insertion part.
