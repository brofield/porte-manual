Function explanation


The signal from the engine control computer performs the transmission from 1st to 4th speed by combining the transmission solenoid No.1 (S1) and the transmission solenoid No.1 (S2) ON-OFF.If one of the solenoid valve systems is disconnected or short -circulated, the engine control computers turn ON or OFF the other solenoid valve.If all solenoid valves are failed, only the mechanical hydraulic circuit will be used, and it will be a manual shift.(In the case of disconnection and short circuit, the engine control computer stops the power of the failed solenoid valve).
