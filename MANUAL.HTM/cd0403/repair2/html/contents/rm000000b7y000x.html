Front seat ASSY RH decomposition

printing

List of preparation



1. RH seat Front Seat Track Bracket LWR RR RH is removed



  1. Remove the four nuts and remove the left and right front seat track brackets LWR RR RH.



2. Remove the front sheet leg ASSY RH

  1. Remove the two nuts and remove the front seat leg ASSY RH.



3. Remove the front seat leg cover RH

  1. Remove the front seat leg cover RH.



4. Front sheet leg cover LH (RH seat yo) should be removed

  1. Remove the front seat leg cover LH.



5. Remove the front seat headrest ASSY

  1. Remove the front seat headrest ASSY.



6. Remove Vertical Adjusta Cover RH



  1. Use a minus thin -blade driver with a protective tape to remove the claws and remove the vertical agasta cover RH.



7. Remove Vertical Adjusta Handle RH

  1. Remove the two screws and remove the Vertical Adjusta handle RH.



8. Reclining Adjustment Relley Relley Handle RH is removed



  1. With the handle pulled up, use a minus thin -blade driver with a protective tape to remove the claws, and remove the reclining adjuster release handle RH.



9. Remove the front seat cushion shield RH

  1. Take off the screw.

  2. Remove the upper claw on the upper side.




  3. Shift the direction of the arrow in the figure to remove the clip and claws, and remove the front seat cushion shield RH.



10. Remove the front seat cushion shield Inn RH

  1. Take off the screw.

  2. Remove the upper claw on the upper side.




  3. Shift the cover in the direction of the arrow in the figure to remove the clip and claws, and remove the front seat cushion shield INN RH.



11. Remove the front seat innabelt ASSY RH

  1. Cut the wire harness clamp.

  2. Remove the bolt and remove the front seat innabelt asy RH.



12. Remove the front seat back hook

  1. Wake up the hook.

  2. Take off the screw.




  3. Use a flathead driver with a protective tape to remove the front seat cross -hook.



13. Remove the front seat headrest support



  1. Turn the front seat cross -cover cover and remove the three hogling.

  2. Remove two hogling.




  3. Remove the claws and remove two front seat headrest support.



14. Front seat backpad (cover Tsuki) RH is removed

  1. Take off the front seat Batsuku cover & pad RH.

    □ Participation □
    If there is no pull -in wire when replacing the front seat cross cover & pad, reuse the pull -in wire.



15. Front seat cushion cover & pad RH removed



  1. Use a flathead screwdriver with a protective tape to cause the claws in the front seat cushion cover and pad RH RH front.

  2. Remove the J hook on the front of the cushion cover.

  3. Remove the hogling and remove the front seat cushion cover & pad RH.



16. Remove Vertical Adjusta Spacer



  1. Remove the claws and remove the vertical agasta spacer.



17. Reclining adjuster inside cover RH (RH seat yo)



  1. Remove the screw and remove the claws and remove the reclining adjustment side cover RH ((RH seat yo)).



18. Reclining Adjustment Inside Cover LH (RH Seat Yo)

  1. Remove the screw and remove the claws, and remove the reclining adjustment side cover LH (RH seat yo).



19. Reclining Adjustment Inside Cover RH is removed



  1. Remove the screw and remove the reclining adjustment side cover RH.

    ■ Attention ■
    If the vertical mechanism is not set to the top, the screw will not come off.



20. Reclining adjuster inside cover LH removed

  1. Remove the screw and remove the reclining adjustment side cover LH.



21. Front seat cushion shield LWR RH is removed



  1. Remove the claws and remove the front seat cushion shield LWR RH.

    ■ Attention ■
    Make the seat to the front mortoal position.



22. Front seat cushion shield LWR LH removed

  1. Remove the claws and remove the front seat cushion shield LWR LH.

    ■ Attention ■
    Make the seat to the front mortoal position.
