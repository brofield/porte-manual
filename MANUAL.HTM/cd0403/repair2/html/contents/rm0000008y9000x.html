Circuit description


CO, HC, NO in exhaust gasxA triple catalyst is used to be efficiently purified.The three -primary catalyst works most efficiently near theoretical air -fuel ratio.Therefore, if the engine is not accurately controlled near the theory air -fuel ratio, Co, HC, No.xCannot be purified efficiently.Oxyzenesensa is installed for this control.Oxyzing ensenser uses the characteristic that when the oxygen concentration in the exhaust gas is discharged when a normal combustion is performed near the theoretical air -fuel ratio, the output power suddenly changes, and accurately the air -fuel ratio control is accurately controlled.The signal is sent to the engine control compilation so that it can be performed.When the air -fuel ratio becomes thinner, the oxygen concentration in the exhaust gas increases, and the oxyzen sensor sends information that the air -fuel ratio is thin in the engine control compilation.(Small power activation: <0.45V) When the air -fuel ratio becomes thicker, the oxygen concentration in the exhaust gas is reduced, and the oxidian ensenser sends information that the air -fuel ratio is strong in the engine control composer.(Large power activation:> 0.45V) The engine control compiler determines whether the air -fuel ratio is thick or thin due to the oxidate ensensor's electromotive signal, and the injection amount is controlled.The oxyzes ensenser with a heater has a heater that warms the diliconium element, which is controlled by the engine control compilation, and the oxyzen sensor cools down when the temperature of the exhaust gas is low (when the amount of inhaled air is low).The current flows to the heater to prevent it from being able to work accurately.
