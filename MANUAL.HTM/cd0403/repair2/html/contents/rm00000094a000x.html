List of preparation



1. Remove the rear bosorver cap


2. Remove the rear tire


3. Skid control sensor wire cut off

  1. Cut the connector of the skid control sensor.

    ■ Attention ■
    Do not adhere to the connector or mounting part of the skid control sensor.



4. Rear brake tube No.4 cut off



  1. Use a union nut wrench to separate the brake tube.

    ■ Attention ■
    Do not damage the rear brake tube and flexible hose

  2. Remove the clip.



5. Rear brake tube No.3 separation

□ Participation □
The RH side is performed in the same procedure as the LH side.



6. Flexible hose bracket No.4



  1. Remove the bolt and remove the flexible hose bracket.



7. Flexible hose bracket No.3

□ Participation □
The RH side is performed in the same procedure as the LH side.



8. Remove the rear brake drum



  1. Unlock the parking brake and remove the rear brake drum.

    □ Participation □
    If the rear brake drum is not easy to remove, remove the rear brake hole plug, use a flathead driver from the backing plate service hole to press the rear brake automatic adjustable lever LH, and use another flather.Turn the adjuster and contract.



9. Remove the rear axle hub & bearing Assy LH



  1. Remove the nut and separate the parking brake cable ASSY No.3 from the rear axle beam.




  2. Remove the four bolts and remove the rear axle hub & bearing Assing LH.

    ■ Attention ■
    1. The rear axle hub & bearing Assy LH is hung with a rope or needle.
    2. Do not bend the brake tube and parking brake cable ASSY.

  3. Cut the rear brake ASSY from the rear axle beam.



10. Remove the rear axle hub & bearing Assy RH

□ Participation □
The RH side is performed in the same procedure as the LH side.



11. Rear Axle Beam ASSY loosening

  1. Loosen the two bolts of the rear axle beam.

    ■ Attention ■

    The bolt is not removed just by loosening it.



12. Shock absorber ASSY RR LH separation



  1. Fix the two -sided width of the rear shock absorber ASSY LH and loosen the two nuts.




  2. Support the rear axle beam with a jack via a piece of wood.

  3. Remove the nut and the rear shock absorbaspaca, and separate the rear shock absorber ASSY LH (Lower side) from the rear axle beam ASSY.



13. Shock absorber ASSY RR RH separation

□ Participation □
The RH side is separated by the same procedure as the LH side.



14. Coil spring RR LH removed



  1. When reusing the rear coil spring LH, check if the identification paint is attached, and if it is gone, attach the identification paint to the lower side of the vehicle and the rear of the vehicle.

  2. Gradually lower the jack and remove the rear coil spring LH.

  3. Remove the rear coil spring insulator UPR LH and rear coil spring insulator LWR LH.



15. Coil spring RR RH removed

□ Participation □
The RH side is performed in the same procedure as the LH side.



16. Remove the rear axle beam ASSY

  1. Remove the two bolts and remove the rear axle beam.
