Back door glass removal

printing

List of preparation



1. Remove the backdoor trim board



  1. Remove the nine clips and remove the Batsukudo Atlim board.



2. Centaster top lamp cover removed



  1. Press the center tsuplan cover in the direction of ① and pull it in the direction of ②, and separate the two places of the claws in parts A.

  2. Pull the center tsuplan cover in the direction of ③, remove the clip two places, and remove the center tsuplan cover.



3. Centaster top ramp ASSY removed

  1. Remove the clamp and separate the harness of the center tsuplamp ASSY from the centust plasticquetto.




  2. Press the center tsupramp ASSY in the direction of ① and rotate in the direction of ②, and separate the two places of the claws of the part B.

    ■ Attention ■
    Pay attention to the claw damage due to the overloading of the centist tsupramp ASSY.

  3. Remove the connector and remove the center tsupramp ASSY.



4. Remove the rear waiper arm head cap


5. Remove the rear wai arm asser

  1. Remove the nut and remove the rear wiperem.



6. Back window glass channel RH removed

  1. Use the clip remover to remove the two clips and remove the Batsukudo Garaschi Yannel RH.

  2. Warm the double -sided tape remaining in the door with an infrared lamp.

    ■ Attention ■
    Watch out for the heat of the heated door and infrared lamps.

    □ Participation □
    3-5 minutes for 40-60 ° C

  3. Rub the double -sided tape remaining in the body with a rag.



7. Back window glass channel LH ​​removed

  1. The RH side is performed in the same procedure as LH.



8. Remove the backdoor glass

■ Attention ■
  1. When reusing the glass, put a gum tape or the like on the Batsukudo Glass and the Batsukudo Apanel part before removing it, and mark the matching when installing it with a pen.


  2. At the point of the glass diagram, a spacer and clip are attached to cut it with caution so that it does not damage the crumbed glass.
  3. Depending on the disconnection site and the situation, the work shield knife and the piano wire are used properly.

  1. Cut each connector.

  2. Put the suction rubber on the glass.

  3. Put a protective tape on the body surface on the outer periphery of the glass.

  4. Apply soap water sufficiently to the adhesive part.




  5. Insert a wind shield knife into the adhesive part.

    □ Participation □
    The window shield knife is inserted from a place where the interval between glass and the body is wide and the work is performed.

  6. While holding the edge and the edge of the glass at a right angle, the blade of the wind shield knife is adjusted to the glass surface and the end, and the adhesive is drawn parallel to the glass end to cut the adhesive.

    ■ Attention ■
    Absolutely no wind shield knife.




  7. As shown in the figure, the piano wire (0.6 mm is appropriate) is passed through) and both ends are attached to the Mighty Player.

    ■ Attention ■
    1. Do not contact the piano wire to the end of the glass.
    2. Note that if you cross the piano line, it will cut.

  8. Pull the piano line alternately to leave the spacer and clip, and cut the adhesive.

  9. Remove the spacer and clip and remove the crumbous glass with suction rubber.
