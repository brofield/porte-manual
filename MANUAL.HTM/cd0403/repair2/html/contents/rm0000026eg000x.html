Brake piping (4WD) Notes

printing



    1. For important security parts, if you find a leakage of brake fluid, be sure to decompose parts and replace it with a new one if there is any abnormality.


      2. When removing each brake configuration, cover the connection so that foreign substances such as garbage or dust do not enter.


        3. Do not damage the brake tube when removing or intentionally deforms.


          4. Check that the brake tube is passing through the center of the grommet when attaching the grometto to the body.


            5. When installing a brake tube and flexible hose, make sure that there is no twist or break.


              6. If the base of the flexible hose does not match the bracket groove, twist the flexible hose at the minimum angle.


                7. Do not reuse the flexible hose mounting clip.


                  8. Do not adhere to the flexible hose such as absorba oil or grease.


                    9. When attaching a brake tube to the resin clamp, the brake tube is ensured to be inserted into the clamp and confirm that the brake tube is not rattled or bitten.


                      10. When the brake tube and flexible hose are attached, confirm that they are not interfered with other parts.


                        11. Do not attach the brake fluid to the painted surface such as a body.In the unlikely event that it is attached, wipe it off quickly and wash it with water.


                          12. When attaching a brake tube clamp or away with a bolt, the brake tube may be deformed, so fix it so that it does not turn the clamp and tighten the bolt.
