DTC P1760/77 Line pressure control linear SOL system

printing

List of preparation


Function explanation


It is controlled by the duty ratio determined by the engine control computer based on the signal from the throttle position sensor.This adjusts the line pressure that matches the throttle opening and engine output.


DTC No.
DTC detection conditions
1. Diagnosis conditions 2. Abnormal state 3. Announced period
Inspection site
P1760/77
  1. The engine is rotating
  2. Solenoid SLT circuit disconnection or short circuit
  3. 1 second or more continuous
  1. Wire harness and connector (SLT signal system)
  2. Transmission wire
  3. Line pressure control Solenoid ASSY (SLT)
  4. Engine control computer
