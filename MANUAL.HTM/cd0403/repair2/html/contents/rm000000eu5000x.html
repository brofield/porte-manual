Air Mix Dampa servo SUB-ASSY Single Inspection

printing


1. Air Mix Dampa servo SUB-ASSY inspection

  1. Air Mix Dampa Operating Circuit Inspection




    1. Confirm that the arm rotates smoothly from Max Cold to Max Hot when the battery is connected to the connector 5 (MHOT) terminal and the battery minus to the 4 (MCOLD) terminal.

    2. Confirm that the arm is smooth from Max Hot position to Max Cold when the battery is connected to the connector 4 (McOLD) terminal and the battery minus to the 5 (MHOT) terminal.

      □ Participation □
      Since the arm does not stop automatically at the time of the servo single inspection, the power supply is cut near the Max Cold position at the time of inspection.

  2. Air Mix Dampa Servo Poten Show Circuit Inspection

    1. Use SST (Toyota Electrical Tester) to measure the resistance value between the connector 1 (VZ) terminal ← → 2 (GND) terminal.

      Reference value
      6.0 ± 1.8kΩ

    2. Use a SST (Toyota Electrical Tester) to measure the resistance value between the connector 3 (Pt) terminal ← → 2 (GND) terminals when the arm is stopped at the Max Hot or Max Cold position.

      Reference value
      Arm position
      		Reference value
      Max Hot
      		4.8 ± 1.44kΩ
      Max Cold
      		1.2 ± 0.36kΩ

    3. Use SST (Toyota Electrical Tester) to confirm that the resistance value between the connector 3 (PT) terminal at the time of the arm rotation changes continuously.
