3. Steering wheel play inspection

  1. Start the engine and go straight to the vehicle.

  2. Move the steering wheels lightly with your fingers and check the play.

    limit
    Within 30mm



4. Oil reservoir level inspection

  1. Let the vehicle horizontal.

  2. The engine speed is 1000R/min or less, repeating the lock -locking of the steering wheels to increase the fluid oil temperature.

    Reference value
    40-80 ° C

  3. Remove the reservoir cap from the oil reservoir.

    □ Participation □
    Remove the reservoir bacco covers first.

  4. Make sure that there is no fluid foaming and cloudiness from the oil reservoir's mouth.

  5. Measure the difference in the fluid level between the engine rotation and the stop.If it is outside the reference value, the air should be removed.If the effect is not effective, check the fluid leakage of the hydraulic system.

    Reference value
    Within 5mm




  6. Inspect that the fluid level is within the standard.If it is less than the standard, replenish the power steering fluid so that the upper limit is within the standard range.

    standard
    In the HOT level of A in the figure (at the time of oil temperature 40-80 ° C)

    □ Participation □
    If the oil temperature is low (0-20 ° C), it is checked that it is within the COLD level in the B in the figure.




  7. Attach a reservoir cap and reservoir.

    ■ Attention ■
    Check that the reservoir bassa is floating.



5. Power steering air removal

  1. Lift up the vehicle.

  2. Slowly perform the lock -lock of the steering wheels while stopping the engine.

    ■ Attention ■
    Perform while replenishing so that the oil reservoir fluid does not disappear.

  3. Lift the vehicle.

  4. Start the engine without stepping on the accelerator pedal and leave it for a few minutes.

  5. The engine speed is 1000R/min or less, and the steering wheel lock -lock is repeated several times.

  6. With an engine speed of 1000R/min or less, the steering wheels are kept in a delinquent hold (5-6 seconds with the steering wheels filled with a full cut) at 2-3 seconds.

    ■ Attention ■
    With the steering wheels cut into full, do not hold for more than 10 seconds.

  7. After stopping the engine until the foaming and cloudiness of the oil reservoir disappears, the engine starts again and confirms that there is no foaming and cloudiness even if lock -tsu locks and stationary holding in idling rotation are performed.

    ■ Attention ■
    If the effect is not effective, check the fluid leakage of the hydraulic system.

  8. Perform oil reservoir level level inspection.



6. Power steering fluid leak inspection


  1. Check for fluids from the location in the figure.
