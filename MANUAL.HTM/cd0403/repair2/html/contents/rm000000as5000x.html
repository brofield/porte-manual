1. Cooling solution (Toyota genuine super LLC) extract

■ Warning ■
Do not remove the radiator kyatsup when the engine and radiator are hot to avoid the danger of burns.Liquid and steam pop out by pressure.

  1. Open the radiator kayatsu.

  2. Loosen the radiator drain kotsu plug and extract the cooling solution.



2. The engine Anda cover LH is removed

  1. Remove two bolts and two screws and remove the engine undercover LH.



3. The engine Anda cover RH is removed

  1. Remove two bolts and two screws.

  2. Remove the nut and remove the engine undercover RH.

    □ Participation □
    The nut is tightened with the LH side.



4. Radiator Inlet House Cut

  1. Remove the clamp and remove the radiator inlet hot.



5. Radiator outlet hole cut off

  1. Remove the clamp and remove the radiator outlet hot.



6. Oil cool line lettuce hole cut off

  1. Remove the clamp and disconnect the oil cool line lettuce hose.



7. Oil Cooler Outlet House Cut

  1. Remove the clamp and separate the oil cooler outlet hot.



8. Radiator Grill W/Radiator Support Seal UPR

  1. Use the clip resover to remove the six clips.




  2. Paste the protective tape around and on the radiator support seal UPR and the radiator support seal UPR.



  3. Use a flathead screwdriver with a protective tape to remove the support seal behind the vehicle with the three claws in the figure.

    □ Participation □
    Put the flathead driver diagonally from the service hall, turn the driver like the arrow in the figure, and remove the claws.






  4. Lift the radiator support seal UPR and remove the radiator grilled claws from the gaps.

    □ Participation □
    Radiator support seal UPR is lifted on one side and restored when the claws are off.




  5. Check that the claws are off and remove the radiator grill.

    ■ Attention ■
    Note that the claws at the bottom of the radiator grill are easily damaged.

  6. Remove the radiator support seal UPR.

  2. Remove the four bolts and remove the bonnet (food) rock support brace.



12. Radiator support SUB-ASSY UPR is removed

  1. Separate the horn ASSY connector and clamp.

  2. Remove the four bolts and one clip and remove the radiator support UPR.
