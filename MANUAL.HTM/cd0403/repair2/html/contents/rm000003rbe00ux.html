DTC P0711/38 Oil temperature sensor system (out of range)

printing

List of preparation


Function explanation


The oil temperature sensor attached to the automatic trance axle ASSY detects the oil temperature in the transxxus hydraulic control circuit, and outputs a signal in accordance with the oil temperature to the engine control control puta.The engine control computer estimates the AT oil temperature due to running time, engine water temperature, inhaled air temperature, etc., and detects the range of the AT oil temperature sensor.


DTC No.
SAE/TCCS

  1. Diagnostic condition
  2. Abnormal state
  3. Abnormal period
  4. others
Inspection site
P0711/38
  1. The engine cooling water temperature and inhaled air temperature when the engine starts is -10 ° C or more, and it runs for 30 minutes and 11km or more
  2. AT fluid oil temperature (THO1) is less than 20 ° C
  3. 3 seconds or more
  4. 2 trips
