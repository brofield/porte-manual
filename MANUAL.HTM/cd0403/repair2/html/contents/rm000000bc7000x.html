Attach a rear bumper

printing

List of preparation



1. Renaavan cover installation (license plate standard)

  1. Attach a reflex recked with a nut.

  2. Combine the two clips and attach the rear barpion seal LH and the rear bump side seal RH.

  3. Align the screws and clips, attach the rear bump -side LH, the rear bampo side retainer RH, the rear pampaside support LH, and the rear pampaside support RH.

  4. Put a protective tape around the opening and quota panel around the rear vamp cover.

  5. Lift the rear vamp cover and pass the upper side of the license plate through the bumper opening.




  6. Combine the claws of the rear vampo side, and attach the rear vamp cover with four clips at the bottom of the rear ban cover and two side portions.

  7. Use two rear bump cushions with a torque wrench (T30).

  8. Remove the protective tape.



2. Rena vamp cover installation (license plate -shaped light type)

  1. Attach a reflex recked with a nut.

  2. Combine the two clips and attach the rear barpion seal LH and the rear bump side seal RH.

  3. Align the screws and clips, attach the rear bump -side LH, the rear bampo side retainer RH, the rear pampaside support LH, and the rear pampaside support RH.

  4. Put a protective tape on the lower part of the rear vamp cover and around the opening and the quota panel.

  5. Lift the rear banned cover and diagonally the rear ban cover.




  6. Pass the lower side of the license plate from the bumper opening.




  7. Raise the rear vamp cover diagonally, dent the upper part of the license plate, and pass through the rear bump cover opening.




  8. While straightening the rear vamp cover, the upper right of the license plate is passed through the rear bump cover opening.




  9. Combine the claws of the rear vampo side, and attach the rear vamp cover with four clips at the bottom of the rear ban cover and two side portions.

  10. Use two rear bump cushions with a torque wrench (T30).

  11. Remove the protective tape.
