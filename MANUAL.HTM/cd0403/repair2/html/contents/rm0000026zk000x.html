Bane pump assembly (4WD) assembly

printing

List of preparation



1. Attach the vane pump housing oil seal

  1. Apply a small amount of MP grease No.2 to the lip of the new housing oil seal.




  2. Use SST and press to press the housing oil seal until the upper end of the housing front.

    SST
    09950-60010 (09951-00280)
    09950-70010 (09951-07100)

    ■ Attention ■
    1. The mounting direction of the housing oil seal is not wrong.
    2. Do not damage the housing oil seal.



2. Shaft SUB-ASSY installation

  1. Apply power steering fluid to the shaft.




  2. Wrap the protective tape around the shaft spline and attach it to the housing front.

    ■ Attention ■
    1. Do not damage the housing oil seal.
    2. After inserting the shaft with pulley, make sure that the lip part of the housing oil seal is not inverted.



3. Bane pump side plate front mounting

  1. Apply a power steering fluid to the new side plate O -ring No.2.




  2. Attach the side plate O -ring No.2 to the housing front.




  3. Apply a power steering fluid to the new side plate O -ring No.1.

  4. Attach the side plate O -ring No.1 to the side plate front.




  5. Match the semicircle hole on the side plate front and the semicircular hole on the housing front and attach the side plate front.

    ■ Attention ■
    Do not make a mistake in the mounting position.



4. Installation of vane pump cam ring



  1. The scanning mark is up, the semicircle hole of the vane pump cam ring and the semicircular hole on the side plate front are attached, and the vane pump cam ring is attached to the housing front.

    ■ Attention ■
    There is no mistake in the mounting direction and mounting position.



5. Installation of vane



  1. Attach the vane ponpra to the housing front.

    □ Participation □
    There is no direction in the installation of Vane Pon Proa.




  2. Apply power steering fluid to 10 vane pump plates.

  3. With the round side of the end face on the outside, attach 10 bean pump plates to the vannopon procta.

    ■ Attention ■
    There is no mistake in the installation direction.



6. Bane pump shaft snapping installation



  1. Use a SST and minus thin blade driver to attach a new shaft snap ring.

    SST
    09905-00012

    ■ Attention ■
    1. Do not expand the shaft snap ring more than necessary.
    2. Confirm that the shaft snap ring is firmly in the groove.
    3. Do not damage vane ponpra and vane pump shaft.



7. Bane pump Houjin Griller installation

  1. Apply a power steering fluid to the new housing O -ring.

  2. Attach the housing O -ring to the Houjin Griller.

    ■ Attention ■
    Put the housing O -ring in the Houjin Griller O -ring groove.




  3. Match the straight pin to the pin hole in the vane pump cam ring and attach the Houjinglia to the housing front.

    ■ Attention ■
    Make sure that the housing O -ring is not protruding, and attach the Houjinglia.




  4. Attach the housing liller to the housing front with four bolts.

    Reference value
    T = 22 n ・ M {224 kgf / cm}



8. Preload inspection

  1. Make sure that the shiaftwise pulley rotates smoothly and has no noise.

  2. Attach a bolt (M10 × P1.25) to the sheaftway pulley.




  3. Inspect the rotation torque of the shiaftwise pulley.

    Reference value
    T = 0.27n · m {2.8kgf · cm} (rotating)

    □ Participation □
    In the case of outside the reference value, check for the lip of the housing oil seal.Also, check if the installation is normal.



9. Power steering oil pressure sensor installation

  1. Apply power steering fluid to the O -ring.

  2. Attach the oil pressure sensor to the housing front.

    Reference value
    T = 21 N ・ M {214 kgf / cm}



10. Flow control valve installation

  1. Attach the compression spring to the housing front.

  2. Apply a power steering fluid to the flow control valve.

  3. Attach the flow control valve to the housing front.

    ■ Attention ■
    The direction of the flow control valve is not wrong.

  4. Apply power steering fluid to the new pressureport union O -ring.

  5. Attach the pressureport union O -ring to the pressureport union.




  6. Use a socket wrench 27mm to attach the pressureport union to the housing front.

    Reference value
    T = 69 n ・ m {700 kgf / cm}



11. Pump bracket FR installation

  1. Attach the bracket FR and bracket RR with two bolts.

    Reference value
    T = 44 N ・ M {440 kgf / cm}



12. Bane pump oil reservoir assertion

  1. Apply a power steering fluid to the new O -ring and attach it to the oil reserve ass.

  2. With three bolts, attach the oil reservoir associate to the vane pump housing.

    Reference value
    T = 9.0 n ・ m {92 kgf / cm}
