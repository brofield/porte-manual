  1. Power window regulator motor ASSY LH operation inspection

    ■ Attention ■
    Since the pulse sensor inside the motor is damaged, the voltage is not applied except for the 1 and 3 terminals of the motor.





    1. When a battery voltage is applied to each connector terminal, check that the motor operates smoothly.

      standard
      Measurement conditions
      		Direction of operation
      Battery Plus ← → 3 terminals
      Battery minus ← → 1 terminal
      		Round right to the drive axis
      Battery Plus ← → 1 terminal
      Battery minus ← → 3 terminals
      		Left rotation to the drive axis

  2. PTC (Power window motor protection function) operation inspection

    ■ Attention ■
    Perform a power window regulator, motor and door glass with the vehicle.


    1. Set SST (Toyota Electrical Tester DC400A Prove) on the wire harness of one terminal of the connector of the passenger seat power window regulator motor.

      ■ Attention ■
      Point the probe arrow to the motor

    2. The passenger seat door glass is fully closed.

    3. After about 60 seconds, when the passenger seat power window switch is continued to be operated on the up side (first time), check the time until the current of the motor changes from about 16-34A to 1A or less.(Inspection inspection)

      standard
      About 4-90 seconds

    4. About 60 seconds after the inspection, operate the passenger seat power window switch to confirm that the door glass descends.(Return inspection)
