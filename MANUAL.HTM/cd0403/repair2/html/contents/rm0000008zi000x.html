1. Camshaft timing gear assessed



  1. The key groove of the camshaft timing gear assy is shifted to the straight pin of the camshaft as shown in the figure.

  2. Press the camshaft timing gear ASSY lightly to the direction of the arrow in the figure (left rotation), and press further when the key structure and the straight pin match.

    ■ Attention ■
    Do not rotate the camshaft timing gear in the late angle direction (right rotation).

  3. Confirm that there is no gap between the camshaft timing gear Ass and the flange part of the camshaft.

  4. Tighten the flange bolt while being careful not to rotate the camshaft timing gear.

    Reference value
    T = 64 n { 653 kgf / cm }

    ■ Attention ■
    1. It is too tight with vise and does not hurt the camshaft.
    2. When bolts are tightened, do not fix the camshaft timing gear ASSY.
    3. If you lock at the latest angle position, release the lock pin in the camshaft timing gear and then tighten the bolt.

  5. After tightening, confirm that the camshaft timing gear ASSY rotates in the late angle direction (right rotation) and moves, and confirms that the camshaft timing gear Assicy locks at the movable end.
