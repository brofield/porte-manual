Installation of front brake

printing

List of preparation



1. Front disc brake cylinder mounting LH installation

  1. With two bolts, attach the front disc brake cylinder mounting LH to the steering natsukuru.

    Reference value
    T = 88 n ・ m {900 kgf / cm}



2. Lon desk brake bushswush boots installation

  1. Apply rubber grease to the two new front disc brake bush dust boots.

  2. Push two front disc brake bush dust boots into the front disc brake cylinder mounting LH and attach them.



3. Front disc brake cylinder slide pin mounting

  1. Apply rubber grease to the sliding portions and portions of two front disc brake cylinder slide pins.

  2. Push two front disc brake cylinder slide pins into the front disc brake bush dust boots and attach them.



4. Front disc brake pad support plate No.2 installation


5. Front disc brake pad support plate No.1 installation


6. Lon Dicek brake Patsudo stick mounting (patsudo only)



  1. Apply disk brake glass to the inside of the anti -squealsim No.2 and attach it to the disc brake pad along with the anti -Square Sim No.1.



7. Disc brake cylinder Assy LH installation

  1. Attach the front disc brake cylinder ASSY LH with two bolts.

    Reference value
    T = 34 N ・ M {347 kgf / cm}

  2. Connect the front -front frame -by hose to the front disc brake cylinder ASSY LH with the front brake Flexible hose union bolt via a new flexible hose gasket.

    Reference value
    T = 30 N ・ M {310 kgf / cm}



8. Brake fluid replenishment

  1. Brake fluid replenishment




    1. Stand up the brake fluid can on the reservoir.

      ■ Attention ■
      Make sure that the can has enough brake fluid.

  2. Master Cylinda Air

    □ Participation □
    If the master cylinder is detached or the reserve is empty, the air of the master cylinder is removed.


    1. Use Union Nut Wresten 10 to separate the two brake tubes from the brake master cylinder Assy.




    2. Slowly step on the brake pedal and hold that state.




    3. Close the tube hole with your finger and release the brake pedal.

    4. Release your finger, slowly step on the brake pedal, hold the tube hole again, release the brake pedal, and repeat it three or four times.

    5. Use the Union Nut wrench 10 to connect two brake tubes to the brake master cylinder asser.

      Reference value:
      T = 15N · m {155kgf · cm} (specified tightening torque) T '= 14n · m {143kgf · cm} (Union nut wrench and torque wrench 230F read)

  3. Brake -based system air removal

    1. Connect the vinyl tube to the buder plug.

    2. Press the brake pedal several times and loosen the buder plug while stepping on the brake pedal.

    3. When the brake fluid does not come out, tighten the bruder plug and put the brake pedal.

    4. Repeat until the air is mixed in the brake fluid.

      Reference value:
      T = 8.0N · m {82kgf · cm}
      T = 8.4n · m {86kgf · cm} (rear brake bridder plug)

    5. Similarly, remove the air from the brake line of each wheel.

  4. Brake fluid quantity inspection

    1. Check the amount of brake fluid, and replenish the brake fluid to the MAX position if necessary.
