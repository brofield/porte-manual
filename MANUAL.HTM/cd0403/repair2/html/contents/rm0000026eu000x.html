Brake (4WD) Notes

printing



    1. Be careful to replace each part properly.It may affect the performance of the brake system or cause danger when driving.Use the same or equivalent parts for replacement.


      2. When the brake system is maintained, keep the parts and surroundings clean.


        3. If the vehicle is equipped with a radio, etc., please refer to the precautions in the general theory.


          4. The replacement work of the steering sensor has an effect on SRS airbags.Before that work, be sure to check the precautions of the SRS airbag.
