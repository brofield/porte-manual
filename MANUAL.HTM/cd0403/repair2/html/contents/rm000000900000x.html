  1. Resistance inspection

    1. Use SST (Toyota Electrical Tester) to measure resistance between the injector terminals.

      SST
      09082-00030
      09083-00150

      Reference value
      13.45-14.15Ω (21 ° C)

      □ Participation □
      In other cases other than the reference value, replace it with normal parts.




  2. Inspection of injection

    ■ Attention ■
    Inspections are performed with attention to fire in ventilation places.


    1. Prepare a new fuel tube and remove only the connector.

      □ Participation □
      Part number 23901-21030




    2. Connect the SST and fuel tube connector to the fuel pipe (vehicle side).

      SST
      09268-41047 (90467-13001, 95336-08070)

      ■ Attention ■
      Pay attention to the handling of the fuel tube connector.




    3. Attach the O -ring to the injector.

    4. Assemble the SST into the injector.

      SST
      09268-41047 (09268-41110, 09268-41300, 90467-13001, 95336-08070)

    5. Set the injector to the female cylinder.

      □ Participation □
      Attach just the right size vinyl tube so that the fuel does not scatter.

    6. Activate the fuel pump.(See the point)




    7. Connect the SST to the injector.

      SST
      09842-30080

    8. Connect the SST to the battery, perform a fuel injection (15 seconds) 2-3 times to calculate the average value.

      Reference value

      Quantity
      		The difference between each injector
      47-58ml
      		11ml or less

      ■ Attention ■
      Switching is always done on the terminal side of the battery.

      □ Participation □
      In the case of outside the reference value, replace it with a normal part.




  3. Leak inspection

    1. When the SST is removed from the battery in the above state, confirm that there is no leak from the injector.

      Reference value
      1 drop or less/12 minutes
