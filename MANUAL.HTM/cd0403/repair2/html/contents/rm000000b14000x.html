Circuit explanation


  1. Airbag Warnin Gramp is built in the combination meter Assy.
  2. If the airbag system is normal, the Airbag Warnin Gramp turns on for about 6 seconds after turning on the IG switch and turns off automatically.If the airbag system has a problem, the airbag warnin gland will light or flash to inform the driver a defect.
  3. When the DLC3 TC-CG terminal is short-circuited, the airbag warnin group in the combination meter is turned on or flashed and the DTC is displayed.
  4. If there is a problem with the combination meter Ass, LA line wire harness, or the airbag sensor ASSY CTR, the airbag warnin group will be used.
