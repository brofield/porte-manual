  1. Power window regulator motor ASSY RH operation inspection

    ■ Attention ■
    Since the pulse sensor inside the motor is damaged, the voltage is not applied except for the 1 and 2 terminals of the motor.





    1. When the battery voltage is applied to each connector terminal, check that the motor operates smoothly.

      standard
      Measurement conditions
      		Direction of operation
      Battery Plus ← → 1 terminal
      Battery minus ← → 2 terminals
      		Round right to the drive axis
      Battery Plus ← → 2 terminals
      Battery minus ← → 1 terminal
      		Left rotation to the drive axis

  2. PTC (Power Wind motor protection function) operation inspection

    ■ Attention ■
    Perform a power window regulator, motor and door glass with the vehicle.


    1. Set the SST (Toyota Electrical Tester DC400A Prove) on the two -terminal wire harness of the connector of the power window regulator motor ASSY RH.The probe arrow is pointed at the motor.

    2. The driver's door glass is fully closed.

    3. After about 60 seconds, when the driver's seat power windows switch is operated on the UP again (first time), check the time until the current of the motor changes from about 16-34A to 1A or less.(Inspection inspection)

      standard
      About 4-90 seconds

    4. About 60 seconds after the inspection, operate the driver's power window switch to confirm that the door glass descends.(Return inspection)
