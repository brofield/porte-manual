Circuit explanation


The airbag sensor ASSY CTR is composed of G sensor, sapping sensor, drive circuit, diagnostic circuit, and ignition control.The airbag sensor ASSY CTR receives a signal from the G sensor and determines whether the airbag (horn button ASSY, instrument panel passenger airbag ASSY) and pliten shower (front seat outa belt ASSY RH and LH) will operate., Detect a problem with the airbag system.

When the connector of the airbag sensor ASSY CTR is a half -matched state, a half -checked pin or an airbag sensor ASSY CTR is detected, a diag code B1100/31 is recorded.
