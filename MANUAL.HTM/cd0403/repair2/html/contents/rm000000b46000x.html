Headlamp Damed Switch ASSY removal

printing


1. Remove the steering column cover



  1. Turn the steering wheel 90 ° left and right to remove two screws.

  2. Remove the lower screw and remove the steering column cover.



2. Remove headlamp demand switch ASSY

  1. Cut the connector.




  2. Press the claws in the figure, remove the bite, and remove the head lamp damed switch ASSY.

    ■ Attention ■
    Note that if you press it strongly, the claw will be damaged.
