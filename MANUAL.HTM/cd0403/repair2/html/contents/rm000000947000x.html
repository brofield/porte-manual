List of preparation



1. Coil spring RR LH installation

  1. Rear coil spring insulator LWR LH is attached to Axle Beam ASSY.




  2. Attach the coil spring LH lh to the end surface of the upper side and the rear coil spring insulator UPR LH stop.




  3. The identification paint is set to the lower and rear of the vehicle, and the rear coil spring LH is attached to the rear axle beam ASSY.



2. Shock absorber ASSY RR LH temporary tightening



  1. Jack supports the rear axle beam ASSY via a piece of wood.

  2. Gradually raise the jack, attach the shock absorber ASSY LH (lover side) to the rear axle beam ASSY, and temporarily tighten it with a nut via the rear shock absorbus pacer.




  3. Fix the two -sided width of the rear shock absorber ASSY LH and tighten the new nut to the standard value.

    Reference value
    15-18mm

  4. Fix the two -sided width of the rear shock absorber ASSY LH and tighten the new lock nut.

    Reference value
    T = 24.5 n ・ M {250 kgf / cm}

9. Skid control sensor wire connection



  1. At the bolt, attach the skid control sensor wire to the rear axle beam asser.

    Reference value

  2. Attach the connector to the rear axle hub.

    2. Skid control sensor wires do not twist and do not attach.

  3. □ Participation □

10. Calm down the vehicle

  1. Attach the rear tire.

    Reference value
    T = 103 n ・ m {1050 kgf / cm}

  2. The vehicle is jacked down and shakes up and down several times to calm the rear suspension.



11. Rear Axle Beam ASSY main closure

  1. Calm the vehicle.

  2. Equipped with weight (about 90 kg) in the center of the lagage room.




  3. Through a piece of wood, jack up the rear axle beam until the shock absorber becomes a reference value, and tighten the mounting bolt.

    Reference value
    T = 82 n ・ m {836 kgf / cm}

    Reference value
    Approximately 250mm

14.

  1. Brake fluid replenishment




    1. Stand up the brake fluid can on the reservoir.

      ■ Attention ■
      Make sure that the can has enough brake fluid.

  2. Master Cylinda Air

    □ Participation □
    If the master cylinder is detached or the reserve is empty, the air of the master cylinder is removed.


    1. Use Union Nut Wresten 10 to separate the two brake tubes from the brake master cylinder Assy.




    2. Slowly step on the brake pedal and hold that state.




    3. Close the tube hole with your finger and release the brake pedal.

    4. Release your finger, slowly step on the brake pedal, hold the tube hole again, release the brake pedal, and repeat it three or four times.

    5. Use the Union Nut wrench 10 to connect two brake tubes to the brake master cylinder asser.

      Reference value:
      T = 15N · m {155kgf · cm} (specified tightening torque) T '= 14n · m {143kgf · cm} (Union nut wrench and torque wrench 230F read)

  3. Brake -based system air removal

    1. Connect the vinyl tube to the buder plug.

    2. Press the brake pedal several times and loosen the buder plug while stepping on the brake pedal.

    3. When the brake fluid does not come out, tighten the bruder plug and put the brake pedal.

    4. Repeat until the air is mixed in the brake fluid.

      Reference value:
      T = 8.0N · m {82kgf · cm}
      T = 8.4n · m {86kgf · cm} (rear brake bridder plug)

    5. Similarly, remove the air from the brake line of each wheel.

  4. Brake fluid quantity inspection

    1. Check the amount of brake fluid, and replenish the brake fluid to the MAX position if necessary.
