Circuit description



  1. The platium filament is used for the intake airflow meter SUB-ASSY.The configuration includes a heater, a tempara chain and a control circuit consisting of a platium filament, and a control circuit.In the operation, a heater in the bypass flow path of the housing is cooled by the inhalation air, and the temperature change is used by the tempara chasensa.The circuit configuration is measured as an intake air flow meter that forms a bridge circuit and controls the heater (the potential difference between A, B), which controls the bridge circuit and maintains the heater at a fixed temperature.。
