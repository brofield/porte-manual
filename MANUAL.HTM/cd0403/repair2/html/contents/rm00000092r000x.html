Automatic Transx Axle ASSY precautions

printing



  1. Before disassembly, the sand, mud, etc. attached to the outside of the transxle are washed and detached so that they do not enter during the decomposition.


    1. Until the oil pan is removed, the oil pan side does not turn up because there is a risk of foreign substances in the valve bondy.


      1. When disassembling light alloy parts such as cases, it is lightly shocked with a plastic hammer without twisting with a driver or the like.


        1. During detachment and decomposition work, work is performed using bare hands or vinyl gloves.Great gloves and rings are not used to prevent mixed lint.


          1. The disassembled parts are always organized, tidy, and protected from dust.


            1. Each part is thoroughly washed before mounting, and after drying, apply Toyota genuine auto fluid type T-ⅳ.However, aluminum and rubber parts are not washed with alkaline drugs.In addition, O -rings, gaskets and rubber -quality parts of oil seals are never washed with washing oil (white gasoline, etc.).


              1. Apply Toyota genuine auto fluid type T-ⅳ on the sliding surface and rotation surface.


                1. When installing the O-ring and oil sealing ring, apply the Toyota genuine auto fluid type T-II and attach it so that it does not hurt.


                  1. Be careful not to damage the matching surface of the case because it may cause oil leakage.


                    1. Be careful with erroneous installation and missing items such as O -rings and snapplings.


                      1. The fluid to be injected uses the Toyota genuine auto fluid type T-ⅳ.
