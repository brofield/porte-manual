            4. When outside the call area

            5. When the line is crowded, such as when the line is crowded

            6. When the phone is broken

            7. When the phone is not connected

            8. When the battery level of the phone is insufficient

            9. When the power of the phone is off

            10. In emergency call with helpnet, during automatic regular inspection or manual maintenance inspection

            11. Other when the phone itself cannot be used

          2. When contracting for interrupt calls (catchphones) or three -way calls, use the interrupt call (catchphone) or three -way calls on the telephone itself.
