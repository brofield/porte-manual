Step 5Driving test

    ■ Warning ■
    Check the road safety carefully.

    ■ Attention ■
    1. Be sure to perform the engine check and adjust.
    2. Perform at an oil temperature (50-80 ° C) in the normal driving state.
    3. The air conditioner is turned off.


    □ Participation □
    Driving test list

    Inspection item
    		Inspection and confirmation guidelines
    D -range shifting function
    		Inspect the first speed ← → 2nd speed ← → 3rd speed ← → 4th gear in normal driving (general city driving).
    D -range shift shock level when running
    		Inspection of the shock level during each uploading shift in normal driving.
    The operation of transmitsuyon control control
    		In the 4 -speed speed range, the transmissing Tsushon control is turned ON ← → OFF, and check to shift from 4th speed ← → 3rd speed.
    Kickdown function
    1. Kick -down operations are performed with each gear and checking to downshift.
    1. Check the shock level when kicking down.
    The action of the engine brake
    1. While running with D-range 3-speed gear (about 40-50km/h), shift to D → 2 range, and inspect the engine brake effect in 2-speed gear.
    1. 2 While running in 2-speed 2-speed gear (about 20-30km/h), shift to 2 → L range, and check the engine brake effect in 1-speed gear.
    Gift point when the accelerator pedal is fully opened
    		Start with the D -range with the accelerator pedal fully open, and check that the upshift speed from 1st to 2nd speed is compatible with the shifting point.
    Lockup function
    		While driving on a flat road at a constant lock -up speed range, confirm that the engine speed does not change significantly when the accelerator pedal is broken lightly.
    Passing P range
    		Stop the vehicle on a slope (about 5 ° or more), shift to the P range, and check that the vehicle does not move when the parking brake is released.
    Unusual noise, vibration
    		Inspect the noise and vibration during driving and shifting.
    Family control function
    1. When climbing, make sure that the gear is fixed to 3rd gear.
    1. When the brake is acted at the time of falling hills, make sure that it is fixed to the 3rd speed gear.
    Leakage
    		After the driving test, check each part and check that there is no oil leakage.
