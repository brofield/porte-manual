Differential carrier ASSY RR (4WD) Installation

List of preparation



1. Installation of rear dialogue carrier gasket

  1. Use white gasoline to remove garbage and fats on the bonding surface of the rear axle housing assembly with the rear dialogue career Assembly.

  2. Apply a seal packing 1281 on both sides of the new rear dialogue carrier gasket, and attach a rear dial -housing carrier gasket to the rear axle housing Assembly.



2. Differential carrier ASSY RR installation

  1. Put a differential career ASSY on the jack, jack up, and attach it to the rear axle housing ASSY.

    ■ Attention ■
    1. Differential is a heavy object, so it is definitely supported by jack.
    2. Differential career does not damage the mounting surface when installing ASSY.




  2. Apply adheriv 1344 to the screw of the mounting bolt and attach a differential career ASSY with 10 nuts and spring washer.

    Reference value
    T = 31 n ・ m {316 kgf / cm}



3. Proposhaft ASSY RR installation



  1. Combine the combined mark of the interimide shaft asser and propeller shaft ASSY RR, and install the interimi -shaft ASSY with four bolts, washer and nuts each.

    Reference value
    T = 37 n ・ m {377 kgf / cm}

    ■ Attention ■
    1. Washer is attached to the bolt side.
    2. At the time of tightening, do not adhere to the washer and nuts.




  2. Combine the combined mark of the propeller shaft asser rr and a differential flange, and attach the propellashaft Assemia RR with four bolts, washer and nuts each.

    Reference value
    T = 35 N ・ M {357 kgf / cm}

    ■ Attention ■
    1. Washer is attached to the bolt side.
    2. At the time of tightening, do not adhere to the washer and nuts.



4. Lear Axle Housing End Gasket Installation (LH side)

  1. Use white gasoline to clean the backing plate and axle housing.




  2. Round the backing plate and attach the end gasket.

    ■ Attention ■
    1. Do not apply unreasonable power to the brake tube.
    2. Make the gasket cut off down.

  3. Set 4 bolts.



5. Learn axle housing end gasket installation (RH side)

□ Participation □
Install in the same procedure as the LH side.



6. Installation of rear axle shaft (LH side)



  1. Put the protrusion of the bearing retainer OUT downward, use SST, and insert the axle shaft into the axle housing.

    SST
    09520-00031

    ■ Attention ■
    1. Do not damage the oil seal.
    2. Do not damage the speed sensor rotor.




  2. With 4 nuts, attach the bearing retainer OUT to the backing plate.

    Reference value
    T = 66 N ・ M {670 kgf ・ CM}

12. Rear axle swing and axial rattling inspection

  1. Remove the rear tire

  2. Remove the rear brake drum

  3. Rear Axle Hub Hub Bearing Axial Ruts Inspection




    1. The dial gauge is set in the position of the rear axle hub, and the rattling direction of the rear axle hub bearing is inspected.

      limit
      0.05mm

      ■ Attention ■
      If it exceeds the limit, replace it with a new rear axle shaft bearing.

  4. Rear axle hub swing inspection




    1. Set the dial gauge in the position of the rear axle hub to check the swing of the rear axle hub.

      limit
      0.07mm

      ■ Attention ■
      If it exceeds the limit, replace it with a new rear axle shaft.

15. Adjustment of brake drums and lining gaps



  1. Turn the adjuster with a flathead screwdriver from the backing plate service hall, and let the brake shoe ASSY contact the rear brake drum.




  2. Turn the adjuster by turning the adjuster with another flathead screwdriver while pressing the rear brake automatic adjust lever LH with a flathead screwdriver.

  3. The brake shoe Assy is no longer in contact with the rear brake drum, and the adjuster is further reduced to contract to contract.



16. Speed ​​sensor RR LH installation

  1. Attach the speed sensor RR LH with bolts.

    Reference value
    T = 8.0 n ・ m {82 kgf / cm}

    ■ Attention ■
    1. When the sensor body is installed, the sensor is not damaged.
    2. Do not bite foreign matter.
    3. When installing, make sure that the sensor wire does not twist.

18. Differential oil replenishment

  1. Remove the rear axle housing film plug and gasket.




  2. Check that there is oil within 0-5 mm from the hole end of the rear axle housing fila plug.

    ■ Attention ■
    1. Excessive oil and underlying can cause trouble.
    2. Stop the vehicle on a flat road.

  3. If the amount of oil is small, replenish the oil after checking the oil leak.

  4. Tighten the rear axle housing fila plug via a new gasket.

    Reference value
    T = 49 n ・ m {500 kgf / cm}

    ■ Attention ■
    When the oil is changed, reconfirm the amount of oil after driving.
