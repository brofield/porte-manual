Function explanation


By combining S1 and S2 ON-OFF by the signal from the engine control computa, the gears from 1st to 4th gears are performed.If the solenoid is opened or short as a fail-safe function, the engine control compilation stops power to a failed solenoid and turns ON-OFF of other solenoids.
