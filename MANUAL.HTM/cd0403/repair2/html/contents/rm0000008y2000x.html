Circuit description


Platium filament is used for intake airflomor.The configuration includes a heater, a tempara chain and a control circuit consisting of a platium filament, and a control circuit.As for the operation, the heater in the bypass flow circle of the housing is cooled by the inhalation air, and the method of detecting the temperature change is used, and the circuit configuration is the heater and the tempara chasenser.The function of the powertressa (the potential difference between A, B), which is formed and controls so that the heater can be maintained at a fixed temperature, is measured as the output voltage of the intake air floometer.
