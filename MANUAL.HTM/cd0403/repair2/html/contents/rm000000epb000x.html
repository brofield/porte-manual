Column shift lever decomposition

printing

List of preparation



1. Remove the column shift lever

  1. Remove the wire guide and the wire harness clamp.




    1. Unlock the claws and separate the wire guide from the control shaft.




    2. Use a minus thin blade driver to remove the wire harness clamp.

  2. Remove the steering column bracket ASSY UPR.

    1. Use a drill to make a hole in which the reverse tap is standing on the steering rock set bolt.




    2. Use the reverse tap to remove the steering lock set bolt.




    3. Use a ping -punch and a hammer to remove the steering lock pin and remove the steering column bracket ASSY UPR and a column shift lever.

  3. Remove the bolt and remove the column shift shifting bell crank ASSY and harness connector bracket from the column shift lever.

  4. Remove the column shift control shaft.




    1. Use SST to remove the snap ring.

      SST
      09904-00010 (09904-00050)




    2. Remove the control shaft, spaca, wavesha and bush from the column shift lever.
