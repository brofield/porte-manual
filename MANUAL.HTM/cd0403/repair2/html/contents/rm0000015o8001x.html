ABS & BA system (4WD) function inspection with EBD

printing

List of preparation



1. Brake system display lamp initial check

  1. Turn on IG switch.

  2. Inspect the ABS Warning Lamp and the brake warnin gland lamps and turns off after about 3 seconds.

    □ Participation □
    If the lamp does not light up, check out the valve, the output terminal of the meter, and the wire harness between the brake actuator asser and the meter.



2. Brake actuator operation inspection

  1. Preparation before inspection

    1. Connect SST (Tascan) to DLC3 and start the engine.

      SST
      09991-70200

    2. Turn on the SST (Tascan) power, and select "Menu (Diagnosis)" → "Chassis" → "Abs / VSC" → "Active Test".

  2. Actuator motor operation inspection

    ■ Attention ■
    The motor does not drive for more than 5 seconds to protect the motor.If you want to operate continuously, you will have a space for more than 20 seconds.


    1. Turn on the motor relay.

      standard
      There is a driving noise of the motor

    2. Turn off the motor relay.

    3. Hold the brake pedal for 15 seconds while stepping on with a certain force.

      standard
      The pedal does not enter

    4. Turn on the motor relay while stepping on the brake pedal.

      standard
      There is no pulsation on the pedal

    5. Turn off the motor relay and release the brake pedal.

  3. Solenoid operation inspection

    ■ Attention ■
    1. Motors and solenoids are not driven for more than 5 seconds to protect motors and solenoids.If you want to operate continuously, you will have a space for more than 20 seconds.
    2. The active testing time of each solenoid is about 2 seconds and is automatically turned off.
    3. The brake pedal is not stepped on only the decompression valve alone.
    4. Do not turn on solenoids without instructions.


    1. Step the brake pedal and hold it as it is.

    2. Turn on SFRH and SFRR solenoids at the same time.

      standard
      The pedal does not enter

    3. Check the condition of the brake pedal when SFRH and SFRR solenoids are turned off.

      standard
      The pedal enters

    4. Turn on the motor relay.

      standard
      The pedal is returned

    5. Similarly, check the solenoids of other wheels.

      □ Participation □
      Front right (SFRH, SFRR), front left (SFLH, SFLR), rear right (SRRH, SRRR), rear left (SRLH, SRLR)
