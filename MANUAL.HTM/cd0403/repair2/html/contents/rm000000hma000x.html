Column Shift Parking Lock Cable ASSY vehicle inspection

printing

List of preparation



1. Column Shift Parking Lock Cable ASSY Function Inspection

  1. Inspect that the cable is not bent unnaturally.

  2. Inspect the ignition key to ACC and that the shift lever can be operated when the brake pedal is stepped on.

  3. Inspect that the shift lever cannot be operated except for the P -range, with the ignition key an ACC and the brake pedal is released.

  4. When the ignition key is lock, check that the shift lever cannot be operated other than the P range even if the brake pedal is stepped on.

  5. When the shift lever is shifted to a non -P range, check that the ignition key cannot be returned to the LOCK position.



2. Column Shift Parking Lock Cable Assembly Inspection

  1. Set the ignition switch to ACC and make the shift lever into P range.

  2. Inspect the parking lock cable associate near the cushion center of the brake pedal.




  3. Check the tip of the lock cable and the gap between the cushion of the brake pedal.

    Reference value
    0-1.8mm
