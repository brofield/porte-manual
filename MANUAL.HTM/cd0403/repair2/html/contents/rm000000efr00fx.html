List of preparation


Function explanation


If there is an conductor between DLC3 6 (Canh), 14 (CANL) ← → 16 (BAT),+B short of a can bus.


Symptoms
Suspicated part
There is a conduction between 6 (Canh), 14 (CANL) ← → 16 (BAT) of DLC3
  1. (1), (1*), (2), (2), (3), (3), (3), (4), (4), (5), (5)+B shorts
  2. ABS & Traction Actuator Assy (VSC ECU)
  3. Steering sensor (steering angle sensor)
  4. York -rate sensor (yaw / G sensor)
  5. Navigation receiver ASSY (navigation ECU)
  6. Wiring harness connector (can J/C)
