Brake Master Cylinder ASSY removal

printing

List of preparation



1. Brake fluid removal


2. Brake Master Cylinder SUB-ASSY

□ Participation □
By operating the brake pedal several times and removing the negative pressure in the brake booster, it is easy to remove the brake master cylinder ASSY.




  1. Use Union Nut Wresten 10 to separate the two brake tubes from the brake master cylinder Assy.

  2. Shift the two clips and separate the brake master cylinder reservoir No.1 and brake master cylinder reserved batum No.2 from the brake master cylinder union.




  3. Remove the two nuts and remove the brake master cylinder ASSY.
