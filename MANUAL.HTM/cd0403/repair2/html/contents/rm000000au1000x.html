Front axle hub LH decomposition

printing

List of preparation



1. Remove the front axle hub holes napping LH



  1. Use SST to remove the hole slap ring.

    SST
    09905-00013 (09904-00090)



2. Remove the front axle hub LH

  1. Fix the axle ASSY with a vise via an aluminum plate.

    ■ Attention ■
    Don't tighten your vise too strongly.




  2. Use SST to remove the axle hub.

    SST
    09520-00031




  3. Use SST to remove the hub bearing inner lace (outer side) from the axle hub.

    SST
    09950-60010 (09951-00370)
    09950-40011 (09951-04020, 09952-04010, 09953-04020, 09954-04010, 09955-04011, 09957-04010, 09958-04011)

    ■ Attention ■
    1. When the axle hub is damaged when removing the inner race, replace the axle hub with a new one.
    2. Apply oils and fats to the screw and tip of the SST center bolt (09953-04020).



3. Disc brake dust cover FR LH removed

  1. Remove the three bolts and remove the dust cover.



4. Remove the front axle hub bearing LH

  1. Attach the removed hub bearing inner lace (outer side) to the outer side of the hub bearing.




  2. Use SST and press to remove the hub bearing from the steering knuckle.

    SST
    09223-15020
    09950-60010 (09951-00630)
    09950-70010 (09951-07100)
    09387-02010
