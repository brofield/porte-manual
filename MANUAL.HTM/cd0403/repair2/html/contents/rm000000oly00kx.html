    1. When you replace either transmission ASSY, valve body ASSY, transmission wire, engine ASSY, engine control computer, perform [Attain AT learning value].
    2. AT learning value initialization is performed after 1 minute or more after the battery terminal connection.

    □ Participation □
    The learning value is not initialized (erased) just by removing the battery minus terminal.

    1. Confirm that the shift position is in the P range, connect the SST (Tascan) to the DLC3, and turn on the IG ON and SST power supply.

    2. AT learning value initialization implementation
