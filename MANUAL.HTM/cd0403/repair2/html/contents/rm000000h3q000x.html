1. Speed ​​sensor FR RH inspection
SST
09082-00030
09083-00150

  1. Inspect that there is no deformation and corrosion in the connector case and terminal.

    standard
    No deformation and corrosion

    □ Participation □
    If the connector on the vehicle harness side is abnormal, repair the connector.

  2. Use SST (Toyota Electrical Tester) to check the resistance between the connector terminals.

    Reference value
    1.60 ± 0.20kΩ (20 ° C)

  3. Use SST (Toyota Electrical Tester) to inspect the resistance value between the connector terminal and the hub bearing (body-up).

    Reference value
    10mΩ or higher
