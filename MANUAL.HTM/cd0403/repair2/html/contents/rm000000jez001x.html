1. Windglas [print type antenna] correction



  1. Clean the disconnected area with white gasoline.

  2. Stir the repair agent sufficiently, then apply a small amount to a small brush and apply it to the repair location.

  3. If the repair location is long, apply the masking tape and apply the repair agent.

  4. Remove the masking tape after 2-3 minutes.
