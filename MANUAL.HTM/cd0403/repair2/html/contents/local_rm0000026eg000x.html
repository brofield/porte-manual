Brake piping (4WD) Notes



  1. If you find a leakage of brake fluid for important security parts, be sure to disassemble the parts and replace it with a new one if there is any abnormality.

  1. When removing each brake configuration, cover the connection so that foreign substances such as garbage or dust do not enter.

  1. Do not damage the brake tube when removing it or deforms it intentionally.

  1. When attaching the grometto to the body, make sure that the brake tube is passing through the center of the gromet.

  1. When attaching a brake tube and flexible hose, make sure that there is no twist or break.

  1. If the base of the flexible hose does not match the bracket groove, twist the flexible hose at the minimum angle.

  1. Do not reuse the flexible hose mounting clip.

  1. Do not adhere to flexible hoses such as absorba oil or grease.

  1. When attaching the brake tube to the resin clamp, the brake tube is ensured to be inserted into the clamp and confirm that the brake tube is not rattled or bitten.

  1. When the brake tube and flexible hose are attached, make sure that they are not interfering with other parts.

  1. Do not adhere to the brake fluid on the painted surface such as a body.In the unlikely event that it is attached, wipe it off quickly and wash it with water.

  1. When attaching a brake tube clamp or away with a bolt, the brake tube may be deformed, so fix the clamp so that it does not turn and tighten the bolt.
