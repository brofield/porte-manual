    1. Diagnostic activation and cancellation

      ■ Attention ■
      After turning on the IG switch, there are items that cannot be inspected unless the diagnodes mode is launched after confirming that the map screen appears.

      □ Participation □
      There are two types of diagnostic startup methods.



      1. Starting method 1


        1. Make the vehicle stopped.

        2. Start IG Switch ACC, ON or Engine.




        3. Navigation receiver ASSY presses the "information" key, and operate the headlap Dimaswitch as OFF → Tail → OFF → OFF → Tail → OFF.




        4. When the diagnostic is started, the "service inspection" screen is displayed, and a service inspection is performed.




        5. Select the "Inspection Menu" and display the diagram inspection menu screen.
          Diag inspection menu screen display
          display
          		Content
          Service inspection
          		Collect system inspection+diag memory, and display the current and past test results of each connection device from the results of both sides.
          Display inspection
          		Display the screen for performing a unique inspection of the display part
          Navigation inspection
          		Display the screen for performing unique inspections of the navigation ECU part



      2. How to start 2


        1. Make the vehicle stopped.

        2. Start IG Switch ACC, ON or Engine.




        3. Press the "image quality / erasure" key of the navigation receiver ASSY to display the image quality adjustment screen.




        4. Touch the upper left and lower left of the image quality adjustment screen slowly, as in the upper left → lower left → lower left → lower left → lower left → lower left.




        5. When the diagnostic is started, the "service inspection" screen is displayed, and a service inspection is performed.




        6. Select the "Inspection Menu" and display the diagram inspection menu screen.
          Diag inspection menu screen display
          display
          		Content
          Service inspection
          		Collect system inspection+diag memory, and display the current and past test results of each connection device from the results of both sides.
          Display inspection
          		Display the screen for performing a unique inspection of the display part
          Navigation inspection
          		Display the screen for performing unique inspections of the navigation ECU part

      3. Method of unlocking


        1. The release method is turned off the IG switch.Or press the “image quality · erasing” key for 3 seconds or more.
