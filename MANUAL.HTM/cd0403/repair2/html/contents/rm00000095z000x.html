3. Inspection of brake drums and lining

  1. Apply chalk to the inside of the brake drum and rub with the brake shoe.

    standard
    There is no significant hit

  2. If the hit is bad, polished the brake shoe lining is polished.

    ■ Attention ■
    After the inspection, wipe off the chalk.



4. Wheel Cylindo Day Inspection

  1. Inspect that there is no damage inside the wheel cylindabose.

    □ Participation □

5. Rear drum brake installation inspection




  1. Inspect that each part is properly attached.

    ■ Attention ■
    1. Confirm that the wheel cylinder boots are not scratched and no oils and fats such as grease are attached.
