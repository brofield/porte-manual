List of preparation


Function explanation


If the resistor between 6 (Canh) and 14 (Canl) of DLC3 is 54Ω or less, a between CAN bus line is considered.


Symptoms
Suspicated part
The resistance between 6 (CANH) ← → 14 (CANL) of DLC3 is 54Ω or less
  1. (1), (2), (3), (4), (5) between the trains
  2. ABS & Traction Actuator Assy (VSC ECU)
  3. Steering sensor (steering angle sensor)
  4. York -rate sensor (yaw / G sensor)
  5. Navigation receiver ASSY (navigation ECU)
  6. Wiring harness connector (can J/C)
