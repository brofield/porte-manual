Inspection procedure

Step 1Pre -inspection



  1. Brake pedal height inspection

    □ Participation □
    The pedal height adjustment is performed after each inspection to identify the failed site.

    1. Operate the brake pedal and check for mechanical problems in the pedal operation.

    2. Check the height of the brake pedal.

      ■ Attention ■
      Inspect with the carpet flipped.

      Reference value
      181.9 ± 5mm




  1. Brake pedal play inspection

    1. After the engine is stopped, the pedal is stepped on several times, and there is no negative pressure in the brake booster.

    2. Inspect the play of the brake pedal.

      Reference value
      1-6mm




  1. Brake pedal left tracking.

    Reference value
    107mm or more (step force 500N {51kgf})

    ■ Attention ■
    Inspect with the carpet flipped.

  1. Wheel brake inspection

    1. Inspect that disc brake pads, brake shoes and return springs of each wheel brake are properly attached.

    2. Inspect buder plugs, union bolt looseness, gaskets, and fluid leaks from wheel cylinders.

  1. Brake fluid leak, air inspection inspection

    1. Check the fluid amount of the brake master cylinder reservoir.

    2. The brake pedal is repeated repeatedly to check that there is no discomfort (air entry) during pedal operation.

    3. Inspect the change in the fluid amount of brake master cylinder reservoir.

    4. When the fluid amount is reduced, check flexible hoses, brake tubes damage, unionut nut loosening, and fluid leakage due to defective master cylinder stickers.

  1. Brake -based system air removal

    1. The brake pipeline and the air out of the brake actuator are removed.
