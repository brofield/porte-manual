Front brake inspection

printing

List of preparation



1. Disc brake piston and cylinder inspection

  1. Inspect that there is no damage on the side and inside the disc brake piston.



2. Disc brake pad thickness inspection

limit:
1.0mm
Reference value:
11.0mm (when new)



3. Front disc brake pad support plate No.1 inspection

  1. Front disc brake brake cylinder mounting LH, cleaning the disk brake pad hitting surface with brake cleaners, etc. When the front disc brake pad support plate No. 1 is installed, looseness, deformation, cracks, rust, and difficult to remove it.Check for foreign substances.

  2. When attaching the disc brake pad, check that the front disc brake pad support plate No. 1 has spring power, and after installation, the front disc brake pad does not easily drop off.



4. Front disc brake pad support plate No.2 inspection

□ Participation □
Check the same procedure as the front disc brake pad support plate No.1.



5. Disk thickness inspection

limit:
20.0mm
Reference value:
22.0mm (when new)



6. Remove the front disc

□ Participation □
Attach the mark to the front disc and axle hub.



7. Installation of the front disc

  1. Match the front disk and the front axle hub matching mark and attach the front disc.

    ■ Attention ■
    When replacing the front disk, select the mounting position where the front disk swing is minimized.



8. Disk swing inspection

  1. Before checking the front disk swing, check the back of the front wheel bearing and the swing of the front axle hub.

  2. With a hub nut, fix the front disk.

    Reference value
    T = 103 n ・ m {1050 kgf / cm}




  3. Use the dial gauge to check the swing inside 10 mm from the outer periphery of the front disc.

    limit:
    0.05mm

    ■ Attention ■
    If the swing is exceeded, change the position of the front disk and axle hub so that the swing is minimized.Cut the front disk if the swing is reached even after changing the position of the front disk and axle hub.When cutting a disk, if the thickness exceeds the thickness limit of the front disk, replace the disk with a new one.
