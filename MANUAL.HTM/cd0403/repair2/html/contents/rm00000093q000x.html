1. Shock absorber ASSY FR LH abolition

  1. How to eliminate drilling

    1. The piston rod is extended and fix it diagonally with a vise.




    2. Use a gold saw or the like about 50 mm from the upper end of the shock absorber to drain the gas slowly.

      ■ Attention ■
      1. Gas that out is colorless, odorless, harmless.
      2. Drilling holes with a rag on the gold saw, as the cut powder may pop out due to the power of the gas.
