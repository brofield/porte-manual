3. Side slip inspection

  1. Check the side slip with a side slip tester.

    Reference value:
    0 ± 5mm (per 1 m)

    ■ Attention ■
    1. Entering the side slip tester is less than walking in the straight direction (about 4 km/h), and is performed in a neutral state.
    2. Do not step on the brake on the side slip tester.
    3. Do not perform steering operations on the side slip tester.

  2. In the case of outside the reference value, check the wheel alignment.



4. Toin inspection

  1. Shake the vehicle and stabilize the vehicle.

  2. Push the vehicle straight and move forward to move forward.

    ■ Attention ■
    When the vehicle retreats, be sure to move forward.




  3. The guideline of the toein gauge is set on the back of the tire according to the front wheel axis center height.

  4. The tread center is applied to the rear of each front tire, and the distance between the marks (B dimensions) is measured.

  5. Press the vehicle slowly to move forward and rotate the front wheels 180 °.

    ■ Attention ■
    Do not rotate 180 ° or more, and if it is over -rotated, start over from the place to set the toein gauge.




  6. The distance between the marks (A dimension) is measured in the front of the vehicle.

  7. Seeking to in.

    Reference value
    Toin 1 ± 2mm

    □ Participation □
    Tour-in = B dimension-A dimension



5. Tour -in adjustment



  1. Measure the length of the screw part of the left and right rack ends.

    standard
    Left and right difference 1.5mm or less

  2. Remove the rack boots clip.

  3. Loosen the tie rod end rock nut.

  4. If the left and right differences between the rack end screws are not the standard, make adjustments.

    1. If the measured value of the to -in is out of the out side, adjust the rack end with the shorter dimensions in the direction of extending.

    2. If the measured value of the to -in is out of the in -side, adjust the rack end with the longer dimensions.

    3. Check the toe -in again.

  5. Turn the left and right rack ends in the opposite direction of each and adjust so that the to -in enters within the reference value.

    Reference value
    Toin 1 ± 1mm

  6. Tighten the tie rod end rock nut.

    Reference value
    T = 47 n ・ m {480 kgf / cm}

    ■ Attention ■
    Fix the hexagon of the steering rack end so that the rock nut and the steering rack end do not turn around, temporarily tighten the lock nut, then fix the two sides of the tie rod end and tighten it.




  7. Use the pliers to attach the clip to the range of the figure.

    ■ Attention ■
    Turn the boots and do not attach them.



6. Wheel cut angle inspection



  1. Set the turning radius gauge.

  2. Inspect the cutting angle of the wheel.

    Reference value

    ■ Attention ■
    1. Perform the vehicle in an empty state (without spare tires and tools).
    2. Inspect using a brake pedal pressure or the like so that the front wheels do not rotate in an empty vehicle.
    3. Remove the stop lamp fuse so that the stop lamp does not turn on.



7. Camber, casters, king pin angle inspection
SST
09772-30010

  1. Remove the wheel cap or center ornament.

  2. Lift up the vehicle in a flat place.

  3. Attach a tire clamp compen seta (TCK-01).

    1. Turn the clamp dial of the tire clamp compen seta and spread the tire clamp arm.

      ■ Attention ■
      Be careful because the tire clamp arm is expanded too much to damage the body.




    2. Turn the dial, match the two lower claws to the wheel size, apply the lower part of the wheel rim to the upper side claw according to the wheel rim.

      ■ Attention ■
      The claw does not apply to the balance, and the wheel air valve is attached so that it is as high as possible.




    3. Turn the clamp dial, shrink the tire clamp arm and attach it to the tire.

      ■ Attention ■
      The tire clamp arm tip is applied to the tire groove, and the tire clamp compen seta is pushing firmly on the wheel.

    4. Attach the removal prevention wire to the wheel air valve.

  4. Correct the tire clamp compen seta.




    1. Turn the correction dial of the tire clamp compen seta and adjust it within the range of the Mecha Zero line (red line).




    2. Attach the camber caster king pin gauge to the mounting plate of the tire clamp compen seta and match the matching.




    3. Rotate the wheel a little, match the horizontal bubbles of the camber caster kinging pin in the center, adjust the caster bubbles to 0 ° with the adjusted screw of the camber caster kinging pin.




    4. Rotate the wheels 180 °, match the combination of the camber caster kinging pin Gauge and the tire clamp compen seta, and adjust the horizontal bubbles.(*1)




    5. The caster bubbles in the above state are read, and the correction dial of the tire clamp compen seta is turned so that the value is 1/2 of the value.(*2)

      ■ Attention ■
      Do not move the camber caster king pin gauge adjustment screw.

    6. Repeat the procedure (*1) and (*2), and even if the scale of the caster bubbles is 180 ° positively, the set of the camber caster kinging pin is completed if the wheel is 180 ° positively.

  5. Set the turning radius gauge and drop the vehicle.

  6. Inspect camber, casters and king pin angles.

  7. Remove the camber caster king pin gauge and tire clamp compen seta.

  8. Attach a wheel cap or center ornament.



8. Camber adjustment

  1. Jack the vehicle and remove the front tire.




  2. Remove the bolt and separate the speed sensor and flexible hose.




  3. Loosen the two mounting nuts on the shock absorbarower side.




  4. Move the lower side of the shock absorber and adjust the camber with the rubber of the bolt and the bolt hole in the steering knuckle.




  5. Tighten two nuts.

    Reference value
    T = 132 n ・ m {1350 kgf / cm}




  6. Attach a flexible hose and a speed sensor with bolts.

    Reference value
    T = 29 n ・ m {300 kgf / cm}

    ■ Attention ■
    Flexible hose and speed sensor are not twisted and do not attach.

  7. Attach the front tire.

    Reference value
    T = 103 n ・ m {1050 kgf / cm}

  8. Jack the vehicle, shake up and down several times, and calm down the suspension.

  9. Check the camber.

    ■ Attention ■
    1. Perform the vehicle in an empty state (without spare tires and tools).
    2. The left and right differences between camber and casters are within 30 '.

    □ Participation □
    The range that can be adjusted with a set bolt is 0.1 ° to 0.5 °.

  10. In the case of the reference value, the toe -in inspection should be performed.

  11. In the case of outside the reference value, jack up to remove the front tire, cut the speed sensor and flexible hose separately, and perform the following work.




    1. Remove two and two bolts on the shock absorbarower side.
