Circuit description


DTC is output when the engine is not started or has a long starting time, or if the engine is long, or if the start time is long, or stalled immediately after the engine is entered.

The situation when DTC is output can be used to check freeze -frame data using SST (Tascan).The freeze -frame data is a part of the engine operation state in the event of a defect, which is useful for troubleshooting.

In addition, since the start of the start of the gas missing is also detected, it is necessary to confirm whether there is a missing gas before work.

    1. If there is a freeze -frame data, but it is not reproducible and does not know the defect status


      1. If the engine speed of freeze frame data is 0 rpm (not cranking)

        □ Participation □
        Battery rises, engine friction large, starter bad.Alternatively, the breakdown of the crank angle sensor system is considered.

        1. If the battery voltage is less than 9 V, the engine friction may not be normal.

        2. If the battery voltage fluctuates during the cranking, it can be judged that it is actually cranking.When the engine speed is 0 RPM, a breakdown of the crank angle sensor and the engine control computer may be failed.



      2. If the engine speed of the freeze frame data is 100 to 250 RPM (cranking does, it does not explode)

        □ Participation □
        If the engine speed is 100 to 250 RPM (if there is no first explosion), it may be that the wiring or the fuel -based / fuel -based components may have a complete failure.


        1. Due to the water temperature sensor abnormality, the amount of fuel injection may be extremely large or decreased, and it may not be possible to start.



      3. If the engine speed of the freeze frame data is 250 RPM or more (first bomb, slow complete explosion)

        □ Participation □
        When the engine speed is 250 RPM or more (when the first explosion, a slow bombing), it is often difficult to identify the cause.

        1. Due to the water temperature sensor abnormality, the amount of fuel injection may be extremely large or decreased, and the start of the start may be poor.
        2. If the F/B learning value is shifted, there is a possibility that there is a lack of fuel supply due to the injector, the fuel pump, etc.

        3. If the cranking rotation speed is high, there is a possibility that the compression is removed due to the bite of the valve carbon.
