      □ Participation □
      1. The value of the data monitor is difficult to indicate a large difference in measurement, the difference in the measurement environment, or the change of the vehicle, etc., and a clear reference value (judgment value).Therefore, it may be a defect even in the standard value.
      2. For subtle phenomena such as breathing and rough idols, it is necessary to comprehensively judge from all items on the data monitor, using a method of comparing the same type of car under the same conditions.

      □ Participation □

      4. *2: The maximum value is shown in data monitors other than [Active Test].
