Circuit description


  1. Solenoid Relay supplies power to ABS Solenoids.
  2. Immediately after the IG switch is turned on, the relay is turned on if the solenoid is determined to be normal due to a self -diagnosis during the initial check, and the relay is turned off if the disconnection or shorts are detected.
  3. If the voltage to the Solenoid Relay (+BS) decreases from the DTC detection condition due to lack of output of an alternator or alternator, this code may be remembered.
