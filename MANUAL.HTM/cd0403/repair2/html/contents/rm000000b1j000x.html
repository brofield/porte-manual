Circuit explanation


The passenger airbag ignition circuit consists of an airbag sensor ASSY CTR and an instrument panel passenger airbag ASSY.When the operation conditions are satisfied, the instrument panel passenger airbag ASSY is activated.

When a disconnection is detected in the passenger airbag spark circuit (P+, P-line), the airbag sensor ASSY CTR is recorded a diag code B0106/54.
