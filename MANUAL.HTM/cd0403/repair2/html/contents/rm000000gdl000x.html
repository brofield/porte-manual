Speed ​​sensor FR LH removal

printing


1. Remove the front tire


2. Speed ​​sensor FR LH is removed

  1. Separate the speed sensor FR LH connector.

  2. Remove the grommet from the body.




  3. Remove the bolt A and B, and separate the two -speed sensor FR LH clamps from the body and the front shock absorber, respectively.

    □ Participation □
    Bolt B is tightened together with the brake flexible hose clamp.




  4. Cut the clip from the front shock absorber.

  5. Remove the bolt and remove the speed sensor FR LH.

    ■ Attention ■
    No foreign matter is not adhered to the speed sensor FR LH tip and mounting portion.
