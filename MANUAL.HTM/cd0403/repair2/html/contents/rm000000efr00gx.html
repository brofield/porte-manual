List of preparation


Function explanation


If there is an conductor between DLC3 6 (Canh), 14 (CANL) ← → 16 (BAT),+B short of a can bus.


Symptoms
Suspicated part
There is a conduction between 6 (Canh), 14 (CANL) ← → 16 (BAT) of DLC3
  1. (1), (1*), (2), (2*), (3), (3)+B shorts
  2. Steering sensor (steering angle sensor)
  3. Navigation receiver ASSY (navigation ECU)
  4. Wiring harness connector (can J/C)


Circuit diagram






Inspection procedure


■ Attention ■
  1. When performing a short -circuit inspection between Canh, Canl ← → BAT, cut the battery minus terminal before performing it.
  2. If the battery minor Star Minal is separated, the work is performed during the battery clear after work.(The point isreference)
