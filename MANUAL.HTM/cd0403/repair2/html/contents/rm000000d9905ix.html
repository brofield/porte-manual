    4. Precautions when the airbag and Priten Shoner are abolished (before operation)


      1. Absolutely not abolished in an untruffable state.(Be sure to operate using SST)

      2. The operation is performed in a flat place where outdoor safety can be ensured.Also, in residential areas, avoid as much as possible.

      3. Since the operation sound is quite loud, it is thoroughly informed that it will operate to the surrounding people in advance.

      4. When operating an airbag and a pliten shoner, it is more than 5m away from the airbag Preten Shoner using SST.

      5. Since static electricity may operate an airbag and a pliten shoner, it will take measures against static electricity in advance.

    5. Precautions when the airbag and the Priten Shoner are abolished (after operation)


      1. The temperature of the operated airbag pliten shonner is several hundred ° C depending on the part, so leave it for more than 30 minutes after operation.

      2. Do not apply water to the operating airbag and Priten Shoner.

      3. When handling the operating airbags and pliten shonor, wear dust protection glasses and gloves.

      4. The operated airbag and Priten Shoner are put in a durable and transparent plastic bag, sealed and discarded.

      5. After the work, be sure to wash your hands.

    6. Precautions for airbag storage


      1. If you take it off temporarily, such as during work, be sure to store it with the development surface up.

        ■ Warning ■
        When the airbag is operated for some reason, if the development surface is facing down, it may lead to a serious accident that is related to life.

      2. When storing, do not place things on the airbag or put the airbags.

        3. Electrical system



        1. Dress and detach of battery terminal


          1. When performing an electrical system, the battery minuser is separated in advance to prevent the burns of short circuit.

          2. When removing the battery terminal, turns off the engine switch and writing switch, completely loosen the terminal nut so that it does not twist.

          3. When the battery terminal is separated, a memory such as a clock and a diagnostic will be deleted, so check the contents in advance.

          4. The DVD navigation system remembers various memory and settings in about 70 seconds after IG OFF, so always cut off the battery minor stormy after IG OFF.(DVD navigation system equipped car)

          5. Since the HDD navigation system remembers various memory and settings in about 1 minute after IG OFF, the battery minor stormy is separated after 1 minute or more after IG OFF.(Car with HDD navigation system)

          6. There is a system that requires the setting work when removing the battery terminal, so be sure to implement it.




        2. Handling of electronic components


          1. The ECU cover or case cannot be opened.(If you touch the IC terminal, the IC may be damaged by static electricity)

          2. It does not impact on electronic components such as computers, sensors and relays.Replace if you drop it.

          3. Do not expose electronic components to high temperatures and humid.

          4. When cleaning the engine room with steam, be careful not to directly water the electronic components, air filters and emission -related parts.

          5. It does not touch the connector terminal because there is a risk of deformation and static electricity.

          6. No impact wrench is used when removing temperature switches and temperature sensors.

            1. Work in a well -ventilated, welding machine, grinder, drill, electric motor, and stove without fire.

            2. The vaporized fuel does not work near the pit that may be filled or near it.

          2. Dress and detach of fuel -based parts


            1. Prepare a fire extinguisher before the start of work.

            2. To prevent static electricity, the fuel changer, vehicles, fuel tanks, etc. are attached to earth rays, and water is watered so that the feet are not slippery.

            3. Electric equipment such as electric pumps and work lamps is not used because sparks may fly or become hot.

            4. Iron hammer is not used because sparks may fly during work.

            5. The fuel attached to the fuel is separated.



            5. Dress and detach of engine intake parts



            1. When foreign matters such as metal pieces are mixed into the intake path, it has a negative effect on the engine itself.

            2. When removing the intake parts, the removed intake parts and the opening of the engine side are closed with a clean wess or gum tape to prevent the contamination of foreign substances.

            3. When attaching a intake part, confirm that there is no contamination of foreign matter.

                              14. Vehicle with catalyst converter

                              1. If a large amount of unburned gasoline flows into the converter, the catalyst can be damaged.In order to prevent such a thing, keep the following precautions.


                                1. Use only lead -free gasoline.

                                2. If it is necessary to check the spark plug, remove the injector connector and stop the fuel injection.

                                3. When measuring the compression pressure of the engine, remove the injector connector and stop the fuel injection.

                                4. The fuel tank does not rotate the engine in almost empty state.When the engine is rotated, a misfire may occur and an extra load on the converter may be applied.
