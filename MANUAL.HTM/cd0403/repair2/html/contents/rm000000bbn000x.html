1. Cleaning Quota Window ASSY RH

  1. Remove the pin and spacer.(When the glass is reused)

  2. Use a scraper to cut off the remaining adhesive.(When the glass is reused)

    ■ Attention ■
    Be sure to leave a little adhesive.




  3. Clean the glass outer periphery with white gasoline.

    ■ Attention ■
    Clean the new glass.



2. Installation of quota window glass spacer

  1. Apply a dedicated primer to the spacer and the pin mounting part.

    ■ Attention ■
    1. Do not paint thickly.
    2. Install after drying (about 3 minutes).




  2. Attach a quota window glass spacer and pin to the position of the figure.

    □ Participation □
    1. Attach it to the position where the spacer and the pin are attached.
    2. Attach inside from the primer application range.



3. Quota window Assisted

  1. Body mounting face cleaning




    1. If the adhesive on the vehicle side is remarkably uneven, smooth with a knife.

      ■ Attention ■
      Be sure to leave a little adhesive.

  2. Work before glass installation

    1. Put the suction rubber on the glass.

    2. The glass is placed on the body, and the width of the glass is uniform (the gap between the body and the glass end surface is uniform).

    3. Put the gum tape etc. on the glass and the body panel part, and mark the match when installing it with a pen.(New article)

      □ Participation □
      In the case of glass reusing, check and modify the matching position.

    4. Take off the glass.

    5. Apply a dedicated primer to the part where the body surface on the vehicle is exposed (no adhesive remains).

      ■ Attention ■
      1. Do not paint thickly.
      2. Do not apply to the adhesive.
      3. Panel seams and spot welding positions are applied with special attention.
      4. Glue the glass after drying (about 3 minutes).



    6. Apply a dedicated primer to the glass adhesive as shown in the figure.

      ■ Attention ■
      1. Do not paint thickly.
      2. Apply the adhesive after drying (about 3 minutes).




    7. Set the adhesive on the sealant gun.



    8. Apply the adhesive into a bead as shown in the figure.

      □ Participation □
      Cut the nozzle tip as shown in the figure.




  3. Glass installation

    1. Use suction rubber to match the matching mark and attach the glass.

      ■ Attention ■
      Attach glass after the primer on the body side dries (about 3 minutes).

    2. Press the front of the glass lightly to completely adhere.

      □ Participation □
      Pressing power 98n {10kgf} or more

    3. Fix the adhesive protruding and deficiency with a spatula.

    4. Press the glass with a protective tape until the adhesive is cured.

    5. Extra adhesive is removed before hardening.

      □ Participation □
      For the minimum drying time and driving time after bonding, follow the instructions or instruction manuals of each adhesive manufacturer.
