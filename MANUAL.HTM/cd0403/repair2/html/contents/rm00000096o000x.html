  1. Explanation range of repair work

    1. This book is created based on the SAE standard J2008.

    2. If the repair work is roughly divided, it can be divided into three processes: [Diagnosis], [Dress, replacement, decomposition, assembly, inspection / adjustment work], and [Completed Inspection].

    3. This book describes [Diagnosis] in the first process (posted in the system title in each section) and the second process [Detention, replacement, disassembly, assembly, inspection, and adjustment work], and the third step.The explanation of [Completion Inspection] was omitted.

    4. In this book, the description of the following element work is omitted, but it will be implemented in the actual work.


      1. Jack and lift work.

      2. Clean and clean the removal parts to be performed as needed.

      3. Visual inspection.



    1. Preparation

      1. There are SST, tools, instruments, oils and fats and others to prepare before work, and they are listed as the preparation list section.However, among the preparation items, we have omitted the preparation items that are considered to be constantly in general maintenance shops such as Toyota Tool Stands, Jack, and Rigid Luck.
