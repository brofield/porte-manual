Circuit description


  1. It is detected when the fuel correction amount due to Kuunenhusensa, a catalyst, exceeds a certain value.
    There are two types of air -fuel ratio corrections: normal driving air -fuel ratio correction (hereinafter referred to as F/B correction) and driving status memory air -fuel compensation (hereinafter referred to as F/B learning).
    F/B correction is a fuel correction used to maintain the theoretical air fuel ratio, the engine control computer has a signal from Kuunen Hena, and the current air -fuel ratio is darker than theoretical aircraft fuel ratio (hereinafter rich (hereinafter rich).By receiving the state of the state or the thin (hereinafter referred to as lean) as a signal, the injection amount is reduced in the case of rich, and the injection amount in the case of lean has increased.
    F/B learning changes according to individual engine differences (caused by aging or changes in the usage environment) when F/B correction is performed for a certain period of time, and F/B correction is near zero.It is a control for approaching.
    If the fuel correction amount (the total of F/B correction values ​​and F/B learning values) exceeds a certain value and turns lean or rich, the engine control computer lights the check engine warnin group.
    In addition, when gas missing is caused, the fuel correction amount is+35 % or more, and if (2 trips) continuously, lean abnormality, P0171/25 may be output.
