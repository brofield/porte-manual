DTC P0617/43 Starter system (High)

List of preparation


Circuit explanation


While the engine is cranking, the starter operation signal is also transmitted to the STA terminal of the engine control computer.Starter operating signals are mainly used to dip the fuel injection when starting the engine.

Inspection site
P0617/43

  2. The STA signal was entered

  4. 1 trip
