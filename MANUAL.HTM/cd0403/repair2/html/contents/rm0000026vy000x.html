Remove the rear axle ASSY (4WD)

printing

List of preparation



1. Remove the rear tire


2. Speed ​​sensor RR LH separation

  1. Remove the bolt and separate the speed sensor RR LH.

    ■ Attention ■
    Do not adhere to the tip and mounting portion of the speed sensor.



3. Remove the rear brake drum

  1. Take off the rear brake drum.



4. Parking brake shoe strat set LH removed



  1. Use SST to remove the strat set.

    SST
    09703-30010



5.

  1. Remove the brake zeuling spring.



6. Remove the rear axle shaft



  1. Remove the four nuts from the service hall and separate the bearing retainer OUT and bearing retainer gasket from the backing plate.

  2. The bearing retainer OUT and the bearing retainer gasket are separated from the backing plate, and the backing plate is temporarily tightened with four nuts.

    □ Participation □
    Perform to prevent the backing of the backing plate.




  3. Use SST to remove the axle shaft.

    SST
    09520-00031

    ■ Attention ■
    1. The oil is quickly wiped off to prevent the oil attached to the shaft from getting into the bearing.
    2. Do not damage the oil seal with the shaft tip spline.
    3. Do not damage the skid control rotor.



7. Remove the rear axle shaft oil seal



  1. Use SST to remove the oil seal.

    SST
    09308-00010



8. Remove the rear axle housing end gasket removal

  1. Remove the four temporary nuts from the backing plate.

  2. Remove four bolts while supporting the backing plate.

    ■ Attention ■
    Do not give the brake tube an unreasonable power.




  3. Float the backing plate and remove the end gasket.

    ■ Attention ■
    Do not give the brake tube an unreasonable power.



9. Remove the rear axles yaft inner tenalite



  1. Shave a part of the inalite with a grinder, and use tagane and hammer to remove it.

    ■ Attention ■
    Does not hurt the axle shaft.



10. Remove the rear skid control rotor

□ Participation □
Remove in the same procedure as the rear axle shaft innerita.



11. Remove the rear axles yaft bearing



  1. Use SST and press to remove the bearing.

    SST
    09527-30010

    ■ Attention ■
    Do not drop the axle shaft.

  2. Remove the bearing retainer gasket.

  3. Remove the bearing retainer OUT.
