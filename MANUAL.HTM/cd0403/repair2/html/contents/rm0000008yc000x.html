Circuit explanation


The camshaft position sensor (G2 +signal) is composed of a pickup coil and a timing rotator provided in the Kamshijaft.When the camshiyaft rotates, the air gap on the timing rotor's protrusion and the pickup coil change, which changes the magnetic field, causing the power in the pickup coil.
