        3. Handling of electronic components


          1. The ECU cover or case cannot be opened.(If you touch the IC terminal, the IC may be damaged by static electricity.)

          2. It does not impact on electronic components such as computers, sensors and relays.Replace if you drop it.

          3. Do not expose electronic components to high temperatures and humid.

          4. When cleaning the engine with a steam, be careful not to directly water the electronic components, air filters and emission -related parts.

          5. It does not touch the connector terminal because there is a risk of deformation and static electricity.

          6. Use an impact wrench to remove or install a temperature switch or temperature sensor.



          4. Desidation of fuel -based parts

          1. Removal work place for fuel -based parts


            1. Work in a well -ventilated, welding machine, grinder, drill, electric motor, and stove without fire.

            2. Do not work in pits that may fill the vaporized fuel or near it.

          2. Dress and detach of fuel -based parts


            1. Prepare a fire extinguisher before the start of work.

            2. To prevent static electricity, the ground wire is attached to fuel changers, vehicles and fuel tanks, and water is watered so that the feet do not slip.

            3. Electrical equipment such as electric pumps and work lamps is not used because sparks may fly or become hot.

            4. Iron hammer is not used because sparks may fly during work.

            5. The fuel attached to the fuel is separated.




            5. Dress and detach of engine intake parts

            1. When a piece of metal is entered in the intake path, it has a negative effect on the engine itself and the turbo charger.

            2. When removing the intake parts, block the removed intake parts and the opening of the engine side with a clean rag tape or gum tape.

            3. When attaching a intake part, confirm that there is no mix of metal pieces.




              6. handling hose clamps

              1. Before removing the hose, check the depth of the hose and the clamp position so that the restoration can be performed reliably.

              2. Deformation and lumpy clamps are replaced with new ones.

              3. When reusing a hose, attach the clamp according to the hose clamp mark.

              4. The board spring clamp is applied by applying force to the direction of the arrow after installation.




                7. Moving communication equipment installation

                1. The antenna is attached to a place as far away as possible from the electronic system of the vehicle, such as a computer sensor.

                2. The antenna code is attached at least 20 cm away from the electronic system of the vehicle, such as a computer and sensor.

                3. Do not combine the antenna code and other wiring.The antenna code and other wires are separated as much as possible.

                4. For retrofit, it is securely attached according to the individual mounting guidelines.

                5. No high -output mobile communication equipment is attached.

                        11. Precautions for full -time 4WD car inspection and maintenance

                        1. Speedometer tester measurement

                          ■ Attention ■
                          1. Testers with the load setting mechanism cannot be used.
                          1. Inspection is performed on the front wheels.
                          2. Sudden start, sudden acceleration is performed.
                          3. The maximum speed is 60km/h or less.(50km/h or less when using a free roller)
                          4. The operation time is within 1 minute.


                          1. Ride the front wheels on the roller.

                          2. Free the rear wheels with a free roller or rigid rack.

                          3. Fix the vehicle with a rockchain.

                          4. Start the engine and gradually increase the vehicle speed with the D -range.

                          5. After the measurement is completed, the brake gradually decelates and stops.

                        2. Brake tester measurement

                          ■ Attention ■
                          1. Testers with load measurement mechanisms cannot be used.
                          2. High -speed brake tester cannot be used.


                          1. Enter the roller the wheels to be measured (front or rear wheels).

                          2. Make the shift position neutral.

                          3. Drive and measure the roller of the tester.

                            □ Participation □
                            If the dragging force is excessive, jacking up the left and right axis to check the rotation status of the wheels.If there is no abnormality, there is no problem due to the viscous torque of the biscask coupling.

                              1. 大量の未燃焼ガソリンがコンバータ内に流れると、触媒がダメージを受ける原因になる。In order to prevent such a thing, keep the following precautions.
