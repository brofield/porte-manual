  1. Automatic correction of the neutral point of the rudder angle

    1. If the steering wheel is cut to the left and right sides to the full side, and the rudder angle exceeds the maximum ruddered specification value of 15.0 ° or more, the steering angle is corrected.

      □ Participation □
      1. The correction in this case shows that if the memory value is 660 ° and the steering angle is 700 ° for example, the neutral point is 40 °.
      2. Even if the rudder angle exceeds the maximum ruddered spectrum value, the expected course will continue.



    1. Correction of shift of the rudder angle neutral point

      1. If the screen is displayed on the screen due to a removal of the battery terminal, a connector removal of a steering sensor, etc., "System Initialization" is displayed, and one of the following operations is performed to correct the shift of the rudder angle neutral point.


        1. Method 1: Cut the steering wheels on both sides of the left and right sides to correct the steering angle.

        2. Method 2: Driving as much as possible, curve, and less traffic congestion, and drive for more than 20 km/h or more for 5 minutes.Then stop the vehicle and check whether the neutral point has been learned.If you are not learning, run again.(Judging a straight state and learning the neutral point)

      2. Display due to causes other than shift of the rudder angle neutral


        1. If the mounting angle of the steering wheel is shifted, when the steering wheel is straight, the width extension and the expected course shift.

        2. When the camera optical axis adjustment is not correctly adjusted, the width extension and the expected course shift.

          1. Inspection / adjustment



            1. Preparation before adjustment


              1. Stop the vehicle with the steering wheel straight.

              2. Install the adjustment target bar at the position of the figure.(Only when adjusting the camera optical axis)

                Reference value
                1250mm (a)

                □ Participation □
                1. The adjustment target bar is a tape with a width of about 2 cm and a length of 2m on the floor.
                2. Before stopping the vehicle, be sure to move the vehicle back and forth to confirm that not only the steering wheel but also the tire is straight.




            2. Diag activation


              1. Start the engine.

              2. Start diagnostic.(The point isreference)

              3. Select "Camera Inspection" on the diagram test menu screen.




            3. Red angle adjustment


              1. Confirm that the steering wheel is in a position where the vehicle goes straight (almost ± 5 degrees or less), and press "Red angle in the steering angle".

                □ Participation □
                1. Since there is no need to adjust the steering angle when removing and replacing the television camera ASSY, select "Next" on the steering angle adjustment screen.
                2. Even if only the memory of the rudder angle neutral point is performed, it is not memorable without the "next" ... "completed" and the diag inspection screen is completed.

              2. After remembering the steering angle, turn the steering to the left and press the "maximum steering angle memory".Similarly, turn the right to the right and press "Maximum Hudtual Memory".At the time of being pushed, the left and right rudder angle memory is made.

                □ Participation □
                "Next" is not accepted even if you touch it until you have a maximum steering angle memory and the maximum steering angle memory.




            4. Camera optical axis adjustment


              1. Press the rotating switch A and B to rotate C so that it is parallel to the adjustment target bar.

              2. Press up, down, left and right switches to move C up, down, left and right, so that the target bar for adjustment is entered the center of D.

              3. Select "Next"




              4. A (indicates [1210mm] from the bumper edge) and confirm that the adjustment target bar is almost overlapped.Also, when the steering is straight, confirm that the expected course is straight (overlapped with the extension of the vehicle width).

                □ Participation □
                Confirm that the displayed vehicle type matches the actual vehicle.If it is different from the actual vehicle, set the vehicle type again.

              5. Select completion, return to the diag test menu and finish adjustment.

                □ Participation □
                Due to the adjustment completion and the speaker not connected, pressing "Finish" sounds for 3 seconds.

            5. Adjustment confirmation


              1. When the maximum rudder angle memory is performed, the screen is confirmed on the screen that the predictable course is linked to the end of the steering wheel after the diag test is completed.

                □ Participation □
                When the expected course does not move before the steering is cut to the left and right, the maximum steering angle is not remembered correctly, so adjustment is required again.
