Throttle body ASSY single inspection

printing


1. Throttle body ASSY



  1. Confirm that there is no rattling of the throttle valve shaft.

  2. Confirm that there is no clogging of each port.

  3. Confirm that the opening and closing of the throttle valve is smooth.

  4. Confirm that there is no gap between the throttle stop screw and the lever at the full closing of the throttle valve.

    ■ Attention ■
    The throttle stop screw does not adjust.
