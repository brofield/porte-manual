DTC P0560/96 Battery abnormality

printing

List of preparation


Circuit explanation


  1. Even during IG OFF, the battery power supply is supplied to the BATT terminal of the engine control computer, and it is used for diameter cord memory, freeze -frame data memory, etc.If an abnormality occurs in the BATT terminal system, the engine control computer detects a diag code.

Step 3Battery single inspection
SST
09082-00030

  1. Liquid inspection

    standard
    There is a liquid amount in the specified range and there is no liquid amount difference




  1. Compatible inspection

    1. Check the battery specific gravity of each cell.

      Reference value
      1.25 to 1.29 (liquid temperature 20 ° C)

      □ Participation □
      In case of outside the reference value, charge the battery.

  1. Voltage inspection

    1. In order to remove the charging part, turn the engine after running, turn it into IG ON (within 20 seconds) and turn on the electric device (headlight, blow motor, rear deffoga, etc.).

    2. IG OFF and turn off the electric device switch.

    3. The battery voltage between the terminals is measured using SST (Toyota Electrical Tester).

      Reference value
      1.25 to 1.29 (liquid temperature 20 ° C)

      □ Participation □
      In case of outside the reference value, charge the battery.

    4. Measure the battery voltage value during cranking.

      Voltage value
      Approximately 9.6V or more
