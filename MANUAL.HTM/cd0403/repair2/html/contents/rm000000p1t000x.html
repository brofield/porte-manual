Function explanation


  1. The skid control relay supplies power to ABS solenoids and TRC solenoids.
  2. After about 1.5 seconds after the IG switch ON, the relay is turned on, and the relay is turned off if the solenoid is disconnected or short by self -diagnosis at the start of the run.
  3. Solenoid Relay is built into a skid control computer in the brake actuator.
