1. Precautions at the time of inspection

  1. The wireless driver's tsurimort control operates only when satisfied with the following three conditions.

    1. All doors are closed.

    2. The key plate is not inserted into the Ignitsushi Jon Toshiro Tsukushilinda.

    3. Electric dwarch works normally.

  2. Wireless Door Tsukurimote control range

    1. The range of operation varies depending on the person who operates and how to hold it.

    2. Due to the effects of the body shape of the vehicle, the surrounding environment, etc., the range of operation may be narrowed, or a part that does not operate may occur.

    3. Because the weak radio waves are used, if the strong radio waves or frequencies used have noise, the range of operation may be narrowed or not operated.

    4. When the battery is exhausted, the range of operation becomes narrower or not operating.

      □ Participation □
      Leaving the door control (electrical key) transmitter in a direct sunlight, such as on a dashboard, the battery life may be shorter or a failure.



2. Vehicle initial state (satisfied condition for wireless operation)

  1. The key is not inserted into the Ignitsushi John Tirotsu Tsukushilinda.

  2. All doors are closed.(The door warning is off)

  3. All doors are Lock.
