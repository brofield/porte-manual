C0215/34
  1. At the speed of 10 km/h or more, the speed sensor signal circuit disconnected or short state for 15 seconds or more
  2. Speed ​​sensor signal disconnection continues for 0.5 seconds or more
  3. When the instantaneous missing of the pulse signal from the speed sensor occurs more than 7 times
  1. Speed ​​sensor disconnection or short
  2. Speed ​​sensor mounting status or abnormality from the sensor rotor
  3. Wire harness disconnection or short
  4. Connector's incentive, terminal deformation, corrosion
  5. Foreign substance attached to speed sensor
  6. Sensor rotor abnormality (foreign substance adhesive, tooth loss)
  7. Speed ​​sensor circuit wiring resistance
  8. Brake actuita ASSY

  1. The speed sensor detects the wheel speed and sends a signal to the skirt control compilation.These signals are used for ABS control.
  2. A coil and magnet are built in the tip of the speed sensor, and when the sensor rotor is rotated, a magnetic voltage is generated in proportion to the rotation speed.
  3. By the frequency of this AC voltage, the skitt control compiler detects the speed of each wheel.
