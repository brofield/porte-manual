printing

List of preparation


Function explanation


If the resistor between 6 (Canh) ← → 14 (Canl) of DLC3 is 69Ω or more, a disconnection of the CAN bus main line and the DLC3 branch line may be considered.


Symptoms
Suspicated part
The resistance between 6 (Canh) ← → 14 (CANL) of DLC3 is 69Ω or more
  1. CAN bus main line or connector
  2. Wiring harness connector (can J/C)
  3. Steering sensor (steering angle sensor)
  4. DLC3 branch or connector
