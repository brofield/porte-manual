Rear brake degradation


  1. Remove the rear tire

  2. Brake fluid pull -out

  3. Remove the rear brake drum

  4. Remove the brake shoe (front)

  5. Rear brake automatic adjust lever LH removed

  6. Remove the brake shoe (rear)

  7. Rear brake parking brake shoe lever SUB-ASSY LH removed

  8. Remove the rear drum brake buder plug

  9. LH Fr or UPR Rear Wheel Brake Cylinder ASSY

  10. Rear Hoi-Lucilinda Cup Kit removed
