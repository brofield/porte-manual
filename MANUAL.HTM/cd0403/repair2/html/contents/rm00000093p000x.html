3. Side slip inspection

  1. Check the side slip with a side slip tester.

    Reference value:
    0 ± 5mm (per 1 m)

    ■ Attention ■
    1. Entering the tester is less than walking in the straight direction (about 4 km/h), and is performed in a neutral state.
    2. Do not step on the brake on the tester.
    3. Do not perform steering operations on the tester.

  2. In the case of outside the reference value, check the wheel alignment.



4. Toin inspection

  1. Shake the vehicle and stabilize the vehicle.

  2. Push the vehicle straight and move forward to move forward.

    ■ Attention ■
    When the vehicle retreats, be sure to move forward.




  3. The guideline of the toein gauge is set on the back of the tire according to the front wheel axis center height.

  4. The tread center is applied to the rear of each front tire, and the distance between the marks (B dimensions) is measured.

  5. Press the vehicle slowly to move forward and rotate the front wheels 180 °.

    ■ Attention ■
    Do not rotate 180 ° or more, and if it is over -rotated, start over from the place to set the toein gauge.




  6. The distance between the marks (A dimension) is measured in the front of the vehicle.

  7. Seeking to in.

    Reference value:
    0 ± 2mm

    □ Participation □
    Tour-in = B dimension-A dimension



5. Tour -in adjustment



  1. Measure the length of the screw part of the left and right rack ends.

    standard:
    Left and right difference 1.5mm or less

  2. Remove the rack boots clip.

  3. Loosen the tie rod end rock nut.

  4. If the left and right differences between the rack end screws are not the standard, make adjustments.

    1. If the measured value of the to -in is out of the out side, adjust the rack end with the shorter dimensions in the direction of extending.

    2. If the measured value of the to -in is out of the in -side, adjust the rack end with the longer dimensions.

    3. Check the toe -in again.

  5. Turn the left and right rack ends in the opposite direction of each and adjust so that the to -in enters within the reference value.

    Reference value
    0 ± 1mm

  6. Tighten the tie rod end rock nut.

    Reference value
    T = 47 n ・ m {479 kgf / cm}

    ■ Attention ■
    Fix the hexagon of the steering rack end so that the rock nut and the steering rack end do not turn around, temporarily tighten the lock nut, then fix the two sides of the tie rod end and tighten it.




  7. Use the pliers to attach the clip to the range of the figure.

    ■ Attention ■
    Turn the boots and do not attach them.



6. Wheel cut angle inspection

  1. Set the turning radius gauge.




  2. Inspect the cutting angle of the wheel.

    Reference value:
    Inside (inspection range)
    		Outside (reference)
    39 ° ± 2 °
    		33 °

    ■ Attention ■
    1. Perform the vehicle in an empty state (without spare tires and tools).
    2. Inspect using a brake pedal pressure or the like so that the front wheels do not rotate in an empty vehicle.
    3. Remove the stop lamp fuse so that the stop lamp does not turn on.



7. Camber, casters, king pin angle inspection

  1. Set the turning radius gauge.

  2. Remove the wheel cap or center ornament.




  3. Attach the front wheel alignment attachment and wheel alignment adapter to the front hub.




  4. Attach the tip of the center rod of the gauge according to the bolt center of the attachment.

  5. Inspect camber, casters and king pin angles.

    Reference value:
    Camber
    		caster
    		King pin angle
    -0 ° 45 '± 45'
    		2 ° 00 '± 45'
    		10 ° 30 '± 45'

    ■ Attention ■
    1. Perform the vehicle in an empty state (without spare tires and tools).
    2. The left and right differences between camber and casters are within 30 '.

  6. Remove the camber caster kinging pin and attachments.

  7. Attach a wheel cap or center ornament.



8. Camber adjustment

  1. Jack the vehicle and remove the front tire.




  2. Remove the bolt and front brake Flexible hose No.1 And the speed sensor FR is separated from the front shock absorber W/coil spring.




  3. Remove two mounting nuts on the shock absorbarower side.

  4. Temporarily attach the nut.




  5. Move the lower side of the shock absorber and adjust the camber with the rubber of the bolt and the bolt hole in the steering knuckle.

  6. Tighten the nut.

    Reference value
    T = 132 n ・ m {1350 kgf / cm}




  7. Bolt, front brake Flexible hose No.1 And the speed sensor FR is attached to the front shock absorber W/coil spring.

    Reference value
    T = 29 n ・ m {300 kgf / cm}

    ■ Attention ■
    Turn the speed sensor FR and do not attach it.

  8. Attach the front tire.

    Reference value
    T = 103 n ・ m {1050 kgf / cm}

  9. Jack the vehicle, shake up and down several times, and calm down the suspension.

  10. Check the camber.

    FF:
    -0 ° 45 '± 30'

    ■ Attention ■
    1. Perform the vehicle in an empty state (without spare tires and tools).
    2. The left and right differences between camber and casters are within 30 '.

    □ Participation □
    The range that can be adjusted with a set bolt is 0.1 ° to 0.5 °.

  11. In the case of the reference value, the toe -in inspection should be performed.
