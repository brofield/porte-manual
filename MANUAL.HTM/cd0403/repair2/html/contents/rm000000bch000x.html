Roof drip side finish molding LH removal

printing

List of preparation



1. Roof drip side finish molding LH removed



  1. Roof drip side finish molding Molding LH Put a protective tape on the body panel on the outer periphery.

  2. Use the roof molding rimover to remove the clip and remove the roof drip side finish molding LH.

    ■ Attention ■
    1. Do not remove the clip.
    2. If the clip is damaged, replace it with a new clip.
