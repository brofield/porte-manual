Proposhaft with Center Bearing ASSY (4WD) Remove

printing

List of preparation



1. Proposhaft with center bearing ASSY



  1. Attach the mark to the point in the figure, remove four bolts, washer and nuts, and separate the propeller shaft ASSY RR from the default flange.

    □ Participation □

    If the flange joint is hard, tip to one bolt and nut, use a brass bar and a hammer to hit the root of the joint yoke.




  2. Remove the four bolts and remove the Propellawiz Center Bearing Shaft ASSY.

    ■ Attention ■
    Do not damage the oil seal when removing it from the transfa.




  3. After removing the Propellawiz Center Bearing Shaft ASSY, the SST is inserted into the extension housing to prevent the leakage of the transfile.

    SST
    09325-20010

    ■ Attention ■
    Do not damage the oil seal.



2. Proposhaft ASSY



  1. Attach the mark to the point in the figure, remove the four bolts, the washer and the nuts, and separate the propeller shaft ASSY from the interimide shaft ASSY.



3. Propelline Tam Iight Sijaft ASSY



  1. Attach the mark to the point in the figure, remove the four bolts, washer and nuts each, and separate the interimide shaft asser from the propeller shaft ASSY RR.



4. Center Support Bearing ASSY No.1 Remove (Front)



  1. Use tagane and hammer to solve the nuts.

    ■ Attention ■
    Solve the nuts completely.




  2. Use SST to hold the universal joint flange (front side) and remove nuts and washer.

    SST
    09330-00021




  3. Confirm that the mark is marked with the flange and shaft.

    ■ Attention ■
    If it is difficult to confirm the matching mark, attach the mark according to the flange and shaft.




  4. Use SST to remove the universal joint flange (front side).

    SST
    09950-30011 (09951-03010, 09953-03010, 09954-03010, 09955-03030, 09956-03020)

    ■ Attention ■
    Apply oils and fats to the screw and tip of the SST center bolt (09953-03010).




  5. Remove the washer and the center support bearing ASSY No.1 (front).



5. Center Support Bearing ASSY No.1 Remove (rear)



  1. Use tagane and hammer to solve the nuts.

    ■ Attention ■
    Solve the nuts completely.




  2. Use SST to hold the universal joint flange (rear side) and remove nuts and washer.

    SST
    09330-00021




  3. Check if the mark is marked with the flange and shaft.

    ■ Attention ■
    If it is difficult to confirm the matching mark, attach the mark according to the flange and shaft.




  4. Use SST to remove the universal joint flange (rear side).

    SST
    09950-30011 (09951-03010, 09953-03010, 09954-03010, 09955-03030, 09956-03020)

    ■ Attention ■
    Apply oils and fats to the screw and tip of the SST center bolt (09953-03010).




  5. Remove the washer and the center support bearing ASSY No.1 (rear side).
