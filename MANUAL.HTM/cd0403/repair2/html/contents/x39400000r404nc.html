Circuit description




  1. E.F.I. The throttle position sensor is attached to the throttle body to detect the opening of the throttle valve.When the throttle valve is fully closed, a voltage of about 0.7V is applied to the VTA terminal of the engine control compilation.The voltage on the VTA terminal of the engine control compilation is larger in proportion to the opening of the throttle valve, and is about 3.5 to 5V when fully opened.The engine controlled compilation is determined the vehicle driving status from the voltage entered from the VTA terminal, consists of air -fuel ratio, fuel cut control, etc.
