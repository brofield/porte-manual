CAN communication system (without VSC) CAN bus GND short inspection

printing

List of preparation


Function explanation


If there is an conductor between 4 (CG) ← → 6 (Canh) and 14 (CANL) of DLC3, GND shorts of the CAN bus are considered.


Symptoms
Suspicated part
DLC3 4 (CG) ← → 6 (CANH), 14 (CANL).
  1. (1), (1*), (2), (2*), (3), (3*) GND shorts
  2. Steering sensor (steering angle sensor)
  3. Navigation receiver ASSY (navigation ECU)
  4. Wiring harness connector (can J/C)
