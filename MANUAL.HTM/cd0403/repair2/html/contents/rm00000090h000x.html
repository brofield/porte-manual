List of preparation



1. Exhaust front pipe ASSY installation

  1. Use caliper to measure the free length of the compression spring.

    limit
    40.5mm

    □ Participation □
    In the case of below the limit, replace the spring with a new one.




  2. The new gasket is gradually driven to the exhaust manifold, using a piece of wood and a hammer.

    ■ Attention ■
    1. There is no mistake in the mounting direction of the gasketto.
    2. The gasket that has been removed once is not reused.
    3. By assembling an exhaust pipe, you will never push the gasket.

  3. Attach the exhaust front pipe ASSY to the exhaust manifold.

    Reference value
    T = 43 n ・ m {440 kgf / cm}
