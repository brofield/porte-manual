List of preparation



1. Proposhaft ASSY inspection



  1. Inspect that the shaft tube is not damaged.

  2. Set a propeller shaft asser in the V block, and use a dial gauge to check the swing of the propeller shaft ASSY.

    limit
    0.8mm

    ■ Attention ■
    The dial gauge is attached to the center of the propeller shaft ASSY at a right angle.



2. Properaciah foot katsupling ASSY inspection



  1. Fix the interimide shaft ASSY to the vise as shown in the figure, and use a torque wrench to inspect the operating torque during the rotating coupling.

    ■ Attention ■
    Don't tighten your vise too strongly.

    Inspection item
    		standard
    Torque wrench rotation speed and operation torque change
    		Increasing the rotation speed of the torque wrench increases the number of operating torque.

  2. Inspect that there is no oil leak from the coupling part.



3. Center Support Bearing Assy No.1 Inspection



  1. Rotus from the center of Center Support Bearing ASSY No.1 while applying force to the outer periphery, checking that there is no crack or deformation in the bracket, the bracket.

    □ Participation □
    If there is an abnormality, replace it with a new center support bearing Assing No.1.



4. Procellash Yaft ASSY RR inspection



  1. Inspect that the shaft tube is not damaged.

  2. Set a propeller shaft Assassed rr on the V block, use a dial gauge to check the swing of the propeller shaft ASSY RR.

    limit
    0.8mm

    ■ Attention ■
    The dial gauge is mounted at a right angle to the center of the propeller shaft ASSY RR.



5. Universal joint spider ASSY inspection

  1. Confirm the propeller shaft asser and propellashaft ASSY RR to the vise via an aluminum plate.

    ■ Attention ■
    Don't tighten your vise too strongly.




  2. Rotate the spider part to check if there is no catch.

    □ Participation □
    If there is an abnormality, replace it with a new propeller center bearing shaft ASSY.




  3. Strong with both hands, the spider portion is shaken in the axial direction and a right angle direction to check if there is any play in the joint.

    □ Participation □
    If there is an abnormality, replace it with a new propeller center bearing shaft ASSY.
