Front axle hub LH assembly

printing

List of preparation



1. Lonxle hub bearing LH installation



  1. Use SST and press to press the new hub bearing to the end face of the steering knuckle.

    SST
    09950-70010 (09951-07100)
    09951-00700

    ■ Attention ■
    1. The hub bearing has a built -in oil seal, so the inner race is not removed.
    2. If the inner race is off, do not use the bearing.
    3. The grease attached to the new bearing is not wiped off.



2. Disc brake dust cover FR LH installation

  1. Attach the dust cover with three bolts.

    Reference value:
    T = 8.0N · m {82kgf · cm}



3. Front axle hub LH installation



  1. Use SST and press to press the axle hub into the steering knuckle.

    SST
    09950-70010 (09951-07100)
    09608-32010
    09951-00700



4. Lord axle hole hole napping LH installation



  1. Use SST to attach a new hole slap ring.

    SST
    09905-00013 (09904-00090)
