List of preparation



1. Radiator Grill W/Radiator Support Seal UPR

  1. Use the clip resover to remove the six clips.




  2. Paste the protective tape around and on the radiator support seal UPR and the radiator support seal UPR.



  3. Use a flathead screwdriver with a protective tape to remove the support seal behind the vehicle with the three claws in the figure.

    □ Participation □
    Put the flathead driver diagonally from the service hall, turn the driver like the arrow in the figure, and remove the claws.






  4. Lift the radiator support seal UPR and remove the radiator grilled claws from the gaps.

    □ Participation □
    Radiator support seal UPR is lifted on one side and restored when the claws are off.




  5. Check that the claws are off and remove the radiator grill.

    ■ Attention ■
    Note that the claws at the bottom of the radiator grill are easily damaged.

  6. Remove the radiator support seal UPR.



2. Remove the front fenda liner LH

  1. Remove the two screws and remove the front (part) front (part).

    □ Participation □
    Remove the screw as long as the front vampo cover can be removed.




  2. Rotate the clip 90 ° and remove it.



3. Remove the front fenda liner RH

  1. Remove in the same procedure as the front fenda liner LH.



4. Remove the front vampa cover



  1. Put a protective tape on the front fenda or bumper so that it does not hurt the vanper.

  2. Take two clips using the clip remover.

  3. Remove six screws at the bottom of the front vampa.

  4. Remove two bolts at the bottom of the front vampa.

  5. Demons the front vampkind part by hand, remove the tsus and remove the front vampo cover.

  6. Cut the fog lamp connector.(Car with fog lamp)

  7. Remove the front cover so that it does not hurt.

  8. Remove the two screws and remove the front vampaside bracket LH and RH.
