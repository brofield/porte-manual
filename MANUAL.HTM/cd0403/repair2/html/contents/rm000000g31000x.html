When the power slide door engine rotates or stops, it does not operate when the door control switch (front seat operation switch) is pressed.

Circuit explanation


The power slide door is controlled by the door control relay and drives the sliding door motor.

□ Participation □
Self -diagnostic mode inspection with a diagnodes system is performed, and this flowchart is performed when the code does not output.



Circuit diagram





Step 1Basic function inspection

  1. Activation condition at the time of the ignition switch

    1. Battery voltage 11V or more

    2. The electric door lock system operates normally

    3. At the time of the condition of either stepping on the shift lever P range or foot brake at a vehicle speed of 3km/h or less, or stepping on the parking brake

    4. Auto Slide Demon Switch ON (protruding)

    5. Ferrid closed (when fuel lid opening switch is turned on)

    6. The sliding door is Unlock (when the position switch is turned on)

    7. Power slide door sensor (touch sensor) disconnection detection

  1. Activation conditions during the ignition switch OFF

    1. Battery voltage 11V or more

    2. The electric door lock system operates normally

    3. Auto Slide Demon Switch ON (protruding)

    4. Ferrid closed (when fuel lid opening switch is turned on)

    5. The sliding door is Unlock (when the position switch is turned on)

    6. Power slide door sensor (touch sensor) disconnection detection

  1. Basic behavior

    1. When the sliding door is fully opened or fully closed, the door control switch or the door control switch (front seat operation switch or rear seat operation switch) is pressed, the buzzer is about 0.1 seconds (during the closed operation, after 0.1 seconds, it is running.Inspect that the sliding door is opened or closed.

      ■ Attention ■
      When the child lock protector is in Lock, the sliding door is open (assist) can be operated.
