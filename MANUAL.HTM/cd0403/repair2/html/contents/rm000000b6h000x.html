Remove Low Pitched Horn ASSY

printing

List of preparation



1. Remove the radiator grill

  1. Use the clip resover to remove the six clips.




  2. Paste the protective tape around and on the radiator support seal UPR and the radiator support seal UPR.



  3. Use a flathead screwdriver with a protective tape to remove the support seal behind the vehicle with the three claws in the figure.

    □ Participation □
    Put the flathead driver diagonally from the service hall, turn the driver like the arrow in the figure, and remove the claws.






  4. Lift the radiator support seal UPR and remove the radiator grilled claws from the gaps.

    □ Participation □
    Radiator support seal UPR is lifted on one side and restored when the claws are off.




  5. Check that the claws are off and remove the radiator grill.

    ■ Attention ■
    Note that the claws at the bottom of the radiator grill are easily damaged.

  6. Remove the radiator support seal UPR.



2. Remove the low pitched horn ASSY

  1. Cut the connector.




  2. Remove the bolt and remove the low pit horn ASSY.
