  1. Electric storage door mirror operation inspection

    1. Enter the ignition switch to ACC.




    2. When the left and right of the door mirror are the running position, when the storage switch is set to the storage position, it is confirmed that the left and right of the door mirror move to the storage position.




    3. When the door mirror left and right are the storage position, when the storage switch is set to the running position, it is confirmed that the left and right of the door mirror move to the running position.




    4. When the left and right of the door mirror are the running position, when the door mirror on one side is tapped forward by hand and the storage Suitichi is set to the storage position, it is confirmed that the door mirror left and right moves to the storage position.




    5. When the left and right of the door mirror are on the running position, when the door mirror on one side is defeated by hand and the storage switch is set to the storage position, it is confirmed that the door mirror left and right moves to the storage position.




    6. When the left and right of the door mirror are in the storage position, when the door mirror on one side is raised by hand and the storage switch is set to the running position, it is confirmed that only the other door mirror moves to the running position.

    7. When the door mirror is operated, the door mirror stops when the ignition switch is turned off, and the door mirror resumes the operation when the ignition switch is changed to ACC again.

      □ Participation □
      After stopping the door mirror with the ignition switch off, switching the storage swit and turning the ignition switch to an ACC, moving in the opposite direction.

    8. When the door mirror is stopped while the door mirror is operated, the door mirror stops, and when the storage Suit is switched, it will move to the opposite.
