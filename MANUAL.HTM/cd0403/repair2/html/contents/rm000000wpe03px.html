Circuit explanation


The stop lamp switch detects the operation state of the brake pedal and outputs it to the engine control computer.The stop lamp switch is attached to the brake pedal bracket and is connected to the engine control computer via the wire harness.The purpose of this circuit is to prevent engine stalls during rapid braking while driving.
