1. Headlamp manual leveling function inspection (car with halogen head lamp)

  1. The ignition switch ON, head lamp Dimaswitch ASSY is turned off → head position, and operating the head lamp leveling switch operates and the light axis of the headlamps changes.



2. Head lamp auto -leveling function inspection (car with discharge head lamp)

  1. Put about 60kg of weight on the vehicle lagge.

  2. When the head lamp Dimaswitch ASSY is turned off → HEAD position while the Ignition Switch ON and the vehicle stopped, the reflector is operated and the optical axis of the head lamp changes.



3. Illuminated entry system operation inspection

  1. The lamps that light up as the illuminated entry system are shown below.

    □ Participation □
    Room lamp ASSY No.1, map lamp ASSY

  2. When one door is opened, the lamp lights up and turns off after about 15 seconds after the door is closed.

  3. Before turning off about 15 seconds after closing the above door, lock the IG switch ON or the driver's seat door, check that it will turn off immediately.

  4. When the IG switch is OFF, the all doors and the driver's seat door are locked, and when the driver's seat is unlocked with the ignition key, the lamp lights up.

  5. When the IG switch is turned off, the lamp turns on when the door is closed and the driver's seat door is locked, and when the door is locked wirelessly.



4. Battery saver operation inspection

  1. Inspect that the lighting room lamp ASSY No.1 and the map lamp ASSY will turn off about 20 minutes after the ignition key is pulled out.

    □ Participation □
    Battery saver control is canceled when the Ignition key is inserted or opened and closed within 20 minutes after the lighting.



5. Light control computer inspection (car with discharge head lamp)

  1. If the light control computer is not damaged or the impact is applied but it lights up properly, a lighting diagnosis is performed in the following method, and it can be reused if all items are normal.

    1. Cold start (lighting from a state that has been turned off for more than 10 minutes), hot start (lighting for 15 minutes → turn off 1 minute → re -lighting) several times and lightly lit.

    2. The lighting status must be monitored from immediately after the cold start to the stable state (for about 5 minutes), and there is no unstable state such as flickering.

    3. The left and right headlamps are lit for about 30 minutes with the same valve attached, and the brightness has no clear difference on the left and right, and the lighting state is stable.
