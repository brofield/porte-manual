Television camera ASSY installation

printing


1. Television camera ASSY installation

  1. Assemble a television camera ASSY and a television camera wire cover with two screws.



2. Television camera ASSY installation

  1. Attach the television camera ASSY W/television camera wire cover and tighten with two bolts.

  2. Attach a connector and clamp.



3. Attach the backdoor trim board



  1. Found nine clips and attach the Batsukudo Atlim board.



4. Television camera ASSY optical axis adjustment



  1. Press the rotating switch A and B to rotate C so that it is parallel to the adjustment target bar.

  2. Press up, down, left and right switches to move C up, down, left and right, so that the target bar for adjustment is entered the center of D.

  3. Select "Next"




  4. A (indicates [1210mm] from the bumper edge) and confirm that the adjustment target bar is almost overlapped.Also, when the steering is straight, confirm that the expected course is straight (overlapped with the extension of the vehicle width).

    □ Participation □
    Confirm that the displayed vehicle type matches the actual vehicle.If it is different from the actual vehicle, set the vehicle type again.

  5. Select completion, return to the diag test menu and finish adjustment.

    □ Participation □
    Due to the adjustment completion and the speaker not connected, pressing "Finish" sounds for 3 seconds.
