Circuit explanation


The engine control computer uses two sensors attached before and after the three -format converter to monitor the purification of the catalyst.Kunen Hena sends a air -fuel ratio signal in front of the catalyst to the engine control computer.The oxygen sensor sends a signal of oxygen concentration after catalyst to the engine control computer.The engine control computer calculates the oxygen storage capacity (hereinafter referred to as OSC) based on the output voltage of the Kunen Hena and oxygen sensor, and detects catalytic deterioration.

The engine control computer performs active air -fuel control for about 30 seconds or more when driving after the engine warm -up.At this time, the engine control computer is forcibly adjusted the air -fuel ratio into lean or rich state.Here, if the lean/rich cycle of the oxygen sensor is long, the OSC will be large.

If the OSC value is less than the judgment value, the engine control computer is determined to have deteriorated the catalyst and lights the check engine warnin glander.

Confirmation driving
By performing a confirmation, you can confirm the reproduction of the diag code.

  2. Ig ON.
  3. Erase the diag code according to the screen display of SST (Tascan).(The point isreference)

Catalytic deterioration detection sensor output status confirmation




ST
09991-70201

  1. Connect SST (Tascan) to DLC3.(A)
  2. Ig ON.(B)
  3. Turn on the main switch of SST (Tascan).(C)
  4. Start the engine, turn off all accessories switches, and warm up until the engine cooled water temperature is stabilized.(Engine cooling water temperature is 75 ° C or higher) (D)
  5. For about 3 minutes, hold the engine speed at 2500 RPM.(E)
  6. After that, the engine speed is kept at 2500 RPM for 2 seconds, and then held for 2 seconds at 1500 RPM.(F)
  7. According to the screen display of SST (Tascan), the output voltage of [ECU Data Monitor] - [O2 sensor voltage B1S2] and [A/F sensor voltage B1S1] are confirmed.

□ Participation □
  1. If the output voltage of Kunen Hena or Oxygen Sensor does not fluctuate or has no noise, the sensor may be broken.
  2. When the output voltage of both sensors remains lean or rich, the air -fuel ratio may be extremely lean or rich.
  3. When the catalyst deteriorates, the output voltage of the oxygen sensor changes greatly even during normal driving.





□ Participation □
By implementing [fuel injection] in the active test, the problem can be identified.This active test can increase the amount of fuel injection of -12.5%or 24.8%.
