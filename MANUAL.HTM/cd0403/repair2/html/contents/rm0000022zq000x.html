printing

List of preparation



1. Hight control sensor SUB-ASSY RR RH inspection (drive system 2WD)



  1. Preparation before inspection

    1. Connect three batteries (1.5V) in series.

    2. Connect the battery plus to the 3 (SHB) terminal of the connector, connect the negative of the battery to the 1 (SHG) terminal, and apply 4.5V voltage between the 3 (SHB) ← → 1 (SHG) terminal.

  2. Voltage inspection

    1. Use SST (Toyota Electrical Tester) to check the voltage between 2 (SHRR) ← → 1 (SHG) terminals when the link is slowly raised and lowered and lowered with a voltage.

      SST
      09082-00030
      09083-00170

      standard
      Link angle (vehicle height)
      		Output voltage
      +45 ° (high)
      		Approximately 4.05V
      0 ° (standard)
      		Approximately 2.25V
      -45 ° (low)
      		Approximately 0.45V
