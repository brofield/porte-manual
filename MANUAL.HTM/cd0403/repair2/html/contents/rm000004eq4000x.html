Water pump ASSY car inspection

printing


1. Cooling liquid leak inspection

□ Participation □
  1. Before this inspection, confirm that there is no leakage of the cooling solution except for the water pump ASSY.
  2. This inspection is performed during the engine cold.

  1. Engine room visual inspection

    1. A large amount of cooling is attached to the V -belt lapping in the engine room and on the back of the hood.

    2. The liquid cooling solution is dropped around the water pump.

    3. The cooling amount in the reservoir tank is below the lower limit and there is a leak mark around the water pump.

      □ Participation □
      1. If any of the above is applicable, replace the water pump ass.
      2. If not applicable, perform the following inspection (inspection around the water pump).

  2. Inspection around the water pump

    □ Participation □
    Check the condition of the cooling solution (solid) attached to the drain plug of the water pump.


    1. Take off the V belt.(The point isreference)

    2. Take off the water pumpuri.(The point isreference)




    3. Press the paper toro towel on the drain plug of the water pump or the coolant (solid) attached to the drain plug edge and check the status of the paper towel.

      □ Participation □
      1. If the paper toro gets wet, replace the water pump ASSY.
      2. If the paper toro does not get wet, clean the area around the water pump.

  3. Attach the water pumpuri.(The point isreference)



2. Water pump ASSY inspection



  1. Turn the water pumpuri and make sure that the water pump bearing moves smoothly and quietly.

    □ Participation □
    If it is caught or has a strange noise, replace the water pump ASSY.

  2. Attach the V belt.(The point isreference)
