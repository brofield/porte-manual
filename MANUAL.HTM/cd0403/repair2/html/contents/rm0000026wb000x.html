Front suspension arm SUB-ASSY LWR No.1 LH (4WD) Remove

List of preparation


□ Participation □
The composition map isreference



1. Front suspension arm SUB-ASSY LWR No.1 LH inspection

  1. Jack up the front of the vehicle.




  2. Sprinkle up and down with the power of the hand (294N {30kgf} or higher) to confirm that there is no rattling in the ball joint.



2. Remove the front tire

  1. Take off the front tire.



3. Remove the front stabilizer bolt

  1. Fix the front stabilizer bolt with a spanner (10mm) and remove the two nuts.

  2. Remove four front stabilizer barceds reteners No. 1, 4 front stabilizer cushions, front stabilizer bar cushion retainer No.2 and front stabilizer bolt.




  3. The other side is removed in the same procedure.



4. Front suspension Menbarin horsement LH is removed

  1. Remove the two bolts and remove the front suspension Menbarin Housing LH.



5. Front suspension Menbarin horsement RH is removed

□ Participation □
The RH side is performed in the same procedure as the LH side.



6. Front suspension arm SUB-ASSY LWR No.1 LH



  1. Loosen the bolt (A).

  2. Fix the nut and loosen the bolt (b).

    ■ Attention ■
    1. Since the nut uses a nut with a protrusion for a rotation, make sure to turn the bolt and loosen it.
    2. The bolt is not removed just by loosening it.

  3. Remove the clip and castle nut.




  4. Use the SST to separate the ball joint of the rower arm from the steering knuckle.

    SST
    09628-00011

    ■ Attention ■
    1. Does not hurt the dust cover of the ball joint.
    2. Be sure to hang it with a string to prevent SST from falling off.




  5. Remove two bolts and nuts and separate the engine mounting insulator RR.




  6. Support the suspension cross member in mission jacks.

  7. Loosen the four bolts at the position in the figure.

    ■ Attention ■
    The bolt is not removed just by loosening it.




  8. Use a horn axis flash screwdriver to press the bottom of the mission with the suspension cross member as a fulcrum to secure a gap in the bolt (front side).

  9. Remove the bolt (a), (b) and nuts and remove the suspension rower arm.
