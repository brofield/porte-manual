Differency system (4WD) Notes



  1. Before disassembly, the sand, mud, etc. attached to the outside of the rear dial ASSY are washed, so that they do not enter the interior of the rear dial ASSY during disassembly and mounting.

  1. When disassembling the joints of light alloy parts such as the rear dialable carrier cover, it is lightly given by a plastic hammer with a driver or the like.

  1. The disassembled parts are always organized, organized, and protected from dust.

  1. Each part is well washed and dried before mounting, and then applied to Toyota genuine high pooid gear oil SX.Aluminum, rubber and ring gear set bolts are not washed with alkaline drugs.In addition, rubber material parts such as O -rings and oil seals are not washed with washing oil (white gasoline, etc.).

  1. To the sliding surface and the rotating portion, the Toyota genuine high pooid gear oil SX is sufficiently applied and installed.

  1. When fixing the parts to the vise, do not fix it directly to the vise, but always fix it through an aluminum plate.

  1. Be careful not to damage it, as it may cause oil leakage if you damage the case of the case.

  1. Before applying the seal agent, the old sealing agent attached to the seal is removed cleanly and the seal is washed with white gasoline.

  1. After attaching the seal part, leave the oil immediately and leave it for more than an hour or more.

  1. Be careful not to damage the oil sticker, O -ring, and the contact surface with the gasket, as it may cause oil leakage.

  1. Be careful not to damage the lip and outer periphery of the oil seal when the oil seal is pressed.

  1. When replacing the bearing, replace the outer race and the inner race as a set.
