Circuit explanation


A triple catalyst is used to efficiently purify CO, HC, and NOX in exhaust gas.The three -primary catalyst works most efficiently near theoretical air -fuel ratio.For this reason, if the engine is not accurately controlled near the theory air -fuel ratio, CO, HC, and NOX cannot be efficiently purified.Oxygen sensor is installed for this control.The oxygen sensor can accurately control the air -fuel ratio by using the characteristics that the output voltage changes suddenly when the oxygen concentration in the exhaust gas is discharged when normal combustion is performed near the aorial air -fuel ratio.The signal is sent to the engine control computer.When the air -fuel ratio becomes thinner, the oxygen concentration in the exhaust gas increases, and the oxygen sensor sends information that the air -fuel ratio is thin on the engine control computer (small power launch: <0.45V).As the air -fuel ratio becomes thicker, the oxygen concentration in the exhaust gas is reduced, and the oxygen sensor sends information that the air -fuel ratio is strong on the engine control computer (large power launch: 0.45V).The engine control computer determines whether the air -fuel ratio is strong or thin due to the electromotive signal of the oxygen sensor, and the injection amount is controlled.The oxygen sensor with a heater has a heater that warms the zirconium element, which is controlled by the engine control computer, and when the temperature of the exhaust gas is low (when the amount of inhaled air is small), the oxygen sensor cools down to operate accurately.Due to the heater to prevent it from being impossible.




Diag detection conditions
  1. Active air -fuel ratio control
    The engine control computer performs active air -fuel control for about 30 seconds or more when driving after the engine warm -up.At this time, the engine control computer is forcibly adjusted the air -fuel ratio into lean or rich state.Here, the deterioration of the trio catalyst and the abnormalities of the oxygen sensor are detected.

  2. Oxygen sensor voltage abnormality
    The engine control computer is forced to adjust the air -fuel ratio to lean or rich state during active air -fuel ratio.When a bug occurs in the oxygen sensor, the output voltage of the oxygen sensor changes smaller.
    When the output voltage of the oxygen sensor is output between 0.21 V and 0.69 V during the active air -fuel ratio control, the engine control computer is judged to be an oxygen sensor voltage abnormality and remembers P0136/27.Then light the check engine warnin gland.




  3. Oxygen sensor circuit disconnection or short
    The engine control computer is forced to adjust the air -fuel ratio to lean or rich state during active air -fuel ratio.
    In the case of the active air -fuel ratio control, despite the rich goal, if the output voltage of the oxygen sensor is 0.21 V or less (lean), the engine control computer determines that there is an abnormality in the oxygen sensor circuit, and it remembers P0137/27.Then light the check engine warnin gland.
    If the output voltage of the oxygen sensor is 0.69 V or more (rich), despite the active air -fuel ratio control, the engine control computer determines that the oxygen sensor circuit has an abnormality, and it is memorized P0138/27.Then light the check engine warnin gland.

Inspection procedure


□ Participation □
By implementing [fuel injection] in the active test, the problem can be identified.This active test can increase the amount of fuel injection of -12.5 %or +24.8 %.


ST
09991-70201

  1. Connect SST (Tascan) to DLC3.
  2. Start the engine, turn off all accessories switches, and warm up until the engine cooled water temperature is stabilized.(Engine cooling water temperature is 75 ° C or more)
  3. Hold the engine speed at 2500 RPM for about 3 minutes to warm each sensor.
  4. According to the SST (Tascan) screen display, select [Active Test] - [fuel injection amount], and select [ECU Data Monitor] - [A/F sensor voltage B1S1] and [O2 sensor voltage B1S2].
  5. Idling and read the output voltage of Kunenshena and oxygen sensor when the fuel injection amount is increased or decreased by the active test.
