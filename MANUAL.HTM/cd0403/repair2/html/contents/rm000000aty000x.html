Front axle hub LH installation

printing

List of preparation



1. Lonxle ASSY LH installation

  1. Attach the Roi Arm ASSY to the front axle ASSY LH and tighten it with a castle nut.

    Reference value
    T = 98 n ・ M {1000 kgf / cm}

    ■ Attention ■
    Do not adhere to the screws and tapered portions.

  2. Attach a new clip.

    ■ Attention ■
    The clip holes after tightening the castle nut are performed in the tightening direction within 60 °.




  3. Attach the axle ASSY to the shock absorber ASSY, insert two bolts from the front of the vehicle, and tighten two nuts.

    Reference value
    T = 132 n ・ m {1350 kgf / cm}

    ■ Attention ■
    Apply the engine oil to the screw and seats of the nut only when reusing bolts and nuts.



2. Tie rod end sub-assy LH installation

  1. Attach the tie rod end to the steering knuckle and tighten with a castle nut.

    Reference value
    T = 49 n ・ m {500 kgf / cm}

    ■ Attention ■
    Do not adhere to the screws and tapered portions.

  2. Attach a new cottal pin.

    ■ Attention ■
    Castle nuts are performed in a higher tightening direction within 60 ° after tightening.



3. Stabilizer bar FR installation

  1. Attach 2 cushions retainer No. 1, 2 cushions and stabilizer bars in the direction of the figure, and tighten with a nut.




  2. Fix the stabilizer bolt with a spanner (10mm) and tighten a new nut.

    Reference value
    T = 18 n ・ m {184 kgf / cm}



4. Installation of front disc

  1. Mark the disc and axle hub and remove the disk.

    ■ Attention ■
    Do not adhere to fats and oils on the disk surface.



5. Front Dicek Brake Kiki ASSY LH Installation



  1. Attach the Daisk Brake Kiki ASSY to the steering knuckle with two bolts.

    Reference value
    T = 88 n ・ m {900 kgf / cm}



6. Lonxle shaft nut LH installation

  1. Use a soketsu trench (30mm) to attach a new hub nut.

    Reference value
    T = 216 N ・ M {2200 kgf / cm}

    □ Participation □
    When tightening, step on the brake pedal.



7. Front axle swing and axial rattling inspection
(The point isreference)


8. Speed ​​sensor FR LH installation

  1. In the bolt, attach the speed sensor wire and flexible hose to the shock absorber ASSY.

    Reference value
    T = 29 n ・ m {300 kgf / cm}




  2. Attach the critip to the shock absorber ASSY.

    ■ Attention ■
    Do not install with a burden on the speed sensor wire.




  3. Attach the speed sensor FR to the steering knuckle with bolts.

    Reference value
    T = 8.0 n ・ m {82 kgf / cm}

    ■ Attention ■
    1. When the sensor body is installed, the sensor tip does not damage.
    2. Do not bite foreign matter.
    3. When installing, make sure that the sensor wire does not twist.



9. Lonxle shaft nut LH installation



  1. Use tagane and hammer to make a hub nut.
