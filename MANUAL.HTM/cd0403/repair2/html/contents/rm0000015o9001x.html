DTC C1249/49 Stop lamp disconnection

printing

List of preparation


Function explanation


  1. The skid control computer detects the state of the brake operation by inputting the stop lamp switch signal.
  2. The skid control computer has a disconnection detection circuit inside, and outputs this code when the stop lamp switch is disconnected during the stop lamp switch OFF or the disconnection on the stop lamp circuit earth side.


Dtcno.
DTC detection conditions
DTC output -related items
1249/49
The IG terminal voltage is 9.5-17.2V, and the stop lamp switch circuit (between A-B or A-Body Earth) continues for 0.3 seconds or more during the brake pedal opening (STP OFF).
  1. Stop lamp runs out, ground disconnection or resistance disconnection
  2. Stop lamp circuit-Brake actuator between wire harness disconnection
  3. Failure of connectors, deformation of terminals, corrosion
