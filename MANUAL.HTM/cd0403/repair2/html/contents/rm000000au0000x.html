Front axle hub LH removal

printing

List of preparation



1. Remove the front tire


2. Remove the front axle shaft nut LH



  1. Set SST according to the groove of the drive shaft, and use a hammer to solve the kashime.

    SST
    09930-00010

    ■ Attention ■
    1. Set the flat surface of the SST to the groove.
    2. Never cut the SST tip with a grinder.
    3. When removing the front axle hub nut, completely solves the kashime.
    4. It does not hurt the screws of the drive shaft.

  2. Use Soketsu trench (30mm) to remove the hub nut.



3. Speed ​​sensor FR LH separation

  1. Remove the bolt and separate the speed sensor wire and flexible hose from the shock absorber ASSY.




  2. Cut the clip from the shock absorber ASSY.




  3. Remove the bolt and separate the speed sensor FR from the steering knuckle.

    ■ Attention ■
    Do not adhere to the tip and mounting portion of the speed sensor.



4. Stabilizer bar cut separation



  1. Fix the bolt with a spanner (10mm) and remove the nut.

  2. Take two and two cushions and two cushions and separate the stabilizer bar.



5. Tie rod end sub-assy LH disconnection

  1. Remove the cotter pin and castle nut.




  2. Use SST to separate the tie rod end from the steering knuckle.

    SST
    09628-62011

    ■ Attention ■
    Does not hurt the dust cover of the ball joint.



6. Front Dicek Brake Kiki ASSY LH Cut off



  1. Remove the two bolts and separate the Dysque Brake Kikiki ASSY from the steering knuckle.

    ■ Attention ■
    The caliper is suspended with a string.



7. Remove the front disc

  1. Mark the disc and axle hub and remove the disk.

    ■ Attention ■
    Do not adhere to fats and oils on the disk surface.



8. Front axle ASSY LH separation

  1. Use a plastic hammer to lightly hit the tip of the drive shaft ASSY and remove the shaft and axle asser.

    □ Participation □
    If you are hard, use a brass bar and a hammer to hit the tip of the drive shaft.

  2. Press the front axle ASSY to the outside of the vehicle and pull out the drive shaft ASSY from the axle ASSY.

    ■ Attention ■
    1. Do not push the front axle ASSY out of the vehicle more than necessary.
    2. Do not damage the drive shaft outboard boots.
    3. The speed sensor harness clamp does not transform.
    4. Do not damage the speed sensor rotor.
    5. Drive shaft ASSY is suspended with a string.



9. Shock absorber ASSY FR LH separation



  1. Remove two bolts and two nuts, and separate the shock absorber Assesy from the axle ASSY.



10. Front suspension arm SUB-ASSY LWR No.1 LH disconnection

  1. Remove the cotter pin and castle nut.




  2. Using SST, Rewer Arm No.Separate the ball joint part of 1 from the steering knuckle.

    SST
    09628-00011

    ■ Attention ■
    1. Does not hurt the dust cover of the ball joint.
    2. Be sure to hang it with a string to prevent SST from falling off.
    3. Be sure to suspend it with a string to prevent the axlukiralia ASSY from falling off.
