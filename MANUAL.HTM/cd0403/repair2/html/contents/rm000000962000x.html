2. Brake system air removal

  1. Brake fluid replenishment




    1. Stand up the brake fluid can on the reservoir.

      ■ Attention ■
      Make sure that the can has enough brake fluid.

  2. Master Cylinda Air

    □ Participation □
    If the master cylinder is detached or the reserve is empty, the air of the master cylinder is removed.


    1. Use Union Nut Wresten 10 to separate the two brake tubes from the brake master cylinder Assy.




    2. Slowly step on the brake pedal and hold that state.




    3. Clean the tube hole with your finger and release the brake pedal.

    4. Release your finger, slowly step on the brake pedal, hold the tube hole again, release the brake pedal, and repeat it three or four times.

    5. Use the Union Nut wrench 10 to connect two brake tubes to the brake master cylinder asser.

      Reference value:
      T = 15N · m {155kgf · cm} (specified tightening torque) T '= 14n · m {143kgf · cm} (Union nut wrench and torque wrench 230F read)

  3. Brake -based system air removal

    1. Connect the vinyl tube to the buder plug.

    2. Press the brake pedal several times and loosen the breeder plug while stepping on the brake pedal.

    3. When the brake fluid does not come out, tighten the bruder plug and put the brake pedal.

    4. Repeat until the air is mixed in the brake fluid.

      Reference value:
      T = 8.0N · m {82kgf · cm}
      T = 8.4n · m {86kgf · cm} (rear brake bridder plug)

    5. Similarly, remove the air from the brake line of each wheel.

  4. Brake fluid quantity inspection

    1. Check the amount of brake fluid and replenish the brake fluid to the maxal position if necessary.
