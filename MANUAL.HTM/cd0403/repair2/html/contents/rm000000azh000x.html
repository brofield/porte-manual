Hita & Air Condition Syona System Pre -inspection

printing



  1. Before trouble shooting

    1. Check the battery voltage using SST (Toyota Electrical Tester).

      SST
      09082-00030

      standard
      10-14V (when the engine is stopped)

    2. Inspection of places that can be visually confirmed, such as running out of fuse, wire harness disconnection, short circuit, and poor connector connection.

    3. Warm the engine sufficiently.

    4. Inspect according to the how to proceed with trouble shooting.
