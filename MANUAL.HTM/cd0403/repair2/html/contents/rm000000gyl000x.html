printing


1. Transmitter battery installation

  1. Combine (lithium battery (CR2032)] is attached on the plus side.

    ■ Attention ■
    1. Do not deform the terminal.
    2. Confirm that there is no twisting deformation in the O ring.

  2. Attach the cover.

    ■ Attention ■
    If the claws of the cover are not ensured, it may cause poor contact between the combination [lithium battery (CR2032)] and the terminal part.
