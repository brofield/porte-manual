Fuel pump (1NZ-FE (4WD)) Single inspection

printing

List of preparation



1. Fuel pump



  1. Resistance inspection

    1. Use SST (Toyota Electrical Testa) to measure resistance between terminals.

      SST
      09082-00030
      09083-00170

      Reference value
      0.2-3.0Ω (20 ° C)

  2. Operation inspection

    1. Put the battery voltage between the terminals and confirm that the motor rotates.

      ■ Attention ■
      1. Inspection is performed for a short time (10 seconds or less).
      2. The pump is released from the battery as much as possible.
      3. Switching is always done on the terminal side of the battery.
