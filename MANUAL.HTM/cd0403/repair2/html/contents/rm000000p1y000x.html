Function explanation


  1. Output these codes when the ferrous sensor tip, foreign substance attached to the sensor rotor and the rotation of the rotor.
  2. The adhesion of a foreign substance and the losing the rotor are determined by the abnormality of the waveform input from the sensor.
  3. It may be detected by the connector terminal of the speed sensor circuit or a defect of the wire harness.

Inspection procedure

Step 1Speed ​​sensor output waveform inspection

  1. Exit the connector of the actuator and confirm that there is no defect such as looseness.




  1. In Ig OFF, separate the brake actuator Asset Assy connector.



  1. Using an oscilloscope, wire 3 (FR +) ← → 17 (FR-), 18 (FL +) ← → 4 (FL-), 5 (RR +) ← → 19 (RR-), 20 (RL +), 20 (RL +)← → 6 (RL-) Check the waveform between the terminals.




    1. Osciroscope waveform

      standard
      The same voltage waveform is output to the left and right wheels, and no noise is mixed in the waveform.

      ■ Attention ■
      1. The higher the vehicle speed (wheel rotation), the shorter the cycle, and the output voltage amplitude increases.
      2. If the gap between the speed sensor and the rotor is large, the output voltage is lower.
      3. If a foreign substance adheres to the speed sensor, the output voltage of the corresponding wheel increases.

      □ Participation □
      1. Measurement terminal FR + ← → RR-, FL + ← → RL-, RR + ← → RR-, RL + ← → RL-
      2. Instruments set 1V/Div, 2ms/Div
      3. Measurement condition car speed is about 30km/h

  1. Sprinkle the connector and harness lightly to give a light vibration to check that the waveform does not change.

    standard
    There is no change in the waveform

    □ Participation □
    If the waveform changes due to vibration, it is considered that there is an abnormality in the connector or wire harness of the relevant part.
