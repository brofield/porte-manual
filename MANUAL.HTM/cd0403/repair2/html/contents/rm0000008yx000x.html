1. Throttle body body idol speed control valve ASSY inspection

■ Attention ■
  1. Since an IC circuit is built into the ISCV, and the duty signal from the ECU is converted into a drive signal, the resistance inspection and the operation point cannot be performed by itself.
  2. After the inspection, delete the diagnostic cord.

□ Participation □
When the ISCV system is defective (excluding sticking), a diag code is detected.

  1. Remove the throttle body body idol speed control valve.

    1. Separate the connector of the throttle position sensor and idol speed control valve.




    2. Remove the bolts and two nuts and remove the accelerator control cable bracket and throttle body ASSY.

    3. Take off the gasket from the intake manifold.




    4. Remove the three screws and remove the idol speed control valve and gasket.

  2. Operation inspection (initial check)

    1. Connect the wire harness of the vehicle to the ISCV alone.




    2. When the ignition switch is turned on, check the activation of the valve.

      standard
      Neutral (50%open) → Full closing → fully open → neutral (50%open)

      □ Participation □
      It operates within 0.5 seconds.
