List of preparation



1. Speed ​​sensor FR LH installation

■ Attention ■
Attach the wire harness of the speed sensor FR LH so that it does not twist.

  1. At the bolt, attach the speed sensor FR LH.

    Reference value:
    T = 8.0N · m {82kgf · cm}

    ■ Attention ■
    Confirm that the speed sensor FR LH tip and the mounting portion are not attached to the mounting portion.




  2. Attach the clip to the front shock absorber.




  3. In the bolt A and B, the speed sensor FR LH clamps are attached to the body and the front shock absorber, respectively.

    Reference value:
    T = 8.0N · m {82kgf · cm} (bolt A)
    T = 29n · m {300kgf · cm} (bolt B)

    ■ Attention ■
    Bolt B tightens the brake flexible hose clamp and the speed sensor FR clamp so that the brake flexible hose clamp is on the top.

  4. Pass the connector of the speed sensor FR LH through the engine room and attach the grommet to the body.

  5. Connect the speed sensor FR LH connector.
