Circuit explanation


  1. Airbag Warnin Gramp is built into the combination sijon meter ASSY.
  2. If the airbag system is normal, the Airbag Warnin Gramp turns on for about 6 seconds after turning on the IG switch and turns off automatically.If the airbag system has a problem, the airbag warnin gland will light or flash to inform the driver a defect.
  3. When the DLC3 TC-CG terminal is short-circuited, the airbag war Ninin Grand lamp in the combination synchon meter is displayed and the DTC is displayed.
  4. Combinated Syon Meter ASSY, LA Line wire harness, or airbag sensor ASSY CTR If there is a problem, the airbag warnin gland will be light.
