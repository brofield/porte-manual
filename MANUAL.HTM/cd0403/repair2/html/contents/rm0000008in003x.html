    1. Precautions when maintaining vehicle maintenance

    1. The maximum rudder angle setting work must be worked with the engine starting, so apply a parking brake, step on the brake pedal, make the shift lever in the P range, and be careful not to stop the vehicle out.

    2. When the battery terminal is removed, "System Initialization" is displayed, and it is necessary to correct the rudder angle neutral.(The point isreference)



      2. Precautions of configuration parts

      1. Television camera controller


        1. When each adjustment is not performed at the time of replacement, when the shift lever is made in the R range, "The initial setting is not made" is displayed.Therefore, it is necessary to set up the maximum steering angle and the neutral point of the steering angle and adjust the camera optical axis.(The point isreference)

      2. Steering sensor


        1. If "System Initialization" is displayed by replacing the steering sensor or attaching and detaching, etc., it is necessary to correct the scoring angle neutral point.(The point isreference)

      3. Television camera ASSY


        1. If the camera is shifted at the time of replacement or for some reason, the camera optical axis adjustment work is required.(The point isreference)

        2. If water droplets, snow, or mud are attached to the television camera ASSY lens, you will not get a clear image.In that case, wash with water and wipe off with a soft cloth.If the dirt is severe, wash it off using a neutral detergent.
