□ Participation □
If only the headlamp unit LH mounting portion is damaged, you can fix it in a cheap method by using a repair bracket.However, the headlamp ASSY LH body is not damaged.

  1. After cutting the damaged mounting portion, it is shaped with sandpaper.

    ■ Attention ■
    After roughly cutting the diagonal line, the head lamp bracket LWR LH is attached to the boss part, the interference part is cut little by little, and the shape can be installed.
