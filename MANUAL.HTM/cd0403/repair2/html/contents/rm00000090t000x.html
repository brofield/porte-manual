1. Cooling solution (Toyota genuine super LLC) extract

■ Warning ■
Do not remove the radiator kyatsup when the engine and radiator are hot to avoid the danger of burns.Liquid and steam pop out by pressure.

  1. Open the radiator kayatsu.

  2. Loosen the radiator drain kotsu plug and extract the cooling solution.



2. Cooling solution (Toyota genuine super LLC) replenishment

  1. Close the radiator drain kotsuku plug and injection of cooling water until overflowing from the radiator injection.[ * 1]

    □ Participation □
    The radiator -in -retto hose and the radiator outretzhose are compressed several times by hand and the water level of the radiator injection is added.

  2. Tighten the radiator kayatsu.

  3. Inject the cooling solution into the radiator reserve tank to the upper limit.

  4. Warm up the engine until the thermostatsu opens.

    □ Participation □
    Compress the radiator -inlets hose and outlet hose several times during warm -up.

  5. Stop the engine, wait for the cooling solution to cool, remove the radiator kyatsu and check the water level.

  6. If the water level is lowered, repeat from [ * 1].

  7. When the water level does not fall, adjust the cooling solution of the radiator reservoir battank.
