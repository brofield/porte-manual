1. Washer nozzle adjustment

  1. Washer zle adjustment adjustment




    1. Use a flathead driver to separate the washer nozzle as shown in the figure.

      ■ Attention ■
      Be careful not to damage the glass.




    2. Remove the washa nozzle from the washa hose.

      ■ Attention ■
      Washyanozzles cannot be reused.



    3. Select and replace the washa nozzle so that the landing position of the washer injection solution is within the reference range.
