Valve clearance inspection

printing

List of preparation



1. The engine Anda cover RH is removed


2. Remove the cylinder head cover No.2



  1. Remove the four nuts and remove the Cylinder Hetted Cover No.2.



3. Cut the ventilation hose



  1. Remove the clip and separate the ventilation hose from the cylinder head cover.



4. Ventilation hose No.2 disconnected

  1. Remove the clip and ventilation hose from the cylinder head cover.Cut 2.



5. Ignition coil No.1

  1. Remove the connector and bolt of Ignitsushiyon coil and remove the entire Ignitsushiyon coil.



6. Cylinder head cover SUB-ASSY



  1. Remove the bolts, nine and two nuts, and remove the cylinder head cover.



7. Valve clearance inspection



■ Attention ■
Inspection of valve clearance must be performed during the cold.

  1. No.1 Cylinder compressed death point set

    1. Rotate the crankshaft damper correctly and match the timing mark (notch) to the chain cover 0 °.

    2. Confirm that there is a timing mark of Kamshi Sayftting Timing Gear at the position in the figure.

      □ Participation □
      If the mark does not match, rotate the crankshaft and match it again.




  2. Inspect the valve clearance of the location in the figure.

    1. Use a chickness gauge to measure clearance between Valbrifta and Kamshijaft.

      Reference value
      IN 0.15-0.25mm (cold time)
      EX 0.25-0.35 (cold time)

      □ Participation □
      In other cases other than the reference value, record the clearance.

  3. The crankshaft is turned one rotation in the positive rotation direction, and the No.4 cylinder is compressed and a death point.




  4. Inspect the valve clearance at the point in the figure.

    1. Use a chickness gauge to measure clearance between Valbrifta and Kamshijaft.

      Reference value
      IN 0.15-0.25mm (cold time)
      EX 0.25-0.35 (cold time)

      □ Participation □
      In other cases other than the reference value, record the clearance.
