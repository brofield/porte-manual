    1. Discharge head lamp handling precautions


    1. When touching the high -voltage socket part of the disguise headlamp during headlap Dimaswitch HEAD, a high voltage of about 20000 V may occur instantaneously, leading to a serious accident.

    2. Measurements performed by connecting testers to the high -voltage socket part of the disguise headlamp will never be performed because high voltage may lead to serious accidents.

    3. When working on a disguise headlamp, perform the following conditions.


      1. A place where water does not hit (for preventing electric shock) such as rain.

      2. OFF the head lamps.

      3. Remove the battery terminal and disconnect the 4 -pole connector clamped in the headlamp ass (block the power supply to the headlamp light control computer ASSY).

    4. When working on the disguise headlamp, when lighting the valve, it is performed after the assembly is completely completed, and it will never be turned on when the valve is not attached.

    5. Do not turn on the discharge headlamps using a power supply other than 12V of the vehicle.

    6. If the disguise headlamps (especially the head lamp light control computer ASSY) have the following damage, replace them with a new one.


      1. If there is an obvious deformation of a computer (sled, twist, dent, dent, crack, cracking).(*)

      2. If there is a crack, hanging of the resin part of the connector, a high -pressure socket, or a deformation of the terminal part.(*)

      3. When the contact with the reflector ground ground due to transformation of the socket cover is unstable.(*)

      4. When high -pressure harness and power supply wire harness are scratched.(*)

        □ Participation □
        If it breaks down even if it is temporarily operated, the fail -safe function works and the lighting is interrupted.

    7. If the headlamp light control computer ASSY does not damage the appearance, such as (*), or if the impact is applied, the lighting diagnosis is performed in the following method.As a result of the diagnosis, it can be reused if all items are normal.


      1. Cold start (lighting from the light turned off for more than 10 minutes), hot start (lighting for 15 minutes → 1 minute lighting → re -lighting) several times, and it can be reused if you light up reliably.

      2. Monitor the lighting state from immediately after the cold start to the stable state (for about 5 minutes), and reuse if there is no unstable state such as flicker.

      3. With a valve with the same use time, light the left and right headlamps for about 30 seconds, then reuse if the lighting condition is stable and the lighting state is stable.Judge it is possible.

    8. The light emission color of the discharge headlamp changes as the usage time passes.

    9. If only one side is replaced, there may be left and right differences in the light color.



      2. Precautions when replacing the valve of each lamp

      1. The discharge head lamp and the halogen lamp become hot when used, so the life of the valve becomes shorter when fats and fats are attached to the valve surface.When replacing the valve, hold the flange part and do not touch the glass part.

      2. Discharge head lamps and halogen lamp valves have high pressure inside the glass ball, so if they drop, hit, or scratch, they may be damaged and the glass may splatter.

      3. If you leave the valve removed for a long time, the lens may have garbage or moisture, so the replacement of the valve will be replaced after preparing a new valve.

      4. The valve to be replaced is always the same standard and wattage.

        ■ Attention ■
        The discharge headlamp always uses a D4R valve or D4S, and does not light up when using a D2R valve.

      5. If only one side is replaced, there may be a difference between the left and right brightness and light emission color.

      6. The poor mounting of the socket can be obtained after replacing the valve, as the lens may be cloudy and water.

      7. The used discharge head lamp valve is discarded without cracking, as the glass fragments may scatter and cause injury.

      8. The discharge head ramp valve cannot be seen whether it is bad visually.Therefore, the inspection is judged by replacing it with a discharge head lamp valve that lights up normally and lighting up.
