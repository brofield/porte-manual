List of preparation



1. E.F.I. Throttle position sensor

  1. Resistance inspection

    1. Separate the connector of the throttle position sensor.




    2. Use SST (Toyota Electrical Tester) to measure the resistance between 1 (VC) ← → 2 (E2) terminals.

      SST
      09082-00030
      09083-00150

      Reference value:
      2.5-6.0kΩ

    3. When the throttle lever is fully opened from the full closure, the change in resistance between the terminals is measured 3 (VTA) ← → 2 (E2).

      standard:
      With the opening of the throttle lever, the resistance increases proportionally.

      □ Participation □
      Throttle valve condition
      		resistance
      Fully closed
      		0.2-5.7kΩ
      At the time of fully open
      		2.0-10.2kΩ
