1. Torque converter ASSY inspection
SST
09350-32014 (09351-32010, 09351-32020)



  1. Torque converter one -way clutch inspection

    1. Insert the SST (tool) into the status shaft.

    2. SST (stopper) is inserted from the sleeve part of the torque converter and matched the star thread washer cutting.

    3. Stand a torque converter and turn the SST (tool).

      standard
      Rotate lightly when turning to the right and lock when turning to the left.

  2. Torque converter ASSY judgment




    1. If the results of the torque converter apply to the standard content, replace the torque converter.

      standard
      Metal sound is made in the stall test or the torque converter at the time of neutral.
      One -way clutches are free or locked in both directions.
      Fine flour is mixed in the auto fluid or more (see limit sample).

      □ Participation □
      1. The limited edition of the auto fluid -in -flour flour is an auto fluid from the torque converter that has been removed about 25 ml (35 mm film case) into a share.
      2. For the limited edition of the auto fluid, refer to the "Notes bundled in the rebuilt A/T" or the "A/T rebuilt product handling manual (part number 63264)" issued in January 1998.

  3. Auto fluid exchange in the torque converter

    1. When the auto fluid is odorous, stir the auto fluid in the torque converter well and remove the auto fluid with the mounting surface up.

  4. Cleaning inspection of oil cooler and oil pipeline




    1. When the torque converter is inspected and the auto fluid exchange is performed, the oil cooler and pipeline are cleaned.

      □ Participation □
      1. 196kpa {2kgf/cm from the inlet hose2Blow and clean the compressed air of｝.
      2. If a large amount of fine powder is mixed in the auto fluid, inject a new auto fluid with a bucket pump and clean it again.

    2. If the auto fluid is cloudy, check the oil cooler (radiator).

  5. Prevention of torque converter deformation and oil pump gear damage prevention

    1. If there is an interference mark on the tip of the torque converter mounting bolt and the bottom of the torque converter mounting hole, replace the bolt and torque converter.




    2. The mounting bolt length is all the same (within 0.5 mm) (there is no missing Spring washer).



2. Drive plate & ring gear SUB-ASSY inspection

  1. Drive plate inspection




    1. Check the swing of the drive plate using the dial gauge.

      Reference value
      0.20mm

      □ Participation □
      In the case of outside the reference value, replace the drive plate & ring gear.

  2. Ring gear inspection

    1. Inspect the status of the ring gear.

      □ Participation □
      If it is damaged, replace the drive plate & ring gear.
