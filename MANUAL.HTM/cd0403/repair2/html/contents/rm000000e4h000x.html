DTC B/L Outside air sensor circuit

printing

List of preparation


Circuit explanation


  1. The thermista asser (outside air sensor) is attached to the front of the condenser to detect the outside air temperature and control the "auto" of the heater and air conditioner.The resistance value of the thermistor ASSY (outside air sensor) changes due to changes in the outside air temperature, the lower the outside air temperature, the greater the resistance value, and the higher the outside air temperature, the lower the resistance value.The Center Cluster Module Switch outputs a voltage (5V) to the thermista asser (outside air sensor), and reads changes in voltage due to changes in the resistance value of the thermista ASSY (outside air sensor).
