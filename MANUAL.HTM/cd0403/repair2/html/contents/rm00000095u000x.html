Assembling front brake

printing

List of preparation



1. Front disk brake bleeder plug temporary tightening


2. Front disk brake bleeder plug cap mounting


3. Attach a piston seal

  1. Apply rubber grease to the entire circumference of the new piston seal and securely attach it to the groove of the front disc brake cylinder ASSY LH.



4. Install the front disc brake piston

  1. Disk brake piston is applied thinly on the outer periphery of the piston and attach it to the front disc brake cylinder ASSY LH.

    ■ Attention ■
    Don't forcibly screw the front disc brake piston.



5. Installation of cylinder boots

  1. Apply rubber grease to the new cylinder boots and attach it to the front disc brake cylinder ASSY LH.

    ■ Attention ■
    Surely attach cylinder boots to the disc brake cylinder ASSY LH and front disc brake piston groove.




  2. Use a minus thin blade driver to attach a new front disc brake piston set ring.

    ■ Attention ■
    Does not hurt cylinder boots.
