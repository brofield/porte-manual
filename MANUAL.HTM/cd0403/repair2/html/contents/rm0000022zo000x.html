Headlamp assembly

printing


1. Head lamp leveling motor LH installation

  1. Rotate the motor and attach the head rampral baling motor LH.




  2. Rotate the headlamp leveling motor LH and attach it to the position shown in the figure.



2. Headlamp No.1 valve installation (halogen headlamp)

  1. Attach the headlamp valve No.1 with a set spring.

    ■ Attention ■
    Do not touch the glass part of the valve.



3. Installation of headlamp socket cover (halogen headlamp)




  1. Attach the head lamp socket cover to the lamp bodie.

    ■ Attention ■
    1. Push the outer periphery of the socket cover until it hits the lamp bondy.
    2. Push the inner circumference of the socket cover (around the valve) until the valve bracket part can be confirmed all over the circumference.



4. Installation of headlamp socket cover (discharge headlamp)

  1. Attach the head lamp socket cover.



5. Installation of headlamp gasket (discharge headlamp)

  1. Attach the head lamp gasket.



6. Headlamp light control computer installation (discharge headlamp)

  1. Connect the connector.





  2. Attach the head lamp light control computer ASSY LH with four screws.

    □ Participation □
    If there is no gap between the gaps to be attached, the left and right adjustment of the screw is left and right to make the gap between the left and right.If it is difficult to install only for left and right adjustment, rotate the up and down adjustment of the Eming screw.

    ■ Attention ■
    If you turn the Eming screw more than necessary, the reflector may interfere with the headlamp bondy or damage the Eming screw.




  3. Attach the reflector ass. with three screws.



7. Discharge headlamp valve installation (discharge headlamp)



  1. At the set spring, attach the discharge head lamp valve.




  2. Connect the socket of the light control computer.



8. Head lamp back cover No.1 installation (discharge headlamp)



  1. Combine the claws and attach the No. 1 head lamp back cover.



9. Installation of head lamp socket cover (to discharge)



  1. Connect the connector.

  2. Turn the socket and attach the head lamp socket cover.



10. Clearance lamp valve installation

  1. Attach a clearance lump valve to the valve socket.



11. Clearance lamp socket installation



  1. Turn the valve socket and attach a valve socket and a clearance lamp valve integrally.



12. Lord -turn signal lamp valve installation

  1. Attach the front turn signal lamp valve to the valve socket.



13. Lord -turn signal lamp socket installation



  1. Turn the valve socket and attach the valve socket and the front turn signal lamp valve together.
