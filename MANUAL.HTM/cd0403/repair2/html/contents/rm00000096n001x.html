          Simulation method of defective phenomenon

          □ Participation □
          The most difficult thing in troubleshooting is that there is no problem.In such a case, it is necessary to thoroughly analyze a customer analysis from the customer, and it is necessary to reproduce it in the same situation and environment as when a bug occurs in the customer vehicle.If you carry out trouble shooting without confirming the problem, you will miss the important points in the repair work, misunderstanding, and eventually get stuck.For example, as long as the engine is hot or the defect caused by a vibration caused by the road condition during the driving road, where is the bug?It cannot be identified.Vibration, heat, or water (humidity) intrusion is a cause of non -reproducible defect, but the defectation reproduction method introduced here is effective in giving external factors to the stopped vehicle.It is.
          Important points of defective simulation tests:
          In the defective phenomenon reproduction method, the defect phenomenon should be confirmed, but it is also necessary to find the range and part of the defect.In order to perform these, narrow down the circuit that contains a defect based on the defect phenomenon before performing the test, and connect the tester in advance.After that, a defect reproduction method is implemented, determined whether the circuit during the test is defective or normal, and at the same time confirms the malfunctional phenomenon.In order to narrow the cause of the malfunctional phenomenon, refer to the reference list of defective phenomena for each system.




          1. Covering method: When vibration may cause a defect

            1. Parts and sensors


              1. It is given vibration such as tapping lightly by hand on the estimated causal components, and checking for problems.

                □ Participation □
                Relays may open points when given a strong impact.

            2. connector


              1. Sprinkle the connector lightly up and down and left and right.

            3. Wire harness


              1. Lightly shake the wire harness up and down and left and right to check for problems.(Especially in the wire harness, focus on the base of the connector, the fulcrum of the vibration, and the penetration part of the body.)




            1. Cooling method: If a problem may occur during cold or warmth

              1. Use a hairdryer or a coolant to heat or cool the estimated defective parts to check if there is a problem.

                ■ Attention ■
                1. When heating, do not add more than+ 60 ° C (enough to be touched by hand).
                2. Open the lid of the ECU and do not heat and cool the electronic components directly.

                □ Participation □
                Cooling agents can be obtained at electronic component dealers.




              1. Watering method: If a problem may occur in rainy or high temperatures

                1. Put water on the vehicle to check if there is any problem.

                  ■ Attention ■
                  1. Do not directly water the engine room, but spray it in the front of the radiator to the front of the radiator, and indirectly changes the temperature and humidity.
                  2. Do not apply water directly to electronic components.



                1. Others: When the electrical load is thought to cause a problem when excessive

                  1. The electrical components such as the heater blower, headlamps, and rear defoga are activated to increase the electric load, and check for problems.
