Circuit description


The power slide door is controlled by the door control relay and drives the sliding door motor.During the motor closure, when the power slide sensor (touch sensor) for pinching detection is contacted and the resistance value changes, the sandwich is detected.The pulse sensor detects the speed change of the door, and if there is a change in speed, it is judged that there is something that is sandwiched in the direction of travel, and it is reversed.(It may stop during the opening operation. Also, it will stop in half door while the Eye Clarer is operating.)
