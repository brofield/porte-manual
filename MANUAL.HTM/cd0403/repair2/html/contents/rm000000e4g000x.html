Circuit explanation


  1. Cooler Thermista No.1 (after -Evapo sensor) is attached to the evaporator of the air conditioner unit to detect the cool air temperature after passing the evaporator and control the air conditioner cool compressor.Cooler Thermista No.1 (Evapa -after -after -Eva sensor) changes the resistance value due to changes in the cool air temperature after passing the evaporator, lower the cold temperature, and the higher the cold temperature, the higher the cooling temperature.The resistance value becomes smaller.The center cruster module switch outputs a voltage (5V) to the No. 1 (after Evapo sensor), and reads changes in voltage due to the change in the resistance value of the Cooler Thermista No.1 (after Eva).
