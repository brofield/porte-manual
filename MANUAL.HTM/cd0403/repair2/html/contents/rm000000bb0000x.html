2. Frontside Fix window Assembly cleaning



  1. Use a scraper to cut off the adhesive remaining in the glass.(When the glass is reused)

    2. If the windmolding is significantly damaged, replace the glass with a new one.

3. Mounting the front side fix window Assing LH

  1. Body mounting face cleaning




    1. If the adhesive on the vehicle side is remarkably uneven, smooth with a knife.

      ■ Attention ■
      Be sure to leave a little adhesive.

  2. Work before glass installation

    1. Apply a dedicated primer to the part where the body surface is exposed (no adhesive remains) on the vehicle adhesion.

      ■ Attention ■
      1. Do not paint thickly.
      2. Do not apply to the adhesive.
      3. Panel seams and spot welding positions are applied with special attention.

    2. Apply a dedicated primer to the glass adhesion.

      ■ Attention ■
      1. Do not paint thickly.
      2. The adhesive is applied after the primer dried (about 3 minutes).

    3. Paste the butyl tape to the damaged clip mounting position.(In case of clip damage due to glass reusing)

    4. Set the adhesive on the sealant gun.



    5. The tip of the nozzle is cut out as shown in the figure, and the adhesive is applied in a bead along the molding.



  3. Put the suction rubber.

  4. Glass installation

    1. The clip is mixed and the glass is attached to the vehicle.

      ■ Attention ■
      1. Confirm that the clip is in line with the room.
      2. Attach glass after the primer on the body side dries (about 3 minutes).

  5. Press the entire glass lightly to completely adhere.

    □ Participation □
    Pressing power: 98n {10kgf} or more

  6. Fix the adhesive protruding and deficiency with a spatula.

    ■ Attention ■
    Apply the adhesive to the glass end surface.

  7. Until the adhesive is cured, hold the glass with a protective tape or the like.(In case of clip damage due to glass reusing)



4. Water leak inspection and repair

  1. After the adhesive, apply water from the outside of the vehicle to confirm that there is no leak in the room.

  2. In the event of a leak, the three cement black is applied after drying.
