Circuit explanation


The front printen Shonor LH ignition circuit consists of an airbag sensor ASSY CTR and a front seat outa belt ASSY LH (the passenger side pliten shoner).When the operation conditions are satisfied, the front -printen Shonor LH is activated.

When a short circuit is detected in the front printen Shonor LH ignition circuit (PL+, PL-line), a diag code B0138/72 is recorded in the airbag sensor ASSY CTR.
