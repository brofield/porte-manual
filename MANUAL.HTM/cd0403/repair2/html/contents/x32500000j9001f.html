Trans -facy system (4WD) Notes

printing



    1. Before disassembling, the sand, mud, etc. attached to the outside of the transfa ASSY are washed, disassembled, so that they do not enter the transfa at the time of mounting.


      2. When decomposing the joints of light alloy components such as transfascover, a driver or the like should be broken lightly with a plastic hammer.


        3. The disassembled parts are always organized, tidy, and protect them from dust.


          4. Each part is well washed and dried before installation, and then applied to Toyota genuine gear oil supermarket.Aluminum, rubber and ring gear set bolts are not washed with alkaline drugs.In addition, rubber material parts such as O -rings and oil seals are not washed with washing oil (white gasoline, etc.).


            5. Apply the Toyota genuine gear oil supermarket to the sliding part and the rotating portion.


              6. When fixing the parts to the vice, do not fix it directly to the vise, but always fix it through an aluminum plate.


                7. Exchange or deformed snap rings with new ones.


                  8. Be careful not to damage the case of oil leakage, as it may cause oil leakage.


                    9. Before applying the sealing agent, remove the old seal agent attached to the sticker and wash the seal with white gasoline.


                      10. After attaching the seal, leave the oil immediately and leave it for more than an hour or more.


                        11. Be careful not to damage the oil sticker, O -ring, and the contact surface with the gasket, as it may cause oil leakage.


                          12. Be careful not to damage the lip and outer periphery of the oil seal when pressing the oil seal.


                            13. When replacing the bearing, replace the outer lace and inner lace as a set.
