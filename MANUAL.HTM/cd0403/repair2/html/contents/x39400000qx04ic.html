Circuit description



  1. The VVT ​​system controls the intake cam shaft to supply the optimal valve timing corresponding to each driving conditions.This control is based on conditions such as inhaled air, throttle position, engine cooling water temperature, etc.The engine control computer adjusts the oil control valve based on the signals from each sensor, resulting in the optimal position of the camshaft, improving the engine torque and fuel efficiency, and cleansing exhaust gas.The engine control computer uses the signal from the cam position sensor to detect the actual valve timing, control feedback, and achieve the ideal valve timing.
