Centaster top lamp ASSY installation

printing


1. Centaster top lamp ASSY installation

  1. Connect the connector to the center top lamp ASSY.

  2. Centaster top lamps are inserted into the center top lamp bracket of two places of claws of ASSY B.




  3. Rotate the center top lamp ASSY in the direction of ① and attach the center top lamp ASSY to the center top lamp bracket.

  4. In the clamp, attach the harness of the center top lamp ASSY to the center top lamp bracket.



2. Attach the center top lamp cover



  1. Press the center top lamp cover in the direction of ① and attach two clips on the clip.

  2. Press the center top lamp cover in the direction of ②, and attach the center top lamp cover in two places in parts A.
