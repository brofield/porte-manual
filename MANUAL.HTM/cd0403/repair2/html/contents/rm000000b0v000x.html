    1. Handling / Work precautions

    1. If the airbag Priten Shonor system is not performed in the right procedure and method, it may lead to a serious accident that will operate during work and be life -threatening.Also, if you do the wrong repair, it may not work when you need it.At the time of service (removal of parts, inspections, replacements, etc.), the following items are often read and work in the correct procedures and methods described in this book.



      2. Turn off the power

      1. The work starts after checking the diagnostic cord, turning the IG switch to (Lock), separating the battery's minihana starminal, and then 90 seconds.


        1. Since the airbag Priten Shonor system has a backup power supply, the airbag and pliten shoner may operate when the work starts after 90 seconds after separating the battery minor stormy.

        2. When the battery's minihana stormy is removed, the memory content of each system is recorded before the work is recorded, and the work is set after the work is completed.



        3. Overall precautions

        1. Use Toyota Electrical Testers for electrical inspection.

        2. Follow the instructions of the label describing the precautions around the system.

        3. Never disassemble.

        4. If there is a drop, cracking, dent, or missing, replace it with a new Toyota genuine component.

        5. The airbags of other vehicles and the Priten Shonor parts are absolutely not used, but always install a new one when replacing the parts.

        6. Do not directly expose to high fever or fire.

        7. Diagnostic inspections are performed even if the airbag and Priten Shoner have not been operated during a collision.

        8. Grease, cleaning agents, oil and water are not attached.If it adheres, wipe it off quickly with a dry cloth.

        9. It is stored and handled in a high temperature (atmosphere temperature of 93 ° C or more (Priten Shoner is 80 ° C or more)], which is less susceptible to electric noise that avoids high humidity.

        10. When scrapping or discarding a single item, be sure to operate using SST.



        11. No resistance measurement of airbag parts is performed.

          ■ Warning ■
          It is very dangerous because the tester's current may operate the airbags and pliten shoner.





          4. Precautions when the airbag and the Priten Shoner are abolished (before operation)

          1. Absolutely do not discard it in an untruffable state.

          2. The operation of airbags and Priten Shoner is performed in flat places where outdoor safety can be ensured.In addition, the operation is avoided as much as possible in residential areas.

          3. Since the sound of airbags and Priten Shonor is quite loud, it will be informed in advance that it will work in advance to operate.

          4. To operate an airbag and pliten shonner, use SST to be more than 5m away from the airbag and pliten shoner.

          5. Since the airbag and pritten shoner may operate due to static electricity, the water pipe or steel frame is touched with bare hands to discharge it before working.



          6. When operating an airbag, be careful not to go down.





            5. Precautions when the airbag and the priten shoner are abolished (after operation)

            1. The operated airbag and pliten shoner should be left for more than 30 minutes after operation because the temperature is several hundred ° C or higher depending on the site.

            2. Do not apply water to airbags and pliten shonor.

            3. When handling airbags and pliten shonor, wear dust protection and gloves.

            4. The operated airbag and Priten Shoner are put in a durable and transparent plastic bag, sealed and discarded.

            5. Be sure to wash your hands at the end of the work.



              6. Horn button ASSY (driver's airbag side airbag) & Instrument panel passenger airbag ASSY (passenger side airbag) & front seat outert ASSY (Priten Shonor) precautions

              1. If you take it off temporarily, such as during work, be sure to store it with the development surface up.

                ■ Warning ■
                In the unlikely event that the airbag is operating for some reason, if the development surface is facing down, it may lead to a serious accident that will be involved in life.

              2. When storing, do not place things on the airbag or put the airbags.



                7. Notes on the spiral cable (built -in combination switch)

                1. Be sure to centering the spiral cable when installing the spiral cable and removing the steering wheel.

                  ■ Attention ■
                  If the steering wheel is operated without centering, the spiral cable may be disconnected.



                  8. Airbag sensor ASSY CTR & Front Airbag Sensor's precautions

                  1. When the airbag and priten shoner operate due to a collision (including when only the airbag or pliten shoner is operated), replace the sensor ASSY (the center and front as a set).



                    9. Notes on the wire harness connector

                    1. Except for the unprecedented parts in the engine room, the wiring harness is unified in yellow, and a special connector is adopted, so be careful.



                      10. Treatment of vehicles damaged by collisions, etc.

                      1. When using an electrical welding machine, remove the airbags and pliten shoner before working.

                      2. If there is a possibility that the airbag sensor ASSY CTR and front airbag sensor may be impacted, remove it in advance.

                      3. Do not expose the airbag sensor ASSY CTR and front airbag sensor to high temperatures.



                        11. Cases that require an inspection of airbags and Priten Shoner

                        1. If the vehicle is damaged due to a collision, such as the airbags and Priten Shonor, including those that have not been operated.
