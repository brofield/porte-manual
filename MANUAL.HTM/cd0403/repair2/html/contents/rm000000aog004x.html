DTC P1790/65 ST Solenoid system

printing

List of preparation


Function explanation


The signal from the engine control computer performs OFF → ON → OFF at 3rd-4th speed, reducing the shock at the time of shifting.


DTC No.
DTC detection conditions
1. Diagnosis conditions 2. Abnormal state 3. Announced period
Inspection site
P1790/65
  1. 3 ← → 4 Driving with gear gears
  2. Solenoid ST circuit disconnection or short circuit
  3. When 2 is detected twice or more in one trip
  1. Wire harness and connector (ST signal system)
  2. Transmission wire
  3. Transmission Solenoid No.3 (ST)
  4. Engine control computer
