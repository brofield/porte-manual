List of preparation



1. Windglas [print type antenna] inspection
SST
09082-00030



  1. Wrap the aluminum foil as shown in the figure to the tip of the tester stick so that the printed antenna is not damaged, and check the aluminum foil with your finger.

    □ Participation □
    When cleaning the dirt of the glass with a cloth, use a dry soft cloth to wipe along the direction of the antenna so that the antenna does not hurt.

    ■ Attention ■
    Do not use general detergents or glass cleaners.




  2. Use a SST (Toyota Electrical Tester) to apply a tester stick to the printed antenna to check if there is a place where the conduction is gone.
