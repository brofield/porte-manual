7. Remove the front axle shaft nut LH



  1. Set the SST according to the grooves of the front drive shaft asser LH, and use a hammer to solve the bark.

    SST
    09930-00010

    ■ Attention ■
    1. Set the flat surface of the SST on the groove.
    2. Never cut the tip of the SST with a grinder.

  2. Use a socket wrench (30mm) to remove the front axle hub nut LH.

    ■ Attention ■
    1. The front axle hub nut LH is completely solved.
    2. The front drive shaft does not damage the screws of Assy LH.



8. Remove the front axle shaft nut RH

9. Remove the front disc brake caliper ASSY LH



  1. Remove the two bolts and separate the Dysque Brake Kikiki ASSY from the steering knuckle.

    ■ Attention ■

  2. Attach the mark to the disc and axle hub and remove the front disc.

    ■ Attention ■

10. Front disc brake caliper Assy RH removed

□ Participation □
Perform in the same procedure as the LH side.



11. Speed ​​sensor FR LH separation



  1. Remove the speed sensor FR LH clip and the bolt of the flexible hose and separate the front shock absorber ASSY LH.




  2. Remove the bolt and separate the speed sensor FR LH from the steering knuckle.

    ■ Attention ■
    1. No foreign substances are attached to the tip and mounting portion of the speed sensor FR LH.
    2. Speed ​​sensor FR LH does not rotate immediately.
    3. Do not pull the speed sensor FR LH harness.



12. Speed ​​sensor FR RH separation

□ Participation □
Perform in the same procedure as the LH side.



13. Tie rod end LH separation



  1. Remove the cotter pin and castle nut.

  2. Use SST to separate the tie rod end LH from the steering knuckle.

    SST
    09628-62011

    ■ Attention ■
    Does not hurt the dust cover of the ball joint.



14. Tie rod end RH separation

□ Participation □
Perform in the same procedure as the LH side.



15. Front axle assigned separation



  1. Remove the two bolts and two nuts and separate the front axle Ass, from the front shock absorber ASSY LH.

  2. Use a plastic hammer to lightly hit the tip of the front drive shaft ASSY LH and remove the front drive shaft ASSY LH and the front axle ASSY LH.

    □ Participation □
    If you are firm, use a brass bar and a hammer to hit the tip of the front drive shaft ASSY LH.




  3. Press the front axle ASSY LH to the outside of the vehicle and pull out the front drive shaft ASSY LH from the front axle ASSY LH.

    ■ Attention ■
    1. Do not push the front axle ASSY LH to the outside of the vehicle more than necessary.
    2. Do not damage the drive shaft outboard boots.
    3. Do not damage the speed sensor rotor.
    4. The front drive shaft Assy LH is suspended with a string.



16. Front axle assigned separation

□ Participation □
Perform in the same procedure as the LH side.



17. Front drive shaft ASSY LH removed



  1. Remove the SST to the position of the figure on the front drive shaft ASSY LH.

    SST
    09520-01010
    09520-24010 (09520-32040)

    ■ Attention ■
    1. Do not damage the oil seal.
    2. The front drive shaft does not damage the boots of Assy LH.
    3. Do not drop the front drive shaft ASSY LH.



18. Remove the front drive shaft ASSY RH



  1. Press the bearing bracket hole napping ring with a square axis flather and remove it from the bearing bracket.

  2. Remove the No. 1 drive shaft bearing bracket bolt.




  3. Remove the front drive shaft ASSY RH from the drive shaft bearing bracket.

    ■ Attention ■
    1. Do not damage the oil seal.
    2. Do not damage boots.
    3. Do not drop the drive shaft.

    □ Participation □
    If you are firm, use a brass bar and a hammer to lightly remove the end surface of the inboord joint Assy RH.
