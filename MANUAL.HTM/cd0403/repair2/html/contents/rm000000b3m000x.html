    2. When one door is opened, the lamp lights up and checks to turn off about 15 seconds after the door is closed.

    3. Before turning off about 15 seconds after closing the above door, check the lights off when the IG switch ON or the driver's seat door will be turned off immediately.

    1. Battery saver operation inspection

      1. Inspect that the lighting room lamp and pine plamp, etc., which are lit by the illuminated entry control, will turn off about 20 minutes after the Ignition Key is pulled out.

        □ Participation □
        Battery saver control is canceled when the Ignition key is inserted or opened and closed within 20 minutes after the lighting is opened and closed.



      1. Light Control Company ASSY inspection (car with discharge head lamp)

        1. If the light control computer is not damaged or the impact is applied but it lights up properly, a lighting diagnosis is performed in the following method, and it can be reused if all items are normal.


          1. Cold start (lighting from a state that has been turned off for more than 10 minutes), hot start (lighting for 15 minutes → 1 minute lighting → re -lighting) several times, and lightly lit.

          2. The lighting state must be monitored immediately after the cold start to the stable state (for about 5 minutes), and there is no unstable state such as flickering.

          3. The left and right headlamps are lit for about 30 minutes with the same valve attached, and the brightness is not clear on the left and right, and the lighting state is stable.
