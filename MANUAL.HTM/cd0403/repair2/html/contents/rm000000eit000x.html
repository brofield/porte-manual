3. Reardoor window frame molding FR LH installation

  1. Vehicle mounting face cleaning (when using a solvent)

    1. Put the protective tape on the whole acrylic form tape in the body.

      □ Participation □
      To protect the body, put masking tape around the protective tape.

    2. Use a polybera or dash to apply the remover bond 7 to the acrylic form tape remaining in the body.

      ■ Attention ■
      1. The remover bond 7 shakes the can before use and stirs it.
      2. Removal bond 7 is not used for regular dry coating.
      3. If the remover bond 7 is directly attached to the coating surface, the coating may be swollen, so wipe it immediately.
      4. If the coating is swollen, the defect is heated to 80 ° C with an infrared lamp, and the work is repeated several times by holding it with a scenner every 4-5 minutes.

    3. Warm the acrylic form tape remaining in the body with an infrared lamp.

      ■ Attention ■
      If the surface is warm until the surface is dry, it will be difficult for the acrylic form tape to be peeled off in the next process, so it will be discontinued before that.

      □ Participation □
      3-5 minutes for 40-60 ° C

    4. Shave the acrylic form tape remaining in the body using a polybera.

      ■ Attention ■
      Make the thickness of the acrylic form tape thinner and then move to the next process.

    5. Apply the remover bond 7 to the acrylic form tape remaining in the body.

    6. Warm the acrylic form tape remaining in the body with an infrared lamp.

      ■ Attention ■
      Dry to the inside of the acrylic form tape.(The surface is dry white)

      □ Participation □
      10-15 minutes for 40-60 ° C

    7. Rub the acrylic form tape remaining in the body with a rag.

      ■ Attention ■
      If the acrylic form tape remains, apply the remover bond 7 and rub it with a polybera.Remove the protective tape and clean the body with white gasoline.

  2. Vehicle mounting face cleaning (when using a tape re -move disk)

    1. Put the protective tape full of the acrylic form tape remaining in the body.

      □ Participation □
      To protect the body, put masking tape around the protective tape.

    2. Set 8 disks on the holder and attach the remover disk to the air drill or electric drill.

      ■ Attention ■
      The electric drill uses a non-load rotation of 2000-3000R/min.

    3. Put on protective glasses and cut off the acrylic form tape.

      ■ Attention ■
      1. Watch out for the rotation direction of the disk and the direction of work.
      2. Do it while visually checking the cutting unit so that the body is not damaged and the body is not damaged.

    4. Clean the adhesive surface with white gasoline.

  3. Reard and window frame molding FR LH installation




    1. Combine the five clips and attach a new rear window frame molding FR LH with acrylic form tape.
