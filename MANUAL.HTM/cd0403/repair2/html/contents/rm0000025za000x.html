Assembling fuel pump (1NZ-FE (4WD))

printing

List of preparation



1. Installing fuel pump harness

  1. Attach the fuel pump harness.



2. Fuel Senda Gauge ASSY installation

  1. Connect the sender gauge to the connector of the fuel suction plate section.



3. Attach a fuel pump filter

  1. Use a new clip to attach the fuel pump filter to the fuel pump.



4. Fouel pump assigned to ASSY W/filter

  1. Assemble a fuel pump spacer to the fuel pump.

  2. Assemble a new fuel pump filtera to the fuel pump.

  3. Apply gasoline to the fuel pump filter seal.

  4. Assemble a fuel pump on the fuel tank fuel filter.



5. Fuel pressure regulator ASSY (Jet Pump Tsuki) Installation

  1. Attach the fuel pressure w/jet pump plasticator.



6. Attach fuel pump cushion rubber

  1. Attach the fuel pump cushion rubber.



7. Fuel suction support No.2 installation

  1. Attach No.2 fuel suction support.



8. Fuel suction plate installation

  1. Attach a new O -ring to the fuel suction plate.

  2. Connect the fuel pump harness to the fuel pump.

  3. Apply gasoline to the O -ring and assemble a fuel suction plate.
