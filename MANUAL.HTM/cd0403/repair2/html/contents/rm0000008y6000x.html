Circuit explanation


The notcon control sensor is attached to the cylinder block and detects engine knocking.Inside the notcon control sensor, a sensor that generates voltage by vibrating and deforming cylinder blocks by knocking is provided, and when engine knocking occurs, engine controlled compilation is delayed to reduce knocking.


DTC No.
DTC detection conditions

  3. Abnormal period
  4. others
Inspection site
P0325/52
  1. After the engine warm-up, when driving with engine speed 2000-5200R/min
  2. Knock sensor circuit disconnection or short circuit
  3. 10 seconds or more
  4. 1 trip
