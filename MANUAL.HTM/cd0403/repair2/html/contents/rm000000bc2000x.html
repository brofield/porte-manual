Installation of front bumper

printing


1. Installation of front vampa cover

  1. Connect a fog lamp connector.




  2. Combine the front vampaside claws, and attach the front vampo cover with two clips and two screws bolts.

  3. Attach the front vampaside bracket RH and LH with two screws.



2. Front fenda liner LH installation

  1. Press the clip as shown in the figure and fix it.




  2. Attach the front fendarina LH (front) with two screws.



3. Front fenda liner RH installation

  1. Attach the front fendarina RH (front) with three screws.



4. Radiator grill W/radiator support seal UPR installation

  1. Assemble radiator grill and radiator support seal UPR.




  2. Combine the claws at the bottom of the radiator grill and attach the radiator grill W/radiator support seal UPR with 6 clips.
