6. Wind shield glass installation

  1. Body mounting face cleaning




    1. If the adhesive on the vehicle side is remarkably uneven, smooth with a knife.

      ■ Attention ■
      Be sure to leave a little adhesive.

  2. Work before glass installation

    1. Put the suction rubber on the glass.

    2. Put the glass on the body and set it to a position where the width of the upper part of the glass and the side of the side (uniform between the gap between the body and the edge of the glass end).

    3. Confirm that there is no problem in attaching the outside molding.

    4. Paste the gum tape on the glass and the body panel, and attach the matching mark when installing it with a pen (when new)

      ■ Attention ■
      Confirm that the stopper is in line with the room.

      □ Participation □
      When reusing glass, check and modify the matching position.

    5. Take off the glass.

    6. Apply a dedicated primer to the part where the body surface on the vehicle is exposed (no adhesive remains).

      ■ Attention ■
      1. Do not paint thickly.
      2. Do not apply to the adhesive.
      3. Panel seams and spot welding positions are applied with special attention.
      4. After drying (about 3 minutes), Stotspa No.Attach 1.



    7. Apply the dedicated primer to the glass adhesion as shown in the figure.



      ■ Attention ■
      1. Do not paint thickly.
      2. Apply the adhesive after drying (about 3 minutes)

    8. Set the adhesive on the sealant gun.



    9. Apply the adhesive to beads as shown in the figure along the adheriv due to the adherive dam.



      □ Participation □
      Cut the nozzle tip as shown in the figure.

  3. Glass installation

    1. Use suction rubber to match the matching mark and attach the glass.

      ■ Attention ■
      Attach glass after the primer on the body side dries (about 3 minutes).

    2. Press the front of the glass lightly to completely adhere.

      □ Participation □

      Pushing power
      98n {10kgf}




    3. Fix the protruding and deficiency of the adhesive with a spatula.

      ■ Attention ■
      Apply the adhesive to the glass end surface.



7. Wind shield outside molding installation

  1. Before the adhesive is cured, the glass position deciding dot (upper left corner part) and the outside molding assembly standard paint (upper left side corner) are combined to attach a new winds shielded outside moding.

  2. Until the adhesive is cured, hold the outside molding and glass with a protective tape.

  3. Extra adhesive is removed before hardening.

    □ Participation □
    For the minimum drying time and driving time after bonding, follow the instructions or instruction manuals of each adhesive manufacturer.



8. Water leak inspection and repair

  1. After adhesion, apply water from the outside of the vehicle to confirm that there is no leak in the room.

  2. In the event of a leak, the three cement black is applied after drying.
