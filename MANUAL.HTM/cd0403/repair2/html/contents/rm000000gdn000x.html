List of preparation



1. Speed ​​sensor FR LH inspection

  1. Inspect that there is no deformation and corrosion in the connector case and terminal.

    standard
    No deformation and corrosion

    □ Participation □
    If the connector on the vehicle harness side is abnormal, repair the connector.

  2. Use SST (Toyota Electrical Tester) to check the resistance value between the connector terminals.

    SST
    09082-00030
    09083-00150

    Reference value
    1.60 ± 0.20kΩ (at 20 ° C)

  3. Use SST (Toyota Electrical Tester) to inspect the resistance between the connector terminal and the body earth.

    SST
    09082-00030
    09083-00150

    Reference value
    10mΩ or higher
