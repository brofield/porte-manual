Fuel system function inspection

printing



  1. Fuel pressure inspection


    1. Inspection preparation





      1. Prepare a new Fuyuelchu Ubu and remove only the connector.

        □ Participation □
        Part number 23901-21060

    2. Work to prevent fuel outflow (the point isreference)

    3. Remove the Fuuelchu Ubu clamp and separate the Fuuelchu Ub (Fuuelch Youve connector).(The point isreference)




    4. Assemble the quick connector prepared for the inspection preparation, SST and instrument.

      SST
      09268-41047 (90467-13001, 95336-08070)
      EFI fuel pressure gauge [TB-707N]
      T type joint [TB727]
      EFI fuel pressure gauge [EFI-T8]
      T type joint [EFI5T]

    5. Wipe off the spilled gasoline.

    6. Start the engine.

    7. Measure the fuel pressure during idle rotation.

      Reference value
      304-343kpa {3.1-3.5kgf/cm2}

    8. Stop the engine.

    9. Confirm that the fuel pressure does not change for 5 minutes after the engine is stopped.

      Reference value
      147kpa {1.5kgf/cm2} Or more

      □ Participation □
      In the case of outside the reference value, check the Fuuel pump, the Fuuell Tetsuzi Reggeyer or indiga.

    10. After the measurement, cut the battery minor Star Minal and remove the SST and the Fuuelch Youve connector while being careful not to scatter gasoline.

    11. Connect the Fuuelchu Ubu (Fuuelch Youve connector).

      2. Inspection of fuel leakage


        1. Confirm that there is no leak in the fuel system with the fuel pressure applied.
