Step 2Air Mix Dampa servo SUB-ASSY Single Inspection

  1. Remove the air mix Dampa servo.




  1. Confirm that the arm rotates smoothly from Max Cold to Max Hot when the battery is connected to the connector 5 (MHOT) terminal and the battery minus to the 4 (MCOLD) terminal.

    □ Participation □
    Since the arm does not stop automatically at the time of the servo single inspection, the power supply is cut near the Max Hot position at the time of inspection.

  1. Confirm that the arm is smooth from Max Hot position to Max Cold when the battery is connected to the connector 4 (McOLD) terminal and the battery minus to the 5 (MHOT) terminal.

    □ Participation □
    Since the arm does not stop automatically at the time of the servo single inspection, the power supply is cut near the Max Cold position at the time of inspection.
