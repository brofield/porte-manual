Refrincer HFC-134A [R134A] Replacement

printing

List of preparation



1. Refrincer HFC-134A (R134A) extract

  1. Turn on the A/C switch.

  2. Driving a cooler compressor for 5-6 minutes for about 1000R/min, circulating the refrigerant cycle in the refrigerant, and collecting the compressor oil remaining in each functional component into the cooler compressor as much as possible.

  3. Stop the engine.

  4. Connect the regeneration machine, extract the refrigerant gas.

    ■ Attention ■
    Freon recovery and playback machine are used correctly according to the instruction manual.



2. Fireworks HFC-134A (R134A)

  1. Use a vacuum pump to do a vacuum drawing.



  2. Fill the refrigerant HFC-134A (R134A).

    Reference value:
    380 ± 30g

    ■ Attention ■
    Cooler compressor does not drive before filling the refrigerant, as driving without a refrigerant may be burned due to poor lubrication.

    □ Participation □
    When reusing the recovered refrigerant gas, the reinforcement is about 90 %, so the reinforcement is required, so it is necessary to replenish the service can, so prepare in advance.






3. Engine warm -up

■ Attention ■
After filling the refrigerant, run for 2 minutes or less for 2 minutes or less for two minutes or less.



4. Inspection of refrigerant gas

  1. After filling the refrigerant gas, use a halogenery quest to check the leakage of the refrigerant gas.
