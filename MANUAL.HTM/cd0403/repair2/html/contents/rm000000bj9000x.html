Installation of engine rear oil seal

printing

List of preparation



1. Installation of engine rear oil seal

  1. Apply a small amount of MP grease No.2 to the lip of the new oil seal.

    ■ Attention ■
    Do not adhere to the lip part such as sandwiching and sand.




  2. Use the SST to even hit the oil seal to the end of the cylinder.

    SST
    09223-56010

    ■ Attention ■
    1. Do not driven the oil seal diagonally.
    2. Wipe off the grease attached to the crankshaft.



2. Drive plate & ring gear SUB-ASSY installation



  1. Use SST to fix the crankshaft damper.

    SST
    09213-58013 (91111-50845)
    09330-00021

  2. Done of the removed bolt and bolt holes.

  3. Drive plate & ring gear Attached to 2-3 screws to the bolt tip of the bolt with Adherive 1234.




  4. Tighten the six bolts in the order of the figure and attach the drive plate & ring gear.

    Reference value
    T = 88 n ・ m {900 kgf / cm}

    ■ Attention ■
    After assembling, leave the engine without starting the engine within an hour.
