Circuit explanation

The pressure switch is attached to the high -pressure pipe block at the front of the capacitor to detect abnormalities in the refrigerant pressure.If an abnormality occurs in the refrigerant pressure, the pressure switch sends off the switch OFF signal to the engine control compilation.The engine control compilation stops the control of the cooler compressor at the signal.
