DTC P0713/38 Oil temperature sensor system (High)

List of preparation


Function explanation


The oil temperature sensor is attached to the valve body, the oil temperature in the transmission hydraulic control circuit is detected, and a signal is entered in the engine control computer according to the oil temperature.If an abnormality occurs in the oil temperature sensor, the four -speed and lock -up control will be discontinued as the fail -safe function.
