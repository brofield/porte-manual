Important points of defective simulation tests

In the defective phenomenon reproduction method, the defect phenomenon should be confirmed, but it is also necessary to find the range and part of the defect.In order to perform these, narrow down the circuit that contains a defect based on the defect phenomenon before performing the test, and connect the tester in advance.After that, a defect reproduction method is implemented, determined whether the circuit during the test is abnormal or normal, and at the same time confirms the defective phenomenon.In order to narrow the cause of the defecting phenomenon, refer to the reference list of defective phenomena for each system.In addition, in order to reproduce the diagcode output, a reproduction method according to the detection condition and detection time of the diagcode are required.


  1. Covering method: When vibration may cause a defect



    1. Parts and sensors


      1. Give vibrations such as lightly tapping by hand on the estimated causal components, and check for problems.

        □ Participation □
        Relays may open points when given a strong impact.

    2. connector


      1. Shake the connector lightly up and down and left and right.

    3. Wire harness


      1. Lightly shake the wire harness up and down, left and right, and check for problems.

        □ Participation □
        In particular, the focus is on the base of the connector, the fulcrum of the vibration, and the penetration portion of the body.

    4. Antenna cable


      1. Lightly shake the antenna cable up and down and left and right to check for problems.

        ■ Attention ■
        If the antenna cable is intensely applied, the disconnection or the connector may be damaged, so excessive force is not applied.

        □ Participation □
        In particular, the focus is on the base of the connector, the fulcrum of the vibration, and the penetration of the body.



    1. Cold heating method: If a problem may occur during cold or warmth



      1. Use a hairdryer or a coolant to heat or cool the estimated defective parts to check if there is a problem.

        ■ Attention ■
        1. When heating, do not add more than+60 ° C (enough to be touched by hand).
        2. Open the lid of the ECU and do not heat and cool the electronic components directly.

        □ Participation □
        Cooling agents can be obtained at electronic component dealers.



      1. Watering method: If a problem may occur in rainy or high temperatures



        1. Put water on the vehicle to check if there is any problem.

          ■ Attention ■
          1. Do not directly water the engine room, but spray it in the front of the radiator to the front of the radiator, indirectly change the temperature and humidity.
          2. Do not apply water directly to electronic components.



        1. Others: When the electrical load is thought to cause a problem when excessive

          1. The electrical components such as Hita Blow, headlamps, and rear defoga are activated to increase electricity loads, and check for problems.
