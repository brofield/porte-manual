6. Front Axle Outboard Joint Boots Clamp LH No.2 Installation

  1. Use the new front axle outboard joint boots clamp LH No.2 to attach to the outboard joint boots and temporarily bend the lever.

    1. Set correctly in the guide groove.
    2. The band and the lever do not bend when bending the lever.
    3. Set it correctly in the guide groove and place it inside the vehicle as much as possible.




  2. With the joint pressed on the work surface, put your weight on one hand, turn forward, insert the lever and press the joint on the work surface, and the joint is turned forward until the "click" sounds.roll.

    2. The joint does not float from the work.




  3. Hit the buckle with a plastic hammer to adjust the lever float so that the gap between the end of the buckle and the end of the lever becomes the best.

    Don't hurt your boots.
