Circuit description


The VVT ​​system controls the timing of the intake valve opening and closing at an appropriate time according to the driving situation.The engine control compilation controls the camshaft and camshaft oil control valve to properly adjust the opening and closing timing of the inhaled valve, and changes the relative position between camshaft and crankshaft by operating the camshaft timing gear.


DTC No.
DTC detection conditions
  1. Diagnostic condition
  2. Abnormal state
  3. Abnormal period
  4. others
Inspection site
P1349/59
  1. Engine rotation 500-4000R/min, cooling water temperature 80-110 ° C
  2. The target valve timing cannot be controlled to ± 5 °, or the valve timing is fixed and does not change.
  3. 5 seconds or more
  4. 1 trip
