List of preparation



1. Brake actuator operation inspection
SST
09991-70200

  1. Preparation before inspection

    1. Connect the SST (TASCAN) and start the engine.

    2. Operate according to the SST (TASCAN) screen and select the active test mode.

  2. Activation inspection of act yeuta

    ■ Attention ■
    Do not operate the motor for more than 5 seconds to protect the motor.If you want to operate continuously, leave more than 20 seconds.


    1. Turn on the motor relay.

      standard
      There is a driving noise of the motor.

    2. Turn off the motor relay.

    3. Hold the brake pedal for 15 seconds while stepping on with a certain force.

      standard
      The pedal does not enter

    4. Turn on the motor relay while stepping on the brake pedal.

      standard
      There is no pulsation on the pedal.

    5. Turn off the motor relay and release the brake pedal.

  3. Solenoid operation inspection

    ■ Attention ■
    1. Do not drive the motor for more than 5 seconds to protect the motor.If you want to operate continuously, leave more than 20 seconds.
    2. When using Tascan, stop the forced driving of solenoids in about 2 seconds to protect solenoids.Forced driving is prohibited for a certain period of time after operation.
    3. Do not step on the brake pedal while operating alone with only the decompression valve.
    4. Do not turn on solenoids without instructions.


    1. Step the brake pedal and hold it as it is.

    2. Turn on SFRH solenoids.

      standard
      The pedal does not enter

    3. Check the state of the pedal when SFRR solenoids are turned on.

      standard
      The pedal enters

    4. Turn on the motor relay.

      standard
      The pedal is returned

    5. Inspect the solenoids of other wheels in the same way.

      □ Participation □
      Front right (SFRH, SFRR), front left (SFLH, SFLR), rear right (SRRH, SRRR), rear left (SRLH, SRLR)
