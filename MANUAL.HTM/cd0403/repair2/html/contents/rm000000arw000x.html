Installation of name plate

printing

List of preparation



1. Luggage compartment door name plate No.2 Installation

  1. Warm the double -sided tape remaining in the body with an infrared lamp.

    □ Participation □
    1-2 minutes for 40-60 ° C

  2. Remove the double -sided tape remaining in the body with a rag or the like.

    ■ Attention ■
    If you attach a nameplate with double -sided tape remaining, it will cause defective adhesive, so it will be done until it can be removed sufficiently.



  3. After cleaning the body pasting surface with white gasoline, attach the nameplate to the position of the figure.



    ■ Attention ■
    The nameplate pasting work is performed at an atmosphere temperature of 20 ° C or more.If the atmosphere temperature is less than 20 ° C, warm the body to 20-30 ° C with an infrared lamp, etc. and then attach the nameplate.
