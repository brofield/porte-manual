Slide de Iiglorer System Function Inspection

printing



  1. Basic operation inspection

    1. When the sliding door is half -door, check that the motor operates and the door is fully closed (full lock).

    2. After the inspection, check that the sliding door opens.

    3. When the sliding door is half -door, the motor is drawn on the outside or inside handle while the motor is operating, it is checked that the motor is turned up and the operation stops.

    4. Inspect that if the sliding door is half -door and the motor is pulled outside or the inside handle while the motor operates, the motor stops operation.

    5. The sliding door is half -door, and immediately (the motor is not operating) and pull the outside handle or inside handle to check that the motor does not work.
