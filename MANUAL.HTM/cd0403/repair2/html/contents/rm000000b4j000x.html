Waipa & Washer System Notes

printing



    1. handling notes of washer nozzle (diffusion type)

    1. Since the washer nozzle cannot adjust the injection position, it is replaced with a nozzle of ± 1 ° and ± 2 ° in the injection angle.

    2. If the washer nozzle is removed from the hood, it cannot be reused.

    3. If the washer nozzle is clogged, cleaning is performed using a soft resin brush or the like without using metal items such as safety pins.
