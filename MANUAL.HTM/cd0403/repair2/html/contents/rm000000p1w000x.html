  1. At the speed of 10km/h or more, the speed sensor signal circuit disconnection or short state continues for 1 second or more
  2. Speed ​​sensor signal circuit disconnection for 0.5 seconds or more
  3. Instant loss of pulse signal from the speed sensor occurred 7 times or more
  1. Speed ​​sensor disconnection or short
  2. Speed ​​sensor mounting status or abnormality from the sensor rotor
  3. Wire harness disconnection or short
  4. Connector's incentive, terminal deformation, corrosion
  5. Foreign substance attached to speed sensor
  6. Sensor rotor abnormality (foreign substance attached, tooth loss)
  7. Speed ​​sensor circuit wiring resistance
  8. Brake actuator ASSY

  1. The speed sensor detects wheel speed and sends a signal to a skid control computer.These signals are used for ABS control.
  2. A coil and magnet are built in the tip of the speed sensor, and when the sensor rotor is rotated, a magnetic force is generated in proportion to the score.


  3. The skid control computer detects the speed of each wheels by the frequency of this AC voltage.
