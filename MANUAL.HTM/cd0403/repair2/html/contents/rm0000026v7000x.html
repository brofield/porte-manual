1. Transfer extension housing oil seal installation

  1. Use the SST and hammer to the extension housing to the extension housing to the position of the figure.

    SST
    09325-20010

    ■ Attention ■
    Do not attach an oil seal diagonally.




  2. Apply a small amount of MP grease No.2 to the lip of the oil seal.



2. Procellation Shaft with Center Bearing Assem

    ■ Attention ■

  2. Temporarily tighten the interimide shaft ASSY with four bolts.




  3. Combine the combined mark of the propeller shaft asser rr and a differential flange, and attach the propellashaft Assemia RR with four bolts, washer and nuts each.

    Reference value

    ■ Attention ■

    2. At the time of tightening, do not adhere to the washer and nuts.



3. Proposhaft with Center Bearing ASSY book

  3. Confirm that the center support bearing bracket center line is at a right angle to the propeller shaft axis.




  4. Tighten the four bolts.
