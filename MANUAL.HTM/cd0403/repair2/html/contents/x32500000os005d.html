    1.SRS Airbag System Handling · Working Precautions (reference)

    ■ Attention ■
    This maintenance work has a work that affects SRS airbags.Be sure to check the notes of the SRS airbag before work.



      2. Precautions for harness repair



      1. After soldering the repair part, tape winding.

        ■ Attention ■
        1. The bus (communication line) is always twisted and assembled.At that time, there is no gap between Can-H and Can-L.
        2. The difference in length of Can-H and Can-L should be within 10cm.
        3. The twisting of the connector connection is within 8cm.




      2. There is no bypass connection at the restoration site.

        ■ Attention ■
        If you do a bypass connection, you will lose the characteristics of the twist line.



        3. Method at the time of conducting point inspection



        1. Conduct inspection is basically performed by applying a tester from the back of the connector.




        2. Use a repair wire if you cannot apply the tester from the back of the connector.
