1. Head lamp leveling computer ASSY initialization procedure

  1. Vehicle status confirmation

    1. Make the vehicle in the following state.

      □ Participation □
      1. Make the remaining gasoline to about 10 liters.
      2. Lower luggage that can be loaded and unloaded outside the car (equipped with spare tires and in -vehicle tools).
      3. The crew is zero.
      4. The headlamp turns off.
      5. There is no change in the vehicle height at the stop (vehicle posture is stable).

  2. Warning display confirmation (*1)

    1. OFF to ON the ignition switch, and check the warning display.

      standard
      The leveling warning in the combination meter flashes.(Head lamp leveling computer assignment)
      The leveling warning in the combination meter lights for 3 seconds and turns off.(Replacing the height control sensor, when replacing the suspension or normal)

      □ Participation □
      1. The flashing of the leveling warning is displayed only when replaced with a new headlamp leveling computer ASSY.When the head lamp leveling computer ASSY is attached, the height control sensor replaces / detached, and the suspension is replaced, proceed to "initialization operation".
      2. In the case of non -standard, check the headlamp leveling computer ASSY, wire harness and connector.
