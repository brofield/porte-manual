Power slide door function inspection

printing


1. Basic operation inspection

  1. Activation condition at the time of the ignition switch

    1. Battery voltage 11V or more

    2. The electric door lock system operates normally

    3. At the time of the condition of either stepping on the shift lever P range or foot brake at a vehicle speed of 3km/h or less, or stepping on the parking brake

    4. Auto Slide Demon Switch ON (protruding)

    5. Ferrid closed (when fuel lid opening switch is turned on)

    6. The sliding door is Unlock (when the position switch is turned on)

    7. Power slide door sensor (touch sensor) disconnection detection

  2. Activation conditions during the ignition switch OFF

    1. Battery voltage 11V or more

    2. The electric door lock system operates normally

    3. Auto Slide Demon Switch ON (protruding)

    4. Ferrid closed (when fuel lid opening switch is turned on)

    5. The sliding door is Unlock (when the position switch is turned on)

    6. Power slide door sensor (touch sensor) disconnection detection

  3. Basic behavior

    1. When the sliding door is fully closed or fully opened, the door control switch (front seat operation switch or rear seat operation switch) is pressed for about 0.5 seconds or more, the buzzer blew for about 0.5 seconds, the sliding door is opened or closed (closed (closed (closed ().Inspect to perform a buzzer intermittently during operation.

    2. When the sliding door is fully closed or fully opened, when the PWR Door switch (wireless operation) is pressed for 0.9 seconds or more, the buzzer shouts for about 0.9 seconds, the sliding door is opened or closed (during closed operation, the buzzer is intermittent.Inspect to do it.

    3. When the sliding door is fully closed or fully opened, the buzzer blows or the sliding door is opened for about 0.1 seconds (0.3 seconds during closed operation), or the buzzer is intermitted (during closed operation.Inspect to do).

  4. Activation during other input detection during opening and closing

    1. Operate the door control switch (front seat operation switch or rear seat operation switch) or PWR Door switch (wireless operation), manipulate the door handle, and press the sliding door opening or closed (().When operated, check that the buzzer blows for about 0.1 seconds and the operation of the sliding door is inverted in the direction of the door.

  5. Activation when the operation condition is missing during the buzzer

    1. After the sliding door is fully closed or fully opened, press the door control switch (front seat operation switch or rear seat operation switch) or PWR DOOR switch (wireless operation), or operate the door handle in a fully open state, and then the buzzer blows.Inspection that the buzzer does not operate for about 2 seconds and does not operate the slide when one of the operating conditions is out of the way.

  6. Activation when the operation condition is missing during the opening and closing operation

    1. Door control switch (front seat operation switch or rear seat operation switch) or PWR door switch (wireless operation) or manipulate the door handle, and the sliding door is opened and the sliding door is on the process of opening.Or when the sliding door is closed and the vehicle speed is 3 km/h or more, or when the power slide door sensor (touch sensor) disconnection is detected, the slide operation stops and the buzzer is about 2 seconds (touch sensorAt the time of disconnection, check for about 3 seconds).

  7. Activation when a power slide door sensor (touch sensor) disconnected just before the entire closing

    1. When the slide door is detected when the sliding door is detected in a half -door state in which the slide door is closed by pressing the door control switch (front seat operation switch or rear seat operation switch) or PWR door switch (wireless operation).After the closed operation, blew the buzzer for about 3 seconds.

    2. The electric door lock system operates normally

    3. Auto Slide Demon Switch ON (protruding)

    4. Ferrid closed (when fuel lid opening switch is turned on)

    5. The sliding door is Unlock (when the position switch is turned on)

  3. Basic behavior

    1. When the sliding door is fully opened or fully closed, the buzzer blows for about 0.1 seconds (after 0.1 seconds during closed operation), and the sliding door is opened or closed.Inspect that.

  4. Activation during other input detection during opening and closing

    1. Associate the door handle, press the door control switch (front seat operation switch or rear seat operation switch) or PWR DOOR switch (wireless operation) during sliding door opening or closed operation, or operate the door handle again.It is inspected that the activation of the sliding door is inverted in the direction of the door in the direction of the door in about 0.1 seconds.

  5. Activation when the operation condition is missing during the buzzer

    1. When the door handle is assisted while the sliding door is fully closed, the buzzer is blowing for about 0.1 seconds, and the buzzer is about 2 seconds when one of the operation conditions (during engine rotation or stopping) is off.Inspect to blow.

  6. Activation when the operation condition is missing during the opening and closing operation

The door handle is assisted, and the sliding door is opened and the sliding door is in the opening or the sliding door is being closed.At the time, or when the power slide door sensor (touch sensor) disconnection is detected, the slide operation stops and the buzzer is about 2 seconds (about 3 seconds during the touch sensor disconnection) and flips.

  7. Activation when a power slide door sensor disconnection is detected just before the entire closing

    1. When the door handle is assisted and the sliding door detects a power slide door sensor (touch sensor) disconnection in a half -door state in the closed operation, the buzzer is shouted for about 3 seconds after the end of the operation (after the Easy Clarer).Inspect what you do.



3. Inspiration prevention function inspection

  1. Sliding door -to -sandwiching prevention function inspection when the sliding door is opened

    1. Press the door control switch (front seat operation switch or rear seat operation switch) or PWR door switch (wireless operation) or operate the door handle to open the sliding door.

    2. Inspect that the sliding door is stopped when the sliding door is operated from 20cm before the sliding door is fully opened, and the slide door is stopped by preventing it (slowing down the operation speed).Also, on the closing side of about 20 cm before the full open, it is inspected to be inverted in the direction of the door.

  2. Sliding door prevention function inspection when closed

    1. Press the door control switch (front seat operation switch or rear seat operation switch) or PWR door switch (wireless operation) or operate the door handle to operate the sliding door.

    2. While the sliding door is activated, if the hands are prevented (slower the operation speed), the buzzer will blow for about 0.1 seconds and the operation of the slide door will inverse in the direction of the door travel.

      □ Participation □
      After the operation starts, check after the door operates 30cm or more.

  3. Sliding door closing activation prevention function inspection (touch sensor)

    ■ Attention ■
    When inspecting the power slide door sensor (touch sensor), work with care not to be inserted.


    1. When the sliding door is in a closed operation or a half -door state in the closed road, you can touch a part of the power slide door sensor (compressing the sensor), and the 0.1 second buzzer blew and the sliding door is inverted.Inspect.

      □ Participation □
      Even if the sliding door is touched with the power slide door sensor (touch sensor) while opening, the sliding door does not work.
