Circuit description



  1. The rotary solenoid type ISCV is attached to the throttle body assy, ​​and the inhaled air that bypass the throttle valve is led by the ISCV passage to the ISCV.The engine control computer controls the inhaled air passage of the ISCV and sends a signal to the target idling air passage to control the specified idling speed.
