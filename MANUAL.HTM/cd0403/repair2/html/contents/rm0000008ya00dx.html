Circuit explanation


  1. There are two types of air -fuel ratio corrections: normal driving air -fuel compensation (hereinafter referred to as air -fuel ratio F/B) and driving state memory fuel ratio correction (hereinafter referred to as air -fuel ratio F/B learning).The air -fuel ratio F/B is an air -fuel ratio correction used to maintain the theoretical air fuel ratio, the engine control compilation compilation is darker than the theoretical air -fuel ratio, which is the current air -fuel ratio.By receiving a signal whether it is written as a rich) or a thin state (hereinafter referred to as lean), the injection amount is reduced in the case of rich, and the injection amount is increased in the case of lean.The air -fuel ratio F/B learning is the center point according to the individual engine difference (caused by a decrease in performance due to aging or a change in the usage environment, etc.) when the air -fuel ratio F/B value is performed intermittently for a long period of time.Changes.When the air -fuel ratio F/B value and the air -fuel ratio F/B learn exceed a certain value and become lean or rich, the engine control compilation lights the check engine warnin gland.
