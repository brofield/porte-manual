Engine rear oil seal removal

printing

List of preparation



1. Automatic Transx Axle ASSY removal
(The point isreference)


2. Drive plate & ring gear SUB-ASSY



  1. Use the SST to fix the crankshaft damper and remove 6 drive plates & ring gear mounting bolts.

    SST
    09213-58013 (91111-50845)
    09330-00021

    ■ Attention ■
    After installing the SST, the SST fixing bolt may contact the chin cover, so attach the SST while checking the mounting position.

  2. Remove the drive plate & ring gear.



3. Remove the engine rear oil seal



  1. Use a cutter knife to cut the lip of the oil seal.

  2. Use a flathead screwdriver with a protective tape to remove the oil seal.

    ■ Attention ■
    After removing the oil seal, make sure that the crankshaft part is not scratched.If there is a scratch, modify it with sandpaper ( # 400).
