Skid control sensor removal

printing

List of preparation



1. Remove the rear tire


2. Skid control sensor wire cut off


3. Remove the rear brake drum



  1. Unlock the parking brake and remove the rear brake drum.

    □ Participation □
    If the rear brake drum is not easy to remove, remove the rear brake hole plug, use a flathead driver from the backing plate service hole to press the rear brake automatic adjustable lever LH, and use another flather.Turn the adjuster and contract.



4. Rear Axle Hub & Bearing Assy LH removed



  1. Remove the four bolts and remove the hub and bearing asser.



5. Remove the skid control sensor
SST
09520-00031 (09520-00040, 09521-00020)
09950-00020

  1. Attach 4 hub nuts to the rear axle hub bolt, and fix the rear axle hub to the vice via an aluminum plate.

    ■ Attention ■
    If you drop or give a strong impact, replace it with a hub and bearing asser.

  2. Use a ping-punch (3mm) and a hammer to remove two pins from the SST (09520-00031) and remove the Attachment (09521-00010).




  3. Use SST and two bolts (90101-12007) to remove the skid control sensor from the rear axle hub.

    ■ Attention ■
    1. Pull out the skitt control sensor straight so that it does not contact the Skitsudo Control Sensor Rota.
    2. If the skitt control sensor rotor is damaged or deformed, replace it with a hub bearing asser.
    3. Do not damage the contact surface of the skitt control sensor and the rear axle hub.
    4. Do not mix foreign substances in the sensor rotor part.
