DTC P0758/63 S2 Solenoid system

List of preparation


Function explanation


By combining S1 and S2 ON-OFF by signal from the engine control computer, shifting from 1st to 4th gear is performed.If one of the solenoid valve systems is disconnected or short -circulated as a failed function, the engine control computer turns other solenoid valves.If all the solenoid valves are failed, only the mechanical hydraulic circuit will be used, and it will be a manual shift.(In the case of disconnection and short circuit, the engine control computer stops the power of the failed solenoid valve.)
