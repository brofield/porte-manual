List of preparation



1. Driving inspection



  1. Connect the battery plus to the transmission Solenoid ASSY (S1/S2) terminal, the battery negative to the body, and check for ventilation when air is blown as shown in the figure.

    standard
    Without ventilation

    ■ Attention ■
    Since the return spring is not incorporated into the solenoid, check the air while blowing the air.




  2. Check for ventilation when the air is blown as shown in the figure without connecting the battery.

    standard
    Ventilation



2. Resistance inspection

  1. Use SST (Toyota Electrical Tester) to inspect the resistance between the solenoid terminal and the body.

    SST
    09082-00030
    09083-00150

    Reference value
    11-15Ω (20 ° C)
