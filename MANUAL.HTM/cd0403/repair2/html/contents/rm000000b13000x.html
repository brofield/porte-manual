Circuit explanation


The SRS airbag has a built-in pressure circuit (DC-DC converter) in the airbag sensor ASSY CTR for a decrease in the power supply voltage.

When the power supply voltage is reduced, the increased pressure circuit (DC-DC converter) operates to normalize the power supply voltage.

The defect display in this circuit, unlike other circuits, is different from the other circuit, and when the DTC is a normal code, the airbag warnin glander lights up, indicating a power supply voltage.

The bug in this circuit is not recorded in the airbag sensor ASSY CTR, and when the power supply voltage returns to normal, the airbag warnin gland is automatically turned off.
