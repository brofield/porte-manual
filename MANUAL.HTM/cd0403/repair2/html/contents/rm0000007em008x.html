Circuit explanation


The "Wide Multi-AV Station" system performs communication between devices by AVC-LAN.In the AVC-LAN circuit, if abnormalities such as a short circuit of the communication line,+B short and GND short occur, the "Wide Multi-AV Station" system will not work properly due to interruption of communication.In this case, by separating the abnormal occurrence site from the AVC-LAN circuit, the system returns normal, so that the abnormal occurrence site can be identified.
