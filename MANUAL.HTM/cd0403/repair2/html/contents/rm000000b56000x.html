Wind Shield Wiper Switch ASSY removal

printing


1. Remove the steering column cover



  1. Turn the steering wheel 90 ° left and right to remove two screws.

  2. Remove the lower screw and remove the steering column cover.



2. Remove the windshield wiper switch ASSY



  1. Cut the connector.

  2. Press the claw position in the figure, remove the bite, and remove the windshield pipus tsutsuchi ASSY.

    ■ Attention ■
    Be careful as the claws will be destroyed if pressed strongly.
