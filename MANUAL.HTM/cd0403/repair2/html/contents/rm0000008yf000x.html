Circuit explanation


The rotary solenoid type ISCV is attached to the throttle body, and the inhaled air that bypass the throttle valve is led by the ISCV passage to the ISCV.In order to control the specified idling rotation speed, the engine control compilation is controlled to the ISCV inhaled air passage and sends a signal to the target idling rotation speed.
