Column shift lever assembly

printing

List of preparation



1. Installation of column shift lever

  1. Attach a column shift control shaft.




    1. Apply a body grease W to the control shaft and two bushs and attach it to the column shift lever.

    2. Attach a wavesha and spacer.




    3. Use SST to attach a new snap ring.

      SST
      09904-00010 (09904-00050)

  2. Install the column shift shift shifting bell crank ASSY.

    1. Apply a body grease W to the sliding surface of the Velcank ASSY and the shift lever.

    2. In the bolt, attach the Berkank ASSY and the hanesconector bracket to the column shift lever.

      Reference value
      T = 6.9 n ・ m {70 kgf / cm}

  3. Attach the steering column bracket ASSY UPR.




    1. The steering column bracket Assy UPR and the column shift lever are temporarily attached with a new steering rock set bolt.

      □ Participation □
      Tighten the bolt to the extent that it can be tightened by hand.




    2. Use a ping -punch (φ3mm) and a hammer to hit a new steering lock pin into the steering column bracket ASSY UPR and column shift lever.

      □ Participation □
      The steering lock pin is driven into the end of the column shift lever.

    3. Tighten the steering lock set bolt until the screw is cut.

  4. Attach a wire harness clamp and wire guide.




    1. Attach the wire harness clamp to the seat of the shift lever retainer.

      ■ Attention ■
      Attach the wire harness clamp so that it does not bite when operating the shift lever.

    2. Attach the wire guide to the control shaft pin.
