Thermostat single inspection

printing

List of preparation



1. Thermostat



□ Participation □
In the thermostat, the valve opening temperature is stamped.




  1. Soak the thermostat in water and gradually heat it.

  2. Check the temperature of the valve opening.

    Reference value:
    80-84 ° C

    □ Participation □
    If the opening temperature of the valve is out of the reference value, replace the thermostat.




  3. Inspect the amount of lift of the valve.

    Reference value:
    Lift amount 8.5mm or more
    Fully open temperature 95 ° C

    □ Participation □
    If the valve lift is out of the standard value, replace the thermostat.

  4. Check that the valve is fully closed when the thermostat is low (77 ° C or less).

    □ Participation □
    If the valve is not fully closed, replace the thermostat.
