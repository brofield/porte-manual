    1. Precautions for disassembly and inspection work of combination sijon meter Assembly

    ■ Attention ■
    When conducting and inspection work of combination sijon meter asser Assembly, we will always use recommended tools for static electricity measures to prevent system damage (ESD) due to static electricity.



    1. Static electricity countermeasures required work


      1. Combine Syon Meter ASSY attachment / inspection / replacement work




      2. Instruction / inspection / replacement work of internal wiring

        □ Participation □
        The Illustration of the task that requires static electricity measures is described in the figure in the figure in the figure.

    2. Recommended tool


      1. Wristband for static electricity prevention (body removal of the body)

      2. Conductive mat (product prevention)

        □ Participation □
        Tools: Elepass service kit



    3. Precautions during work





      1. Attach a wristband to prevent static electricity on your wrist.

      2. Fix the ground clip of the conductive mat to the ground point of the building or workbench.

      3. Fix the earth clip of a wristband for static electricity to prevent static electricity to the predetermined part of the conductive mat.

      4. To prevent damage to the electronic device, when attaching and detaching the main unit or peripheral device, work is performed after removing the battery power.

      5. Never touch the electronic components on the board or the IC pin.

      6. At the time of work, the clothing does not approach or contact the product.
