        3. Handling of electronic components


          1. The ECU cover or case cannot be opened.(If you touch the IC terminal, the IC may be damaged by static electricity.)

          2. It does not impact on electronic components such as computers, sensors and relays.Replace if you drop it.

          3. Do not expose electronic components to high temperatures and humid.

          4. When cleaning the engine with a steam, be careful not to directly water the electronic components, air filters and emission -related parts.

          5. It does not touch the connector terminal because there is a risk of deformation and deformation due to static electricity.

          6. Use an impact wrench to remove or attach or attach or attach a temperature sensor.



          4. Desidation of fuel -based parts

          1. Removal work place for fuel -based parts


            1. Work in a well -ventilated, welding machine, grinder, drill, electric motor, and stove without fire.

            2. Do not work in pits that may fill the vaporized fuel or near it.

          2. Dress and detach of fuel -based parts


            1. Prepare the digestive organs before the start of work.

            2. To prevent static electricity, the ground wire is attached to fuel changers, vehicles and fuel tanks, and water is watered so that the feet do not slip.

            3. Electrical equipment such as electric pumps and work lamps is not used because sparks may fly or become hot.

            4. Iron hammer is not used because sparks may fly during work.

            5. The fuel attached to the fuel is separated.




            5. Dress and detach of engine intake parts

            1. When a piece of metal is entered in the intake path, it has a negative effect on the engine body and turbo charger.

            2. When removing the intake parts, block the removed intake parts and the opening of the engine side with a clean rag tape or gum tape.

            3. When attaching a intake part, confirm that there is no mix of metal pieces.




              6. handling hose clamps

              1. Before removing the hose, check the depth of the hose and the clamp position so that the restoration can be performed reliably.

              2. Deformation and lumpy clamps are replaced with new ones.

              3. When reusing a hose, attach the clamp according to the hose clamp mark.

              4. The board spring clamp is applied by applying force to the direction of the arrow after installation.




                7. Moving communication equipment installation

                5. No high -output mobile communication equipment is attached.

                      10. Vehicle with catalyst converter

                      1. If a large amount of unburned gasoline flows into the converter, the catalyst can be damaged.In order to prevent such a thing, keep the following precautions.


                        1. Use only lead -free gasoline.

                        2. If you need to check the spark plug, remove the injector connector and stop the fuel injection.

                        3. When measuring the compression pressure of the engine, remove the injector connector and stop the fuel injection.

                        4. The fuel tank does not rotate the engine in almost empty state.When the engine is rotated, a misfire may occur and an extra load on the converter may be applied.
