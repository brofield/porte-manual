Circuit description


  1. Output these codes when the ferrous sensor tip, foreign substance attached to the sensor rotor and the rotation of the rotor.
  2. The skid control computer is determined by the adhesion of a foreign substance and the laundry of the rotor by the abnormality of the waveform input from the sensor.
  3. It may be detected due to the connector terminal of the speed sensor circuit or a problem with the wire harness.

Inspection procedure

Step 1Speed ​​sensor output waveform inspection



  1. Using an oscilloscope, brake actuator wire 31 (FR +) ← → 30 (FR-) terminals, 9 (FL +) ← → 8 (FL-) Between terminals or 33 (RR +) ← → 34 (RR-) terminalsCheck the waveform between the between and 11 (RL +) ← → 12 (RL-) terminals.




    1. Osciroscope waveform

      standard:
      The same voltage waveform is output to the left and right wheels, and no noise is mixed in the waveform.

      ■ Attention ■
      1. The higher the vehicle speed (wheel rotation), the shorter the cycle, and the output voltage amplitude increases.
      2. If the gap between the speed sensor and the rotor is large, the output voltage is lower.
      3. If a foreign substance adheres to the speed sensor, the output voltage of the corresponding wheel increases.

      □ Participation □
      1. Instrument set 1V / DIV, 2ms / DIV
      2. Measurement condition car speed is about 30km / h

  1. Give a light vibration by spraying a connector and harness, and checking that the waveform does not change.

    standard:
    There is no change in the waveform

    □ Participation □
    If the waveform changes due to vibration, it is considered that there is an abnormality in the connector or wire harness of the relevant part.
