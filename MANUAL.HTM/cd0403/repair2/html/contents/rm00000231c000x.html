                  6. G-BOOK usage resumption operation


                    1. Press the "information · G" switch to display the information screen.




                    2. Press the "main menu" switch on the information screen and start G-Book.




                    3. Press "g- book.com" on the main menu screen.

                    4. Confirm that g-book.com is displayed.

                    5. Since the guidance of usage procedures is displayed, the G-BOOK usage will be reopened according to the instructions on the screen.

                  7. Helpnet service start operation (manual maintenance inspection)


                    1. After the G-BOOK use resumption operation, manual maintenance and inspection will be performed as a service start operation.(The point isreference)

                      □ Participation □
                      The provision of a helpnet service starts when the service start operation (manual maintenance inspection) ends normal.



                  1. G-SOUND license recovery

                    □ Participation □
                    If you change the navigation receiver ASSY with the purchased G-SOUND indefinite license, perform the G-SOUND license recovery.

                    1. G-Book Support Center Contact


                      1. In contact with the G-Book Support Center, the G-Book terminal ID and GAZOO · ID before and after replacement are informed, and request recovery processing of the target license.

                      2. G-BOOK Support Center contacts and implements a G-SOUND license recovery procedure.



                    2. G-SOUND license recovery

                      □ Participation □
                      The screen is a display example, and may differ from the actual screen.


                      1. Press the "information · G" switch to display the information screen.




                      2. Press the "amusement" switch on the information screen and display the amusement menu screen.




                      3. Press the "G-SOUND catalog" switch on the amusement menu screen and display the G-Sound catalog screen.




                      4. Press the "G recommended" switch on the G-SOUND catalog screen.[*1]




                      5. Press the "advanced" switch on the license recovery screen.




                      6. The license is downloaded.




                      7. After obtaining the license, it will be displayed as "Do you want to play now?", So select "Yes" to confirm that the music can be played.

                      8. If there are multiple songs required for license recovery, repeat the same procedure from [*1].
