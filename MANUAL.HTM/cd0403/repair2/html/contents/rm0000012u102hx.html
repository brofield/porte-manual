List of preparation


Circuit explanation


  1. The knock control sensor is attached to the cylinder block and detects engine knocking.Inside the knock control sensor, a sensor that generates voltage by vibrating and deforming cylinder blocks by knocking is provided. When knocking occurs, the engine control computer delays the ignition timing and suppresses knocking.

Function explanation


By performing a confirmation, you can confirm the reproduction of the diag code.

09991-70201

  1. Connect SST (Tascan) to DLC3.
  2. Ig ON.
  3. Follow the screen display of SST (Tascan) to delete the diag code.(The point isreference)
  4. Idling for more than 10 seconds.(A)
  5. According to the screen display of SST (Tascan), check the diagcode judgment result.(The point isreference)

    □ Participation □
    If the diagcode judgment result is determined or cannot be determined, the idling is performed for more than 5 minutes (b), and then confirm the diagcode judgment result.
