  1. Diagnowsmode startup method



    1. Press the "Disc" switch three times while pressing "1" and "6" on the preset button at the same time in the audio off and IG switch ACC state.



    1. All element lighting mode

      □ Participation □
      In all element lighting mode, the display inspection of the element and the operation point of the panel switch are possible.



      1. Confirm that there is no abnormality in the lighting of the element.



      2. When each panel switch is pressed, make sure that the beep sounds.

        □ Participation □
        The “Seek/Track Up” switch shifts to the service inspection screen when pressed, so that the operation is confirmed by the screen migration.



      1. Service inspection mode

        1. Press "Seek/ Track Up" switch in all element lighting mode.

          □ Participation □
          1. Move to service inspection mode.
          2. It can take about 40 seconds to complete the test.
          3. When the service inspection mode is migrated, a system inspection and a diag memory test are performed, and the test results are displayed in a small order of the device code (physical address).



        1. Diagnowsmode removal method

          1. Press the "DICS" switch for more than 2 seconds.Or turn off the IG switch.
