Automatic Transxle ASSY (U340F) Notes

printing



    1. Before disassembly, wash the sand, mud, etc. attached to the outside of the transxle, and detach it, so that it does not enter the inside.


      2. Until you remove the oil pan, do not turn the oil pan side up because foreign matter may enter the valve bondy.


        3. When disassembling the light alloy portion such as a case, lightly shocks it with a plastic hammer without twisting with a driver or the like.


          4. When removing and decomposing, work using bare hands or vinyl gloves.Great gloves and rings are not used to prevent mixed lint.


            5. The disassembled parts are always organized, tidy and protected from dust.


              6. Wash each part well before installation, and after drying, apply Toyota genuine auto fluid type T-IV.However, aluminum and rubber parts are not washed with alkaline drugs.In addition, O -rings, gaskets and rubber -quality parts of oil seals are never washed with washing oil (white gasoline, etc.).


                7. Apply Toyota genuine auto fluid type T-IV to the sliding surface and rotation surface.


                  8. When installing the rings and oil sealing, apply the Toyota genuine auto fluid type T-IV and attach it so that it does not hurt.


                    9. Be careful not to damage the matching surface of the case because it may cause oil leakage.


                      10. Be careful about incorrect and missing similar items such as rings and snapplings.


                        11. Injecting fluids use Toyota genuine auto fluid type T-IV.
