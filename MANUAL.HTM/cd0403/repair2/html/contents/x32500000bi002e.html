Brake Boo Star ASSY (with VSC) Car inspection

printing



  1. Brake Boo Star Simple Inspection

    1. Brake Boo Star Antiative Inspection


      1. Start the engine, idling for 1-2 minutes and stops.

      2. The brake pedal is stepped several times with the step force that usually uses the brake to check the change in the height of the brake pedal.

        standard
        The height of the brake pedal when you step twice or three times is higher than when you step on the first time (the first, second, second and third intervals are 5 seconds or more)

    2. Brake Boo Star Movement Inspection


      1. Step on the brake pedal several times with the same step force while stopping the engine, checking that the height of the brake pedal does not change.

      2. Start the engine while stepping on the brake pedal to check the change in the height of the brake pedal.

        standard
        When the engine starts while stepping on the pedal, the pedal goes into the back a little

    3. Brake Boo Star load airtight function inspection


      1. Step on the brake pedal during the engine rotation, stop the engine in that state, hold it for 30 seconds, and check the change in the height of the brake pedal.

        standard
        The height of the pedal does not change



    1. Portable brake booster tester usage inspection

      1. Slip the clip and separate the vacuum hose from the vacuum check valve.



      2. Connect the portable brake booster tester and Mighty back as shown in the figure, and remove the tester.



      3. Anti -tightening inspection


        1. Using a Mighty bag, the negative pressure in the brake boot star ASSY is set to 66.7 kpa {500 mmhg} to check the change in negative pressure.

          standard
          No negative pressure decreases for 15 seconds

      4. Power airtightness inspection inspection


        1. While stepping on the brake pedal with 196N {20kgf}, use a Mighty bag to check the change in negative pressure by setting the negative pressure in the brake boot star ASSY to 66.7 kpa {500 mmhg}.

          standard
          The negative pressure decreased in 15 seconds is 3.3 kpa {25 mmhg}

      5. Unfoltaneous operation inspection


        1. Inspection of the liquid pressure with each step force without the negative pressure in the brake boo star ASSY.

          Reference value

          Polical force [N {KGF}]
          		MPA {kgf/cm2}]
          196 {20}
          		0.8 {7.8}
          294 {30}
          		1.5 {14.8}

      6. Double force operation inspection


        1. While the brake pedal is stepped on with each step, use the Mighty Back to check the negative pressure in the brake boot star ASSY to 66.7 kpa {500 mmhg}, and check each liquid pressure.

          Reference value

          Polical force [N {KGF}]
          		MPA {kgf/cm2}]
          49 {5}
          		2.4 {24.9}
          98 {10}
          		5.4 {54.7}
          147 {15}
          		7.2 {73.8}
          196 {20}
          		7.6 {77.3}

      7. Remove the portable brake booster tester and Mighty back.

      8. Connect the vacuum hose to the vacuum check valve.

      9. Brake system air removal (guidelinesreference)



      1. Brake Vacuum Check Valve ASSY inspection

        1. Sift the clip and separate the vacuum hose.

        2. Remove the vacuum check valve ASSY from the brake boot star ASSY.

        3. Check that the engine side is ventilated from the brake boot star ASSY side, and that the brake boot star ASSY side does not ventilate from the engine side.

          □ Participation □
          In the case of bad, replace it with a new vacuum check valve ASSY.

        4. Attach the vacuum check valve to the brake boot star ASSY.




        5. With clip, connect the vacuum hose to the vacuum check valve.
