Brake (4WD) Notes



  1. Be careful to replace each part properly.It may affect the performance of the brake system or cause danger when driving.Use the same or equivalent parts for replacement.

  1. When maintaining the brake system, keep the parts and surroundings clean.

  1. If the vehicle is equipped with a radio, etc., please refer to the precautions of the general theory.

  1. The replacement of the steering sensor can affect SRS airbags.Before that work, be sure to check the precautions of the SRS airbag.
