Camshaft timing oil control valve ASSY single inspection

printing

List of preparation



1. Camshaft timing oil control valve ASSY

  1. Resistance inspection

    1. Use SST (Toyota Electrical Tester) to measure resistance between terminals.

      SST
      09082-00030
      09083-00150

      Reference value:
      6.9-7.9Ω (20 ° C)

  2. Operation inspection




    1. Put a battery voltage between the terminals and confirm that the spool valve is operating.

      ■ Attention ■
      Confirm that there is no fixation of the spool valve.

      □ Participation □
      The poor return of the spool valve due to a foreign substance such as a sludge may cause subtle pressure leaks to the advanced angle, which may cause a diagcode to be detected.
