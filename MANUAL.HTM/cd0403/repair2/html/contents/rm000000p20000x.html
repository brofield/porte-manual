DTC C1249/49 Stop switch disconnection detection

printing

List of preparation


Function explanation


  1. The skid control computer (built -in actuator) is input a stop lamp switch signal and enter the brake operation status.
  2. The skid control computer has a disconnection detection circuit inside, and outputs this code when the stop lamp switch is off or the disconnection of the stop lamp signal entry or the stop lamp circuit ground side.


Dtcno.
DTC detection conditions
DTC output -related items
C1249/49
The IG1 terminal voltage is 9.5-17.2V, and the stop lamp switch circuit cut (between A-B or between A-Body Earth) continues for 0.3 seconds or more.
  1. Stop lamp valve or ground disconnection
  2. Stop lamp circuit -brake actuator between wire harness disconnection
  3. Connector's incentive, terminal deformation, corrosion
