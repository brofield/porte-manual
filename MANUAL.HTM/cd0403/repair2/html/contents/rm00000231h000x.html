2. Manual maintenance and inspection

□ Participation □
  1. In the manual maintenance inspection, the maintenance and inspection of the in -vehicle aircraft (in -vehicle repair and inspection and maintenance inspection after the vehicle repair) are performed, and whether or not they are registered as a member (when joining, canceling, changes in the contract content) are performed., Emergency call call can be made/non -allowable switching can be performed.
  2. The screen is a display example, and may differ from the actual screen.

  1. GPS reception confirmation

    1. Confirm that the GPS mark is displayed on the current screen.

      □ Participation □
      It has confirmed that manual maintenance and inspection can be accurately sent.For this reason, avoid the valley and factories of the building and do it in a place with a good view that can receive GPS.

  2. Manual maintenance inspection

    1. Press the "information · G" button to display the information screen.




    2. Press "Settings" on the information screen.




    3. On the setting screen, press "Helpnet Maintenance Inspection" for 10 seconds or more.




    4. Start connecting to the center.




    5. When connected to the center, it is confirmed that the data communication is first performed, the current location of the vehicle, manual maintenance and inspection, and that it is registered as a member of the center.




    6. After the data communication is completed, it becomes a call mode.




    7. Call with the center through the microphone to confirm that the call can be performed normally and that the call position is correct.




    8. After the conversation, the inspection ends.

    9. If the "Confirm" button is displayed, press "Confirm".

    10. After that, the current screen is displayed.

    11. Press the "information · G" button.




    12. Confirm that the "helpnet" switch is displayed on the information screen.
