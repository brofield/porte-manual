Circuit description


The cam position sensor (G2 signal) is composed of a pickup coil and a timing rotator provided in the camshaft.When the camshaft rotates, the air gap on the timing rotor's protrusion and the pickup coil change, which changes the magnetic field, creating an electromotive force in the pickup coil.The cam position sensor outputs three signals per camshaft.The engine control computer determines a cylinder based on this signal and detects the crankshaft angle by the NE signal.
