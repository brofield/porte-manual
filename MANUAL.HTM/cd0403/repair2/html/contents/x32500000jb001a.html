1. Trans -firer oil inspection

  1. Remove the transfaser plug No.1 (filler plug).




  2. Remove the gasket from the transfaser plug No.1 (filler plug).




  3. Confirm that there is an oil within 0-5mm from the lower end of the hole from the lower end.

    ■ Attention ■
    1. When the oil is changed, reconfirm the amount of oil after driving.
    2. Oil or too small may cause trouble.
    3. When the amount of oil is small, check the oil leak.

  4. Attach the transfastra plug No.1 (filler plug) via a new gasket.

    Reference value
    T = 49 n ・ m {500 kgf / cm}



2. Transfish oil adjustment

  1. Remove the transfaser plug No.1 (filler plug).

  2. Remove the gasket from the transfaser plug No.1 (filler plug).




  3. Put the nozzle and inject Toyota genuine gear oil supermarket (SAE-75W-90 GL-5).

    ■ Attention ■
    1. Point the nozzle tip to the vehicle forward.
    2. If you make a mistake in the nozzle, the oil may blow out.
    3. Slowly inject oil.
    4. The oil injection is repeated several times every few minutes.

  4. After leaving it for 5 minutes, check the amount of oil again.

  5. Attach the transfastra plug No.1 (filler plug) via a new gasket.

    Reference value
    T = 49 n ・ m {500 kgf / cm}
