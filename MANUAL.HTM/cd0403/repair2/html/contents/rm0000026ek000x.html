Parking brake (4WD) adjustment

printing


1. Remove the rear tire


2. Remove the rear brake drum


3. Adjustment of brake drums and lining gaps



  1. Turn the adjuster with a flathead screwdriver from the backing plate service hall, and let the brake shoe ASSY contact the rear brake drum.




  2. Turn the adjuster by turning the adjuster with another flathead screwdriver while pressing the rear brake automatic adjust lever LH with a flathead screwdriver.

  3. The brake shoe Assy is no longer in contact with the rear brake drum, and the adjuster is further reduced to contract to contract.



4. Lear brake drum installation


5. Listed mounting
Reference value
T = 103 n ・ m {1050 kgf / cm}


6. Parking brake pedal step -step inspection

  1. Slowly step on the parking brake pedal and count the operating noise.

    Reference value
    7-10 Notch [Operation 294N {30kgf}]



7. Parking brake pedal stepping adjustment



  1. Press the parking brake pedal once strongly.

  2. Park the parking brake pedal again and unlock.

  3. Loose the lock nut in the parking brake pedal part, and adjust the adjustment nut so that stepping is a reference value.

    Reference value
    7-10 Notch [Operation 294N {30kgf}]

  4. Fix the adjustment nut and tighten the rock nut.

    Reference value
    T = 5.4 n ・ m {55 kgf / cm}

  5. After operating the parking brake pedal at least once, check the steps.

  6. Make sure there is no drag of the parking brake.

  7. When the parking brake pedal is operated, confirm that the brake indigor lamp lights up.

    standard
    The lamp will surely light up in the first notch after starting to step
