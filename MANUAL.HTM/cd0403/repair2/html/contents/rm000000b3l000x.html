Light Control Company (car equipped with discharge head lamp)
situation
Content
Opened
  1. The operation stops about 1 second.
  2. Maintain the operation suspension until the power is re -introduced (Hetsu dramped damus ittsuchi off → ON).
Short between output terminals
  1. The operation is stopped within about 1 second.
  2. Maintain the operation suspension until the power is re -installed (Hetsu dramped day masutchi OFF → ON).
Output terminal-interlined inter-ground leak
  1. The operation is stopped after about 0.1 seconds.
  2. Maintain the operation suspension until the power is re -introduced (Hetsu dramped damus ittsuchi off → ON).
Lamp voltage
  1. The operation stops about 80 seconds.
  2. Maintain the operation suspension until the power is re -introduced (Hetsu dramped damus ittsuchi off → ON).
Lamp voltage excessive
  1. The operation is stopped within about 1 second.
  2. Maintain the operation suspension until the power is re -introduced (Hetsu dramped damus ittsuchi off → ON).
Valve blinking
  1. If the blinking continues for about 60 seconds, the operation is stopped.
  2. Maintain the operation suspension until the power is re -introduced (Hetsu dramped damus ittsuchi off → ON).
Input over voltage
  1. The power supply voltage is stopped at 20 ± 3V.
  2. The operation resumes when the power supply voltage returns to 16V or less.
Input low voltage
  1. When the power supply voltage is 6V or less, the operation is stopped.
  2. The operation resumes when the power supply voltage returns to 10V or more.
