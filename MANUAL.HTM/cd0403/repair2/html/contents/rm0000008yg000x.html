Circuit explanation


A direct ignition system (TDI) is adopted.TDI increases ignition accuracy, suppresses high voltage loss, and enhances the overall reliability of the ignition system.TDI ignites one cylinder with one ignition coil.The engine control compilation determines the ignition time and outputs a ignition signal (IGT) to each cylinder.Based on the IGT signal, the power trap rangista in the ionita cuts the current to the primary coil in the ignition coil, and ignites the spark plug with a reverse electromotive force generated in the secondary coil.At the same time, the igniter sends a ignition confirmation signal (IGF) to the engine control composer.

Inspection procedure


□ Participation □
  1. When the DTC P1300/14 is displayed, check the ignition coil asser (No.1 cylinder) circuit.
  2. When the DTC P1305/15 is displayed, check the ignition coil asser (No.2 cylinder) circuit.
  3. When the DTC P1310/14 is displayed, check the ignition coil asser (No.3 cylinder) circuit.
  4. When the DTC P1315/15 is displayed, check the ignition coil asser (No.4 cylinder) circuit.
  5. Use Tascan to read the freeze frame data.The freeze -frame data records a part of the engine operation state in the event of a problem, which is useful for trouble shooting.
  6. The following shows how to check the No.1 cylinder.If a problem other than the No.1 cylinder has occurred, check the corresponding cylinder circuit with reference to the following procedure.
