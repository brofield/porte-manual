DTC P1790/65 ST Solenoid system

Function explanation


The signal from the engine control componizuta performs OFF → ON → OFF during 3-speed-4 speed shifting, reducing the shock at the time of shifting.


DTC No.
DTC detection conditions

  2. Abnormal state
  3. Abnormal period

  1. 3 ← → 4 Driving with gear gears
  2. Solenoid ST circuit disconnection or short circuit
  3. When 2 is detected twice or more in one trip
  1. Wire harness and connector (ST signal system)
  2. Transmitsu Shi Young Wire
  3. Transmitsu Syon Solenoid No.3 (ST)
