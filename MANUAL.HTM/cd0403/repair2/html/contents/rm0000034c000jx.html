DTC P0751/62 S1 Solenoid system (functional abnormality)

printing

List of preparation


Function explanation


The engine control computer detects the gear position by a signal from the vehicle speed sensor and transmission revolution sensor (NT).Then, when the engine control computer is different from the gear position in which the detected gear position is commanded, the mechanical problem of the relevant solenoid valve, valve body or automatic trance axle ASSY is detected.

□ Participation □
The DTC P0751/62 indicates the OFF or ON sticker of the transmission solenoid No.1 (S1).

Transmission Solenoid No.1 (S1) ON Fixed Announcement:
  1. Engine cooling water temperature 60 ° C or more and D position is running
  2. (a) and (b)
    (a) When the engine control computer orders to switch from 3rd to 4th speed, it does not shift
    (b) When the engine control computer ordered to switch to 4th speed, the engine speed increases.
