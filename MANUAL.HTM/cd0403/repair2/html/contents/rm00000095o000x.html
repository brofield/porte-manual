Brake Boo Star ASSY car inspection

printing


1. Brake boo Star Simple inspection

  1. Brake Boo Star Antiative Inspection

    1. Start the engine, idling for 1-2 minutes and stops.

    2. The brake pedal is stepped several times with the step force that usually uses the brake to check the change in the height of the brake pedal.

      standard
      The height of the brake pedal when you step twice or three times is higher than when you step on the first time (the first and second, second and third intervals are 5 seconds or more)

  2. Brake Boo Star Movement Inspection

    1. While the engine is stopped, the brake pedal is stepped on several times with the same step force, and the height of the brake pedal does not change.

    2. Start the engine while stepping on the brake pedal to check the change in the height of the brake pedal.

      standard
      When you start the engine while stepping on the pedal, the pedal goes into the back a little

  3. Brake Boo Star load airtight function inspection

    1. Step on the brake pedal during the engine rotation, stop the engine in that state, hold it for 30 seconds, and check the change in the height of the brake pedal.

      standard
      The height of the pedal does not change



2. Portable brake booster tester usage inspection

  1. Slip the clip and separate the vacuum hose from the vacuum check valve.



  2. Connect the portable brake booster tester and Mighty back as shown in the figure, and remove the tester.



  3. Anti -tightening inspection

    1. Using a Mighty bag, the negative pressure in the brake booster is set to 66.7 kpa {500 mmhg} to check the change in negative pressure.

      standard
      No negative pressure decreases for 15 seconds

  4. Power airtightness inspection inspection

    1. While stepping on the brake pedal with 196N {20kgf}, use a Mighty back to check the change in negative pressure by setting the negative pressure in the brake booster to 66.7 kpa {500 mmhg}.

      standard
      Within 3.3 Pa {25mmHg} with a decrease in the negative pressure for 15 seconds

  5. Unfoltaneous operation inspection

    1. Check the liquid pressure of each step force without negative pressure in the brake booster.

      Reference value

      Polical force [N {KGF}]
      		MPA {kgf/cm2}]
      196 {20}
      		0.5 {5.5}
      294 {30}
      		1.4 {13.8}

  6. Double force operation inspection

    1. While the brake pedal is stepped on with each step, use a Mighty bag to check the negative pressure in the brake booster to 66.7 kpa {500 mmhg}, and check each liquid pressure.

      Reference value

      Polical force [N {KGF}]
      		MPA {kgf/cm2}]
      49 {5}
      		2.6 {26.7}
      98 {10}
      		5.7 {57.7}
      147 {15}
      		8.2 {83.2}
      196 {20}
      		8.6 {87.3}

  7. Remove the portable brake booster tester and Mighty back.

  8. Connect the vacuum hose to the vacuum check valve.
