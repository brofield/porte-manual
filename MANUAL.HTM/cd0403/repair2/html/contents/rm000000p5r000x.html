    2. Active test inspection


      1. Turn off the IG switch and set the SST (Tascan) on the DLC3.

        SST
        09991-70200

      2. Turn on the IG switch and select [ABS · VSC]-[Active Test].

      3. Select the data monitor item to be confirmed during the active test and press the execution key.

      4. Select the active test item to be implemented, press the execution key, operate the specified key of the screen display, and execute the ON ← → OFF switching of each test.

        ■ Attention ■
        1. The motor driving is automatically stopped in about 5 seconds to protect the motor, but does not intermittently perform a few seconds.
        2. The driving of each solenoid is automatically prohibited in about 2 seconds to protect solenoids, and is allowed at a certain time interval.
        3. Do not step on the brake while only the decompression valve is turned on alone.
        4. Except by moving the holding valve and decompression valve of each wheel, two or more solenoids are not driven at the same time.

        □ Participation □
        In the case of a solenoid active test, two solenoids can be driven at the same time as entering [All Solenoid Drive Select] on the solenoid selection screen.
