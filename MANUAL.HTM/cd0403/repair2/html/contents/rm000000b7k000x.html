printing

List of preparation



1. The front door window frame molding FR RH is removed

■ Attention ■
The front door window frame molding FR RH will be damaged if removed, so it will not be removed unless necessary.




  1. Put a protective tape on the front door panel

  2. Remove the junction of the acrylic form tape and the two clips, and remove the front door window frame molding FR RH upward.



2. Remove the door pull handle

  1. Remove the screw and remove the door -pull handle.



3. Remove the front door router frame bracket garnish RH



  1. Remove the kanchi (upper part) by hand.

  2. Remove the claws (lower) while pulling by hand, and remove the Front Rower Frame Braketto Garanitsui RH.



4. Remove the front door trim board SUB-ASSY RH



  1. Take off the screw.

  2. Use a plus driver to rotate the center of the clip 90 ° and remove the clip.

  3. Use a flathead driver with a protective tape while pulling the trim board inside the vehicle to remove the nine clips in the figure.

  4. Pull up the front door trim board RH and remove it.

    □ Participation □
    It is easy to remove it by putting a finger in the slot at the bottom of the front door trim board RH.



5. Outari View Mirror ASSY RH removed

  1. Cut the connector.

  2. Remove the three screws and remove the Otariyabi Umiller ASSY RH.
