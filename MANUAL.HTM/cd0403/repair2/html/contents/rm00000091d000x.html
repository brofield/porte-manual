1. Starter ASSY

■ Attention ■
Each test is performed in a short time (3-5 seconds).

  1. Remove the nut and separate the C terminal.




  2. When connected as shown in the figure, confirm that the pinion gear pops out.




  3. Even after releasing the C -terminal cable from the state where the pinion gear pops out, confirm that the pinion gear is still out.




  4. Move the pinoon gear towards the armacha and measure the gap between the pinion end and the stop nut.

    Reference value
    1-5mm




  5. Confirm that when the pinion gear is left out of the starter body cable, the pinion gear returns.

  6. Attach the C terminal with a nut.

    Reference value
    T = 9.8 n ・ m {100 kgf / cm}

  7. Fix the starter to the vise.




  8. Connect the cable as shown in the figure, and connect the SST (AC/DC probe 400A) to the plus side cable.

    09083-00300
