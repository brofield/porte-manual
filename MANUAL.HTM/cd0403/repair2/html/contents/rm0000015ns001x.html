Function explanation


The speed sensor detects the wheel speed and sends a signal to the brake actuator ASSY (skid control computer).These signals are used for ABS control.

When the rotor rotates, the magnetic force released by the magnet of the speed sensor generates an AC voltage.Since the frequency of this AC voltage changes nicely to the rotor speed, the brake actuator Assy (skid control computer) detects each wheeling speed.

  1. At the speed of 10 km/h or more, the disconnection or short state of the speed sensor signal cycle continues for 30 seconds or more
  2. When the instantaneous missing of the pulse signal from the speed sensor occurs frequently while driving
  3. When a car speed is 20 km/h or more, the pulse signal from the speed sensor occurs more than 75 times in 5 seconds.
  4. As the vehicle is stopped, the disconnection status of the speed sensor system continues for 1 second
  1. Speed ​​sensor disconnection or short
  2. Speed ​​sensor mounting status or abnormality from the sensor rotor
  3. Wire harness disconnection or short
  4. Failure of connectors, deformation of terminals, corrosion
  5. Foreign substance attached to speed sensor
  6. Sensor rotor abnormality (attached attachment, damage)
  7. Speed ​​sensor circuit wiring resistance
  8. Brake actuator ASSY
