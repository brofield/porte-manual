Step 2TASCAN-Active test

    SST
    09991-70200

  1. Use Tascan to operate according to the screen display, display the "active test" screen and perform an active test.
    [System selection screen: TCCS → active test]

    Item name
    		Test content
    		Restrictions
    ECT shift position
    		Can be set in any shift position
    1. Testing is possible at 50km/h or less
    1. 3 ← → 4 When performing gear gears, be sure to do it in idl -on
    1. Be sure to hold the idl ON state for 2 seconds after the shift and do not perform continuously.
    Lockup SOL
    1. Lockup solenoid ON ← → OFF is possible
    1. ON → 100% duty
    1. OFF → 0% of duty
    1. Testing is possible at 60km/h or more
    1. Only 4th speed test is possible
    Line pressure sol
    1. ON → OFF of line pressure solenoid valve is possible
    1. ON → 100%duty (line pressure when idle)
    1. OFF → 0%of duty ratio (line pressure at stall)
    		Throttle opening is fully closed (when idle) and testing is possible at a vehicle speed of 0km/h
    Timing sol
    		Timing solenoid ON ← → OFF is possible
    		Throttle opening is fully closed (when idle) and testing is possible at a vehicle speed of 0km/h

    □ Participation □
    The active test of the line pressure SOL is tested using oil pressure gauge.
