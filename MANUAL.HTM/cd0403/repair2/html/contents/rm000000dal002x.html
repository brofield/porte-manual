1. Back guide monitor

  1. If the screen is displayed on the screen by removing the battery terminal, attaching the steering sensor a connector, etc., "System Initialization" is displayed on the screen, and one of the following operations is performed to correct the displacement of the rudder neutral point.

    1. Method 1: Cut the steering wheels on both sides of the left and right sides to correct the steering angle.

    2. Method 2: In a situation with less bending angle, curve, and traffic congestion as much as possible, drive for more than 20 km/h or more for 5 minutes.

      □ Participation □
      After driving, stop the vehicle and check if the neutral point has been learned.If you are not learning, run again.(Judging a straight state and learning the neutral point)
