DTC C0274/14 ABS Motor Relay Circuit Short

Circuit explanation


  1. ABS Motalille supplies power to ABS pump motors.
  2. While ABS is operating, the skid control computers turn on the ABS motor relay and activate the ABS pump motor.
  3. This code may be remembered if the voltage of the motorille (+BM) decreases from the DTC detection condition due to lack of output of the battery or alternator.

DTC detection conditions
DTC output -related items
C0273/13
Immediately after Motarille ON ON, the inner contact OFF status in the motorillille continues for 0.2 seconds or more
  1. Reduced voltage to motor relay power (+BM)
  2. Cutting wire harness
  3. Failure of connectors, deformation of terminals, corrosion
  4. Motalillille circuit disconnection in the brake act.
C0274/14
When the IG terminal voltage is 10-14V, when the motorillille is OFF, the inner contact ON state in the motorille continues for 4 seconds or more.
  1. Brake act Yu outer Motalille circuit short
