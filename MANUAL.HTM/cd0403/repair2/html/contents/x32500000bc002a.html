List of preparation


□ Participation □
  1. If the brake system is removed, or if there is a suspicion of an air entry in the brake line, the system air is removed.
  2. Do not attach brake fluid on the painted surface.If it adheres, wash it off immediately.



1. Brake system air removal

  1. Brake fluid replenishment

    1. Stand up the brake fluid can on the reservoir.

      ■ Attention ■
      Make sure that the can has enough brake fluid.

  2. Brake -based air removal

    1. The air out of the normal brake system by pedaling of the brake pedal.

      ■ Attention ■
      1. All air removal is repeated for four wheels until the air is completely out of each wheel.
      2. The addition of the brake fluid during the air is maintained between the min-max line of the reservoir.
      3. If you operate the brake pedal while removing the reserve cap, the brake fluid may overflow, so do not operate the pedal when replenishing the brake fluid, or put the fluid can in the reservoir injection.

  3. Air bleeding in the actuator

    ■ Attention ■
    1. After the normal brake system -type air is implemented, the appropriate brake pedal height or the feeling may not be obtained and there is a risk that air may be mixed.
    2. If air is removed without using SST, it may lead to obstacles or accidents, so be sure to use SST to remove air.


    1. Turn off the ignition switch and step on the brake pedal more than 20 times.

    2. Connect SST (Tascan) to DLC3 while the ignition switch is turned off.

      SST
      09991-70200

    3. Turn on the ignition switch.

      ■ Attention ■
      The engine does not start.

    4. Turn on the SST (Tascan) power switch, select and execute the menu (ABS · VSC) → air removal → VSC actuator.

  4. Inhaled air in actuator

    ■ Attention ■
    1. The air out of the actuator inhalor is performed in the two wheels (front and rear wheels) on the right side of the vehicle.


    1. On the menu screen of SST (Tascan), select and execute inhalation air.

    2. Loosen the buder plug on the right front wheel.

    3. The actuator is activated by pressing the executable key of SST (Tascan).

      □ Participation □
      1. Automatically stops in 4 seconds to protect the actuator.
      2. Release the brake pedal.

    4. Confirm the operation stop on the SST (Tascan) screen.

    5. Repeat the procedure from loosening the buder plug to the operation / stopping actuator until the air is completely out.

    6. Tighten the breeder plug.

      Reference value
      T = 8.0 n ・ m {82 kgf / cm}

    7. Press the return key of SST (Tascan).

    8. Work on the right rear wheel in the same procedure as the right front wheel.

  5. Intrauta -reduced pressure system air removal

    ■ Attention ■
    The air out of the actuator decompression system is performed for each wheel for all four wheels.


    1. Select a decompression air removal on the menu screen of SST (Tascan) and execute it.

    2. Loosen the buder plug on the right front wheel.

    3. Activate the actuator by inputting the executable key of the SST (Tascan), and at the same time, step in the brake pedal in the back.

      ■ Attention ■
      1. At this time, the brake pedal tremor is heavy, but step into the brake fluid from the buder plug.
      2. The brake pedal is kept and does not pedal.

      □ Participation □
      The actuator is automatically stopped in 4 seconds to protect solenoids.In addition, when operating continuously, the interval of 20 seconds or more is opened.

    4. Tighten the buder plug and release the brake pedal.

    5. From the buder plug to loosen the buder plug from the actuator -reduced pressure system, the breader plug is tightened and the brake pedal is released.

    6. Tighten the breeder plug.

      Reference value
      T = 8.0 n ・ m {82 kgf / cm}

    7. Press the return key of SST (Tascan).

    8. Work on the other three wheels in the same procedure as the right front wheel.

  6. Again, the normal brake system -based air is removed by pedaling of the brake pedal.

    ■ Attention ■
    The air is removed for all four wheels until the air is completely out of each wheel.

  7. Brake fluid quantity inspection

    1. Check the amount of brake fluid and replenish the brake fluid to the maxal position if necessary.
